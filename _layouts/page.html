💡 How to use these notes
  1. First pass — just listen. Press play and read along; the playing line is highlighted and follows the audio.
  2. Jump anywhere. Tap a sentence to play from there. Tap the playing line to pause — say it yourself — tap again to continue.
  3. Shadow line by line. Loop the current line and drop the speed to 0.75× in the toolbar until it sticks.
  4. Raise the ladder. Hide EN first, later 拼音 — until the 汉字 (or pure audio) is enough.
  5. Tune the page. Aa: bigger characters, tone colors (same as the videos), 拼音 above each character, three page layouts. Tap any dotted word for its meaning.
  6. Take it away. Export the word table to Anki with example sentences, or print a PDF of just the visible layers. The player has subtitles in 15 languages (CC).
