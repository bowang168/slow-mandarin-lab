Bundled fonts — SSOT 2026-07-19
================================
Hanzi default = FZKai · Hanzi option = QiuHong (Aa panel) · Pinyin = Andika only
· English/UI = Manrope. All faces are free for commercial use.

fzkai.woff2
  方正楷体简体 (FZKai-Z03S), subset to GB2312 + pinyin tone vowels + punctuation.
  One of Founder Group's four free-for-commercial-use fonts (方正黑体/书宋/仿宋/楷体,
  official statement by Beijing Founder Electronics). Embedded subset for this
  site only; not resold.

qiuhong.woff2
  演示秋鸿楷 (Slideqiuhong), subset as above. Free for commercial use, released
  by Keynote Research (秋叶系) with HanYi-derived brush-kai letterforms; see
  https://www.maoken.com/freefonts/6073.html for the grant. Loaded lazily —
  only downloaded when a reader picks 秋鸿楷 in the Aa panel. Same face as the
  video captions.

andika.woff2
  SIL Andika Regular (full character set, incl. all pinyin tone diacritics).
  Copyright (c) SIL International. SIL Open Font License 1.1.

manrope.woff2
  Manrope variable (200-800). Copyright (c) Mikhail Sharanda.
  SIL Open Font License 1.1.
