## In this episode 这一集

- Why forgetting is normal — words are like friends: meet once, you forget; meet often, you remember.
- Why reading ten times beats nothing, but **using a word once beats reading it ten times**.
- The easiest habit that actually works: **a little listening every day**.

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **如果你每天听一点，就会记住更多。**
> Rúguǒ nǐ měitiān tīng yìdiǎn, jiù huì jìzhù gèng duō.
> *If you listen a little every day, you'll remember more.*

## Key vocabulary 重点词

| 词 | Pinyin | English |
|---|---|---|
| 忘了 | wàng le | to forget; forgot |
| 记住 | jìzhù | to remember (keep in memory) |
| 记得 | jìde | to remember (recall) |
| 词 / 新词 | cí / xīn cí | word / new word |
| 办法 | bànfǎ | way, method |
| 常常 | chángcháng | often |
| 见 | jiàn | to see, to meet |
| 用 | yòng | to use |
| 每天 | měitiān | every day |
| 一点 | yìdiǎn | a little |
| 累 | lèi | tired |
| 订阅 | dìngyuè | to subscribe |
| 评论区 | pínglùnqū | comment section |

## Pattern of the day 今日句型：如果……就……

如果 A，就 B = *if A, then B*. 如果 opens the condition; 就 introduces what follows.
They live in the same sentence — listen for the pair.

1. 如果你每天听一点，就会记住更多。 — *If you listen a little every day, you'll remember more.*
2. 如果我每天走路，身体就会很好。 — *If I walk every day, I'll be healthy.*
3. 如果你今天很累，就早点睡觉。 — *If you're tired today, go to bed early.*
4. 如果你订阅，新的一集就会来找你。 — *If you subscribe, the new episode will come find you.*

Rúguǒ nǐ měitiān tīng yìdiǎn, jiù huì jìzhù gèng duō.
*If you listen a little every day, you'll remember more.*

**波哥：**如果你每天听一点，就会记住更多。
Rúguǒ nǐ měitiān tīng yìdiǎn, jiù huì jìzhù gèng duō.
*If you listen a little every day, you'll remember more.*

**莎莎：**你听，前面是"如果你每天听一点"。
Nǐ tīng, qiánmiàn shì "rúguǒ nǐ měitiān tīng yìdiǎn".
*Listen — the first half is "if you listen a little every day".*

**波哥：**后面是"就会记住更多"。
Hòumiàn shì "jiù huì jìzhù gèng duō".
*And the second half is "then you'll remember more".*

**莎莎：**"如果"和"就"，是一对好朋友，常常在一句话里。
"Rúguǒ" hé "jiù", shì yí duì hǎo péngyou, chángcháng zài yí jù huà lǐ.
*"Rúguǒ" and "jiù" are best friends — they usually live in the same sentence.*

**波哥：**那我试试说别的：如果我每天走路，身体就会很好。
Nà wǒ shìshi shuō biéde: rúguǒ wǒ měitiān zǒulù, shēntǐ jiù huì hěn hǎo.
*Let me try another one: if I walk every day, I'll be healthy.*

**莎莎：**太好了！再来一个。
Tài hǎo le! Zài lái yí ge.
*Excellent! One more.*

**波哥：**如果你今天很累，就早点睡觉。
Rúguǒ nǐ jīntiān hěn lèi, jiù zǎodiǎn shuìjiào.
*If you're tired today, go to bed early.*

**莎莎：**对！你看，"如果……就……"，什么都能说。
Duì! Nǐ kàn, "rúguǒ… jiù…", shénme dōu néng shuō.
*Right! See — with "rúguǒ… jiù…" you can say anything.*

**波哥：**莎莎，今天这三个办法，我觉得真的有用。
Shāsha, jīntiān zhè sān ge bànfǎ, wǒ juéde zhēnde yǒuyòng.
*Sasha, these three methods today are really useful.*

**莎莎：**如果你也觉得有用，就订阅我们。
Rúguǒ nǐ yě juéde yǒuyòng, jiù dìngyuè wǒmen.
*If you find them useful too, then subscribe.*

**波哥：**哈哈，你也用了"如果……就……"！
Hāhā, nǐ yě yòng le "rúguǒ… jiù…"!
*Haha, you just used "rúguǒ… jiù…" yourself!*

**莎莎：**对呀。如果你订阅，新的一集就会来找你。
Duì ya. Rúguǒ nǐ dìngyuè, xīn de yì jí jiù huì lái zhǎo nǐ.
*Exactly. If you subscribe, the new episode will come find you.*

**波哥：**我们每个星期都在这里，慢慢说中文。
Wǒmen měi ge xīngqī dōu zài zhèlǐ, mànmàn shuō Zhōngwén.
*We're here every week, speaking slow Chinese.*

**莎莎：**每天听一点，就从这里开始。
Měitiān tīng yìdiǎn, jiù cóng zhèlǐ kāishǐ.
*A little every day — start right here.*

### 快问快答 · Speed round

Answer out loud **before** Bo does.

**莎莎：**好，最后，快问快答！准备好了吗？
Hǎo, zuìhòu, kuài wèn kuài dá! Zhǔnbèi hǎo le ma?
*Okay, finally — the speed round! Ready?*

**波哥：**准备好了！
Zhǔnbèi hǎo le!
*Ready!*

**莎莎：**不记得了，用两个字怎么说？
Bú jìde le, yòng liǎng ge zì zěnme shuō?
*"I don't remember it anymore" — in two characters?*

**波哥：**忘了！
Wàng le!
*Forgot!*

**莎莎：**词为什么会忘？
Cí wèishénme huì wàng?
*Why do words get forgotten?*

**波哥：**因为见得太少了。
Yīnwèi jiàn de tài shǎo le.
*Because we meet them too few times.*

**莎莎：**办法一是什么？
Bànfǎ yī shì shénme?
*What's method one?*

**波哥：**让一个词，来见你很多次。
Ràng yí ge cí, lái jiàn nǐ hěn duō cì.
*Let a word meet you many times.*

**莎莎：**办法二呢？
Bànfǎ èr ne?
*And method two?*

**波哥：**用它说一句话，说给自己听也可以。
Yòng tā shuō yí jù huà, shuō gěi zìjǐ tīng yě kěyǐ.
*Use it in one sentence — even just to yourself.*

**莎莎：**办法三，你来说：如果……
Bànfǎ sān, nǐ lái shuō: rúguǒ…
*Method three — you say it: if…*

**波哥：**如果你每天听一点，就会记住更多！
Rúguǒ nǐ měitiān tīng yìdiǎn, jiù huì jìzhù gèng duō!
*If you listen a little every day, you'll remember more!*

**莎莎：**太好了，全对！

### 总结 · Recap

**莎莎：**好，今天我们说了：忘，不是你的问题，每个人都会忘。
Hǎo, jīntiān wǒmen shuō le: wàng, búshì nǐ de wèntí, měi ge rén dōu huì wàng.
*So, today we said: forgetting is not your problem — everyone forgets.*

**波哥：**词像朋友。见一次，会忘；常常见，就记住了。
Cí xiàng péngyou. Jiàn yí cì, huì wàng; chángcháng jiàn, jiù jìzhù le.
*Words are like friends. Meet once, forget; meet often, remember.*

**莎莎：**学一个词，就用它说一句话。
Xué yí ge cí, jiù yòng tā shuō yí jù huà.
*Learn a word, then use it in one sentence.*

**波哥：**还有最重要的：如果你每天听一点，就会记住更多。
Hái yǒu zuì zhòngyào de: rúguǒ nǐ měitiān tīng yìdiǎn, jiù huì jìzhù gèng duō.
*And the most important one: if you listen a little every day, you'll remember more.*

**莎莎：**别忘了那件小事：用"忘了"写一句话，放在评论区。
