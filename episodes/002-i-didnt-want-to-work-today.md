## In this episode 这一集

- Three calm ways to talk about a low-energy day: 我今天不太想上班 · 我今天状态不太好 · 我只是有点儿累.
- The 先……再…… pattern: turn one heavy day into one small step at a time.
- A story, not a lecture: Xiaolin doesn't become a hero — he just gets up first, then decides.

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **我先起来，再决定要不要请假。**
> Wǒ xiān qǐlái, zài juédìng yào bu yào qǐngjià.
> *I'll get up first, then decide whether to take the day off.*

## Key vocabulary 重点词

| 词 | Pinyin | English |
|---|---|---|
| 上班 | shàngbān | to go to work |
| 起床 | qǐchuáng | to get out of bed |
| 闹钟 | nàozhōng | alarm clock |
| 被子 | bèizi | quilt, duvet |
| 请假 | qǐngjià | to ask for leave |
| 有点儿累 | yǒudiǎnr lèi | a bit tired |
| 状态 | zhuàngtài | state, condition |
| 任务 | rènwù | task |
| 邮件 | yóujiàn | email |
| 同事 | tóngshì | colleague |
| 休息 | xiūxi | to rest |
| 决定 | juédìng | to decide |
| 完成 | wánchéng | to finish, complete |

## Pattern of the day 今日句型：先……再……

先 A，再 B = *first A, then B*. One small step, then the next — the pair stays in one sentence.

1. 我先起来，再决定要不要请假。 — *Get up first, then decide about the day off.*
2. 我先洗脸，再换衣服。 — *Wash my face first, then get changed.*
3. 我先吃一点儿东西，再看今天的任务。 — *Eat something first, then look at today's tasks.*
4. 我先做一件小事，再决定下一步。 — *Do one small thing first, then decide the next step.*

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**莎莎：**我今天不想上班。
Wǒ jīntiān bù xiǎng shàngbān.
*I don't want to go to work today.*

**波哥：**这句话，我想很多人都说过。
Zhè jù huà, wǒ xiǎng hěn duō rén dōu shuō guò.
*I think a lot of people have said that.*

**莎莎：**今天的故事里，小林在星期一早上说了这句话。
Jīntiān de gùshi lǐ, Xiǎolín zài xīngqīyī zǎoshang shuō le zhè jù huà.
*In today's story, Xiaolin says it on a Monday morning.*

**波哥：**那小林最后请假了吗？
Nà Xiǎolín zuìhòu qǐngjià le ma?
*So did Xiaolin take the day off in the end?*

**莎莎：**先别急。到了晚上，小林才明白，自己真正需要的是什么。
Xiān bié jí. Dào le wǎnshang, Xiǎolín cái míngbai, zìjǐ zhēnzhèng xūyào de shì shénme.
*Don't rush. Only by evening does Xiaolin understand what he really needs.*

**波哥：**好，我想听听看。
Hǎo, wǒ xiǎng tīngting kàn.
*Okay, I want to hear this.*

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文，我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén, wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**大家好，我是波哥。
Dàjiā hǎo, wǒ shì Bōgē.
*Hi everyone, I'm Bo.*

**莎莎：**这是一个虚构的小故事。小林今天在家工作。
Zhè shì yí ge xūgòu de xiǎo gùshi. Xiǎolín jīntiān zài jiā gōngzuò.
*This is a fictional little story. Xiaolin works from home today.*

**波哥：**明白了。那天早上发生了什么？
Míngbai le. Nà tiān zǎoshang fāshēng le shénme?
*Got it. So what happened that morning?*

### 星期一早上 · Monday morning

**莎莎：**星期一早上，天还没有完全亮。
Xīngqīyī zǎoshang, tiān hái méiyǒu wánquán liàng.
*Monday morning — the sky isn't fully light yet.*

**波哥：**小林的闹钟响了。
Xiǎolín de nàozhōng xiǎng le.
*Xiaolin's alarm goes off.*

**莎莎：**对。闹钟响了一次，小林没有起床。
Duì. Nàozhōng xiǎng le yí cì, Xiǎolín méiyǒu qǐchuáng.
*Right. The alarm rings once — Xiaolin doesn't get up.*

**波哥：**然后呢？
Ránhòu ne?
*And then?*

**莎莎：**过了五分钟，闹钟又响了。
Guò le wǔ fēnzhōng, nàozhōng yòu xiǎng le.
*Five minutes later, it rings again.*

**波哥：**小林还是没有起来？
Xiǎolín háishì méiyǒu qǐlái?
*And he still doesn't get up?*

**莎莎：**没有。小林把被子拉高了一点。
Méiyǒu. Xiǎolín bǎ bèizi lā gāo le yìdiǎn.
*No. He pulls the quilt up a little higher.*

**波哥：**我已经看到那个画面了。
Wǒ yǐjīng kàndào nàge huàmiàn le.
*I can already picture it.*

**莎莎：**窗外在下雨。房间里很安静，也很暖和。
Chuāng wài zài xiàyǔ. Fángjiān lǐ hěn ānjìng, yě hěn nuǎnhuo.
*It's raining outside. The room is quiet and warm.*

**波哥：**外面又冷又湿，被子里又暖又舒服。
Wàimiàn yòu lěng yòu shī, bèizi lǐ yòu nuǎn yòu shūfu.
*Cold and wet outside; warm and cosy under the quilt.*

**莎莎：**所以第三次听到闹钟时，小林说："我今天不想上班。"
Suǒyǐ dì sān cì tīngdào nàozhōng shí, Xiǎolín shuō: "Wǒ jīntiān bù xiǎng shàngbān."
*So on the third alarm, Xiaolin says: "I don't want to go to work today."*

**波哥：**是不想上班，还是不能上班？
Shì bù xiǎng shàngbān, háishì bù néng shàngbān?
*Is it that he doesn't want to work — or that he can't?*

**莎莎：**这个问题很重要。小林没有生病，也不是不能上班。
Zhège wèntí hěn zhòngyào. Xiǎolín méiyǒu shēngbìng, yě búshì bù néng shàngbān.
*Important question. Xiaolin isn't sick, and it's not that he can't work.*

**波哥：**只是没有力气，不想离开床。
Zhǐshì méiyǒu lìqi, bù xiǎng líkāi chuáng.
*He just has no energy and doesn't want to leave the bed.*

**莎莎：**对。小林又说："我只是有点儿累。"
Duì. Xiǎolín yòu shuō: "Wǒ zhǐshì yǒudiǎnr lèi."
*Right. Then he says: "I'm just a bit tired."*

**波哥：**"有点儿累"比"我累死了"平静多了。
"Yǒudiǎnr lèi" bǐ "wǒ lèi sǐ le" píngjìng duō le.
*"A bit tired" sounds much calmer than "I'm dead tired".*

**莎莎：**也更适合这个早上。小林没有遇到大问题，只是状态不太好。
Yě gèng shìhé zhège zǎoshang. Xiǎolín méiyǒu yùdào dà wèntí, zhǐshì zhuàngtài bú tài hǎo.
*And it fits this morning better. Nothing big is wrong — he's just not at his best.*

**波哥：**状态不太好，就是今天身体和心情都不在最好的时候。
Zhuàngtài bú tài hǎo, jiùshì jīntiān shēntǐ hé xīnqíng dōu bú zài zuì hǎo de shíhou.
*"Zhuàngtài bú tài hǎo" means body and mood are both a bit off today.*

### 小任务 · Your turn

**莎莎：**对。现在轮到你了。用"我今天不太想……"造一个句子，再说一说为什么。把你的句子放在评论区。
Duì. Xiànzài lúndào nǐ le. Yòng "wǒ jīntiān bú tài xiǎng……" zào yí ge jùzi, zài shuō yi shuō wèishénme. Bǎ nǐ de jùzi fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Right. Now it's your turn: make a sentence with "I don't really feel like… today", say why, and post it in the comments.*

**波哥：**比如，"我今天不太想做饭，因为我有点儿累。"
Bǐrú, "Wǒ jīntiān bú tài xiǎng zuòfàn, yīnwèi wǒ yǒudiǎnr lèi."
*For example: "I don't really feel like cooking today, because I'm a bit tired."*

### 先起来，再决定 · Get up first, then decide

**莎莎：**很好。回到故事。小林躺在床上，开始想今天的工作。
Hěn hǎo. Huídào gùshi. Xiǎolín tǎng zài chuáng shàng, kāishǐ xiǎng jīntiān de gōngzuò.
*Nice. Back to the story. Lying in bed, Xiaolin starts thinking about today's work.*

**波哥：**是不是一想到工作，就觉得更累了？
Shì bu shì yì xiǎngdào gōngzuò, jiù juéde gèng lèi le?
*And the moment he thinks about work, he feels even more tired?*

**莎莎：**是的。上午有一个会，还要回几封邮件。
Shì de. Shàngwǔ yǒu yí ge huì, hái yào huí jǐ fēng yóujiàn.
*Yes. There's a meeting in the morning, plus some emails to answer.*

**波哥：**下午呢？
Xiàwǔ ne?
*And the afternoon?*

**莎莎：**下午要完成一个小任务。事情其实不算多。
Xiàwǔ yào wánchéng yí ge xiǎo rènwù. Shìqing qíshí bú suàn duō.
*One small task to finish. It's really not that much.*

**波哥：**但是所有事情一起跑进脑子里，就好像很多。
Dànshì suǒyǒu shìqing yìqǐ pǎo jìn nǎozi lǐ, jiù hǎoxiàng hěn duō.
*But when everything rushes into your head at once, it feels like a lot.*

**莎莎：**没错。小林越想，越不想起床。
Méi cuò. Xiǎolín yuè xiǎng, yuè bù xiǎng qǐchuáng.
*Exactly. The more he thinks, the less he wants to get up.*

**波哥：**那就请假吧？
Nà jiù qǐngjià ba?
*So — just take the day off?*

**莎莎：**小林也想到了请假。
Xiǎolín yě xiǎngdào le qǐngjià.
*Xiaolin thinks about that too.*

**波哥：**请假，就是告诉公司，今天需要休息，或者不能工作。
Qǐngjià, jiùshì gàosu gōngsī, jīntiān xūyào xiūxi, huòzhě bù néng gōngzuò.
*"Qǐngjià" means telling your company you need to rest today, or can't work.*

**莎莎：**有时候是不能去，有时候是需要休息。
Yǒushíhou shì bù néng qù, yǒushíhou shì xūyào xiūxi.
*Sometimes you can't go; sometimes you need the rest.*

**波哥：**那小林是哪一种？
Nà Xiǎolín shì nǎ yì zhǒng?
*Which one is Xiaolin?*

**莎莎：**小林坐起来，认真感受了一下。
Xiǎolín zuò qǐlái, rènzhēn gǎnshòu le yíxià.
*He sits up and honestly checks how he feels.*

**波哥：**头疼吗？
Tóu téng ma?
*Headache?*

**莎莎：**不疼。发烧吗？也没有。
Bù téng. Fāshāo ma? Yě méiyǒu.
*No. Fever? No.*

**波哥：**只是累，而且不太想去。
Zhǐshì lèi, érqiě bú tài xiǎng qù.
*Just tired — and not really wanting to go.*

**莎莎：**对。小林没有马上决定，也没有马上责怪自己。
Duì. Xiǎolín méiyǒu mǎshàng juédìng, yě méiyǒu mǎshàng zéguài zìjǐ.
*Right. He doesn't decide right away — and he doesn't blame himself right away either.*

**波哥：**没有说"我怎么这么懒"。
Méiyǒu shuō "wǒ zěnme zhème lǎn".
*He doesn't say "why am I so lazy".*

**莎莎：**没有。小林先问："我现在最需要什么？"
Méiyǒu. Xiǎolín xiān wèn: "Wǒ xiànzài zuì xūyào shénme?"
*No. First he asks: "What do I need most right now?"*

**波哥：**答案是什么？
Dá'àn shì shénme?
*And the answer?*

**莎莎：**答案很简单。先下床，喝一点儿水。
Dá'àn hěn jiǎndān. Xiān xià chuáng, hē yìdiǎnr shuǐ.
*Very simple: get out of bed first, drink some water.*

**波哥：**不是先解决一整天，只是先下床。
Búshì xiān jiějué yì zhěng tiān, zhǐshì xiān xià chuáng.
*Not solving the whole day first — just getting out of bed first.*

**莎莎：**对。小林对自己说："我先起来，再决定要不要请假。"
Duì. Xiǎolín duì zìjǐ shuō: "Wǒ xiān qǐlái, zài juédìng yào bu yào qǐngjià."
*Right. He tells himself: "I'll get up first, then decide about the day off."*

**波哥：**这句很好。先起来，再决定。
Zhè jù hěn hǎo. Xiān qǐlái, zài juédìng.
*Great sentence. Get up first, then decide.*

**莎莎：**小林把脚放到地上。地板有一点儿凉。
Xiǎolín bǎ jiǎo fàng dào dì shàng. Dìbǎn yǒu yìdiǎnr liáng.
*He puts his feet on the floor. It's a little cold.*

**波哥：**这下真的醒了。
Zhè xià zhēnde xǐng le.
*Now he's really awake.*

**莎莎：**小林走到厨房，喝了一杯温水。
Xiǎolín zǒu dào chúfáng, hē le yì bēi wēn shuǐ.
*He walks to the kitchen and drinks a glass of warm water.*

**波哥：**然后马上充满了力量？
Ránhòu mǎshàng chōngmǎn le lìliàng?
*And then he's instantly full of energy?*

**莎莎：**没有。生活没有那么快。
Méiyǒu. Shēnghuó méiyǒu nàme kuài.
*No. Life doesn't work that fast.*

**波哥：**很好，这样比较真实。
Hěn hǎo, zhèyàng bǐjiào zhēnshí.
*Good — that's more realistic.*

**莎莎：**小林还是累。但是，已经不在床上了。
Xiǎolín háishì lèi. Dànshì, yǐjīng bú zài chuáng shàng le.
*He's still tired. But he's not in bed anymore.*

**波哥：**下一步做什么？
Xià yí bù zuò shénme?
*What's the next step?*

**莎莎：**小林说："我先洗脸，再换衣服。"
Xiǎolín shuō: "Wǒ xiān xǐliǎn, zài huàn yīfu."
*He says: "I'll wash my face first, then get changed."*

**波哥：**先洗脸，再换衣服。
Xiān xǐliǎn, zài huàn yīfu.
*Wash first, then change.*

**莎莎：**洗完脸以后，小林觉得清醒了一点。
Xǐ wán liǎn yǐhòu, Xiǎolín juéde qīngxǐng le yìdiǎn.
*After washing his face, he feels a little clearer.*

**波哥：**然后呢？
Ránhòu ne?
*Then what?*

**莎莎：**小林又说："我先吃一点儿东西，再看今天的任务。"
Xiǎolín yòu shuō: "Wǒ xiān chī yìdiǎnr dōngxi, zài kàn jīntiān de rènwù."
*Then he says: "I'll eat something first, then look at today's tasks."*

**波哥：**每一次，都只想下一件小事。
Měi yí cì, dōu zhǐ xiǎng xià yí jiàn xiǎo shì.
*Each time, he only thinks about the next small thing.*

**莎莎：**是的。这不是假装自己不累。
Shì de. Zhè búshì jiǎzhuāng zìjǐ bú lèi.
*Yes. This isn't pretending he's not tired.*

**波哥：**也不是逼自己马上开心。
Yě búshì bī zìjǐ mǎshàng kāixīn.
*And it's not forcing himself to be instantly happy.*

**莎莎：**只是把很大的一天，变成一件一件的小事。
Zhǐshì bǎ hěn dà de yì tiān, biànchéng yí jiàn yí jiàn de xiǎo shì.
*It's just turning one big day into small things, one at a time.*

**波哥：**听到这里，我有一个问题。
Tīngdào zhèlǐ, wǒ yǒu yí ge wèntí.
*At this point I have a question.*

**莎莎：**什么问题？
Shénme wèntí?
*What is it?*

**波哥：**这是不是说，每次不想上班，都应该坚持工作？
Zhè shì bu shì shuō, měi cì bù xiǎng shàngbān, dōu yīnggāi jiānchí gōngzuò?
*Does this mean that every time you don't feel like working, you should push through?*

**莎莎：**不是。需要休息时，休息很重要。需要请假时，也可以请假。
Búshì. Xūyào xiūxi shí, xiūxi hěn zhòngyào. Xūyào qǐngjià shí, yě kěyǐ qǐngjià.
*No. When you need rest, rest matters. When you need a day off, take it.*

**波哥：**今天的故事不是叫大家带病工作。
Jīntiān de gùshi búshì jiào dàjiā dài bìng gōngzuò.
*Today's story is not telling anyone to work while sick.*

**莎莎：**对。小林只是先看看自己的状态，再做选择。
Duì. Xiǎolín zhǐshì xiān kànkan zìjǐ de zhuàngtài, zài zuò xuǎnzé.
*Right. Xiaolin just checks how he is first, then makes his choice.*

**波哥：**所以重点不是"必须去"，而是"先看清楚"。
Suǒyǐ zhòngdiǎn búshì "bìxū qù", érshì "xiān kàn qīngchu".
*So the point isn't "you must go" — it's "look clearly first".*

### 一件一件的小事 · One small thing at a time

**莎莎：**说得很好。吃过早饭，小林打开电脑，看了一眼今天的任务。
Shuō de hěn hǎo. Chī guò zǎofàn, Xiǎolín dǎkāi diànnǎo, kàn le yì yǎn jīntiān de rènwù.
*Well said. After breakfast, Xiaolin opens his laptop and glances at today's tasks.*

**波哥：**还是觉得很多吗？
Háishì juéde hěn duō ma?
*Does it still feel like a lot?*

**莎莎：**没有刚才那么多了。纸上只有三件事。
Méiyǒu gāngcái nàme duō le. Zhǐ shàng zhǐ yǒu sān jiàn shì.
*Not as much as before. On paper there are only three things.*

**波哥：**先做哪一件？
Xiān zuò nǎ yí jiàn?
*Which one first?*

**莎莎：**先回最简单的一封邮件。
Xiān huí zuì jiǎndān de yì fēng yóujiàn.
*The easiest email first.*

**波哥：**为什么选最简单的？
Wèishénme xuǎn zuì jiǎndān de?
*Why the easiest one?*

**莎莎：**因为小林现在需要一个容易开始的地方。
Yīnwèi Xiǎolín xiànzài xūyào yí ge róngyì kāishǐ de dìfang.
*Because right now he needs an easy place to start.*

**波哥：**先做一件小事，再做下一件。
Xiān zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì, zài zuò xià yí jiàn.
*Do one small thing first, then the next.*

**莎莎：**对。那封邮件只写了几句话。
Duì. Nà fēng yóujiàn zhǐ xiě le jǐ jù huà.
*Right. That email is only a few sentences.*

**波哥：**写完用了多久？
Xiě wán yòng le duō jiǔ?
*How long did it take?*

**莎莎：**不到五分钟。
Bú dào wǔ fēnzhōng.
*Less than five minutes.*

**波哥：**五分钟以后，一整天的问题解决了吗？
Wǔ fēnzhōng yǐhòu, yì zhěng tiān de wèntí jiějué le ma?
*And after five minutes, is the whole day solved?*

**莎莎：**当然没有。但是第一件事完成了。
Dāngrán méiyǒu. Dànshì dì yī jiàn shì wánchéng le.
*Of course not. But the first thing is done.*

**波哥：**完成一件小事，心里会轻一点儿。
Wánchéng yí jiàn xiǎo shì, xīnlǐ huì qīng yìdiǎnr.
*Finish one small thing, and your heart feels a little lighter.*

**莎莎：**小林也是这样。然后，小林给同事发了一条消息。
Xiǎolín yě shì zhèyàng. Ránhòu, Xiǎolín gěi tóngshì fā le yì tiáo xiāoxi.
*Same for Xiaolin. Then he sends a colleague a message.*

**波哥：**说了什么？
Shuō le shénme?
*Saying what?*

**莎莎：**小林说："我今天状态不太好，上午可能晚一点开始。"
Xiǎolín shuō: "Wǒ jīntiān zhuàngtài bú tài hǎo, shàngwǔ kěnéng wǎn yìdiǎn kāishǐ."
*He writes: "I'm not at my best today; I may start a bit late this morning."*

**波哥：**这句话很诚实，也没有说太多私人事情。
Zhè jù huà hěn chéngshí, yě méiyǒu shuō tài duō sīrén shìqing.
*Honest — and it doesn't overshare anything private.*

**莎莎：**同事很快回答："没问题，我们十点再聊。"
Tóngshì hěn kuài huídá: "Méi wèntí, wǒmen shí diǎn zài liáo."
*The colleague replies right away: "No problem, let's talk at ten."*

**波哥：**原来事情可以调整一点儿。
Yuánlái shìqing kěyǐ tiáozhěng yìdiǎnr.
*So things can be adjusted a little after all.*

**莎莎：**可以。小林不用假装今天和每天一样。
Kěyǐ. Xiǎolín búyòng jiǎzhuāng jīntiān hé měitiān yíyàng.
*They can. Xiaolin doesn't have to pretend today is like every other day.*

**波哥：**也不用把今天说成世界上最糟的一天。
Yě búyòng bǎ jīntiān shuō chéng shìjiè shàng zuì zāo de yì tiān.
*And he doesn't have to call it the worst day in the world either.*

**莎莎：**对。如果你喜欢这种能听懂、又能马上开口说的慢速故事，就订阅慢慢中文。我们每周一起练真实生活里的中文。
Duì. Rúguǒ nǐ xǐhuan zhè zhǒng néng tīngdǒng, yòu néng mǎshàng kāikǒu shuō de mànsù gùshi, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒmen měi zhōu yìqǐ liàn zhēnshí shēnghuó lǐ de Zhōngwén.
*If you like slow stories you can understand and use right away, subscribe — every week we practise real-life Chinese together.*

### 走进这一天 · Walking into the day

**波哥：**现在，小林已经完成第一封邮件，也和同事说清楚了。
Xiànzài, Xiǎolín yǐjīng wánchéng dì yī fēng yóujiàn, yě hé tóngshì shuō qīngchu le.
*By now Xiaolin has finished the first email and cleared things with his colleague.*

**莎莎：**接下来，小林去参加上午的会。
Jiē xiàlái, Xiǎolín qù cānjiā shàngwǔ de huì.
*Next, he joins the morning meeting.*

**波哥：**会开得怎么样？
Huì kāi de zěnmeyàng?
*How does the meeting go?*

**莎莎：**很普通。没有大成功，也没有大问题。
Hěn pǔtōng. Méiyǒu dà chénggōng, yě méiyǒu dà wèntí.
*Very ordinary. No big win, no big problem.*

**波哥：**有时候，普通就是好消息。
Yǒushíhou, pǔtōng jiùshì hǎo xiāoxi.
*Sometimes, ordinary is good news.*

**莎莎：**会结束后，小林站起来，走到窗边。
Huì jiéshù hòu, Xiǎolín zhàn qǐlái, zǒu dào chuāng biān.
*After the meeting, he stands up and walks to the window.*

**波哥：**外面还在下雨吗？
Wàimiàn hái zài xiàyǔ ma?
*Still raining outside?*

**莎莎：**雨已经小了。街上的人也多了起来。
Yǔ yǐjīng xiǎo le. Jiē shàng de rén yě duō le qǐlái.
*The rain has eased. More people are out on the street.*

**波哥：**小林现在还不想上班吗？
Xiǎolín xiànzài hái bù xiǎng shàngbān ma?
*Does he still not want to work?*

**莎莎：**还是不太想。但是，那种很重的感觉变小了。
Háishì bú tài xiǎng. Dànshì, nà zhǒng hěn zhòng de gǎnjué biàn xiǎo le.
*Still not really. But that heavy feeling has shrunk.*

**波哥：**工作没有突然变有趣，心情也没有突然变完美。
Gōngzuò méiyǒu tūrán biàn yǒuqù, xīnqíng yě méiyǒu tūrán biàn wánměi.
*The job didn't suddenly become fun, and his mood didn't suddenly become perfect.*

**莎莎：**对。小林只是一步一步地走进了这一天。
Duì. Xiǎolín zhǐshì yí bù yí bù de zǒu jìn le zhè yì tiān.
*Right. He just walked into the day, one step at a time.*

**波哥：**下午的小任务完成了吗？
Xiàwǔ de xiǎo rènwù wánchéng le ma?
*Did he finish the afternoon task?*

**莎莎：**完成了一部分。剩下的，小林和同事约好明天继续。
Wánchéng le yí bùfen. Shèng xià de, Xiǎolín hé tóngshì yuē hǎo míngtiān jìxù.
*Part of it. For the rest, he and his colleague agreed to continue tomorrow.*

**波哥：**没有为了证明自己，一定要全部做完。
Méiyǒu wèile zhèngmíng zìjǐ, yídìng yào quánbù zuò wán.
*He didn't force himself to finish everything just to prove something.*

**莎莎：**没有。晚上六点，小林关上电脑。
Méiyǒu. Wǎnshang liù diǎn, Xiǎolín guān shàng diànnǎo.
*No. At six, he closes the laptop.*

**波哥：**终于下班了。
Zhōngyú xiàbān le.
*Finally off work.*

**莎莎：**小林没有马上看手机，而是安静地坐了一会儿。
Xiǎolín méiyǒu mǎshàng kàn shǒujī, érshì ānjìng de zuò le yíhuìr.
*He doesn't reach for his phone — he just sits quietly for a while.*

**波哥：**然后，小林明白了什么？
Ránhòu, Xiǎolín míngbai le shénme?
*And then — what does he realise?*

**莎莎：**小林发现，自己不是讨厌这份工作。
Xiǎolín fāxiàn, zìjǐ búshì tǎoyàn zhè fèn gōngzuò.
*He realises he doesn't actually hate this job.*

**波哥：**也不是一个懒人。
Yě búshì yí ge lǎn rén.
*And he's not a lazy person.*

**莎莎：**只是前几天睡得太晚，也很久没有好好休息。
Zhǐshì qián jǐ tiān shuì de tài wǎn, yě hěn jiǔ méiyǒu hǎohǎo xiūxi.
*He's just been sleeping too late, and hasn't rested properly in a long time.*

**波哥：**所以早上的"我不想上班"，其实是在说"我需要休息"。
Suǒyǐ zǎoshang de "wǒ bù xiǎng shàngbān", qíshí shì zài shuō "wǒ xūyào xiūxi".
*So the morning's "I don't want to work" was really saying "I need rest".*

**莎莎：**有时候是这样。但每个人的情况不同，不能只靠一句话判断。
Yǒushíhou shì zhèyàng. Dàn měi ge rén de qíngkuàng bù tóng, bù néng zhǐ kào yí jù huà pànduàn.
*Sometimes. But everyone's situation is different — one sentence can't tell you everything.*

**波哥：**小林能做的，是听见自己的感受，再安排明天。
Xiǎolín néng zuò de, shì tīngjiàn zìjǐ de gǎnshòu, zài ānpái míngtiān.
*What Xiaolin can do is hear how he feels, then plan tomorrow.*

**莎莎：**小林决定今晚早点儿睡。
Xiǎolín juédìng jīnwǎn zǎodiǎnr shuì.
*He decides to sleep early tonight.*

**波哥：**还决定了什么？
Hái juédìng le shénme?
*What else?*

**莎莎：**明天先完成最重要的任务，其他事情慢慢来。
Míngtiān xiān wánchéng zuì zhòngyào de rènwù, qítā shìqing mànmàn lái.
*Tomorrow: most important task first — everything else, slowly.*

### 回想 · Recap

Answer out loud **before** Bo does.

**波哥：**我们来回想一下今天的几个句子。
Wǒmen lái huíxiǎng yíxià jīntiān de jǐ ge jùzi.
*Let's recall today's key sentences.*

**莎莎：**第一句，"我今天不太想上班。"
Dì yī jù, "Wǒ jīntiān bú tài xiǎng shàngbān."
*Sentence one: "I don't really feel like working today."*

**波哥：**第二句，"我今天状态不太好。"
Dì èr jù, "Wǒ jīntiān zhuàngtài bú tài hǎo."
*Sentence two: "I'm not at my best today."*

**莎莎：**第三句，"我只是有点儿累。"
Dì sān jù, "Wǒ zhǐshì yǒudiǎnr lèi."
*Sentence three: "I'm just a bit tired."*

**波哥：**第四句，"我先做一件小事。"
Dì sì jù, "Wǒ xiān zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì."
*Sentence four: "I'll do one small thing first."*

**莎莎：**现在你来说。怎么表达今天没有很想工作？
Xiànzài nǐ lái shuō. Zěnme biǎodá jīntiān méiyǒu hěn xiǎng gōngzuò?
*Now you. How do you say you don't much feel like working today?*

**波哥：**我今天不太想上班。
Wǒ jīntiān bú tài xiǎng shàngbān.
*I don't really feel like working today.*

**莎莎：**怎么平静地说明自己的感觉？
Zěnme píngjìng de shuōmíng zìjǐ de gǎnjué?
*How do you calmly describe how you feel?*

**波哥：**我今天状态不太好。
Wǒ jīntiān zhuàngtài bú tài hǎo.
*I'm not at my best today.*

**莎莎：**怎么说一个简单的计划？
Zěnme shuō yí ge jiǎndān de jìhuà?
*How do you state one simple plan?*

**波哥：**我先做一件小事，再决定下一步。
Wǒ xiān zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì, zài juédìng xià yí bù.
*I'll do one small thing first, then decide the next step.*

**莎莎：**很好。故事开始时，闹钟响了三次，小林还是不想起床。
Hěn hǎo. Gùshi kāishǐ shí, nàozhōng xiǎng le sān cì, Xiǎolín háishì bù xiǎng qǐchuáng.
*Good. At the start, the alarm rang three times and Xiaolin still didn't want to get up.*

**波哥：**故事结束时，小林没有变成一个永远不会累的人。
Gùshi jiéshù shí, Xiǎolín méiyǒu biànchéng yí ge yǒngyuǎn bú huì lèi de rén.
*By the end, he hasn't become someone who never gets tired.*

**莎莎：**小林只学会了两件事。先说清楚自己的状态，再做一个小决定。
Xiǎolín zhǐ xuéhuì le liǎng jiàn shì. Xiān shuō qīngchu zìjǐ de zhuàngtài, zài zuò yí ge xiǎo juédìng.
*He only learned two things: say clearly how you are first, then make one small decision.*

**波哥：**这比一边责怪自己，一边什么都不做，更有帮助。
Zhè bǐ yìbiān zéguài zìjǐ, yìbiān shénme dōu bú zuò, gèng yǒu bāngzhù.
*That helps more than blaming yourself while doing nothing.*

### 结尾 · Ending

**莎莎：**下一集，我们聊一个很多人都想过的问题。
Xià yì jí, wǒmen liáo yí ge hěn duō rén dōu xiǎng guò de wèntí.

Měitiān zhǐ tīng shí fēnzhōng Zhōngwén, zhēnde gòu ma?
