## In this episode 这一集

- The maths of small habits: ten minutes a day is over **sixty hours a year**.
- Don't look for new time — put your ten minutes **next to a habit you already have**.
- The 越……越…… pattern: the more you listen, the more you understand.

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **你听得越多，懂得越多。**
> Nǐ tīng de yuè duō, dǒng de yuè duō.
> *The more you listen, the more you understand.*

## Pattern of the day 今日句型：越……越……

越 A 越 B = *the more A, the more B*. The two 越 live in the same sentence.

1. 你听得越多，懂得越多。 — *The more you listen, the more you understand.*
2. 我说得越多，说得越好。 — *The more I speak, the better I speak.*
3. 越听越懂，越懂越想听。 — *The more you listen the more you understand — and the more you want to listen.*
4. 天气越来越冷了。 — *(越来越 = more and more) It's getting colder and colder.*

Liǎng ge "yuè", shì yí duì hǎo péngyou, chángcháng zài yí jù huà lǐ.
*The two "yuè" are best friends — they live in the same sentence.*

**波哥：**越多，越多……那我试试别的：我说得越多，说得越好。
Yuè duō, yuè duō… nà wǒ shìshi biéde: wǒ shuō de yuè duō, shuō de yuè hǎo.
*Let me try another: the more I speak, the better I speak.*

**莎莎：**对！再来一个。
Duì! Zài lái yí ge.
*Right! One more.*

**波哥：**天气越来越冷了。
Tiānqì yuè lái yuè lěng le.
*The weather is getting colder and colder.*

**莎莎：**也对！"越来越"，就是一天比一天。
Yě duì! "Yuè lái yuè", jiùshì yì tiān bǐ yì tiān.
*Also right! "Yuè lái yuè" means day by day, more and more.*

**波哥：**那十分钟呢？十分钟会越来越有用吗？
Nà shí fēnzhōng ne? Shí fēnzhōng huì yuè lái yuè yǒuyòng ma?
*And the ten minutes? Do they get more and more useful?*

**莎莎：**会。第一个月，你懂一点。第三个月，你懂得更多。
Huì. Dì yī ge yuè, nǐ dǒng yìdiǎn. Dì sān ge yuè, nǐ dǒng de gèng duō.
*They do. Month one, you understand a little. Month three, much more.*

**波哥：**越听越懂，越懂越想听。
Yuè tīng yuè dǒng, yuè dǒng yuè xiǎng tīng.
*The more you listen the more you understand — the more you understand, the more you want to listen.*

**莎莎：**这句说得漂亮！就是这样，一个好的圈。
Zhè jù shuō de piàoliang! Jiùshì zhèyàng, yí ge hǎo de quān.
*Beautifully put! Exactly — a good circle.*

**波哥：**说到这里，我发现一件事。
Shuō dào zhèlǐ, wǒ fāxiàn yí jiàn shì.
*Which reminds me of something.*

**莎莎：**什么事？
Shénme shì?
*What?*

**波哥：**我们的一集，差不多就是十几分钟。
Wǒmen de yì jí, chàbuduō jiùshì shí jǐ fēnzhōng.
*One of our episodes is about ten-something minutes.*

**莎莎：**对呀。如果你想每天听一点，就订阅慢慢中文。
Duì ya. Rúguǒ nǐ xiǎng měitiān tīng yìdiǎn, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*Exactly. If you want a little every day, subscribe.*

**波哥：**你的十分钟，我们已经准备好了。
Nǐ de shí fēnzhōng, wǒmen yǐjīng zhǔnbèi hǎo le.
*Your ten minutes — we've already prepared them.*

**莎莎：**每个星期都有新的一集，慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Měi ge xīngqī dōu yǒu xīn de yì jí, mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*A new episode every week — spoken slowly, for slow listening.*

**波哥：**喝咖啡的时候，见！
Hē kāfēi de shíhou, jiàn!
*See you at coffee time!*

### 快问快答 · Speed round

Answer out loud **before** Bo does.

**莎莎：**好，最后，快问快答！准备好了吗？
Hǎo, zuìhòu, kuài wèn kuài dá! Zhǔnbèi hǎo le ma?
*Okay, the speed round! Ready?*

**波哥：**准备好了！
Zhǔnbèi hǎo le!
*Ready!*

**莎莎：**一天十分钟，一年是多少？
Yì tiān shí fēnzhōng, yì nián shì duōshǎo?
*Ten minutes a day — how much in a year?*

**波哥：**六十多个小时！
Liùshí duō ge xiǎoshí!
*Over sixty hours!*

**莎莎：**十分钟放在哪里最好？
Shí fēnzhōng fàng zài nǎlǐ zuì hǎo?
*Where do the ten minutes go?*

**波哥：**放在每天做的事情旁边。
Fàng zài měitiān zuò de shìqing pángbiān.
*Next to something you do every day.*

**莎莎：**比如？
Bǐrú?
*For example?*

**波哥：**喝咖啡的时候，坐车的时候，走路的时候。
Hē kāfēi de shíhou, zuòchē de shíhou, zǒulù de shíhou.
*With coffee, on the bus, while walking.*

**莎莎：**最重要的一句话，你来说：你听得越多……
Zuì zhòngyào de yí jù huà, nǐ lái shuō: nǐ tīng de yuè duō……
*The key sentence — you say it: the more you listen…*

**波哥：**懂得越多！
Dǒng de yuè duō!
*…the more you understand!*

**莎莎：**太好了，全对！
Tài hǎo le, quán duì!
*Perfect — all correct!*

### 结尾 · Ending

**波哥：**所以，十分钟够吗？
Suǒyǐ, shí fēnzhōng gòu ma?
*So — is ten minutes enough?*

**莎莎：**今天的十分钟很小，一年的十分钟是一座小山。
Jīntiān de shí fēnzhōng hěn xiǎo, yì nián de shí fēnzhōng shì yí zuò xiǎo shān.
*Today's ten minutes is tiny; a year of them is a small mountain.*

**波哥：**少，但是每天都在。
Shǎo, dànshì měitiān dōu zài.
*A little — but there every day.*

**莎莎：**学中文是走路，不是跑步。
Xué Zhōngwén shì zǒulù, búshì pǎobù.
*Learning Chinese is walking, not sprinting.*

**波哥：**你听得越多，懂得越多。
Nǐ tīng de yuè duō, dǒng de yuè duō.
*The more you listen, the more you understand.*

**莎莎：**别忘了你的小事：用"每天"写一句话，放在评论区。
Bié wàng le nǐ de xiǎo shì: yòng "měitiān" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Don't forget your little task: one "měitiān" sentence in the comments.*

**波哥：**今天没有下集预告，因为我们想问你一个问题。
Jīntiān méiyǒu xià jí yùgào, yīnwèi wǒmen xiǎng wèn nǐ yí ge wèntí.
*No preview today — because we want to ask you something.*

**莎莎：**你想让我们聊什么话题？写在评论区。
Nǐ xiǎng ràng wǒmen liáo shénme huàtí? Xiě zài pínglùnqū.
*What topic would you like us to cover? Tell us in the comments.*

**波哥：**我们会先看：这个话题适不适合频道，对学习有没有帮助。
Wǒmen huì xiān kàn: zhège huàtí shì bu shìhé píndào, duì xuéxí yǒu méiyǒu bāngzhù.
*We'll check whether it fits the channel and helps learning.*

**莎莎：**也会看它安不安全，是不是我们自己能做好的。
Yě huì kàn tā ān bu ānquán, shì bu shì wǒmen zìjǐ néng zuò hǎo de.
*And whether it's safe, and something we can do well ourselves.*

**波哥：**合适的话题，我们会放进节目单。不能保证每个都做，请别多次留言。
Héshì de huàtí, wǒmen huì fàng jìn jiémùdān. Bù néng bǎozhèng měi ge dōu zuò, qǐng bié duō cì liúyán.
*Suitable topics go into our list. We can't promise every one — one comment is enough.*

**莎莎：**谢谢你和我们在一起。我是莎莎。
Xièxie nǐ hé wǒmen zài yìqǐ. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Thanks for spending time with us. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。我们下期再见。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Wǒmen xià qī zàijiàn.
*I'm Bo. See you next time.*

**莎莎：**下次见。慢慢来。
Xiàcì jiàn. Mànmàn lái.
*See you next time. Take it slow.*
