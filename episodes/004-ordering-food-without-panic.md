A slow Mandarin role-play for adult learners. Every line is shown three ways: **汉字 Hanzi**,
pinyin, and *a short English gloss* — the same three layers you see on screen in the video.

> **Pinyin note:** we write 一 and 不 with the tone you actually hear (yí ge, búyào).
> Neutral-tone syllables have no tone mark (shíhou, míngzi).

3. **Speak** — role-play both sides of the speed round, then post your 我要…… sentence.

## In this episode 这一集

- The **five sentences** that get you through any Chinese restaurant.
- The pointing trick: 手指是最好的翻译 — *your finger is the best translator*.
- One magic question that turns every meal into a Chinese lesson.

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **我要一个这个，不要辣。**
> Wǒ yào yí ge zhège, búyào là.
> *I'll have one of this — not spicy, please.*

**The five sentences 五句话**

| 句子 | Pinyin | When |
|---|---|---|
| 两位。 | Liǎng wèi. | walking in (two people) |
| 有菜单吗？ | Yǒu càidān ma? | sitting down |
| 我要这个。 | Wǒ yào zhège. | ordering (point!) |
| 不要辣。 | Búyào là. | what you don't want |
| 服务员，买单。 | Fúwùyuán, mǎidān. | getting the bill |

Bonus: **这个用中文怎么说？** Zhège yòng Zhōngwén zěnme shuō? — *How do you say this in Chinese?*

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**波哥：**莎莎，我跟你说一件事，你别笑。
Shāsha, wǒ gēn nǐ shuō yí jiàn shì, nǐ bié xiào.
*Sasha, I'll tell you something — don't laugh.*

**莎莎：**好，我不笑，你说。
Hǎo, wǒ bú xiào, nǐ shuō.
*Okay, I won't laugh. Go on.*

**波哥：**我去中国饭馆的时候，很紧张。
Wǒ qù Zhōngguó fànguǎn de shíhou, hěn jǐnzhāng.
*When I go to Chinese restaurants, I get really nervous.*

**莎莎：**紧张？为什么？
Jǐnzhāng? Wèishénme?
*Nervous? Why?*

**波哥：**菜单全是汉字。服务员说话很快。
Càidān quán shì Hànzì. Fúwùyuán shuōhuà hěn kuài.
*The menu is all Hanzi. The waiter talks fast.*

**莎莎：**嗯，然后呢？
Ǹg, ránhòu ne?
*Mm, and then?*

**波哥：**所有人都在等我。我一句话都说不出来。
Suǒyǒu rén dōu zài děng wǒ. Wǒ yí jù huà dōu shuō bu chūlái.
*Everyone's waiting for me — and I can't get a single word out.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，对不起，我还是笑了。
Hāhā, duìbuqǐ, wǒ háishì xiào le.
*Haha — sorry, I laughed anyway.*

**波哥：**你看！所以很多人不敢去中国饭馆。
Nǐ kàn! Suǒyǐ hěn duō rén bù gǎn qù Zhōngguó fànguǎn.
*See! That's why many people don't dare go.*

**莎莎：**其实，点菜只需要五句话。
Qíshí, diǎn cài zhǐ xūyào wǔ jù huà.
*Actually, ordering only takes five sentences.*

**波哥：**五句话？就五句？
Wǔ jù huà? Jiù wǔ jù?
*Five? Just five?*

**莎莎：**对。今天我们一句一句练。最后一句，最好用。
Duì. Jīntiān wǒmen yí jù yí jù liàn. Zuìhòu yí jù, zuì hǎo yòng.
*Yes. Today we practise them one by one. The last one is the most useful.*

**波哥：**好，这一集我等了很久了。
Hǎo, zhè yì jí wǒ děng le hěn jiǔ le.
*Great — I've been waiting for this episode.*

### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。在这里，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Zài zhèlǐ, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*I'm Bo. Here, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**今天是一个真实场景：在饭馆点菜。
Jīntiān shì yí ge zhēnshí chǎngjǐng: zài fànguǎn diǎn cài.
*Today is a real-life scene: ordering at a restaurant.*

**波哥：**我们开始吧，我都饿了。
Wǒmen kāishǐ ba, wǒ dōu è le.
*Let's start — I'm already hungry.*

### 进门：两位 · Walking in

**莎莎：**好。你现在站在饭馆门口。我是服务员。
Hǎo. Nǐ xiànzài zhàn zài fànguǎn ménkǒu. Wǒ shì fúwùyuán.
*Okay. You're standing at the restaurant door. I'm the waiter.*

**波哥：**啊，现在就开始演吗？
À, xiànzài jiù kāishǐ yǎn ma?
*Wait — we're role-playing right now?*

**莎莎：**对，别怕。你好！几位？
Duì, bié pà. Nǐ hǎo! Jǐ wèi?
*Yes — don't panic. Hello! How many?*

**波哥：**几位……是问有几个人吗？
Jǐ wèi… shì wèn yǒu jǐ ge rén ma?
*"Jǐ wèi"… that's asking how many people?*

**莎莎：**对。你一个人，就说：一位。两个人呢？
Duì. Nǐ yí ge rén, jiù shuō: yí wèi. Liǎng ge rén ne?
*Right. Alone, you say: one. And for two people?*

**波哥：**两位！
Liǎng wèi!
*Two!*

**莎莎：**对，就这么简单。来，我们再来一次。你好，几位？
Duì, jiù zhème jiǎndān. Lái, wǒmen zài lái yí cì. Nǐ hǎo, jǐ wèi?
*That simple. Again: hello, how many?*

**波哥：**两位。
Liǎng wèi.
*Two.*

**莎莎：**这边请。你看，第一句只有两个字。
Zhè biān qǐng. Nǐ kàn, dì yī jù zhǐ yǒu liǎng ge zì.
*This way, please. See — sentence one is only two characters.*

**波哥：**两位。我会了。
Liǎng wèi. Wǒ huì le.
*"Liǎng wèi." Got it.*

**莎莎：**坐下以后，你可以说第二句：有菜单吗？
Zuò xià yǐhòu, nǐ kěyǐ shuō dì èr jù: yǒu càidān ma?
*Once seated, sentence two: do you have a menu?*

**波哥：**有菜单吗？
Yǒu càidān ma?
*Do you have a menu?*

**莎莎：**对。菜单，就是写着所有菜的那张纸。
Duì. Càidān, jiùshì xiě zhe suǒyǒu cài de nà zhāng zhǐ.
*Right. The menu is the sheet with all the dishes on it.*

**波哥：**现在很多饭馆，菜单在手机里。
Xiànzài hěn duō fànguǎn, càidān zài shǒujī lǐ.
*These days the menu is often on your phone.*

**莎莎：**对，那就更容易了，慢慢看，不用急。
Duì, nà jiù gèng róngyì le, mànmàn kàn, búyòng jí.
*Even easier — browse slowly, no rush.*

### 点菜：我要这个 · Ordering by pointing

**波哥：**好，菜单来了。可是……我看不懂菜名。
Hǎo, càidān lái le. Kěshì… wǒ kàn bu dǒng càimíng.
*Okay, the menu's here. But… I can't read the dish names.*

**莎莎：**没关系。这就是今天最重要的办法。
Méi guānxi. Zhè jiùshì jīntiān zuì zhòngyào de bànfǎ.
*No problem. This is today's most important trick.*

**波哥：**什么办法？
Shénme bànfǎ?
*What trick?*

**莎莎：**你的手。
Nǐ de shǒu.
*Your hand.*

**波哥：**我的手？
Wǒ de shǒu?
*My hand?*

**莎莎：**对。手指着菜单，说：我要这个。
Duì. Shǒu zhǐ zhe càidān, shuō: wǒ yào zhège.
*Yes. Point at the menu and say: I'll have this.*

**波哥：**我要这个？就这样？
Wǒ yào zhège? Jiù zhèyàng?
*"I'll have this"? That's it?*

**莎莎：**就这样。手指是最好的翻译。
Jiù zhèyàng. Shǒuzhǐ shì zuì hǎo de fānyì.
*That's it. Your finger is the best translator.*

**波哥：**哈哈，手指是最好的翻译！
Hāhā, shǒuzhǐ shì zuì hǎo de fānyì!
*Haha — the best translator!*

**莎莎：**菜单上有图片的时候，这个办法最好用。
Càidān shàng yǒu túpiàn de shíhou, zhège bànfǎ zuì hǎo yòng.
*It works best when the menu has pictures.*

**波哥：**好，我试试。你现在是服务员。
Hǎo, wǒ shìshi. Nǐ xiànzài shì fúwùyuán.
*Let me try. You're the waiter now.*

**莎莎：**你好，想点什么？
Nǐ hǎo, xiǎng diǎn shénme?
*Hello, what would you like?*

**波哥：**我要这个。
Wǒ yào zhège.
*I'll have this.*

**莎莎：**好的。还要别的吗？
Hǎo de. Hái yào biéde ma?
*Sure. Anything else?*

**波哥：**嗯……我还要那个。
Ǹg… wǒ hái yào nàge.
*Hmm… I'll also have that.*

**莎莎：**很好！"这个"和"那个"，你都用了。
Hěn hǎo! "Zhège" hé "nàge", nǐ dōu yòng le.
*Nice — you used both "this" and "that".*

**波哥：**近的是这个，远的是那个。
Jìn de shì zhège, yuǎn de shì nàge.
*Near is "zhège"; far is "nàge".*

### 小任务 · Your turn

**莎莎：**对。现在，请你做一件小事。
Duì. Xiànzài, qǐng nǐ zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì.
*Right. Now, one small thing.*

**波哥：**评论区时间到了。
Pínglùnqū shíjiān dào le.
*Comment-section time.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，对。用"我要"写一句话，放在评论区。
Hāhā, duì. Yòng "wǒ yào" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Haha, yes. Write one sentence with "wǒ yào" in the comments.*

**波哥：**我先来：我要一杯茶。
Wǒ xiān lái: wǒ yào yì bēi chá.
*I'll go first: I'd like a cup of tea.*

**莎莎：**很好。再来一句？
Hěn hǎo. Zài lái yí jù?
*Nice. One more?*

**波哥：**我要两个包子！
Wǒ yào liǎng ge bāozi!
*I'll have two baozi!*

**莎莎：**哈哈，你真的饿了。你也写一句"我要……"，我们会看，也会回你。
Hāhā, nǐ zhēnde è le. Nǐ yě xiě yí jù "wǒ yào……", wǒmen huì kàn, yě huì huí nǐ.
*Haha, you really are hungry. You write one too — we read and reply.*

### 不要辣 · What you don't want

**波哥：**莎莎，我有一个大问题。
Shāsha, wǒ yǒu yí ge dà wèntí.
*Sasha, I have a big problem.*

**莎莎：**你说。
Nǐ shuō.
*Go on.*

**波哥：**我不能吃辣。怎么说？
Wǒ bù néng chī là. Zěnme shuō?
*I can't eat spicy food. How do I say that?*

**莎莎：**三个字：不要辣。
Sān ge zì: búyào là.
*Three characters: not spicy.*

**波哥：**不要辣。就这么直接？
Búyào là. Jiù zhème zhíjiē?
*"Búyào là." That direct?*

**莎莎：**对，点菜的时候，直接说，很正常。
Duì, diǎn cài de shíhou, zhíjiē shuō, hěn zhèngcháng.
*Yes — when ordering, being direct is completely normal.*

**波哥：**那我们把它放进一句话里？
Nà wǒmen bǎ tā fàng jìn yí jù huà lǐ?
*Can we put it into one sentence?*

**莎莎：**好。你听：我要一个这个，不要辣。
Hǎo. Nǐ tīng: wǒ yào yí ge zhège, búyào là.
*Sure. Listen: I'll have one of this — not spicy.*

**波哥：**我要一个这个，不要辣。
Wǒ yào yí ge zhège, búyào là.
*I'll have one of this — not spicy.*

**莎莎：**前面说你要什么，后面说你不要什么。
Qiánmiàn shuō nǐ yào shénme, hòumiàn shuō nǐ búyào shénme.
*First half: what you want. Second half: what you don't.*

**波哥：**要的和不要的，一句话说完。
Yào de hé búyào de, yí jù huà shuō wán.
*Wants and don't-wants — one sentence covers both.*

**莎莎：**对。你再试一句？
Duì. Nǐ zài shì yí jù?
*Try another?*

**波哥：**我要一个这个，不要肉。
Wǒ yào yí ge zhège, búyào ròu.
*One of this — no meat.*

**莎莎：**很好！不吃肉的朋友，就这么说。
Hěn hǎo! Bù chī ròu de péngyou, jiù zhème shuō.
*Great — that's the line for vegetarians.*

**波哥：**等等，如果我说错了呢？上次我想说"不要辣"，说成了"不要拉"。
Děngdeng, rúguǒ wǒ shuō cuò le ne? Shàng cì wǒ xiǎng shuō "búyào là", shuō chéng le "búyào lā".
*Wait — what if I say it wrong? Last time I meant "búyào là" and said "búyào lā".*

**莎莎：**哈哈，没关系。服务员看你的手，也看菜单，能明白。
Hāhā, méi guānxi. Fúwùyuán kàn nǐ de shǒu, yě kàn càidān, néng míngbai.
*Haha, no worries. The waiter sees your hand and the menu — they'll understand.*

**波哥：**真的吗？
Zhēnde ma?
*Really?*

**莎莎：**真的。说错了，再说一次就好。这个我们下一集还要细说。
Zhēnde. Shuō cuò le, zài shuō yí cì jiù hǎo. Zhège wǒmen xià yì jí hái yào xì shuō.
*Really. Say it wrong, say it again. More on this next episode.*

### 买单 + 万能句 · The bill + the magic question

**波哥：**好。那吃完了呢？怎么叫服务员？
Hǎo. Nà chī wán le ne? Zěnme jiào fúwùyuán?
*Okay. And when you're done eating — how do you call the waiter?*

**莎莎：**你说：服务员！买单。
Nǐ shuō: fúwùyuán! Mǎidān.
*You say: waiter! The bill.*

**波哥：**服务员，买单。
Fúwùyuán, mǎidān.
*Waiter, the bill.*

**莎莎：**买单，就是"我要付钱了"。
Mǎidān, jiùshì "wǒ yào fù qián le".
*"Mǎidān" means "I'm ready to pay".*

**波哥：**然后问：多少钱？
Ránhòu wèn: duōshǎo qián?
*Then ask: how much?*

**莎莎：**对。不过现在很多地方，手机扫一下就付了。
Duì. Búguò xiànzài hěn duō dìfang, shǒujī sǎo yíxià jiù fù le.
*Right — though in many places now you just scan with your phone.*

**波哥：**那"买单"还有用吗？
Nà "mǎidān" hái yǒuyòng ma?
*Is "mǎidān" still useful then?*

**莎莎：**有用。你说"买单"，服务员就知道你吃完了。
Yǒuyòng. Nǐ shuō "mǎidān", fúwùyuán jiù zhīdào nǐ chī wán le.
*Yes — say it and the waiter knows you're finished.*

**波哥：**好。现在我会五句话了。
Hǎo. Xiànzài wǒ huì wǔ jù huà le.
*Okay. Now I know all five sentences.*

**莎莎：**来，数一数。
Lái, shǔ yi shǔ.
*Let's count them.*

**波哥：**两位。有菜单吗？我要这个。不要辣。服务员，买单。
Liǎng wèi. Yǒu càidān ma? Wǒ yào zhège. Búyào là. Fúwùyuán, mǎidān.
*Two. Menu, please. I'll have this. Not spicy. Waiter, the bill.*

**莎莎：**全对！不过，我还有一句要送你。
Quán duì! Búguò, wǒ hái yǒu yí jù yào sòng nǐ.
*All correct! But I have one more — a gift.*

**波哥：**最后一句？你开头说的，最好用的那句？
Zuìhòu yí jù? Nǐ kāitóu shuō de, zuì hǎo yòng de nà jù?
*The last one? The most useful one you promised?*

**莎莎：**对。你在饭馆看到一个菜，很好吃，可是你不知道它的名字。
Duì. Nǐ zài fànguǎn kàndào yí ge cài, hěn hǎochī, kěshì nǐ bù zhīdào tā de míngzi.
*Yes. You see a delicious dish — but you don't know its name.*

**波哥：**常常这样！
Chángcháng zhèyàng!
*Happens all the time!*

**莎莎：**你就指着它，问：这个用中文怎么说？
Nǐ jiù zhǐ zhe tā, wèn: zhège yòng Zhōngwén zěnme shuō?
*Point at it and ask: how do you say this in Chinese?*

**波哥：**这个用中文怎么说？
Zhège yòng Zhōngwén zěnme shuō?
*How do you say this in Chinese?*

**莎莎：**服务员会告诉你。这样，每次吃饭，你都学一个新词。
Fúwùyuán huì gàosu nǐ. Zhèyàng, měi cì chīfàn, nǐ dōu xué yí ge xīn cí.
*The waiter will tell you. That way, every meal teaches you a new word.*

**波哥：**哦！饭馆变成教室了。
Ó! Fànguǎn biànchéng jiàoshì le.
*Oh! The restaurant becomes a classroom.*

**莎莎：**对。你吃得越多，学得越多。
Duì. Nǐ chī de yuè duō, xué de yuè duō.
*Right. The more you eat, the more you learn.*

**波哥：**哈哈，这个"越……越……"，我上一集学过！
Hāhā, zhège "yuè… yuè…", wǒ shàng yì jí xué guò!
*Haha — I learned that pattern last episode!*

**莎莎：**记住了，很好！词像朋友，又见面了。
Jìzhù le, hěn hǎo! Cí xiàng péngyou, yòu jiànmiàn le.
*You remembered! Words are like friends — meeting again.*

**波哥：**说真的，这样我就不怕去饭馆了。
Shuō zhēnde, zhèyàng wǒ jiù bú pà qù fànguǎn le.
*Honestly, now I'm not scared of restaurants anymore.*

**莎莎：**如果你想每个星期练一个真实场景，就订阅慢慢中文。
Rúguǒ nǐ xiǎng měi ge xīngqī liàn yí ge zhēnshí chǎngjǐng, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*If you'd like one real-life scene every week, subscribe.*

**波哥：**饭馆、坐车、买东西，我们都会慢慢练。
Fànguǎn, zuòchē, mǎi dōngxi, wǒmen dōu huì mànmàn liàn.
*Restaurants, buses, shopping — we'll practise them all, slowly.*

**莎莎：**你在生活里用得上的中文，才是你的中文。
Nǐ zài shēnghuó lǐ yòng de shàng de Zhōngwén, cái shì nǐ de Zhōngwén.
*Chinese you can actually use in life — that's the Chinese that's yours.*

### 快问快答 · Speed round

Answer out loud **before** Bo does.

**波哥：**好，最后，快问快答！准备好了吗？
Hǎo, zuìhòu, kuài wèn kuài dá! Zhǔnbèi hǎo le ma?
*Okay, speed round! Ready?*

**莎莎：**这次我问你！准备好了吗？
Zhè cì wǒ wèn nǐ! Zhǔnbèi hǎo le ma?
*This time I ask you! Ready?*

**波哥：**准备好了！
Zhǔnbèi hǎo le!
*Ready!*

**莎莎：**进门，服务员问"几位"，你们两个人，怎么说？
Jìn mén, fúwùyuán wèn "jǐ wèi", nǐmen liǎng ge rén, zěnme shuō?
*At the door they ask "how many" — there are two of you. You say?*

**波哥：**两位。
Liǎng wèi.
*Two.*

**莎莎：**想看菜，怎么说？
Xiǎng kàn cài, zěnme shuō?
*You want to see the dishes?*

**波哥：**有菜单吗？
Yǒu càidān ma?
*Do you have a menu?*

**莎莎：**想要那个菜，可是不知道名字？
Xiǎng yào nàge cài, kěshì bù zhīdào míngzi?
*You want that dish but don't know its name?*

**波哥：**我要这个。手指是最好的翻译！
Wǒ yào zhège. Shǒuzhǐ shì zuì hǎo de fānyì!
*I'll have this — the finger is the best translator!*

**莎莎：**不能吃辣？
Bù néng chī là?
*Can't eat spicy?*

**波哥：**我要一个这个，不要辣。
Wǒ yào yí ge zhège, búyào là.
*One of this — not spicy.*

**莎莎：**吃完了？
Chī wán le?
*Finished eating?*

**波哥：**服务员，买单！
Fúwùyuán, mǎidān!
*Waiter, the bill!*

**莎莎：**看到不认识的菜，想学？
Kàndào bú rènshi de cài, xiǎng xué?
*You see an unfamiliar dish and want to learn it?*

**波哥：**这个用中文怎么说？
Zhège yòng Zhōngwén zěnme shuō?
*How do you say this in Chinese?*

**莎莎：**太好了，全对！你可以去饭馆了。
Tài hǎo le, quán duì! Nǐ kěyǐ qù fànguǎn le.
*Perfect, all correct! You're ready for the restaurant.*

### 结尾 · Ending

**波哥：**今天我们学了点菜的五句话，还有一句最好用的。
Jīntiān wǒmen xué le diǎn cài de wǔ jù huà, hái yǒu yí jù zuì hǎo yòng de.
*Today: five ordering sentences, plus the most useful one.*

**莎莎：**两位。有菜单吗？我要这个，不要辣。服务员，买单。
Liǎng wèi. Yǒu càidān ma? Wǒ yào zhège, búyào là. Fúwùyuán, mǎidān.
*Two. Menu, please. I'll have this, not spicy. Waiter, the bill.*

**波哥：**还有：这个用中文怎么说？
Hái yǒu: zhège yòng Zhōngwén zěnme shuō?
*And: how do you say this in Chinese?*

**莎莎：**别忘了你的小事：用"我要"写一句话，放在评论区。
Bié wàng le nǐ de xiǎo shì: yòng "wǒ yào" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Don't forget your task: one "wǒ yào" sentence in the comments.*

**波哥：**下一集，我们聊一个很多人心里的问题。
Xià yì jí, wǒmen liáo yí ge hěn duō rén xīnlǐ de wèntí.
*Next episode, a question many people carry inside.*

**莎莎：**怕说错，怎么办？
Pà shuō cuò, zěnme bàn?
*Afraid of saying it wrong — what then?*

**波哥：**在饭馆说错，在路上说错，怕开口，怎么办？
Zài fànguǎn shuō cuò, zài lùshàng shuō cuò, pà kāikǒu, zěnme bàn?
*Wrong at the restaurant, wrong on the street, afraid to open your mouth — what then?*

**莎莎：**下一集告诉你：说错了，为什么没关系。
Xià yì jí gàosu nǐ: shuō cuò le, wèishénme méi guānxi.
*Next episode: why getting it wrong is okay.*

**波哥：**我们下一集见！
Wǒmen xià yì jí jiàn!
*See you next episode!*

**莎莎：**下次见。慢慢来。
Xiàcì jiàn. Mànmàn lái.
*See you next time. Take it slow.*
