## In this episode 这一集

- Falling is part of walking — and saying it wrong is part of learning to speak.
- The **three things that actually happen** when you make a mistake (none of them is scary).
- Three sentences that save you — the shortest one is for yourself.

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **说错了没关系，再说一次就好。**
> Shuō cuò le méi guānxi, zài shuō yí cì jiù hǎo.
> *Getting it wrong is okay — just say it again.*

## The three repair sentences 三句好用的话

| 句子 | Pinyin | When |
|---|---|---|
| 对不起，我说错了。 | Duìbuqǐ, wǒ shuō cuò le. | right after a mistake |
| 我是说…… | Wǒ shì shuō…… | to correct yourself |
| 没关系。 | Méi guānxi. | **to yourself** |

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**莎莎：**波哥，我问你：学中文，什么最难？
Bōgē, wǒ wèn nǐ: xué Zhōngwén, shénme zuì nán?
*Bo, tell me: what's the hardest part of learning Chinese?*

**波哥：**汉字？不对。声调？也不对。
Hànzì? Bú duì. Shēngdiào? Yě bú duì.
*Characters? No. Tones? Also no.*

**莎莎：**那是什么？
Nà shì shénme?
*Then what?*

**波哥：**是怕。
Shì pà.
*It's fear.*

**莎莎：**怕？
Pà?
*Fear?*

**波哥：**对，怕说错。很多人心里有中文，可是嘴里说不出来。
Duì, pà shuō cuò. Hěn duō rén xīnlǐ yǒu Zhōngwén, kěshì zuǐ lǐ shuō bu chūlái.
*Yes — fear of saying it wrong. The Chinese is in your head, but it won't come out.*

**莎莎：**因为一开口，就想：错了怎么办？
Yīnwèi yì kāikǒu, jiù xiǎng: cuò le zěnme bàn?
*Because the moment you open your mouth, you think: what if it's wrong?*

**波哥：**对。别人会不会笑我？会不会觉得我笨？
Duì. Biéren huì bu huì xiào wǒ? Huì bu huì juéde wǒ bèn?
*Right. Will people laugh? Will they think I'm stupid?*

**莎莎：**今天，我们就聊这个"怕"。
Jīntiān, wǒmen jiù liáo zhège "pà".
*Today we talk about exactly that fear.*

**波哥：**能聊好吗？"怕"看不见摸不着。
Néng liáo hǎo ma? "Pà" kàn bu jiàn mō bu zháo.
*Can we, though? You can't see or touch fear.*

**莎莎：**能。今天三件事：为什么会怕，说错了会发生什么，还有三句好用的话。
Néng. Jīntiān sān jiàn shì: wèishénme huì pà, shuō cuò le huì fāshēng shénme, hái yǒu sān jù hǎoyòng de huà.
*We can. Three things today: why we're afraid, what actually happens when you're wrong, and three useful sentences.*

**波哥：**三句话？
Sān jù huà?
*Three sentences?*

**莎莎：**对。最后一句最短，也最重要。别走开。
Duì. Zuìhòu yí jù zuì duǎn, yě zuì zhòngyào. Bié zǒukāi.
*Yes. The last one is the shortest — and the most important. Don't go away.*

### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。在这里，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Zài zhèlǐ, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*I'm Bo. Here, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**今天不学新场景，我们聊一个心里的问题：怕说错。
Jīntiān bù xué xīn chǎngjǐng, wǒmen liáo yí ge xīnlǐ de wèntí: pà shuō cuò.
*No new scene today — we're talking about something inside: the fear of mistakes.*

**波哥：**这个问题，我太熟悉了。开始吧。
Zhège wèntí, wǒ tài shúxī le. Kāishǐ ba.
*A problem I know all too well. Let's start.*

### 学走路的孩子 · Falling is part of walking

**莎莎：**波哥，你见过小孩学走路吗？
Bōgē, nǐ jiàn guò xiǎohái xué zǒulù ma?
*Bo, have you watched a child learn to walk?*

**波哥：**见过。走两步，就坐到地上了。
Jiàn guò. Zǒu liǎng bù, jiù zuò dào dì shàng le.
*Yes. Two steps, then down they go.*

**莎莎：**对，一天要倒好多次。
Duì, yì tiān yào dǎo hǎo duō cì.
*Right — they fall many times a day.*

**波哥：**然后站起来，再走。
Ránhòu zhàn qǐlái, zài zǒu.
*Then they stand up and walk again.*

**莎莎：**有人会说"这个孩子不会走路"吗？
Yǒu rén huì shuō "zhège háizi bú huì zǒulù" ma?
*Does anyone say "this child can't walk"?*

**波哥：**不会啊。谁都知道，倒，就是学走路的一部分。
Bú huì a. Shéi dōu zhīdào, dǎo, jiùshì xué zǒulù de yí bùfen.
*Of course not. Everyone knows falling is part of learning to walk.*

**莎莎：**说中文也是一样。说错，就是学说话的一部分。
Shuō Zhōngwén yě shì yíyàng. Shuō cuò, jiùshì xué shuōhuà de yí bùfen.
*Speaking Chinese is the same. Mistakes are part of learning to speak.*

**波哥：**哦……说错一句话，就像走路倒了一下。
Ó… shuō cuò yí jù huà, jiù xiàng zǒulù dǎo le yíxià.
*Oh… one wrong sentence is like one stumble.*

**莎莎：**对。倒了一下，不等于不会走。
Duì. Dǎo le yíxià, bù děngyú bú huì zǒu.
*Right. One fall doesn't mean you can't walk.*

**波哥：**说错了一句，不等于不会说。
Shuō cuò le yí jù, bù děngyú bú huì shuō.
*One mistake doesn't mean you can't speak.*

**莎莎：**这两句话，请你记住。
Zhè liǎng jù huà, qǐng nǐ jìzhù.
*Please remember those two sentences.*

**波哥：**可是莎莎，小孩不怕倒，大人怕啊。
Kěshì Shāsha, xiǎohái bú pà dǎo, dàrén pà a.
*But Sasha — kids don't fear falling. Adults do.*

**莎莎：**为什么呢？
Wèishénme ne?
*Why is that?*

**波哥：**大人觉得，说错了，别人会笑。
Dàrén juéde, shuō cuò le, biéren huì xiào.
*Adults think people will laugh at their mistakes.*

**莎莎：**那我问你：你说英文的时候，从来不错吗？
Nà wǒ wèn nǐ: nǐ shuō Yīngwén de shíhou, cónglái bú cuò ma?
*Let me ask: when you speak English, are you never wrong?*

**波哥：**哈哈，当然错。我常常说错。
Hāhā, dāngrán cuò. Wǒ chángcháng shuō cuò.
*Haha, of course I am. All the time.*

**莎莎：**有人笑你吗？
Yǒu rén xiào nǐ ma?
*Does anyone laugh at you?*

**波哥：**很少。大家都在帮我。
Hěn shǎo. Dàjiā dōu zài bāng wǒ.
*Rarely. Mostly people help me.*

**莎莎：**对。你说中文说错的时候，中国人也是这样。
Duì. Nǐ shuō Zhōngwén shuō cuò de shíhou, Zhōngguórén yě shì zhèyàng.
*Exactly. When you get Chinese wrong, Chinese speakers are the same.*

**波哥：**他们在想什么？
Tāmen zài xiǎng shénme?
*What are they thinking?*

**莎莎：**他们在想：这个人在学我们的话，真好。
Tāmen zài xiǎng: zhège rén zài xué wǒmen de huà, zhēn hǎo.
*They're thinking: this person is learning our language — how nice.*

**波哥：**不是"这个人真笨"？
Búshì "zhège rén zhēn bèn"?
*Not "this person is so dumb"?*

**莎莎：**不是。是"我来帮帮他"。
Búshì. Shì "wǒ lái bāngbang tā".
*No. It's "let me help him".*

**波哥：**哦……原来别人比我想的友好。
Ó… yuánlái biéren bǐ wǒ xiǎng de yǒuhǎo.
*Oh… people are friendlier than I imagined.*

**莎莎：**大部分时候，是的。
Dàbùfen shíhou, shì de.
*Most of the time, yes.*

**莎莎：**现在，请你做一件小事。
Xiànzài, qǐng nǐ zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì.

**波哥：**评论区时间到了。
Pínglùnqū shíjiān dào le.

**莎莎：**对。用"没关系"写一句话，放在评论区。
Duì. Yòng "méi guānxi" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Yes. Write one sentence with "méi guānxi" in the comments.*

**波哥：**我先来：说错了？没关系。
Wǒ xiān lái: shuō cuò le? Méi guānxi.
*Me first: said it wrong? No problem.*

**莎莎：**很好。再来一句？
Hěn hǎo. Zài lái yí jù?
*Nice. Another?*

**波哥：**今天下雨，没关系，我在家听中文。
Jīntiān xiàyǔ, méi guānxi, wǒ zài jiā tīng Zhōngwén.
*It's raining today — no problem, I'll listen to Chinese at home.*

**莎莎：**太好了。你也写一句"没关系"，我们会看，也会回你。
Tài hǎo le. Nǐ yě xiě yí jù "méi guānxi", wǒmen huì kàn, yě huì huí nǐ.
*Perfect. Write yours too — we read and reply.*

### 说错以后会发生什么 · What actually happens

**波哥：**好。现在说第二件事：说错了，到底会发生什么？
Hǎo. Xiànzài shuō dì èr jiàn shì: shuō cuò le, dàodǐ huì fāshēng shénme?
*Okay, thing two: what actually happens when you get it wrong?*

**莎莎：**我先给你讲一个小故事。
Wǒ xiān gěi nǐ jiǎng yí ge xiǎo gùshi.
*Let me tell you a little story first.*

**波哥：**好，我爱听故事。
Hǎo, wǒ ài tīng gùshi.
*Great, I love stories.*

**莎莎：**一个学生，在饭馆想点水饺。
Yí ge xuésheng, zài fànguǎn xiǎng diǎn shuǐjiǎo.
*A student at a restaurant wants to order dumplings — shuǐjiǎo.*

**波哥：**水饺，我知道，很好吃。
Shuǐjiǎo, wǒ zhīdào, hěn hǎochī.
*Dumplings — I know them. Delicious.*

**莎莎：**可是他说成了：老板，我要睡觉。
Kěshì tā shuō chéng le: lǎobǎn, wǒ yào shuìjiào.
*But it came out as: boss, I want to sleep — shuìjiào.*

**波哥：**哈哈哈！我要睡觉！
Hāhāhā! Wǒ yào shuìjiào!
*Hahaha! "I want to sleep!"*

**莎莎：**老板笑了笑，然后说：哦，水饺！要几个？
Lǎobǎn xiào le xiào, ránhòu shuō: ó, shuǐjiǎo! Yào jǐ ge?
*The owner smiled and said: ah, shuǐjiǎo! How many?*

**波哥：**等等，老板听懂了？
Děngdeng, lǎobǎn tīngdǒng le?
*Wait — he understood?*

**莎莎：**听懂了。因为他在饭馆，手里拿着菜单。
Tīngdǒng le. Yīnwèi tā zài fànguǎn, shǒu lǐ ná zhe càidān.
*He did. Because they're in a restaurant, menu in hand.*

**波哥：**对啊，没有人在饭馆点"睡觉"。
Duì a, méiyǒu rén zài fànguǎn diǎn "shuìjiào".
*Right — nobody orders "sleep" at a restaurant.*

**莎莎：**这就是第一种情况，也是最常见的：说错了，别人还是懂了。
Zhè jiùshì dì yī zhǒng qíngkuàng, yě shì zuì chángjiàn de: shuō cuò le, biéren háishì dǒng le.
*That's outcome one — the most common: you're wrong, and they understand anyway.*

**波哥：**因为场景在帮你说话。
Yīnwèi chǎngjǐng zài bāng nǐ shuōhuà.
*Because the situation speaks for you.*

**莎莎：**说得好！第二种情况：别人没听懂，问你"什么？"
Shuō de hǎo! Dì èr zhǒng qíngkuàng: biéren méi tīngdǒng, wèn nǐ "shénme?"
*Well said! Outcome two: they don't understand and ask "what?"*

**波哥：**这个最怕了！
Zhège zuì pà le!
*That's the scariest one!*

**莎莎：**不用怕。他问"什么"，你就再说一次，或者用手指。
Búyòng pà. Tā wèn "shénme", nǐ jiù zài shuō yí cì, huòzhě yòng shǒuzhǐ.
*No need. They ask "what", you say it again — or point.*

**波哥：**上一集的办法：手指是最好的翻译。
Shàng yì jí de bànfǎ: shǒuzhǐ shì zuì hǎo de fānyì.
*Last episode's trick: the finger is the best translator.*

**莎莎：**对。第三种情况：别人帮你说对。
Duì. Dì sān zhǒng qíngkuàng: biéren bāng nǐ shuō duì.
*Right. Outcome three: they say it correctly for you.*

**波哥：**就像那个老板：哦，水饺！
Jiù xiàng nàge lǎobǎn: ó, shuǐjiǎo!
*Like the owner: ah, shuǐjiǎo!*

**莎莎：**对。他帮你说了一次对的。你听一遍，就学会了。
Duì. Tā bāng nǐ shuō le yí cì duì de. Nǐ tīng yí biàn, jiù xuéhuì le.
*Yes. They model the correct version once — you hear it, you've learned it.*

**波哥：**等等……这三种情况，好像都不可怕。
Děngdeng… zhè sān zhǒng qíngkuàng, hǎoxiàng dōu bù kěpà.
*Hold on… none of these three is actually scary.*

**莎莎：**对。懂了，问了，帮了。没有第四种。
Duì. Dǒng le, wèn le, bāng le. Méiyǒu dì sì zhǒng.
*Right. Understood, asked, helped. There is no fourth outcome.*

**波哥：**没有人打你，没有人骂你。
Méiyǒu rén dǎ nǐ, méiyǒu rén mà nǐ.
*Nobody hits you, nobody yells at you.*

**莎莎：**说错的结果，比你怕的小得多。
Shuō cuò de jiéguǒ, bǐ nǐ pà de xiǎo de duō.
*The real cost of a mistake is far smaller than the fear.*

**波哥：**来，我说说看：说错了会发生什么？
Lái, wǒ shuōshuo kàn: shuō cuò le huì fāshēng shénme?
*Let me recap: what happens when you're wrong?*

**莎莎：**你说。
Nǐ shuō.
*Go on.*

**波哥：**第一，别人还是懂了。第二，别人问一下。第三，别人帮你说对。
Dì yī, biéren háishì dǒng le. Dì èr, biéren wèn yíxià. Dì sān, biéren bāng nǐ shuō duì.
*One: they understand anyway. Two: they ask. Three: they help you say it right.*

**莎莎：**全对！三种都不可怕。
Quán duì! Sān zhǒng dōu bù kěpà.
*All correct! And none of them is scary.*

### 三句好用的话 · Three sentences that save you

**波哥：**好，那最后，三句好用的话是什么？
Hǎo, nà zuìhòu, sān jù hǎoyòng de huà shì shénme?
*Okay — so what are the three useful sentences?*

**莎莎：**第一句，说错的时候用：对不起，我说错了。
Dì yī jù, shuō cuò de shíhou yòng: duìbuqǐ, wǒ shuō cuò le.
*Number one, right after a mistake: sorry, I said that wrong.*

**波哥：**对不起，我说错了。
Duìbuqǐ, wǒ shuō cuò le.
*Sorry, I said that wrong.*

**莎莎：**第二句，想改口的时候用：我是说……
Dì èr jù, xiǎng gǎikǒu de shíhou yòng: wǒ shì shuō……
*Number two, to correct yourself: what I meant was…*

**波哥：**我是说……水饺，不是睡觉！
Wǒ shì shuō… shuǐjiǎo, búshì shuìjiào!
*I meant… dumplings, not sleep!*

**莎莎：**哈哈，对，就这么用。
Hāhā, duì, jiù zhème yòng.
*Haha — exactly like that.*

**波哥：**那第三句呢？你说它最短，也最重要。
Nà dì sān jù ne? Nǐ shuō tā zuì duǎn, yě zuì zhòngyào.
*And number three? You said it's the shortest and most important.*

**莎莎：**第三句，不是说给别人的。
Dì sān jù, búshì shuō gěi biéren de.
*Number three isn't for other people.*

**波哥：**不是说给别人的？那说给谁？
Búshì shuō gěi biéren de? Nà shuō gěi shéi?
*Not for others? Then for whom?*

**莎莎：**说给你自己：没关系。
Shuō gěi nǐ zìjǐ: méi guānxi.
*For yourself: it's okay.*

**波哥：**没关系……就这三个字？
Méi guānxi… jiù zhè sān ge zì?
*"Méi guānxi"… just those three characters?*

**莎莎：**对。整句是：说错了没关系，再说一次就好。
Duì. Zhěng jù shì: shuō cuò le méi guānxi, zài shuō yí cì jiù hǎo.
*Yes. The full sentence: getting it wrong is okay — just say it again.*

**波哥：**说错了没关系，再说一次就好。
Shuō cuò le méi guānxi, zài shuō yí cì jiù hǎo.
*Getting it wrong is okay — just say it again.*

**莎莎：**前面是"说错了没关系"。
Qiánmiàn shì "shuō cuò le méi guānxi".
*First half: "it's okay to get it wrong".*

**波哥：**后面是"再说一次就好"。
Hòumiàn shì "zài shuō yí cì jiù hǎo".
*Second half: "just say it once more".*

**莎莎：**怕的时候，把这句话说给自己听。
Pà de shíhou, bǎ zhè jù huà shuō gěi zìjǐ tīng.
*When you feel the fear, say that sentence to yourself.*

**波哥：**像一个小朋友倒了，对自己说：没关系，再走。
Xiàng yí ge xiǎopéngyou dǎo le, duì zìjǐ shuō: méi guānxi, zài zǒu.
*Like a child who fell, telling themselves: it's okay — walk again.*

**莎莎：**就是这样。
Jiùshì zhèyàng.
*Exactly.*

**波哥：**莎莎，我发现，在这里练习，其实最安全。
Shāsha, wǒ fāxiàn, zài zhèlǐ liànxí, qíshí zuì ānquán.
*Sasha, you know what — practising here is actually the safest.*

**莎莎：**为什么？
Wèishénme?
*Why?*

**波哥：**跟着你们说，说错了也没有人看我。
Gēnzhe nǐmen shuō, shuō cuò le yě méiyǒu rén kàn wǒ.
*I repeat after you two, and nobody's watching if I get it wrong.*
