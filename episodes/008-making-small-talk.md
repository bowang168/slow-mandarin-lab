## In this episode 这一集

- The hard part of small talk isn't vocabulary — it's the **first sentence**.
- Three openers that work anywhere: 最近怎么样？/ 周末做什么了？/ 今天真冷。
- Short honest answers (还好 / 挺好的 / 有点忙 / 没做什么) and the two-character trick
  that keeps a chat alive: **你呢？**

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **最近怎么样？还好吗？**
> Zuìjìn zěnmeyàng? Hái hǎo ma?
> *How have you been? Everything okay?*

## The three openers 三句开场

| 句子 | Pinyin | When |
|---|---|---|
| 最近怎么样？ | Zuìjìn zěnmeyàng? | any friend, any day |
| 周末做什么了？ | Zhōumò zuò shénme le? | Monday morning |
| 今天真冷。 | Jīntiān zhēn lěng. | anyone, anywhere — weather is a door |

**Your turn:** answer today's real question — 你最近怎么样？ — in Chinese, in the comments.
Short and true beats long and perfect. Don't forget to bounce it back: 你呢？

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**波哥：**莎莎，昨天早上，我在楼下见到了一个中国朋友。
Shāsha, zuótiān zǎoshang, wǒ zài lóuxià jiàndào le yí ge Zhōngguó péngyou.
*Sasha, yesterday morning I ran into a Chinese friend downstairs.*

**莎莎：**很好啊，你们聊了吗？
Hěn hǎo a, nǐmen liáo le ma?
*Nice! Did you chat?*

**波哥：**没有。我说了"你好"，他也说了"你好"。
Méiyǒu. Wǒ shuō le "nǐ hǎo", tā yě shuō le "nǐ hǎo".
*No. I said "nǐ hǎo", he said "nǐ hǎo".*

**莎莎：**然后呢？
Ránhòu ne?
*And then?*

**波哥：**然后……就没有然后了。我们站在那里，谁都不说话。
Ránhòu… jiù méiyǒu ránhòu le. Wǒmen zhàn zài nàlǐ, shéi dōu bù shuōhuà.
*Then… nothing. We just stood there, neither of us speaking.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，我知道这种时候，很难受。
Hāhā, wǒ zhīdào zhè zhǒng shíhou, hěn nánshòu.
*Haha, I know that moment — it's painful.*

**波哥：**对！我不是不会中文，我是不知道说什么。
Duì! Wǒ búshì bú huì Zhōngwén, wǒ shì bù zhīdào shuō shénme.
*Right! It's not that I can't speak Chinese — I just don't know what to say.*

**莎莎：**说得对。很多人难的地方，不是词，是第一句话。
Shuō de duì. Hěn duō rén nán de dìfang, búshì cí, shì dì yī jù huà.
*Exactly. For most people the hard part isn't words — it's the first sentence.*

**波哥：**对，第一句最难。第一句说完，后面就容易了。
Duì, dì yī jù zuì nán. Dì yī jù shuō wán, hòumiàn jiù róngyì le.
*Yes, the first line is the hardest. After that, it gets easy.*

**莎莎：**今天我们就练这个：聊天，怎么开场。
Jīntiān wǒmen jiù liàn zhège: liáotiān, zěnme kāichǎng.
*So that's today's practice: how to open a chat.*

**波哥：**开场？
Kāichǎng?
*"Kāichǎng"?*

**莎莎：**对，开场就是聊天的第一句话。今天给你三句，再加一个两个字的小办法。
Duì, kāichǎng jiùshì liáotiān de dì yī jù huà. Jīntiān gěi nǐ sān jù, zài jiā yí ge liǎng ge zì de xiǎo bànfǎ.
*Yes — the opener is the chat's first line. Today you get three, plus a two-character trick.*

**波哥：**两个字就能让聊天走下去？好，别走开。
Liǎng ge zì jiù néng ràng liáotiān zǒu xiàqù? Hǎo, bié zǒukāi.
*Two characters can keep a chat going? Okay — don't go away.*

### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。在这里，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Zài zhèlǐ, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*I'm Bo. Here, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**今天是一个真实场景：见到人，怎么开始聊天。
Jīntiān shì yí ge zhēnshí chǎngjǐng: jiàndào rén, zěnme kāishǐ liáotiān.
*Today is a real-life scene: you meet someone — how do you start talking?*

**波哥：**这一集我等了很久了。开始吧。
Zhè yì jí wǒ děng le hěn jiǔ le. Kāishǐ ba.
*I've been waiting for this one. Let's start.*

### 第一句：最近怎么样 · Opener one

**莎莎：**第一句，也是最好用的一句：最近怎么样？
Dì yī jù, yě shì zuì hǎoyòng de yí jù: Zuìjìn zěnmeyàng?
*Opener one — the most useful one: how have you been?*

**波哥：**最近怎么样？
Zuìjìn zěnmeyàng?
*"Zuìjìn zěnmeyàng?"*

**莎莎：**最近，就是这些天。见到朋友、同学，都可以问：这些天，你好吗？
Zuìjìn, jiùshì zhèxiē tiān. Jiàndào péngyou, tóngxué, dōu kěyǐ wèn: Zhèxiē tiān, nǐ hǎo ma?
*"Zuìjìn" means "these days". Friends, classmates — you can ask anyone: how are these days going?*

**波哥：**好。那别人问我，我怎么回答？
Hǎo. Nà biéren wèn wǒ, wǒ zěnme huídá?
*Okay. And when someone asks me, how do I answer?*

**莎莎：**最简单的回答，两个字：还好。
Zuì jiǎndān de huídá, liǎng ge zì: Hái hǎo.
*The simplest answer is two characters: hái hǎo.*

**波哥：**还好？就这两个字？
Hái hǎo? Jiù zhè liǎng ge zì?
*"Hái hǎo"? Just that?*

**莎莎：**对。还好，就是不好也不坏。
Duì. Hái hǎo, jiùshì bù hǎo yě bú huài.
*Yes. "Hái hǎo" = not bad, not great — okay.*

**波哥：**那这些天真的很好呢？
Nà zhèxiē tiān zhēn de hěn hǎo ne?
*And if things are actually great?*

**莎莎：**就说：挺好的。
Jiù shuō: Tǐng hǎo de.
*Then say: tǐng hǎo de — pretty good.*

**波哥：**挺好的。那很忙呢？
Tǐng hǎo de. Nà hěn máng ne?
*"Tǐng hǎo de." And if I'm busy?*

**莎莎：**有点忙。你看，回答都很短。
Yǒudiǎn máng. Nǐ kàn, huídá dōu hěn duǎn.
*"Yǒudiǎn máng" — a bit busy. See? All the answers are short.*

**波哥：**还好。挺好的。有点忙。都是两三个字，我都会！
Hái hǎo. Tǐng hǎo de. Yǒudiǎn máng. Dōu shì liǎng sān ge zì, wǒ dōu huì!
*Hái hǎo. Tǐng hǎo de. Yǒudiǎn máng. Two or three characters each — I can do all of them!*

**莎莎：**对，聊天的回答，不用长，真的就好。
Duì, liáotiān de huídá, búyòng cháng, zhēn de jiù hǎo.
*Right — chat answers don't need to be long, just true.*

### 小任务 · Your turn

**波哥：**这个我喜欢。评论区时间到了吧？我猜今天的小事跟这个有关。
Zhège wǒ xǐhuan. Pínglùnqū shíjiān dào le ba? Wǒ cāi jīntiān de xiǎo shì gēn zhège yǒuguān.
*I like this. Comment time, right? I bet today's task is about exactly this.*

**莎莎：**猜对了。今天我们真的想问你：你最近怎么样？用中文回答，写在评论区。
Cāi duì le. Jīntiān wǒmen zhēn de xiǎng wèn nǐ: Nǐ zuìjìn zěnmeyàng? Yòng Zhōngwén huídá, xiě zài pínglùnqū.
*Correct. Today we're really asking you: how have you been? Answer in Chinese, in the comments.*

**波哥：**我先来：我挺好的，就是有点忙，最近在慢慢学中文。
Wǒ xiān lái: Wǒ tǐng hǎo de, jiùshì yǒudiǎn máng, zuìjìn zài mànmàn xué Zhōngwén.
*Me first: pretty good, just a bit busy — slowly learning Chinese these days.*

**莎莎：**很好。短短的，真的，就是最好的回答。我们会看，也会回你。
Hěn hǎo. Duǎnduǎn de, zhēn de, jiùshì zuì hǎo de huídá. Wǒmen huì kàn, yě huì huí nǐ.
*Perfect. Short and true is the best answer. We read and reply.*

### 你呢？两个字的办法 · The two-character trick

**波哥：**可是莎莎，我有一个大问题。
Kěshì Shāsha, wǒ yǒu yí ge dà wèntí.
*But Sasha, I have a big problem.*

**莎莎：**你说。
Nǐ shuō.
*Go on.*

**波哥：**我回答完"挺好的"，话又没有了。三秒以后，又是安静。
Wǒ huídá wán "tǐng hǎo de", huà yòu méiyǒu le. Sān miǎo yǐhòu, yòu shì ānjìng.
*After I say "tǐng hǎo de", I'm out of words again. Three seconds later — silence.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，现在就教你那个两个字的小办法：你呢？
Hāhā, xiànzài jiù jiāo nǐ nàge liǎng ge zì de xiǎo bànfǎ: Nǐ ne?
*Haha — here's the two-character trick: nǐ ne?*

**波哥：**你呢？
Nǐ ne?
*"Nǐ ne?"*

**莎莎：**对。别人问你，你回答，然后把问题送回去：你呢？
Duì. Biéren wèn nǐ, nǐ huídá, ránhòu bǎ wèntí sòng huíqù: Nǐ ne?
*Yes. They ask, you answer — then send the question back: and you?*

**波哥：**挺好的，你呢？
Tǐng hǎo de, nǐ ne?
*Pretty good — and you?*

**莎莎：**就是这样！问题回去了，聊天就不会停。
Jiùshì zhèyàng! Wèntí huíqù le, liáotiān jiù bú huì tíng.
*Exactly! The question goes back, and the chat never stops.*

**波哥：**像打球一样，球又打回去了。
Xiàng dǎqiú yíyàng, qiú yòu dǎ huíqù le.
*Like a ball game — you hit the ball back.*

**莎莎：**对，聊天就是打球，一来一去，谁都不累。
Duì, liáotiān jiùshì dǎqiú, yì lái yí qù, shéi dōu bú lèi.
*Right — chatting is a rally: back and forth, and nobody gets tired.*

**波哥：**那"还好"呢？也能变成问题吗？
Nà "hái hǎo" ne? Yě néng biànchéng wèntí ma?
*What about "hái hǎo"? Can it become a question too?*

**莎莎：**能：还好吗？朋友看起来很累，你就轻轻问一句：还好吗？
Néng: Hái hǎo ma? Péngyou kàn qǐlái hěn lèi, nǐ jiù qīngqīng wèn yí jù: Hái hǎo ma?
*It can: hái hǎo ma? When a friend looks tired, ask softly: are you okay?*

**波哥：**还好吗？这句听起来很暖。
Hái hǎo ma? Zhè jù tīng qǐlái hěn nuǎn.
*"Hái hǎo ma?" That sounds warm.*

**莎莎：**对。两句放在一起，就是今天的目标句：最近怎么样？还好吗？
Duì. Liǎng jù fàng zài yìqǐ, jiùshì jīntiān de mùbiāo jù: Zuìjìn zěnmeyàng? Hái hǎo ma?
*Yes. Put them together and you have today's target sentence: how have you been? Everything okay?*

**波哥：**最近怎么样？还好吗？
Zuìjìn zěnmeyàng? Hái hǎo ma?
*How have you been? Everything okay?*

**莎莎：**一句开门，一句关心。来，你说说看：我问你"最近怎么样"，你怎么回答？
Yí jù kāimén, yí jù guānxīn. Lái, nǐ shuōshuo kàn: Wǒ wèn nǐ "zuìjìn zěnmeyàng", nǐ zěnme huídá?
*One line opens the door, one shows you care. Your turn: I ask "zuìjìn zěnmeyàng" — what do you say?*

**波哥：**挺好的，有点忙。你呢？
Tǐng hǎo de, yǒudiǎn máng. Nǐ ne?
*Pretty good, a bit busy. And you?*

**莎莎：**太好了！回答，加"你呢"，都有了。
Tài hǎo le! Huídá, jiā "nǐ ne", dōu yǒu le.
*Perfect! Answer plus "nǐ ne" — you've got it all.*

### 第二句：周末做什么了 · Opener two

**波哥：**好，第二句开场是什么？
Hǎo, dì èr jù kāichǎng shì shénme?
*Okay, what's opener two?*

**莎莎：**周末做什么了？
Zhōumò zuò shénme le?
*What did you do on the weekend?*

**波哥：**周末做什么了？
Zhōumò zuò shénme le?
*"Zhōumò zuò shénme le?"*

**莎莎：**周末，就是星期六和星期天。星期一见到人，用这句最好。
Zhōumò, jiùshì xīngqīliù hé xīngqītiān. Xīngqīyī jiàndào rén, yòng zhè jù zuì hǎo.
*The weekend is Saturday and Sunday. On Monday, this is the best opener.*

**波哥：**这句是问过去的事。
Zhè jù shì wèn guòqù de shì.
*This one asks about the past.*

**莎莎：**对。所以回答的时候，常常会用到上一集的老朋友："没"。比如：没做什么，在家休息。
Duì. Suǒyǐ huídá de shíhou, chángcháng huì yòngdào shàng yì jí de lǎo péngyou: "méi". Bǐrú: Méi zuò shénme, zài jiā xiūxi.
*Right. So the answer often uses last episode's old friend, 没. For example: didn't do much — rested at home.*

**波哥：**啊？可以说"没做什么"吗？
À? Kěyǐ shuō "méi zuò shénme" ma?
*Wait — you're allowed to say "didn't do much"?*

**莎莎：**可以，很多人都这么说。没做什么，也是一个好回答。
Kěyǐ, hěn duō rén dōu zhème shuō. Méi zuò shénme, yě shì yí ge hǎo huídá.
*Absolutely — lots of people say exactly that. It's a perfectly good answer.*

**波哥：**哈哈，"没"——这件事，没有发生。上一集刚学的。
Hāhā, "méi" — zhè jiàn shì, méiyǒu fāshēng. Shàng yì jí gāng xué de.
*Haha, 没 — the thing didn't happen. Fresh from last episode.*

**莎莎：**对，马上就用上了。我们试一次吧？
Duì, mǎshàng jiù yòng shang le. Wǒmen shì yí cì ba?
*Yes — already in use. Shall we try it?*

**波哥：**好。现在是星期一，你是我的同学。
Hǎo. Xiànzài shì xīngqīyī, nǐ shì wǒ de tóngxué.
*Okay. It's Monday, and you're my classmate.*

**莎莎：**波哥，早！周末做什么了？
Bōgē, zǎo! Zhōumò zuò shénme le?
*Morning, Bo! What did you do on the weekend?*

**波哥：**我……呃……周末……
Wǒ… è… zhōumò…
*I… uh… the weekend…*

**莎莎：**别急，说真的就好，短短的就好。
Bié jí, shuō zhēn de jiù hǎo, duǎnduǎn de jiù hǎo.
*No rush — just say what's true, keep it short.*

**波哥：**我周末去看了一个朋友。你呢？
Wǒ zhōumò qù kàn le yí ge péngyou. Nǐ ne?
*I visited a friend on the weekend. And you?*

**莎莎：**我在家看书。你看，这不就聊起来了吗？
Wǒ zài jiā kànshū. Nǐ kàn, zhè bú jiù liáo qǐlái le ma?
*I read at home. See? Now we're chatting!*

**波哥：**聊起来了！不知道说什么的时候，说真的就好。
Liáo qǐlái le! Bù zhīdào shuō shénme de shíhou, shuō zhēn de jiù hǎo.
*We are! When you don't know what to say — say something true.*

**莎莎：**对，回答不用漂亮。现在，我们再试一次。这次，我说得快一点，像外面真的聊天一样。
Duì, huídá búyòng piàoliang. Xiànzài, wǒmen zài shì yí cì. Zhè cì, wǒ shuō de kuài yìdiǎn, xiàng wàimiàn zhēn de liáotiān yíyàng.
*Right — answers don't need to be pretty. Now let's try again. This time I'll speak fast, like a real chat outside.*

**波哥：**好，来吧。
Hǎo, lái ba.
*Okay, bring it on.*

**莎莎：**波哥早，周末做什么了，去哪儿玩了？
Bōgē zǎo, zhōumò zuò shénme le, qù nǎr wán le?
*MorningBo-whatdidyoudo-ontheweekend-wheredidyougo?*

**波哥：**啊？……你说什么？
À? … Nǐ shuō shénme?
*Huh? … What did you say?*

**莎莎：**哈哈，没听懂，对吗？我再说一次，慢慢的：周末，做什么了？
Hāhā, méi tīngdǒng, duì ma? Wǒ zài shuō yí cì, mànmàn de: Zhōumò, zuò shénme le?
*Haha, didn't catch that, right? I'll say it again, slowly: the weekend — what did you do?*

**波哥：**哦！没做什么，在家休息！这次听懂了。
Ó! Méi zuò shénme, zài jiā xiūxi! Zhè cì tīngdǒng le.
*Oh! Didn't do much, rested at home! Got it this time.*

**莎莎：**你看，没听懂就问一句"你说什么"，一点问题都没有，聊天还在。
Nǐ kàn, méi tīngdǒng jiù wèn yí jù "nǐ shuō shénme", yìdiǎn wèntí dōu méiyǒu, liáotiān hái zài.
*See? You didn't understand, you asked "what did you say" — no problem at all, the chat survived.*

**波哥：**可是说真的，听不懂的那三秒，我很想跑。
Kěshì shuō zhēn de, tīng bu dǒng de nà sān miǎo, wǒ hěn xiǎng pǎo.
*Honestly though — in those three seconds of not understanding, I wanted to run.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，"很想跑"的那个时候，我们下一集专门说。
Hāhā, "hěn xiǎng pǎo" de nàge shíhou, wǒmen xià yì jí zhuānmén shuō.
*Haha — that "want to run" moment gets its own episode, next time.*

### 第三句：今天真冷 · Opener three

**波哥：**好，还有第三句开场呢？
Hǎo, hái yǒu dì sān jù kāichǎng ne?
*Okay — and opener three?*

**莎莎：**第三句最简单：说天气。
Dì sān jù zuì jiǎndān: shuō tiānqì.
*The third is the simplest: talk about the weather.*

**波哥：**天气？
Tiānqì?
*The weather?*

**莎莎：**对。今天真冷。今天真热。今天天气真好。
Duì. Jīntiān zhēn lěng. Jīntiān zhēn rè. Jīntiān tiānqì zhēn hǎo.
*Yes. So cold today. So hot today. Lovely weather today.*

**波哥：**这也算聊天吗？
Zhè yě suàn liáotiān ma?
*Does that even count as small talk?*

**莎莎：**算！说天气，全世界的人都在用。
Suàn! Shuō tiānqì, quán shìjiè de rén dōu zài yòng.
*It counts! The whole world opens with the weather.*

**波哥：**哈哈，也对，天气谁都能说两句。
Hāhā, yě duì, tiānqì shéi dōu néng shuō liǎng jù.
*Haha, true — anyone can say a line or two about the weather.*

**莎莎：**你说"今天真冷"，别人会说"是啊，真冷"。
Nǐ shuō "jīntiān zhēn lěng", biéren huì shuō "shì a, zhēn lěng".
*You say "so cold today", they say "yeah, freezing".*

**波哥：**然后呢？
Ránhòu ne?
*And then?*

**莎莎：**然后门就开了。天气是一扇门，门开了，话就进来了。
Ránhòu mén jiù kāi le. Tiānqì shì yí shàn mén, mén kāi le, huà jiù jìnlái le.
*Then the door is open. Weather is a door — once it opens, words walk in.*

**波哥：**今天真冷……是啊……最近怎么样？
Jīntiān zhēn lěng… shì a… zuìjìn zěnmeyàng?
*So cold today… yeah… how have you been?*

**莎莎：**完美！你把两句开场连起来了。
Wánměi! Nǐ bǎ liǎng jù kāichǎng lián qǐlái le.
*Perfect! You just chained two openers together.*

**波哥：**原来聊天不难，难的只是第一句。
Yuánlái liáotiān bù nán, nán de zhǐshì dì yī jù.
*So chatting isn't hard — only the first line is.*

**莎莎：**对。而且说真的，开场这些话都不难，难的是没有地方练。
Duì. Érqiě shuō zhēn de, kāichǎng zhèxiē huà dōu bù nán, nán de shì méiyǒu dìfang liàn.
*Right. And honestly, none of these lines is hard — the hard part is having nowhere to practise.*

**波哥：**哈哈，对，这就是我的问题：学了，没有地方用。
Hāhā, duì, zhè jiùshì wǒ de wèntí: xué le, méiyǒu dìfang yòng.
*Haha, exactly my problem: I learn it, then have nowhere to use it.*

**莎莎：**所以，如果你想每个星期练一个真实场景，就订阅慢慢中文。
Suǒyǐ, rúguǒ nǐ xiǎng měi ge xīngqī liàn yí ge zhēnshí chǎngjǐng, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*So if you'd like one real-life scene to practise every week, subscribe.*

**波哥：**楼下、公司、饭馆，见到人，你都有话可说。
Lóuxià, gōngsī, fànguǎn, jiàndào rén, nǐ dōu yǒu huà kě shuō.
*Downstairs, at work, in restaurants — you'll always have something to say.*

**莎莎：**先在这里说熟，再到外面去说。
Xiān zài zhèlǐ shuō shú, zài dào wàimiàn qù shuō.
*Get fluent here first, then take it outside.*

### 一次完整的小聊天 · A full mini-chat

**波哥：**好！那最后，我们把今天所有的话，连成一次完整的小聊天。
Hǎo! Nà zuìhòu, wǒmen bǎ jīntiān suǒyǒu de huà, lián chéng yí cì wánzhěng de xiǎo liáotiān.
*Great! To finish, let's chain everything into one complete mini-chat.*

**莎莎：**来。我是你的邻居，我们在楼下见面了。波哥，早！
Lái. Wǒ shì nǐ de línjū, wǒmen zài lóuxià jiànmiàn le. Bōgē, zǎo!
*Let's go. I'm your neighbour; we meet downstairs. Morning, Bo!*

**波哥：**早！今天真冷啊。
Zǎo! Jīntiān zhēn lěng a.
*Morning! So cold today.*

**莎莎：**是啊，真冷。最近怎么样？
Shì a, zhēn lěng. Zuìjìn zěnmeyàng?
*Yeah, freezing. How have you been?*

**波哥：**挺好的，就是有点忙。你呢？
Tǐng hǎo de, jiùshì yǒudiǎn máng. Nǐ ne?
*Pretty good — just a bit busy. And you?*

**莎莎：**我还好。对了，周末做什么了？
Wǒ hái hǎo. Duì le, zhōumò zuò shénme le?
*I'm okay. Oh — what did you do on the weekend?*

**波哥：**没做什么，在家休息。你呢？
Méi zuò shénme, zài jiā xiūxi. Nǐ ne?
*Didn't do much, rested at home. And you?*

**莎莎：**我去看了一个朋友。
Wǒ qù kàn le yí ge péngyou.
*I visited a friend.*

**波哥：**好啊。……那，我去上班了，再见！
Hǎo a. … Nà, wǒ qù shàngbān le, zàijiàn!
*Nice. … Well, off to work — bye!*

**莎莎：**再见！——你看，一次完整的小聊天，一分钟。
Zàijiàn! — Nǐ kàn, yí cì wánzhěng de xiǎo liáotiān, yì fēnzhōng.
*Bye! — And that's a complete little chat, in one minute.*

**波哥：**开场，回答，你呢，再见。我真的会聊天了！
Kāichǎng, huídá, nǐ ne, zàijiàn. Wǒ zhēn de huì liáotiān le!
*Opener, answer, "nǐ ne", goodbye. I can actually make small talk now!*

### 快问快答 · Speed round

Answer out loud **before** Bo does.

**莎莎：**好，最后，快问快答！准备好了吗？
Hǎo, zuìhòu, kuài wèn kuài dá! Zhǔnbèi hǎo le ma?
*Okay, speed round! Ready?*

**波哥：**准备好了！
Zhǔnbèi hǎo le!
*Ready!*

**莎莎：**见到朋友，第一句说什么？
Jiàndào péngyou, dì yī jù shuō shénme?
*You see a friend — first line?*

**波哥：**最近怎么样？
Zuìjìn zěnmeyàng?
*How have you been?*

**莎莎：**朋友看起来很累呢？
Péngyou kàn qǐlái hěn lèi ne?
*Your friend looks exhausted?*

**波哥：**还好吗？
Hái hǎo ma?
*Are you okay?*

**莎莎：**星期一早上呢？
Xīngqīyī zǎoshang ne?
*Monday morning?*

**波哥：**周末做什么了？
Zhōumò zuò shénme le?
*What did you do on the weekend?*

**莎莎：**周末什么都没做，怎么回答？
Zhōumò shénme dōu méi zuò, zěnme huídá?
*You did nothing all weekend — what do you say?*

**波哥：**没做什么，在家休息。
Méi zuò shénme, zài jiā xiūxi.
*Didn't do much, rested at home.*

**莎莎：**最简单的开场呢？
Zuì jiǎndān de kāichǎng ne?
*The simplest opener?*

**波哥：**说天气：今天真冷！
Shuō tiānqì: Jīntiān zhēn lěng!
*Weather: so cold today!*

**莎莎：**回答完，用哪两个字把问题送回去？
Huídá wán, yòng nǎ liǎng ge zì bǎ wèntí sòng huíqù?
*After answering, which two characters send the question back?*

**波哥：**你呢？
Nǐ ne?
*And you?*

### 结尾 · Ending

**莎莎：**全对！今天的三句开场，你再说一遍？
Quán duì! Jīntiān de sān jù kāichǎng, nǐ zài shuō yí biàn?
*All correct! Today's three openers — one more time?*

**波哥：**最近怎么样？周末做什么了？今天真冷。
Zuìjìn zěnmeyàng? Zhōumò zuò shénme le? Jīntiān zhēn lěng.
*How have you been? What did you do on the weekend? So cold today.*

**莎莎：**目标句也别忘了：最近怎么样？还好吗？
Mùbiāo jù yě bié wàng le: Zuìjìn zěnmeyàng? Hái hǎo ma?
*And don't forget the target sentence: how have you been? Everything okay?*

**波哥：**回答很短：还好。挺好的。有点忙。没做什么。然后：你呢？
Huídá hěn duǎn: Hái hǎo. Tǐng hǎo de. Yǒudiǎn máng. Méi zuò shénme. Ránhòu: Nǐ ne?
*Short answers: hái hǎo, tǐng hǎo de, yǒudiǎn máng, méi zuò shénme. Then: nǐ ne?*

**莎莎：**别忘了评论区的问题：你最近怎么样？用中文告诉我们。
Bié wàng le pínglùnqū de wèntí: Nǐ zuìjìn zěnmeyàng? Yòng Zhōngwén gàosu wǒmen.
*Don't forget our question in the comments: how have you been? Tell us in Chinese.*

**波哥：**下一集，我们说一个更难的时候。
Xià yì jí, wǒmen shuō yí ge gèng nán de shíhou.
*Next episode: a harder moment.*

**莎莎：**对方说得又快又长，你一个词都没听懂——怎么办？
Duìfāng shuō de yòu kuài yòu cháng, nǐ yí ge cí dōu méi tīngdǒng — zěnme bàn?
*They talk fast and long, and you catch nothing — what then?*

**波哥：**就是我今天"啊？"的那个时候！
Jiùshì wǒ jīntiān "À?" de nàge shíhou!
*That's my "huh?" moment from today!*

**莎莎：**三句话，帮你把话拿回来。下一集慢慢说。
Sān jù huà, bāng nǐ bǎ huà ná huílái. Xià yì jí mànmàn shuō.
*Three sentences to take the conversation back. Next episode, slowly.*

**波哥：**我们下一集见！
Wǒmen xià yì jí jiàn!
*See you next episode!*

**莎莎：**下次见。慢慢来。
Xiàcì jiàn. Mànmàn lái.
*See you next time. Take it slow.*
