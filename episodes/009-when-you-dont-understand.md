Beginner-friendly slow Mandarin. Every line is shown three ways: **汉字 Hanzi**, pinyin,
and *a short English gloss* — the same three layers you see on screen in the video.

> **Pinyin note:** we write 一 and 不 with the tone you actually hear (yí biàn, búshì).
> Neutral-tone syllables have no tone mark (méi guānxi, yìsi).

**How to use this page**

1. **Listen first** — play the episode once without reading.
2. **Read along** — replay it with this page open.
3. **Speak** — say the three rescue sentences out loud, then post your 请…… sentence.

## In this episode 这一集

- The "好，好，谢谢" escape — everyone has done it, and why you can stop.
- Not understanding is **normal**: too fast, too noisy, or too new — native speakers ask again too.
- Three rescue sentences, and the secret: you understood **more than you think** — three words
  are often enough.

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **请你再说一遍，说慢一点。**
> Qǐng nǐ zài shuō yí biàn, shuō màn yìdiǎn.
> *Please say that again — a little slower.*

## The three rescue sentences 三句救命的话

| 句子 | Pinyin | When |
|---|---|---|
| 请再说一遍。 | Qǐng zài shuō yí biàn. | you didn't catch it |
| 请说慢一点。 | Qǐng shuō màn yìdiǎn. | still too fast |
| "X"是什么意思？ | "X" shì shénme yìsi? | one word is new to you |

**Your turn:** start a sentence with 请 and post it in the comments — 请说慢一点 counts!

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**莎莎：**波哥，我说一件事，你听听是不是你。
Bōgē, wǒ shuō yí jiàn shì, nǐ tīngting shì bu shì nǐ.
*Bo, let me describe something — tell me if this is you.*

**波哥：**好，你说。
Hǎo, nǐ shuō.
*Okay, go ahead.*

**莎莎：**你问路，或者买东西。你的中文说得很好。
Nǐ wèn lù, huòzhě mǎi dōngxi. Nǐ de Zhōngwén shuō de hěn hǎo.
*You ask for directions, or buy something. Your Chinese comes out great.*

**波哥：**嗯，然后呢？
Ǹg, ránhòu ne?
*Uh-huh, and then?*

**莎莎：**然后对方开始回答——说得又快又长。
Ránhòu duìfāng kāishǐ huídá — shuō de yòu kuài yòu cháng.
*Then the other person starts answering — fast and long.*

**波哥：**啊，是我。你怎么知道？
À, shì wǒ. Nǐ zěnme zhīdào?
*Ah. That's me. How did you know?*

**莎莎：**哈哈，因为每个学中文的人，都有这个时候。
Hāhā, yīnwèi měi ge xué Zhōngwén de rén, dōu yǒu zhège shíhou.
*Haha — because every Chinese learner has this moment.*

**波哥：**那个时候，我的头里只有三个字：听不懂。
Nàge shíhou, wǒ de tóu lǐ zhǐ yǒu sān ge zì: tīng bu dǒng.
*In that moment, my head holds three characters only: tīng-bu-dǒng.*

**莎莎：**然后你做了什么？
Ránhòu nǐ zuò le shénme?
*And what did you do?*

**波哥：**我笑一笑，说"好，好，谢谢"，然后走了。
Wǒ xiào yi xiào, shuō "hǎo, hǎo, xièxie", ránhòu zǒu le.
*I smiled, said "okay, okay, thanks" — and walked away.*

**莎莎：**其实你没听懂。
Qíshí nǐ méi tīngdǒng.
*But you hadn't understood.*

**波哥：**对……其实我一个词都没听懂。
Duì… qíshí wǒ yí ge cí dōu méi tīngdǒng.
*Right… I hadn't caught a single word.*

**莎莎：**今天我们就聊这个：听不懂的时候，怎么办？三句话，帮你把话拿回来。最后还有一件事，很多人不知道。
Jīntiān wǒmen jiù liáo zhège: Tīng bu dǒng de shíhou, zěnme bàn? Sān jù huà, bāng nǐ bǎ huà ná huílái. Zuìhòu hái yǒu yí jiàn shì, hěn duō rén bù zhīdào.
*Today's topic: what to do when you don't understand. Three sentences that take the conversation back — plus one thing most people don't know.*

**波哥：**好，别走开。
Hǎo, bié zǒukāi.
*Great — don't go away.*

### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。在这里，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Zài zhèlǐ, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*I'm Bo. Here, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**今天说：听不懂的时候。
Jīntiān shuō: tīng bu dǒng de shíhou.
*Today: the moment you don't understand.*

**波哥：**这个时候人人都有。开始吧。
Zhège shíhou rénrén dōu yǒu. Kāishǐ ba.
*A moment everyone knows. Let's start.*

### 听不懂，很正常 · It's normal

**莎莎：**先问你：你觉得，听不懂，是因为你学得不好吗？
Xiān wèn nǐ: Nǐ juéde, tīng bu dǒng, shì yīnwèi nǐ xué de bù hǎo ma?
*First question: do you think not understanding means you've studied badly?*

**波哥：**是啊。听不懂，就是我不行。
Shì a. Tīng bu dǒng, jiùshì wǒ bù xíng.
*Well, yes. If I don't understand, I'm just not good enough.*

**莎莎：**不对。你想一想，别人说话，你为什么听不懂？
Bú duì. Nǐ xiǎng yi xiǎng, biéren shuōhuà, nǐ wèishénme tīng bu dǒng?
*Wrong. Think about it — why don't you understand?*

**波哥：**因为说得快？
Yīnwèi shuō de kuài?
*Because they talk fast?*

**莎莎：**对，这是第一个原因。他不知道你在学中文，他像平时一样说话。
Duì, zhè shì dì yī ge yuányīn. Tā bù zhīdào nǐ zài xué Zhōngwén, tā xiàng píngshí yíyàng shuōhuà.
*Yes — reason one. They don't know you're learning; they talk at normal speed.*

**波哥：**他说得快，不是我的错，也不是他的错。
Tā shuō de kuài, búshì wǒ de cuò, yě búshì tā de cuò.
*They talk fast — not my fault, not theirs either.*

**莎莎：**对。第二个原因：声音吵。街上有车，饭馆里人很多。
Duì. Dì èr ge yuányīn: shēngyīn chǎo. Jiē shàng yǒu chē, fànguǎn lǐ rén hěn duō.
*Right. Reason two: noise. Cars in the street, crowds in the restaurant.*

**波哥：**对！在安静的房间里我能听懂，街上声音太吵，就不行。
Duì! Zài ānjìng de fángjiān lǐ wǒ néng tīngdǒng, jiē shàng shēngyīn tài chǎo, jiù bù xíng.
*True! In a quiet room I understand — on a noisy street I can't.*

**莎莎：**第三个原因：有的词，你还没学过。这太正常了。
Dì sān ge yuányīn: yǒude cí, nǐ hái méi xué guò. Zhè tài zhèngcháng le.
*Reason three: some words you simply haven't learned yet. Completely normal.*

**波哥：**可是中国人听中文，总能听懂吧？
Kěshì Zhōngguórén tīng Zhōngwén, zǒng néng tīngdǒng ba?
*But Chinese people always understand Chinese… right?*

**莎莎：**也不是。打电话的时候，声音不好，我们也说：喂？再说一遍？
Yě búshì. Dǎ diànhuà de shíhou, shēngyīn bù hǎo, wǒmen yě shuō: Wéi? Zài shuō yí biàn?
*Not always. On a bad phone line, we also say: hello? Say that again?*

**波哥：**哦……"再说一遍"，中国人自己也天天用。
Ó… "zài shuō yí biàn", Zhōngguórén zìjǐ yě tiāntiān yòng.
*Oh… so native speakers use "say it again" every day too.*

**莎莎：**对。听不懂就问，不是学生做的事，是所有人做的事。
Duì. Tīng bu dǒng jiù wèn, búshì xuésheng zuò de shì, shì suǒyǒu rén zuò de shì.
*Right. Asking when you don't understand isn't a student thing — it's an everyone thing.*

**波哥：**听不懂，不是我不行。
Tīng bu dǒng, búshì wǒ bù xíng.
*Not understanding doesn't mean I'm not good enough.*

**莎莎：**对。是那句话太快，声音太吵，或者词太新。
Duì. Shì nà jù huà tài kuài, shēngyīn tài chǎo, huòzhě cí tài xīn.
*Exactly. The sentence was too fast, the place too noisy, or the word too new.*

**波哥：**快，吵，新。跟我没关系。
Kuài, chǎo, xīn. Gēn wǒ méi guānxi.
*Fast, noisy, new. Nothing to do with me.*

**莎莎：**来，你说说看：听不懂，是因为你学得不好吗？
Lái, nǐ shuōshuo kàn: Tīng bu dǒng, shì yīnwèi nǐ xué de bù hǎo ma?
*Your turn: does not understanding mean you've studied badly?*

**波哥：**不是。是说得快，声音吵，或者词太新。
Búshì. Shì shuō de kuài, shēngyīn chǎo, huòzhě cí tài xīn.
*No. It's the speed, the noise, or a new word.*

**莎莎：**对！记住这个，我们再学怎么说。
Duì! Jìzhù zhège, wǒmen zài xué zěnme shuō.
*Correct! Hold on to that — now let's learn what to say.*

### 小任务 · Your turn

**波哥：**好，三句话，快教我。
Hǎo, sān jù huà, kuài jiāo wǒ.
*Okay, three sentences — teach me, quick.*

**莎莎：**别急。先请你做一件小事。
Bié jí. Xiān qǐng nǐ zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì.
*Patience. First, one small thing.*

**波哥：**哈哈，评论区时间到了。
Hāhā, pínglùnqū shíjiān dào le.
*Haha — comment-section time.*

**莎莎：**对。今天用"请"开头，写一句话，放在评论区。
Duì. Jīntiān yòng "qǐng" kāitóu, xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Yes. Today, start a sentence with 请 and post it in the comments.*

**波哥：**我先来：请说慢一点。
Wǒ xiān lái: Qǐng shuō màn yìdiǎn.
*Me first: please speak a little slower.*

**莎莎：**很好，这句今天就会学。再来一句？
Hěn hǎo, zhè jù jīntiān jiù huì xué. Zài lái yí jù?
*Nice — we're learning that one today. Another?*

**波哥：**请给我一杯热水。哈哈。
Qǐng gěi wǒ yì bēi rè shuǐ. Hāhā.
*Please give me a cup of hot water. Haha.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，很中国。你也用"请"写一句，我们会看，也会回你。
Hāhā, hěn Zhōngguó. Nǐ yě yòng "qǐng" xiě yí jù, wǒmen huì kàn, yě huì huí nǐ.
*Haha, very Chinese of you. Write your own 请 sentence — we read and reply.*

### 三句好用的话 · Three rescue sentences

**波哥：**好。现在，第一句救命的话。
Hǎo. Xiànzài, dì yī jù jiùmìng de huà.
*Okay. Now — rescue sentence number one.*

**莎莎：**第一句：请再说一遍。
Dì yī jù: Qǐng zài shuō yí biàn.
*Number one: please say it again.*

**波哥：**请再说一遍。
Qǐng zài shuō yí biàn.
*"Qǐng zài shuō yí biàn."*

**莎莎：**遍，就是一次。再说一遍，就是再说一次。
Biàn, jiùshì yí cì. Zài shuō yí biàn, jiùshì zài shuō yí cì.
*"Biàn" means "one time". Say it one more time.*

**波哥：**跟第五集那句很像：再说一次就好。
Gēn dì wǔ jí nà jù hěn xiàng: zài shuō yí cì jiù hǎo.
*Very close to the episode-five line: just say it again.*

**莎莎：**对，老朋友。听不懂的时候，别走，说：请再说一遍。
Duì, lǎo péngyou. Tīng bu dǒng de shíhou, bié zǒu, shuō: Qǐng zài shuō yí biàn.
*Yes, an old friend. When you don't understand — don't walk away. Say: please say it again.*

**波哥：**请再说一遍。别人会不会不高兴？
Qǐng zài shuō yí biàn. Biéren huì bu huì bù gāoxìng?
*"Qǐng zài shuō yí biàn." Won't people get annoyed?*

**莎莎：**不会。他会想：哦，他没听清楚，我再说一次。
Bú huì. Tā huì xiǎng: Ó, tā méi tīng qīngchu, wǒ zài shuō yí cì.
*No. They'll think: ah, he didn't catch it — I'll say it again.*

**波哥：**好。第二句呢？
Hǎo. Dì èr jù ne?
*Good. Sentence two?*

**莎莎：**他再说了一遍，可是还是很快。这时候用第二句：请说慢一点。
Tā zài shuō le yí biàn, kěshì háishì hěn kuài. Zhè shíhou yòng dì èr jù: Qǐng shuō màn yìdiǎn.
*They said it again — still too fast. That's when you use number two: please speak slower.*

**波哥：**请说慢一点！
Qǐng shuō màn yìdiǎn!
*"Qǐng shuō màn yìdiǎn!"*

**莎莎：**对。两句还可以放在一起，就是今天的目标句：请你再说一遍，说慢一点。
Duì. Liǎng jù hái kěyǐ fàng zài yìqǐ, jiùshì jīntiān de mùbiāo jù: Qǐng nǐ zài shuō yí biàn, shuō màn yìdiǎn.
*Right. And you can join them — today's target sentence: please say it again, a little slower.*

**波哥：**请你再说一遍，说慢一点。
Qǐng nǐ zài shuō yí biàn, shuō màn yìdiǎn.
*Please say it again — a little slower.*

**莎莎：**前面是"再说一遍"，后面是"说慢一点"。
Qiánmiàn shì "zài shuō yí biàn", hòumiàn shì "shuō màn yìdiǎn".
*First half: say it again. Second half: slower.*

**波哥：**一句话，把两件事都说了。
Yí jù huà, bǎ liǎng jiàn shì dōu shuō le.
*One sentence, both requests.*

**莎莎：**对。这句话说出来，大部分人都会慢下来。
Duì. Zhè jù huà shuō chūlái, dàbùfen rén dōu huì màn xiàlái.
*Yes. Say it, and most people will slow right down.*

**波哥：**太好了。那第三句呢？
Tài hǎo le. Nà dì sān jù ne?
*Great. And number three?*

**莎莎：**第三句，听到新词的时候用："X"是什么意思？
Dì sān jù, tīngdào xīn cí de shíhou yòng: "X" shì shénme yìsi?
*Number three is for new words: what does "X" mean?*

**波哥：**是什么意思？
Shì shénme yìsi?
*"Shì shénme yìsi?"*

**莎莎：**比如，别人说"周末"，你没学过这个词。你就问："周末"是什么意思？
Bǐrú, biéren shuō "zhōumò", nǐ méi xué guò zhège cí. Nǐ jiù wèn: "Zhōumò" shì shénme yìsi?
*Say someone uses "zhōumò" and you've never learned it. Just ask: what does "zhōumò" mean?*

**波哥：**"周末"是什么意思？
"Zhōumò" shì shénme yìsi?
*What does "zhōumò" mean?*

**莎莎：**他会用简单的话告诉你：就是星期六和星期天。
Tā huì yòng jiǎndān de huà gàosu nǐ: jiùshì xīngqīliù hé xīngqītiān.
*They'll explain it simply: Saturday and Sunday.*

**波哥：**哦！这样我又学了一个新词。
Ó! Zhèyàng wǒ yòu xué le yí ge xīn cí.
*Oh! And just like that, I've learned a new word.*

**莎莎：**对。你问得越多，学得越多。
Duì. Nǐ wèn de yuè duō, xué de yuè duō.
*Exactly. The more you ask, the more you learn.*

**波哥：**哈哈，"越……越……"，第三集的老朋友。
Hāhā, "yuè… yuè…", dì sān jí de lǎo péngyou.
*Haha — the 越…越 pattern, an old friend from episode three.*

**莎莎：**来，你说说看：三句救命的话？
Lái, nǐ shuōshuo kàn: sān jù jiùmìng de huà?
*Your turn: the three rescue sentences?*

**波哥：**请再说一遍。请说慢一点。还有："X"是什么意思？
Qǐng zài shuō yí biàn. Qǐng shuō màn yìdiǎn. Hái yǒu: "X" shì shénme yìsi?
*Please say it again. Please speak slower. And: what does "X" mean?*

**莎莎：**全对！
Quán duì!
*All correct!*

### 问了会发生什么 · What actually happens

**波哥：**可是莎莎，我还有一个心里的问题。
Kěshì Shāsha, wǒ hái yǒu yí ge xīnlǐ de wèntí.
*But Sasha, there's still something on my mind.*

**莎莎：**你说。
Nǐ shuō.
*Go on.*

**波哥：**我怕问。我问了，别人会不会觉得我很麻烦？
Wǒ pà wèn. Wǒ wèn le, biéren huì bu huì juéde wǒ hěn máfan?
*I'm afraid to ask. Won't people find me annoying?*

**莎莎：**那我们来看看：你问了以后，真的会发生什么。
Nà wǒmen lái kànkan: nǐ wèn le yǐhòu, zhēn de huì fāshēng shénme.
*Then let's look at what actually happens after you ask.*

**波哥：**好。
Hǎo.
*Okay.*

**莎莎：**大部分时候，别人会慢下来，再说一次。有的时候，他会换简单的词，或者用手做给你看。
Dàbùfen shíhou, biéren huì màn xiàlái, zài shuō yí cì. Yǒude shíhou, tā huì huàn jiǎndān de cí, huòzhě yòng shǒu zuò gěi nǐ kàn.
*Usually, they slow down and repeat. Sometimes they switch to simpler words, or show you with their hands.*

**波哥：**哦，就像第四集说的：手指是最好的翻译。
Ó, jiù xiàng dì sì jí shuō de: shǒuzhǐ shì zuì hǎo de fānyì.
*Ah — like episode four said: the finger is the best translator.*

**莎莎：**对。还有的时候，他会笑一笑——不是笑你，是想帮你。
Duì. Hái yǒude shíhou, tā huì xiào yi xiào — búshì xiào nǐ, shì xiǎng bāng nǐ.
*Right. And sometimes they smile — not at you, but because they want to help.*

**波哥：**没有人生气？
Méiyǒu rén shēngqì?
*Nobody gets angry?*

**莎莎：**差不多没有。你想想：你说英文，别人问你"可以再说一遍吗"，你会生气吗？
Chàbuduō méiyǒu. Nǐ xiǎngxiang: nǐ shuō Yīngwén, biéren wèn nǐ "kěyǐ zài shuō yí biàn ma", nǐ huì shēngqì ma?
*Almost never. Think: when you speak English and someone asks "could you say that again?" — do you get angry?*

**波哥：**当然不会！我会说得慢一点。
Dāngrán bú huì! Wǒ huì shuō de màn yìdiǎn.
*Of course not! I'd just slow down.*

**莎莎：**对。你怎么对别人，别人就怎么对你。
Duì. Nǐ zěnme duì biéren, biéren jiù zěnme duì nǐ.
*Exactly. How you treat others is how they treat you.*

**波哥：**问，不是麻烦别人。不问，聊天就停了；问了，聊天才能走下去。
Wèn, búshì máfan biéren. Bú wèn, liáotiān jiù tíng le; wèn le, liáotiān cái néng zǒu xiàqù.
*Asking isn't bothering people. If you don't ask, the chat dies; if you ask, it can go on.*

**莎莎：**说得太好了。
Shuō de tài hǎo le.
*Beautifully said.*

**波哥：**莎莎，我发现，这个节目有一个好地方。
Shāsha, wǒ fāxiàn, zhège jiémù yǒu yí ge hǎo dìfang.
*Sasha, you know what's great about this show?*

**莎莎：**什么好地方？
Shénme hǎo dìfang?
*What?*

**波哥：**在这里，听不懂的时候，没有人在等我。
Zài zhèlǐ, tīng bu dǒng de shíhou, méiyǒu rén zài děng wǒ.
*Here, when I don't understand, nobody is standing there waiting for me.*

**莎莎：**对。你可以停下来，再听一遍，再听十遍。
Duì. Nǐ kěyǐ tíng xiàlái, zài tīng yí biàn, zài tīng shí biàn.
*Right. You can pause — listen again, or ten more times.*

**波哥：**跟真人说话，不能说"请再说十遍"。可是在这里可以。
Gēn zhēn rén shuōhuà, bù néng shuō "qǐng zài shuō shí biàn". Kěshì zài zhèlǐ kěyǐ.
*You can't ask a real person to repeat ten times. But here you can.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，对。如果你想要一个可以听很多遍的地方，就订阅慢慢中文。
Hāhā, duì. Rúguǒ nǐ xiǎng yào yí ge kěyǐ tīng hěn duō biàn de dìfang, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*Haha, true. If you'd like a place you can replay as often as you want, subscribe.*

**波哥：**每个星期都有新的一集，你想听几遍，就听几遍。
Měi ge xīngqī dōu yǒu xīn de yì jí, nǐ xiǎng tīng jǐ biàn, jiù tīng jǐ biàn.
*A new episode every week — listen as many times as you like.*

**莎莎：**先在这里听懂，再到外面去听。
Xiān zài zhèlǐ tīngdǒng, zài dào wàimiàn qù tīng.
*Understand it here first, then take your ears outside.*

### 你其实听懂了一点 · You caught more than you think

**波哥：**好。那最后那件事呢？很多人不知道的那件。
Hǎo. Nà zuìhòu nà jiàn shì ne? Hěn duō rén bù zhīdào de nà jiàn.
*Okay — now that last thing? The one most people don't know?*

**莎莎：**对，现在说。听不懂的时候，你其实不是什么都没听懂。
Duì, xiànzài shuō. Tīng bu dǒng de shíhou, nǐ qíshí búshì shénme dōu méi tīngdǒng.
*Yes, here it is. When you "don't understand", you actually didn't catch nothing.*

**波哥：**什么意思？我真的觉得我什么都没听懂。
Shénme yìsi? Wǒ zhēn de juéde wǒ shénme dōu méi tīngdǒng.
*Meaning what? It really feels like I caught nothing.*

**莎莎：**比如，别人说了十个词，你听懂了三个：明天、饭馆、六点。
Bǐrú, biéren shuō le shí ge cí, nǐ tīngdǒng le sān ge: míngtiān, fànguǎn, liù diǎn.
*Say they used ten words and you caught three: tomorrow, restaurant, six o'clock.*

**波哥：**明天，饭馆，六点……哦！明天六点，去饭馆！
Míngtiān, fànguǎn, liù diǎn… Ó! Míngtiān liù diǎn, qù fànguǎn!
*Tomorrow, restaurant, six… oh! Tomorrow at six, at the restaurant!*

**莎莎：**你看，三个词，你就懂了最重要的事。
Nǐ kàn, sān ge cí, nǐ jiù dǒng le zuì zhòngyào de shì.
*See? Three words gave you the most important part.*

**波哥：**不用每个词都懂！
Búyòng měi ge cí dōu dǒng!
*You don't need every word!*

**莎莎：**对。记住你听懂的词，别的，问。
Duì. Jìzhù nǐ tīngdǒng de cí, biéde, wèn.
*Right. Keep the words you caught — and ask about the rest.*

**波哥：**听懂三个词，加一句"请再说一遍"，就够了。
Tīngdǒng sān ge cí, jiā yí jù "qǐng zài shuō yí biàn", jiù gòu le.
*Three words plus one "qǐng zài shuō yí biàn" — that's enough.*

**莎莎：**对。慢慢的，三个词会变成五个词，十个词。
Duì. Mànmàn de, sān ge cí huì biànchéng wǔ ge cí, shí ge cí.
*Yes. And slowly, three words become five, then ten.*

**波哥：**你听得越多，懂得越多。
Nǐ tīng de yuè duō, dǒng de yuè duō.
*The more you listen, the more you understand.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，对，还是那句话。
Hāhā, duì, háishì nà jù huà.
*Haha — that sentence again.*

### 快问快答 · Speed round

Answer out loud **before** Bo does.

**波哥：**好，最后，快问快答！这次你问我。
Hǎo, zuìhòu, kuài wèn kuài dá! Zhè cì nǐ wèn wǒ.
*Okay, speed round! You ask, I answer.*

**莎莎：**好。没听懂，第一句说什么？
Hǎo. Méi tīngdǒng, dì yī jù shuō shénme?
*Fine. You didn't understand — sentence one?*

**波哥：**请再说一遍。
Qǐng zài shuō yí biàn.
*Please say it again.*

**莎莎：**他还是说得很快呢？
Tā háishì shuō de hěn kuài ne?
*Still too fast?*

**波哥：**请说慢一点。
Qǐng shuō màn yìdiǎn.
*Please speak slower.*

**莎莎：**两句放在一起？
Liǎng jù fàng zài yìqǐ?
*Both together?*

**波哥：**请你再说一遍，说慢一点。
Qǐng nǐ zài shuō yí biàn, shuō màn yìdiǎn.
*Please say it again — a little slower.*

**莎莎：**听到新词"周末"，怎么问？
Tīngdào xīn cí "zhōumò", zěnme wèn?
*You hear the new word "zhōumò" — how do you ask?*

**波哥：**"周末"是什么意思？
"Zhōumò" shì shénme yìsi?
*What does "zhōumò" mean?*

**莎莎：**听不懂，是因为你不行吗？
Tīng bu dǒng, shì yīnwèi nǐ bù xíng ma?
*Not understanding — does it mean you're not good enough?*

**波哥：**不是！是说得快，声音吵，或者词太新。
Búshì! Shì shuō de kuài, shēngyīn chǎo, huòzhě cí tài xīn.
*No! It's the speed, the noise, or a new word.*

**莎莎：**十个词只听懂三个，怎么办？
Shí ge cí zhǐ tīngdǒng sān ge, zěnme bàn?
*You caught three words out of ten — what now?*

**波哥：**记住这三个词，别的，问！
Jìzhù zhè sān ge cí, biéde, wèn!
*Keep those three — and ask about the rest!*

### 结尾 · Ending

**莎莎：**全对！好，今天我们说了：听不懂，人人都有，中国人也有。
Quán duì! Hǎo, jīntiān wǒmen shuō le: tīng bu dǒng, rénrén dōu yǒu, Zhōngguórén yě yǒu.
*All correct! So — everyone has these moments, native speakers included.*

**波哥：**三句话：请再说一遍。请说慢一点。还有：是什么意思？
Sān jù huà: Qǐng zài shuō yí biàn. Qǐng shuō màn yìdiǎn. Hái yǒu: shì shénme yìsi?
*Three sentences: say it again, please. Slower, please. And: what does it mean?*

**莎莎：**合在一起，就是：请你再说一遍，说慢一点。
Hé zài yìqǐ, jiùshì: Qǐng nǐ zài shuō yí biàn, shuō màn yìdiǎn.
*Joined together: please say it again, a little slower.*

**波哥：**说错了没关系，听不懂也没关系。问，就好。
Shuō cuò le méi guānxi, tīng bu dǒng yě méi guānxi. Wèn, jiù hǎo.
*Saying it wrong is okay; not understanding is okay too. Just ask.*

**莎莎：**别忘了你的小事：用"请"写一句话，放在评论区。
Bié wàng le nǐ de xiǎo shì: yòng "qǐng" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Don't forget your task: one 请 sentence in the comments.*

**波哥：**今天没有下集预告，因为我们想听你的。
Jīntiān méiyǒu xià jí yùgào, yīnwèi wǒmen xiǎng tīng nǐ de.
*No preview today — we want to hear from you.*

**莎莎：**你想让我们聊什么话题？写在评论区。
Nǐ xiǎng ràng wǒmen liáo shénme huàtí? Xiě zài pínglùnqū.
*What topic should we cover? Tell us in the comments.*

**波哥：**我们会先看：这个话题适不适合频道，对学习有没有帮助。
Wǒmen huì xiān kàn: zhège huàtí shì bu shìhé píndào, duì xuéxí yǒu méiyǒu bāngzhù.
*We'll check whether it fits the channel and helps learning.*

**莎莎：**也会看它安不安全，是不是我们能做好的。
Yě huì kàn tā ān bu ānquán, shì bu shì wǒmen néng zuò hǎo de.
*And whether it's safe, and something we can do well.*

**波哥：**合适的，我们放进节目单。不能保证每个都做，写一次就够了。
Héshì de, wǒmen fàng jìn jiémùdān. Bù néng bǎozhèng měi ge dōu zuò, xiě yí cì jiù gòu le.
*Suitable ones go on the list. No promises — one comment is enough.*

**莎莎：**谢谢你和我们在一起。我是莎莎。
Xièxie nǐ hé wǒmen zài yìqǐ. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Thanks for being with us. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。我们下期再见。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Wǒmen xià qī zàijiàn.
*I'm Bo. See you next time.*

**莎莎：**下次见。慢慢来。
Xiàcì jiàn. Mànmàn lái.
*See you next time. Take it slow.*
