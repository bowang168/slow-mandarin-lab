> Neutral-tone syllables have no tone mark (biéren, gùshi).

## In this episode 这一集

- Two friends start Chinese together — and one races ahead.
- The teacher's **three questions** that make unfair comparison fall apart.
- A new way to compare: not with him — with yesterday's you.

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **别人是别人，你是你。**
> Biéren shì biéren, nǐ shì nǐ.
> *Others are others; you are you.*

## Key vocabulary 重点词

| 词 | Pinyin | English |
|---|---|---|
| 比较 | bǐjiào | to compare |
| 比 | bǐ | than (the comparison word) |
| 进步 | jìnbù | progress; to improve |
| 放弃 | fàngqì | to give up |
| 着急 | zháojí | anxious, worried (about being slow) |
| 难过 | nánguò | sad, upset |
| 速度 | sùdù | speed, pace |
| 正常 | zhèngcháng | normal |
| 笨 | bèn | stupid, slow-witted |
| 发音 | fāyīn | pronunciation |
| 努力 | nǔlì | effort, hard work |
| 视频 | shìpín | video |
| 本子 | běnzi | notebook |
| 一直 | yìzhí | always, all along |

## Pattern of the day 今日句型：A 比 B ……

A 比 B + adjective = *A is more … than B*. Keep the pair in one sentence.

1. 哥哥比我高。 — *My brother is taller than me.*
2. 今天比昨天冷。 — *Today is colder than yesterday.*
3. 你的发音比我好。 — *Your pronunciation is better than mine.*
4. 今天的我，比昨天的我好一点。 — *Today's me is a little better than yesterday's me.*

**Your turn:** write one 比 sentence in the comments — compare today's you with
yesterday's you, not with anyone else.

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**莎莎：**波哥，我问你：学中文的时候，看到别人说得比你好，你心里会怎么样？
Bōgē, wǒ wèn nǐ: xué Zhōngwén de shíhou, kàn dào biéren shuō de bǐ nǐ hǎo, nǐ xīnlǐ huì zěnmeyàng?
*Bo, tell me: when you see someone speaking better Chinese than you, how does it feel?*

**波哥：**会难过。有时候还会想：我是不是太慢了？
Huì nánguò. Yǒushíhou hái huì xiǎng: wǒ shì bu shì tài màn le?
*It hurts. Sometimes I think: am I too slow?*

**莎莎：**今天的故事，就是关于这件事。
Jīntiān de gùshi, jiùshì guānyú zhè jiàn shì.
*Today's story is about exactly that.*

**波哥：**故事说的是谁？
Gùshi shuō de shì shéi?
*Who is the story about?*

**莎莎：**我们的一个朋友，叫小明。他的中文老师给他起了这个名字。
Wǒmen de yí ge péngyou, jiào Xiǎomíng. Tā de Zhōngwén lǎoshī gěi tā qǐ le zhège míngzi.
*A friend of ours called Xiaoming — his Chinese teacher gave him the name.*

**波哥：**那小明怎么了？
Nà Xiǎomíng zěnme le?
*So what happened to him?*

**莎莎：**他学了一年中文，然后，差一点就不学了。
Tā xué le yì nián Zhōngwén, ránhòu, chà yìdiǎn jiù bù xué le.
*He studied Chinese for a year — then almost quit.*

**波哥：**为什么？
Wèishénme?
*Why?*

**莎莎：**因为一个朋友。这个朋友什么都没有做错。
Yīnwèi yí ge péngyou. Zhège péngyou shénme dōu méiyǒu zuò cuò.
*Because of a friend. A friend who did nothing wrong.*

**波哥：**什么都没做错，可是小明想放弃？
Shénme dōu méi zuò cuò, kěshì Xiǎomíng xiǎng fàngqì?
*Nothing wrong — and yet Xiaoming wanted to give up?*

**莎莎：**对。答案在故事的最后，是一句话，八个字。
Duì. Dá'àn zài gùshi de zuìhòu, shì yí jù huà, bā ge zì.
*Yes. The answer comes at the end — one sentence, eight characters.*

**波哥：**八个字？好，我想听。别走开。
Bā ge zì? Hǎo, wǒ xiǎng tīng. Bié zǒukāi.
*Eight characters? I want to hear this. Don't go away.*

### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。在这里，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Zài zhèlǐ, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*I'm Bo. Here, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**今天是一个虚构的小故事，名字叫：别再比较了。
Jīntiān shì yí ge xūgòu de xiǎo gùshi, míngzi jiào: Bié zài bǐjiào le.
*Today's story is fictional. It's called: Stop Comparing.*

**波哥：**慢慢来，故事开始吧。
Mànmàn lái, gùshi kāishǐ ba.
*Take it slow — let the story begin.*

### 一起开始的两个人 · Two friends, one start

**莎莎：**小明和马克是好朋友。
Xiǎomíng hé Mǎkè shì hǎo péngyou.
*Xiaoming and Mark are good friends.*

**波哥：**马克也学中文吗？
Mǎkè yě xué Zhōngwén ma?
*Does Mark learn Chinese too?*

**莎莎：**对。去年，他们两个人一起开始学。
Duì. Qùnián, tāmen liǎng ge rén yìqǐ kāishǐ xué.
*Yes. Last year they started together.*

**波哥：**一起开始，一起练习，很好啊。
Yìqǐ kāishǐ, yìqǐ liànxí, hěn hǎo a.
*Starting together, practising together — sounds great.*

**莎莎：**一开始真的很好。可是半年以后，马克进步得很快。
Yì kāishǐ zhēnde hěn hǎo. Kěshì bàn nián yǐhòu, Mǎkè jìnbù de hěn kuài.
*At first it was. But after half a year, Mark improved fast.*

**波哥：**进步，就是越来越好，对吗？
Jìnbù, jiùshì yuèláiyuè hǎo, duì ma?
*"Jìnbù" means getting better and better, right?*

**莎莎：**对。进步，就是比以前更好。马克已经能跟中国朋友聊天了。
Duì. Jìnbù, jiùshì bǐ yǐqián gèng hǎo. Mǎkè yǐjīng néng gēn Zhōngguó péngyou liáotiān le.
*Right — better than before. Mark could already chat with Chinese friends.*

**波哥：**那小明呢？
Nà Xiǎomíng ne?
*And Xiaoming?*

**莎莎：**小明还在慢慢学。他能听懂一些，可是说得很慢。
Xiǎomíng hái zài mànmàn xué. Tā néng tīngdǒng yìxiē, kěshì shuō de hěn màn.
*Still learning slowly. He understood some, but spoke very slowly.*

**波哥：**这很正常啊，每个人的速度不一样。
Zhè hěn zhèngcháng a, měi ge rén de sùdù bù yíyàng.
*That's normal — everyone has their own pace.*

**莎莎：**小明一开始也这么想。可是后来，他开始比较了。
Xiǎomíng yì kāishǐ yě zhème xiǎng. Kěshì hòulái, tā kāishǐ bǐjiào le.
*He thought so too, at first. But then he started comparing.*

**波哥：**比较，就是把自己和别人放在一起看？
Bǐjiào, jiùshì bǎ zìjǐ hé biéren fàng zài yìqǐ kàn?
*"Bǐjiào" — putting yourself and someone else side by side?*

**莎莎：**对，看谁快，看谁好。马克说一句长句子——
Duì, kàn shéi kuài, kàn shéi hǎo. Mǎkè shuō yí jù cháng jùzi —
*Yes — who's faster, who's better. Mark says a long sentence —*

**波哥：**小明就想：我说不了这么长。
Xiǎomíng jiù xiǎng: wǒ shuō bu liǎo zhème cháng.
*— and Xiaoming thinks: I could never say that.*

**莎莎：**马克用一个新词，小明就想：我怎么不会这个词？
Mǎkè yòng yí ge xīn cí, Xiǎomíng jiù xiǎng: wǒ zěnme bú huì zhège cí?
*Mark uses a new word — Xiaoming thinks: why don't I know it?*

**波哥：**他越比较，心里越难过。
Tā yuè bǐjiào, xīnlǐ yuè nánguò.
*The more he compared, the worse he felt.*

**莎莎：**对。"越……越……"，第三集的老朋友，这次是不好的那种。
Duì. "Yuè…… yuè……", dì sān jí de lǎo péngyou, zhè cì shì bù hǎo de nà zhǒng.
*Right — the 越…越… pattern from episode three, the bad kind this time.*

**波哥：**越难过，越不想学；越不想学，学得越少。
Yuè nánguò, yuè bù xiǎng xué; yuè bù xiǎng xué, xué de yuè shǎo.
*The sadder he got, the less he wanted to study — and the less he studied.*

**莎莎：**学得越少，就更比不过了。这是一个不好的圈。
Xué de yuè shǎo, jiù gèng bǐ bu guò le. Zhè shì yí ge bù hǎo de quān.
*And the less you study, the further behind you fall. A bad circle.*

### 小任务 · Your turn

**莎莎：**说到"比"，我们停一下，请你做一件小事。
Shuō dào "bǐ", wǒmen tíng yíxià, qǐng nǐ zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì.
*Speaking of 比 — let's pause for one small thing.*

**波哥：**评论区时间到了。
Pínglùnqū shíjiān dào le.
*Comment-section time.*

**莎莎：**对。用"比"写一句话，放在评论区。
Duì. Yòng "bǐ" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Yes. Write one sentence with 比 in the comments.*

**波哥：**我先来：哥哥比我高。
Wǒ xiān lái: gēge bǐ wǒ gāo.
*Me first: my brother is taller than me.*

**莎莎：**很好，最简单的"比"。再来一句？
Hěn hǎo, zuì jiǎndān de "bǐ". Zài lái yí jù?
*Nice — the simplest 比. Another?*

**波哥：**今天比昨天冷，可是我很开心。
Jīntiān bǐ zuótiān lěng, kěshì wǒ hěn kāixīn.
*Today is colder than yesterday — but I'm happy.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，还带了一个"可是"。你也写一句，我们会看，也会回你。
Hāhā, hái dài le yí ge "kěshì". Nǐ yě xiě yí jù, wǒmen huì kàn, yě huì huí nǐ.
*Haha, with a bonus 可是. Write yours too — we read and reply.*

### 老师的三个问题 · The teacher's three questions

**莎莎：**回到故事。有一天，马克给小明看了一个视频。
Huídào gùshi. Yǒu yì tiān, Mǎkè gěi Xiǎomíng kàn le yí ge shìpín.
*Back to the story. One day, Mark showed Xiaoming a video.*

**波哥：**什么视频？
Shénme shìpín?
*What video?*

**莎莎：**视频里，马克用中文介绍自己，说得又快又清楚。
Shìpín lǐ, Mǎkè yòng Zhōngwén jièshào zìjǐ, shuō de yòu kuài yòu qīngchu.
*Mark introducing himself in Chinese — fast and clear.*

**波哥：**小明看了，说什么？
Xiǎomíng kàn le, shuō shénme?
*What did Xiaoming say?*

**莎莎：**他说：真好！可是他心里不是这么想的。
Tā shuō: zhēn hǎo! Kěshì tā xīnlǐ búshì zhème xiǎng de.
*He said: great! But that's not what he felt inside.*

**波哥：**他心里在想什么？
Tā xīnlǐ zài xiǎng shénme?
*What was he really thinking?*

**莎莎：**他想：我们是一起开始的，为什么他那么快？
Tā xiǎng: wǒmen shì yìqǐ kāishǐ de, wèishénme tā nàme kuài?
*He thought: we started together — why is he so fast?*

**波哥：**这个想法，很多人都有过。
Zhège xiǎngfǎ, hěn duō rén dōu yǒu guò.
*A thought many of us have had.*

**莎莎：**那天晚上，小明没有打开他的中文书。第二天，也没有。他想：算了，我可能不适合学中文。
Nàtiān wǎnshang, Xiǎomíng méiyǒu dǎkāi tā de Zhōngwén shū. Dì èr tiān, yě méiyǒu. Tā xiǎng: suàn le, wǒ kěnéng bú shìhé xué Zhōngwén.
*That night he didn't open his Chinese book. Nor the next day. He thought: forget it — maybe Chinese isn't for me.*

**波哥：**等等，这就要放弃了？
Děngdeng, zhè jiù yào fàngqì le?
*Wait — giving up, just like that?*

**莎莎：**放弃，就是不做了，把它放下了。
Fàngqì, jiùshì bú zuò le, bǎ tā fàngxià le.
*"Fàngqì" means to stop — to put it down.*

**波哥：**一年的努力，就这样不要了？
Yì nián de nǔlì, jiù zhèyàng búyào le?
*A whole year of effort, thrown away?*

**莎莎：**他差一点就放弃了。还好，那个星期，他见到了中文老师，王老师。
Tā chà yìdiǎn jiù fàngqì le. Hái hǎo, nàge xīngqī, tā jiàn dào le Zhōngwén lǎoshī, Wáng lǎoshī.
*He almost did. Luckily, that week he saw his Chinese teacher, Teacher Wang.*

**波哥：**老师看出他不对了吗？
Lǎoshī kànchū tā bú duì le ma?
*Did she notice something was wrong?*

**莎莎：**对。王老师一看到他，就问：小明，你怎么了？
Duì. Wáng lǎoshī yí kàn dào tā, jiù wèn: Xiǎomíng, nǐ zěnme le?
*Yes. The moment she saw him she asked: Xiaoming, what's wrong?*

**波哥：**小明说实话了吗？
Xiǎomíng shuō shíhuà le ma?
*Did he tell the truth?*

**莎莎：**说了。他说：老师，马克比我好太多了，我是不是很笨？
Shuō le. Tā shuō: lǎoshī, Mǎkè bǐ wǒ hǎo tài duō le, wǒ shì bu shì hěn bèn?
*He did. He said: Mark is so much better than me — am I just stupid?*

**波哥：**老师怎么回答？
Lǎoshī zěnme huídá?
*And her answer?*

**莎莎：**她没有马上回答，她先问了小明三个问题。第一个：马克每天学多长时间？
Tā méiyǒu mǎshàng huídá, tā xiān wèn le Xiǎomíng sān ge wèntí. Dì yī ge: Mǎkè měitiān xué duō cháng shíjiān?
*She didn't answer right away — she asked three questions. First: how long does Mark study each day?*

**波哥：**小明怎么说？
Xiǎomíng zěnme shuō?
*What did Xiaoming say?*

**莎莎：**马克每天学三个小时。他时间很多。
Mǎkè měitiān xué sān ge xiǎoshí. Tā shíjiān hěn duō.
*Mark studies three hours a day. He has lots of time.*

**波哥：**小明呢？
Xiǎomíng ne?
*And Xiaoming?*

**莎莎：**小明每天只有半个小时。他工作忙，家里还有两个孩子。
Xiǎomíng měitiān zhǐyǒu bàn ge xiǎoshí. Tā gōngzuò máng, jiālǐ hái yǒu liǎng ge háizi.
*Only half an hour a day. A busy job, and two kids at home.*

**波哥：**三个小时和半个小时，这怎么比？
Sān ge xiǎoshí hé bàn ge xiǎoshí, zhè zěnme bǐ?
*Three hours versus half an hour — how is that a fair comparison?*

**莎莎：**对，没办法比。第二个问题：马克住在哪儿？答案：他住在中国。
Duì, méi bànfǎ bǐ. Dì èr ge wèntí: Mǎkè zhù zài nǎr? Dá'àn: tā zhù zài Zhōngguó.
*Exactly — it isn't. Question two: where does Mark live? Answer: in China.*

**波哥：**住在中国，每天出门就能说中文。
Zhù zài Zhōngguó, měitiān chūmén jiù néng shuō Zhōngwén.
*Living in China, he speaks Chinese every time he steps outside.*

**莎莎：**对。小明不住在中国，一个星期才用一次中文。
Duì. Xiǎomíng bú zhù zài Zhōngguó, yí ge xīngqī cái yòng yí cì Zhōngwén.
*Right. Xiaoming doesn't — he uses Chinese maybe once a week.*

**波哥：**天天用和很少用，当然不一样。
Tiāntiān yòng hé hěn shǎo yòng, dāngrán bù yíyàng.
*Daily use versus rare use — of course they differ.*

**莎莎：**第三个问题最重要：一年以前，你会说中文吗？
Dì sān ge wèntí zuì zhòngyào: yì nián yǐqián, nǐ huì shuō Zhōngwén ma?
*The third question matters most: a year ago, could you speak Chinese?*

**波哥：**一年以前……小明一句也不会说。
Yì nián yǐqián…… Xiǎomíng yí jù yě bú huì shuō.
*A year ago… he couldn't say a single sentence.*

**莎莎：**对。可是现在，他能听懂很多话，也能说一些了。
Duì. Kěshì xiànzài, tā néng tīngdǒng hěn duō huà, yě néng shuō yìxiē le.
*Right. And now he understands a lot, and can say quite a bit.*

**波哥：**这就是进步啊！
Zhè jiùshì jìnbù a!
*That IS progress!*

### 别人是别人，你是你 · The eight characters

**莎莎：**老师说：你只看到了马克，没有看到自己。
Lǎoshī shuō: nǐ zhǐ kàn dào le Mǎkè, méiyǒu kàn dào zìjǐ.
*The teacher said: you only saw Mark — you never saw yourself.*

**波哥：**他忘了看自己走了多远。
Tā wàng le kàn zìjǐ zǒu le duō yuǎn.
*He forgot to look at how far he had come.*

**莎莎：**然后，老师说了那句话。
Ránhòu, lǎoshī shuō le nà jù huà.
*And then she said the sentence.*

**波哥：**八个字？
Bā ge zì?
*The eight characters?*

**莎莎：**对。老师说：别人是别人，你是你。
Duì. Lǎoshī shuō: biéren shì biéren, nǐ shì nǐ.
*Yes: others are others; you are you.*

**波哥：**别人是别人，你是你。
Biéren shì biéren, nǐ shì nǐ.
*Others are others; you are you.*

**莎莎：**别人有别人的时间，别人的生活，别人的路。
Biéren yǒu biéren de shíjiān, biéren de shēnghuó, biéren de lù.
*Others have their own time, their own life, their own road.*

**波哥：**你有你的时间，你的生活，你的路。
Nǐ yǒu nǐ de shíjiān, nǐ de shēnghuó, nǐ de lù.
*And you have yours.*

**莎莎：**所以，马克快，不等于小明不好。
Suǒyǐ, Mǎkè kuài, bù děngyú Xiǎomíng bù hǎo.
*So Mark being fast doesn't mean Xiaoming is bad.*

**波哥：**小明慢，也不等于他没有进步。
Xiǎomíng màn, yě bù děngyú tā méiyǒu jìnbù.
*And being slow doesn't mean no progress.*

**莎莎：**我们停一下。波哥，老师的三个问题，你还记得吗？
Wǒmen tíng yíxià. Bōgē, lǎoshī de sān ge wèntí, nǐ hái jìde ma?
*Quick pause. Bo, do you remember the three questions?*

**波哥：**我试试。第一，马克每天学多长时间？时间不一样，没办法比。
Wǒ shìshi. Dì yī, Mǎkè měitiān xué duō cháng shíjiān? Shíjiān bù yíyàng, méi bànfǎ bǐ.
*Let me try. One: how long does Mark study? Different time — no fair comparison.*

**莎莎：**第二个呢？
Dì èr ge ne?
*And the second?*

**波哥：**马克住在哪儿？天天说和很少说，也不一样。
Mǎkè zhù zài nǎr? Tiāntiān shuō hé hěn shǎo shuō, yě bù yíyàng.
*Where does Mark live? Speaking daily versus rarely — also different.*

**莎莎：**第三个最重要的呢？
Dì sān ge zuì zhòngyào de ne?
*And the most important one?*

**波哥：**一年以前，你会说中文吗？——要看自己的进步。
Yì nián yǐqián, nǐ huì shuō Zhōngwén ma? — yào kàn zìjǐ de jìnbù.
*A year ago, could you speak Chinese? — look at your own progress.*

**莎莎：**全对。
Quán duì.
*All correct.*

**波哥：**莎莎，说到这里，我想到我们的听众了。
Shāsha, shuō dào zhèlǐ, wǒ xiǎng dào wǒmen de tīngzhòng le.
*Sasha, this makes me think of our listeners.*

**莎莎：**你说。
Nǐ shuō.
*Go on.*

**波哥：**很多人学中文，也常常跟别人比，越比越着急。
Hěn duō rén xué Zhōngwén, yě chángcháng gēn biéren bǐ, yuè bǐ yuè zháojí.
*Many learners compare themselves too — and get more and more anxious.*

**莎莎：**着急，就是心里很急，怕自己太慢。
Zháojí, jiùshì xīnlǐ hěn jí, pà zìjǐ tài màn.
*"Zháojí" — that hurried feeling of fearing you're too slow.*

**波哥：**如果你也这样，就订阅慢慢中文。在这里，没有人跟你比。
Rúguǒ nǐ yě zhèyàng, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Zài zhèlǐ, méiyǒu rén gēn nǐ bǐ.
*If that's you, subscribe — here, nobody is racing you.*

**莎莎：**我们慢慢说，你慢慢听，你用自己的速度进步。
Wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng, nǐ yòng zìjǐ de sùdù jìnbù.
*We speak slowly, you listen slowly — and improve at your own pace.*

### 跟昨天的自己比 · Compare with yesterday's you

**波哥：**那后来呢？小明还学吗？
Nà hòulái ne? Xiǎomíng hái xué ma?
*So what happened? Did Xiaoming keep going?*

**莎莎：**学。从那天起，他换了一个比法。
Xué. Cóng nàtiān qǐ, tā huàn le yí ge bǐfǎ.
*He did. From that day on, he compared differently.*

**波哥：**什么比法？
Shénme bǐfǎ?
*How?*

**莎莎：**他不跟马克比了，他跟昨天的自己比。
Tā bù gēn Mǎkè bǐ le, tā gēn zuótiān de zìjǐ bǐ.
*Not with Mark anymore — with yesterday's self.*

**波哥：**跟昨天的自己比？怎么比？
Gēn zuótiān de zìjǐ bǐ? Zěnme bǐ?
*With yesterday's self? How does that work?*

**莎莎：**今天的我，比昨天的我，多会了一个词吗？多听懂了一句话吗？
Jīntiān de wǒ, bǐ zuótiān de wǒ, duō huì le yí ge cí ma? Duō tīngdǒng le yí jù huà ma?
*Does today's me know one more word than yesterday's me? Understand one more sentence?*

**波哥：**只要多一点点，就是进步。
Zhǐyào duō yìdiǎndiǎn, jiùshì jìnbù.
*Even a tiny bit more — that's progress.*

**莎莎：**对。小明把那八个字写在了本子的第一页。
Duì. Xiǎomíng bǎ nà bā ge zì xiě zài le běnzi de dì yī yè.
*Right. Xiaoming wrote the eight characters on the first page of his notebook.*

**波哥：**别人是别人，你是你。
Biéren shì biéren, nǐ shì nǐ.
*Others are others; you are you.*

**莎莎：**对。慢慢地，他又开始每天学半个小时了。
Duì. Mànmàn de, tā yòu kāishǐ měitiān xué bàn ge xiǎoshí le.
*Yes. And slowly, his daily half hour came back.*

**波哥：**那马克呢？他们还是朋友吗？
Nà Mǎkè ne? Tāmen háishì péngyou ma?
*And Mark? Still friends?*

**莎莎：**当然。有一天，马克对小明说了一句话。
Dāngrán. Yǒu yì tiān, Mǎkè duì Xiǎomíng shuō le yí jù huà.
*Of course. One day, Mark said something to Xiaoming.*

**波哥：**什么话？
Shénme huà?
*What?*

**莎莎：**他说：小明，你的发音比我好，我一直想跟你学。
Tā shuō: Xiǎomíng, nǐ de fāyīn bǐ wǒ hǎo, wǒ yìzhí xiǎng gēn nǐ xué.
*He said: Xiaoming, your pronunciation is better than mine — I've always wanted to learn from you.*

**波哥：**等等，马克也在跟小明比？
Děngdeng, Mǎkè yě zài gēn Xiǎomíng bǐ?
*Wait — Mark was comparing himself with Xiaoming?*

**莎莎：**哈哈，对。小明觉得马克什么都好；马克看小明，也看到了自己没有的东西。
Hāhā, duì. Xiǎomíng juéde Mǎkè shénme dōu hǎo; Mǎkè kàn Xiǎomíng, yě kàn dào le zìjǐ méiyǒu de dōngxi.
*Haha, yes. Xiaoming thought Mark had everything — and Mark saw in Xiaoming what he himself lacked.*

**波哥：**我们常常拿自己不好的地方，去比别人最好的地方。
Wǒmen chángcháng ná zìjǐ bù hǎo de dìfang, qù bǐ biéren zuì hǎo de dìfang.
*We keep comparing our worst side with other people's best side.*

**莎莎：**对，这样比，谁都会难过。所以——
Duì, zhèyàng bǐ, shéi dōu huì nánguò. Suǒyǐ —
*Right — compared that way, anyone would feel bad. So —*

**波哥：**别人是别人，你是你。
Biéren shì biéren, nǐ shì nǐ.
*Others are others; you are you.*

### 回想 · Recap

Answer out loud **before** Bo does.

**莎莎：**我们来回想一下今天的句子。现在你来说。朋友进步很快，你心里难过，对自己说什么？
Wǒmen lái huíxiǎng yíxià jīntiān de jùzi. Xiànzài nǐ lái shuō. Péngyou jìnbù hěn kuài, nǐ xīnlǐ nánguò, duì zìjǐ shuō shénme?
*Let's recall today's sentences — your turn. Your friend is racing ahead and it hurts. What do you tell yourself?*

**波哥：**别人是别人，你是你。
Biéren shì biéren, nǐ shì nǐ.
*Others are others; you are you.*

**莎莎：**想比较的时候，跟谁比？
Xiǎng bǐjiào de shíhou, gēn shéi bǐ?
*When you do want to compare — with whom?*

**波哥：**跟昨天的自己比。今天比昨天好一点，就够了。
Gēn zuótiān de zìjǐ bǐ. Jīntiān bǐ zuótiān hǎo yìdiǎn, jiù gòu le.
*With yesterday's you. A little better than yesterday is enough.*

**莎莎：**怎么看到自己的进步？
Zěnme kàn dào zìjǐ de jìnbù?
*How do you see your own progress?*

**波哥：**看看一年以前的自己：以前不会的，现在会了吗？
Kànkan yì nián yǐqián de zìjǐ: yǐqián bú huì de, xiànzài huì le ma?

**莎莎：**全对。
Quán duì.

### 结尾 · Ending

**波哥：**今天的故事里，小明没有变成马克。
Jīntiān de gùshi lǐ, Xiǎomíng méiyǒu biànchéng Mǎkè.
*In today's story, Xiaoming never became Mark.*

**莎莎：**他不用变成马克。他慢慢地，变成了更好的小明。
Tā búyòng biànchéng Mǎkè. Tā mànmàn de, biànchéng le gèng hǎo de Xiǎomíng.
*He didn't need to. Slowly, he became a better Xiaoming.*

**波哥：**别人的路是别人的，你的路是你的。
Biéren de lù shì biéren de, nǐ de lù shì nǐ de.
*Their road is theirs; your road is yours.*

**莎莎：**想比，就跟昨天的自己比。慢慢来。
Xiǎng bǐ, jiù gēn zuótiān de zìjǐ bǐ. Mànmàn lái.
*If you must compare, compare with yesterday's you. Take it slow.*

**波哥：**别忘了你的小事：用"比"写一句话，放在评论区。
Bié wàng le nǐ de xiǎo shì: yòng "bǐ" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.

**莎莎：**下一集，我们聊三个很小的词：能、可以、会。
Xià yì jí, wǒmen liáo sān ge hěn xiǎo de cí: néng, kěyǐ, huì.

**波哥：**我会游泳，我能游泳，我可以游泳——一样吗？
Wǒ huì yóuyǒng, wǒ néng yóuyǒng, wǒ kěyǐ yóuyǒng — yíyàng ma?

**莎莎：**不一样。下一集一句一句讲清楚，还有一句最好用的自我介绍。
Bù yíyàng. Xià yì jí yí jù yí jù jiǎng qīngchu, háiyǒu yí jù zuì hǎoyòng de zìwǒ jièshào.

**波哥：**我们下一集见！
Wǒmen xià yì jí jiàn!
