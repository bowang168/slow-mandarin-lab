## In this episode 这一集

- A full flat viewing, start to finish — Sasha plays the landlord.
- The four questions that get you through it (and a tone slip that gets repaired).
- How to bargain politely, and how to leave without saying yes.

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **房租一个月多少钱？**
> Fángzū yí ge yuè duōshao qián?
> *How much is the rent per month?*

## The four steps 看房四步

| 句子 | Pinyin | When |
|---|---|---|
| 房租一个月多少钱？ | Fángzū yí ge yuè duōshao qián? | ask the price |
| 有没有洗衣机？ | Yǒu méiyǒu xǐyījī? | ask what's included |
| 有点儿贵，能便宜一点吗？ | Yǒudiǎnr guì, néng piányi yìdiǎn ma? | bargain politely |
| 我先想想，再告诉你。 | Wǒ xiān xiǎngxiang, zài gàosu nǐ. | decide without pressure |

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**波哥：**莎莎，我下个月要搬家。
Shāsha, wǒ xià ge yuè yào bānjiā.
*Sasha, I'm moving next month.*

**莎莎：**搬家？现在的房子不好吗？
Bānjiā? Xiànzài de fángzi bù hǎo ma?
*Moving? What's wrong with your place?*

**波哥：**现在的房子太小了，东西都放不下，我想租一个大一点的。
Xiànzài de fángzi tài xiǎo le, dōngxi dōu fàng bu xià, wǒ xiǎng zū yí ge dà yìdiǎn de.
*It's too small — my things don't fit. I want to rent something bigger.*

**莎莎：**好事啊。可是你听起来有点儿担心。
Hǎo shì a. Kěshì nǐ tīng qǐlái yǒudiǎnr dānxīn.
*Good news! But you sound a little worried.*

**波哥：**因为这次，我想用中文租房，跟房东说中文。
Yīnwèi zhè cì, wǒ xiǎng yòng Zhōngwén zū fáng, gēn fángdōng shuō Zhōngwén.
*Because this time I want to rent in Chinese — talk to the landlord in Chinese.*

**莎莎：**房东，就是把房子租给你的人。
Fángdōng, jiùshì bǎ fángzi zū gěi nǐ de rén.
*"Fángdōng" — the person who rents the place to you.*

**波哥：**对。我怕听不懂，也怕问错问题。
Duì. Wǒ pà tīng bu dǒng, yě pà wèn cuò wèntí.
*Right. I'm afraid I won't understand — or I'll ask the wrong things.*

**莎莎：**别怕。其实，从进门到出门，看房只需要四个问题。
Bié pà. Qíshí, cóng jìnmén dào chūmén, kàn fáng zhǐ xūyào sì ge wèntí.
*Don't be. Door to door, a viewing only takes four questions.*

**波哥：**四个？就四个？
Sì ge? Jiù sì ge?
*Four? Just four?*

**莎莎：**对。多少钱、有没有、能不能便宜，还有最后怎么回答。
Duì. Duōshao qián, yǒu méiyǒu, néng bu néng piányi, háiyǒu zuìhòu zěnme huídá.
*Yes: how much, what's included, any discount — and what to say at the end.*

**波哥：**最后一个是什么意思？
Zuìhòu yí ge shì shénme yìsi?
*What's the last one about?*

**莎莎：**看完房，不想马上决定，怎么说？这句话最多人不会。
Kàn wán fáng, bù xiǎng mǎshàng juédìng, zěnme shuō? Zhè jù huà zuì duō rén bú huì.
*How to leave without deciding on the spot — the sentence most people don't know.*

**波哥：**好，今天我一定要学会。
Hǎo, jīntiān wǒ yídìng yào xuéhuì.
*Then today I'm learning it.*

### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。在这里，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Zài zhèlǐ, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*I'm Bo. Here, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**今天是一个真实场景：看房、租房。我演房东。
Jīntiān shì yí ge zhēnshí chǎngjǐng: kàn fáng, zū fáng. Wǒ yǎn fángdōng.
*Today's real-life scene: viewing and renting a flat. I play the landlord.*

**波哥：**我演我自己。开始吧！
Wǒ yǎn wǒ zìjǐ. Kāishǐ ba!
*And I play myself. Let's go!*

### 打电话约看房 · Booking a viewing

**莎莎：**第一步，打电话。你在网上看到了我的房子。
Dì yī bù, dǎ diànhuà. Nǐ zài wǎngshang kàn dào le wǒ de fángzi.
*Step one: the phone call. You saw my listing online.*

**波哥：**好。喂，你好！我在网上看到你的房子，想去看看，可以吗？
Hǎo. Wéi, nǐ hǎo! Wǒ zài wǎngshang kàn dào nǐ de fángzi, xiǎng qù kànkan, kěyǐ ma?
*Okay. Hello! I saw your apartment online — may I come and take a look?*

**莎莎：**可以啊。明天下午三点，方便吗？
Kěyǐ a. Míngtiān xiàwǔ sān diǎn, fāngbiàn ma?
*Sure. Tomorrow at 3pm — does that work?*

**波哥：**方便，明天见！——就这么简单？
Fāngbiàn, míngtiān jiàn! — Jiù zhème jiǎndān?
*Works for me, see you tomorrow! — Wait, that's it?*

**莎莎：**就这么简单。"想去看看，可以吗？"——上一集的"可以"。
Jiù zhème jiǎndān. "Xiǎng qù kànkan, kěyǐ ma?" — shàng yì jí de "kěyǐ".
*That's it. "May I come and look?" — last episode's 可以.*

**波哥：**问让不让，用"可以"。我记得！
Wèn ràng bu ràng, yòng "kěyǐ". Wǒ jìde!
*Asking permission — use 可以. I remember!*

**莎莎：**好，现在是明天下午。你到了，我开门。你好，请进！
Hǎo, xiànzài shì míngtiān xiàwǔ. Nǐ dào le, wǒ kāimén. Nǐ hǎo, qǐng jìn!
*Now it's tomorrow afternoon. You arrive, I open the door. Hello, come in!*

**波哥：**你好！哇，这个房间很亮。
Nǐ hǎo! Wā, zhège fángjiān hěn liàng.
*Hello! Wow, this room is bright.*

**莎莎：**对，窗户大，下午太阳很好。
Duì, chuānghu dà, xiàwǔ tàiyáng hěn hǎo.
*Yes — big windows, lovely afternoon sun.*

**波哥：**房子离地铁站远吗？
Fángzi lí dìtiězhàn yuǎn ma?
*Is it far from the metro station?*

**莎莎：**不远，走路五分钟就到。
Bù yuǎn, zǒulù wǔ fēnzhōng jiù dào.
*Not far — five minutes on foot.*

**波哥：**太好了，我每天要坐地铁上班。我可以看看厨房吗？
Tài hǎo le, wǒ měitiān yào zuò dìtiě shàngbān. Wǒ kěyǐ kànkan chúfáng ma?
*Great — I take the metro to work. May I see the kitchen?*

**莎莎：**可以，你随便看，不用急。
Kěyǐ, nǐ suíbiàn kàn, búyòng jí.
*Of course, look around — no rush.*

**波哥：**都很好。现在……最重要的问题来了。
Dōu hěn hǎo. Xiànzài…… zuì zhòngyào de wèntí lái le.
*All good. And now… the most important question.*

### 最重要的问题 · The price question

**莎莎：**你想问什么？
Nǐ xiǎng wèn shénme?
*What do you want to ask?*

**波哥：**我想闻你一下。
Wǒ xiǎng wén nǐ yíxià.
*我想闻你一下 — "I'd like to smell you for a second."*

**莎莎：**什么？！闻我？
Shénme?! Wén wǒ?
*What?! Smell me?*

**波哥：**啊，不对不对！我是说：我想问你一下！
Ā, bú duì bú duì! Wǒ shì shuō: wǒ xiǎng wèn nǐ yíxià!
*No no! I mean: I'd like to ASK you something!*

**莎莎：**哈哈哈！"问"是第四声。你刚才说的"闻"，是第二声。
Hāhāhā! "Wèn" shì dì sì shēng. Nǐ gāngcái shuō de "wén", shì dì èr shēng.
*Hahaha! 问 "wèn" is fourth tone. What you said — 闻 "wén" — is second tone.*

**波哥：**第二声的"闻"，是什么意思？
Dì èr shēng de "wén", shì shénme yìsi?
*And what does second-tone "wén" mean?*

**莎莎：**就是用鼻子闻，闻花的那个"闻"。我又不是花！
Jiùshì yòng bízi wén, wén huā de nàge "wén". Wǒ yòu búshì huā!
*It means to smell — as in smelling a flower. And I'm not a flower!*

**波哥：**哈哈，对不起，对不起！一个声调，差别这么大。我说错了！
Hāhā, duìbuqǐ, duìbuqǐ! Yí ge shēngdiào, chābié zhème dà. Wǒ shuō cuò le!
*Haha — sorry, sorry! One tone, such a big difference. I said it wrong!*

**莎莎：**说错了没关系，再说一次就好。第五集的老朋友。
Shuō cuò le méi guānxi, zài shuō yí cì jiù hǎo. Dì wǔ jí de lǎo péngyou.
*Getting it wrong is okay — just say it again. An old friend from episode five.*

**波哥：**我想问你一下：房租一个月多少钱？
Wǒ xiǎng wèn nǐ yíxià: fángzū yí ge yuè duōshao qián?
*Let me ask you: how much is the rent per month?*

**莎莎：**这句就对了。房租，就是你每个月给房东的钱。
Zhè jù jiù duì le. Fángzū, jiùshì nǐ měi ge yuè gěi fángdōng de qián.
*Now that's right. "Fángzū" — the money you pay the landlord each month.*

**波哥：**房租一个月多少钱？
Fángzū yí ge yuè duōshao qián?
*How much is the rent per month?*

**莎莎：**一个月三千块。
Yí ge yuè sānqiān kuài.
*Three thousand kuai a month.*

**波哥：**一个月三千块……我先记下来。
Yí ge yuè sānqiān kuài…… wǒ xiān jì xiàlái.
*Three thousand a month… let me note that down.*

### 小任务 · Your turn

**莎莎：**先停一下，请你做一件小事。
Xiān tíng yíxià, qǐng nǐ zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì.
*Quick pause — one small thing.*

**波哥：**评论区时间到了。
Pínglùnqū shíjiān dào le.
*Comment-section time.*

**莎莎：**对。用"多少钱"写一句话，放在评论区。
Duì. Yòng "duōshao qián" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Yes. Write one 多少钱 sentence in the comments.*

**波哥：**我先来：这个多少钱？
Wǒ xiān lái: zhège duōshao qián?
*Me first: how much is this?*

**莎莎：**很好，去哪儿都用得上。再来一句？
Hěn hǎo, qù nǎr dōu yòng de shàng. Zài lái yí jù?
*Nice — useful everywhere. Another?*

**波哥：**房租一个月多少钱？
Fángzū yí ge yuè duōshao qián?
*How much is the rent per month?*

**莎莎：**就是今天的句子！你也写一句，我们会看，也会回你。
Jiùshì jīntiān de jùzi! Nǐ yě xiě yí jù, wǒmen huì kàn, yě huì huí nǐ.
*Today's sentence exactly! Write yours too — we read and reply.*

### 有没有…… · What's included

**波哥：**好，回到看房。三千块，包水电吗？
Hǎo, huídào kàn fáng. Sānqiān kuài, bāo shuǐdiàn ma?
*Back to the viewing. Three thousand — are utilities included?*

**莎莎：**水费包，电费不包，你自己付。
Shuǐfèi bāo, diànfèi bù bāo, nǐ zìjǐ fù.
*Water is included, electricity isn't — you pay that yourself.*

**波哥：**包，就是在房租里面，不用另外给钱？
Bāo, jiùshì zài fángzū lǐmiàn, búyòng lìngwài gěi qián?
*"Bāo" means it's inside the rent — nothing extra to pay?*

**莎莎：**对。"包水电"，就是水费电费都在房租里。
Duì. "Bāo shuǐdiàn", jiùshì shuǐfèi diànfèi dōu zài fángzū lǐ.
*Right. 包水电 — water and electricity both inside the rent.*

**波哥：**那网呢？有没有网？
Nà wǎng ne? Yǒu méiyǒu wǎng?
*And internet? Is there internet?*

**莎莎：**有，网也包在房租里。
Yǒu, wǎng yě bāo zài fángzū lǐ.
*Yes — included in the rent too.*

**波哥：**有没有洗衣机？
Yǒu méiyǒu xǐyījī?
*Is there a washing machine?*

**莎莎：**有。洗衣机、冰箱、空调都有，床和桌子也都是新的。
Yǒu. Xǐyījī, bīngxiāng, kōngtiáo dōu yǒu, chuáng hé zhuōzi yě dōu shì xīn de.
*Yes. Washing machine, fridge, air-con — and the bed and table are new.*

**波哥：**太好了。还有别的钱吗？
Tài hǎo le. Háiyǒu biéde qián ma?
*Great. Any other costs?*

**莎莎：**有押金。押金一个月，三千块。
Yǒu yājīn. Yājīn yí ge yuè, sānqiān kuài.
*There's a deposit — one month, three thousand kuai.*

**波哥：**押金是什么？
Yājīn shì shénme?
*What's a "yājīn"?*

**莎莎：**你先放在房东这里的钱。搬走的时候，房子没问题，就还给你。
Nǐ xiān fàng zài fángdōng zhèlǐ de qián. Bān zǒu de shíhou, fángzi méi wèntí, jiù huán gěi nǐ.
*Money you leave with the landlord. When you move out and the place is fine, you get it back.*

**波哥：**哦，是放着的钱，不是花掉的钱。
Ó, shì fàng zhe de qián, búshì huā diào de qián.
*Ah — parked money, not spent money.*

**莎莎：**对。记住问这三样：房租、押金、包不包水电。
Duì. Jìzhù wèn zhè sān yàng: fángzū, yājīn, bāo bu bāo shuǐdiàn.
*Right. Always ask these three: rent, deposit, utilities.*

**莎莎：**我们停一下。看房的问题，你还记得吗？
Wǒmen tíng yíxià. Kàn fáng de wèntí, nǐ hái jìde ma?
*Quick pause — remember the viewing questions?*

**波哥：**记得。第一句：房租一个月多少钱？
Jìde. Dì yī jù: fángzū yí ge yuè duōshao qián?
*I do. First: how much is the rent per month?*

**莎莎：**想知道房子里有什么呢？
Xiǎng zhīdào fángzi lǐ yǒu shénme ne?
*And to find out what's in the flat?*

**波哥：**有没有洗衣机？有没有网？
Yǒu méiyǒu xǐyījī? Yǒu méiyǒu wǎng?
*Is there a washing machine? Is there internet?*

**莎莎：**还有一样放着的钱——
Háiyǒu yí yàng fàng zhe de qián —
*And that parked money —*

**波哥：**押金。搬走的时候还给我。
Yājīn. Bān zǒu de shíhou huán gěi wǒ.
*The deposit — returned when I move out.*

**莎莎：**全对，一个都没忘。
Quán duì, yí ge dōu méi wàng.
*All correct — you didn't miss one.*

### 还价：能便宜一点吗 · Bargaining

**波哥：**房东，这个房子我很喜欢。可是……
Fángdōng, zhège fángzi wǒ hěn xǐhuan. Kěshì……
*Landlord, I really like this place. But…*

**莎莎：**可是什么？你说。
Kěshì shénme? Nǐ shuō.
*But what? Go on.*

**波哥：**三千有点儿贵。能便宜一点吗？
Sānqiān yǒudiǎnr guì. Néng piányi yìdiǎn ma?
*Three thousand is a bit expensive. Could it be a little cheaper?*

**莎莎：**嗯……你想租多长时间？
Ǹg…… nǐ xiǎng zū duō cháng shíjiān?
*Hmm… how long would you rent for?*

**波哥：**一年。我不想常常搬家。
Yì nián. Wǒ bù xiǎng chángcháng bānjiā.
*A year. I don't want to keep moving.*

**莎莎：**好吧，租一年的话，两千八。
Hǎo ba, zū yì nián de huà, liǎngqiān bā.
*Alright — for a year, 2,800.*

**波哥：**两千八……两千五，可以吗？
Liǎngqiān bā…… liǎngqiān wǔ, kěyǐ ma?
*2,800… how about 2,500?*

**莎莎：**两千五不行。两千七，不能再少了。
Liǎngqiān wǔ bù xíng. Liǎngqiān qī, bù néng zài shǎo le.
*2,500 won't work. 2,700 — that's my floor.*

**波哥：**好，两千七，就这么说了！
Hǎo, liǎngqiān qī, jiù zhème shuō le!
*Deal — 2,700 it is!*

**莎莎：**哈哈，停！波哥，你刚才做了一件很厉害的事。
Hāhā, tíng! Bōgē, nǐ gāngcái zuò le yí jiàn hěn lìhai de shì.
*Haha, freeze! Bo, you just did something impressive.*

**波哥：**我做了什么？
Wǒ zuò le shénme?
*What did I do?*

**莎莎：**你还价了。还价，就是觉得贵，请对方少一点。
Nǐ huánjià le. Huánjià, jiùshì juéde guì, qǐng duìfāng shǎo yìdiǎn.
*You bargained. "Huánjià" — it feels expensive, so you ask for less.*

**波哥：**在中国，租房真的可以还价吗？
Zài Zhōngguó, zū fáng zhēnde kěyǐ huánjià ma?
*Can you really bargain on rent in China?*

**莎莎：**很多时候可以。你客气地问，别人不会不高兴。
Hěn duō shíhou kěyǐ. Nǐ kèqi de wèn, biéren bú huì bù gāoxìng.
*Often, yes. Ask politely and nobody minds.*

**波哥：**如果房东说"不能便宜"呢？
Rúguǒ fángdōng shuō "bù néng piányi" ne?
*And if the landlord says no discount?*

**莎莎：**那就想一想值不值。便宜不是最重要的，住得舒服才是。
Nà jiù xiǎng yi xiǎng zhí bu zhí. Piányi búshì zuì zhòngyào de, zhù de shūfu cái shì.
*Then think about whether it's worth it. Cheap isn't everything — comfortable is.*

**波哥：**那句话我再说一次：有点儿贵，能便宜一点吗？
Nà jù huà wǒ zài shuō yí cì: yǒudiǎnr guì, néng piányi yìdiǎn ma?
*Let me say it once more: it's a bit expensive — could it be cheaper?*

**莎莎：**对。"能"，上一集的第二扇门，又见面了。
Duì. "Néng", shàng yì jí de dì èr shàn mén, yòu jiànmiàn le.
*Right — 能, the second door from last episode, back again.*

**波哥：**学会还价，我觉得我像本地人了。
Xuéhuì huánjià, wǒ juéde wǒ xiàng běndìrén le.
*Now that I can bargain, I feel like a local.*

**莎莎：**如果你想每个星期练一个这样的真实场景，就订阅慢慢中文。
Rúguǒ nǐ xiǎng měi ge xīngqī liàn yí ge zhèyàng de zhēnshí chǎngjǐng, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*If you'd like one real-life scene every week, subscribe.*

**波哥：**看房、看医生、坐车，我们都会慢慢练。
Kàn fáng, kàn yīshēng, zuò chē, wǒmen dōu huì mànmàn liàn.
*Flat viewings, doctor visits, getting around — we'll practise them all, slowly.*

**莎莎：**你在生活里用得上的中文，才是你的中文。
Nǐ zài shēnghuó lǐ yòng de shàng de Zhōngwén, cái shì nǐ de Zhōngwén.
*The Chinese you can actually use is the Chinese that's yours.*

### 我先想想，再告诉你 · Leaving politely

**莎莎：**好，最后一步。看完了，你不想马上决定，怎么说？
Hǎo, zuìhòu yí bù. Kàn wán le, nǐ bù xiǎng mǎshàng juédìng, zěnme shuō?
*Last step. You've seen it all and don't want to decide now — what do you say?*

**波哥：**嗯……直接说"我不要"？
Ǹg…… zhíjiē shuō "wǒ búyào"?
*Um… just say "I don't want it"?*

**莎莎：**太硬了。有一句更好的：我先想想，再告诉你。
Tài yìng le. Yǒu yí jù gèng hǎo de: wǒ xiān xiǎngxiang, zài gàosu nǐ.
*Too blunt. There's a better line: let me think it over first, then I'll tell you.*

**波哥：**我先想想，再告诉你。
Wǒ xiān xiǎngxiang, zài gàosu nǐ.
*Let me think it over first, then I'll tell you.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，"先……再……"，第二集的老朋友。
Hāhā, "xiān…… zài……", dì èr jí de lǎo péngyou.
*Haha — 先…再…, an old friend from episode two.*

**波哥：**对！先想想，再决定。
Duì! Xiān xiǎngxiang, zài juédìng.
*Right! Think first, then decide.*

**莎莎：**这句话很客气，房东听了也舒服。
Zhè jù huà hěn kèqi, fángdōng tīng le yě shūfu.
*It's polite — the landlord feels fine hearing it.*

**波哥：**想好了以后呢？
Xiǎng hǎo le yǐhòu ne?
*And once you've decided?*

**莎莎：**想租，就说：我想租，什么时候可以搬进来？
Xiǎng zū, jiù shuō: wǒ xiǎng zū, shénme shíhou kěyǐ bān jìnlái?
*If you want it: I'd like to rent it — when can I move in?*

**波哥：**不想租呢？
Bù xiǎng zū ne?
*And if you don't?*

**莎莎：**发一条消息：谢谢你，我找到别的房子了。
Fā yì tiáo xiāoxi: xièxie nǐ, wǒ zhǎo dào biéde fángzi le.
*Send a message: thank you — I've found another place.*

**波哥：**客气地开始，客气地结束。这个我喜欢。
Kèqi de kāishǐ, kèqi de jiéshù. Zhège wǒ xǐhuan.
*Start politely, end politely. I like that.*

### 快问快答 · Speed round

Answer out loud **before** Bo does.

**莎莎：**好，最后，快问快答！准备好了吗？
Hǎo, zuìhòu, kuài wèn kuài dá! Zhǔnbèi hǎo le ma?
*Okay, speed round! Ready?*

**波哥：**准备好了！
Zhǔnbèi hǎo le!
*Ready!*

**莎莎：**想去看房子，怎么问？
Xiǎng qù kàn fángzi, zěnme wèn?
*You want to view a flat — how do you ask?*

**波哥：**我想去看看，可以吗？
Wǒ xiǎng qù kànkan, kěyǐ ma?
*May I come and take a look?*

**莎莎：**最重要的问题？
Zuì zhòngyào de wèntí?
*The most important question?*

**波哥：**房租一个月多少钱？
Fángzū yí ge yuè duōshao qián?
*How much is the rent per month?*

**莎莎：**想知道房子里有什么？
Xiǎng zhīdào fángzi lǐ yǒu shénme?
*To find out what's in the flat?*

**波哥：**有没有洗衣机？有没有网？
Yǒu méiyǒu xǐyījī? Yǒu méiyǒu wǎng?
*Is there a washing machine? Is there internet?*

**莎莎：**觉得贵了？
Juéde guì le?
*It feels expensive?*

**波哥：**有点儿贵，能便宜一点吗？
Yǒudiǎnr guì, néng piányi yìdiǎn ma?
*A bit expensive — could it be cheaper?*

**莎莎：**不想马上决定？
Bù xiǎng mǎshàng juédìng?
*Not ready to decide?*

**波哥：**我先想想，再告诉你。
Wǒ xiān xiǎngxiang, zài gàosu nǐ.
*Let me think it over first, then I'll tell you.*

**莎莎：**太好了，全对！你可以去看房了。
Tài hǎo le, quán duì! Nǐ kěyǐ qù kàn fáng le.
*Excellent, all correct! You're ready for a viewing.*

### 结尾 · Ending

**波哥：**今天，我们从打电话到再见，练了看房的四步。
Jīntiān, wǒmen cóng dǎ diànhuà dào zàijiàn, liàn le kàn fáng de sì bù.
*Today we walked a viewing from phone call to goodbye — four steps.*

**莎莎：**第一步，问价钱：房租一个月多少钱？
Dì yī bù, wèn jiàqián: fángzū yí ge yuè duōshao qián?
*Step one, the price: how much is the rent per month?*

**波哥：**第二步，问有没有：有没有洗衣机？包不包水电？
Dì èr bù, wèn yǒu méiyǒu: yǒu méiyǒu xǐyījī? Bāo bu bāo shuǐdiàn?
*Step two, what's included: washing machine? Utilities?*

**莎莎：**第三步，还价：有点儿贵，能便宜一点吗？
Dì sān bù, huánjià: yǒudiǎnr guì, néng piányi yìdiǎn ma?
*Step three, bargain: a bit expensive — any discount?*

**波哥：**第四步：我先想想，再告诉你。
Dì sì bù: wǒ xiān xiǎngxiang, zài gàosu nǐ.
*Step four: let me think it over first, then I'll tell you.*

**莎莎：**别忘了你的小事：用"多少钱"写一句话，放在评论区。
Bié wàng le nǐ de xiǎo shì: yòng "duōshao qián" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Don't forget your task: one 多少钱 sentence in the comments.*

**波哥：**今天没有下集预告，因为我们想听你的。
Jīntiān méiyǒu xià jí yùgào, yīnwèi wǒmen xiǎng tīng nǐ de.
*No preview today — we want to hear from you.*

**莎莎：**你想让我们聊什么话题？写在评论区。
Nǐ xiǎng ràng wǒmen liáo shénme huàtí? Xiě zài pínglùnqū.
*What topic should we cover? Tell us in the comments.*

**波哥：**我们会先看话题适不适合频道，对学习有没有帮助，安不安全。
Wǒmen huì xiān kàn huàtí shì bu shìhé píndào, duì xuéxí yǒu méiyǒu bāngzhù, ān bu ānquán.
*We'll check fit, learning value and safety first.*

**莎莎：**合适的，我们放进节目单。不能保证每个都做，写一次就够了。
Héshì de, wǒmen fàng jìn jiémùdān. Bù néng bǎozhèng měi ge dōu zuò, xiě yí cì jiù gòu le.
*Suitable ones go on the list. No promises — one comment is enough.*

**波哥：**谢谢你和我们在一起。我是波哥。
Xièxie nǐ hé wǒmen zài yìqǐ. Wǒ shì Bōgē.
*Thanks for being with us. I'm Bo.*

**莎莎：**我是莎莎。我们下期再见。
Wǒ shì Shāsha. Wǒmen xià qī zàijiàn.
*I'm Sasha. See you next time.*

**波哥：**下次见。慢慢来。
Xiàcì jiàn. Mànmàn lái.
*See you next time. Take it slow.*
