## In this episode 这一集

- Why fast studying leaks: 听得快，忘得快 — and why slow listening sticks.
- A new pair of friends: **因为……所以……** (because → therefore), one sentence at a time.
- An honest, self-referential answer: why does 慢慢中文 speak this slowly?

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **因为你慢慢听，所以你真的懂。**
> Yīnwèi nǐ mànmàn tīng, suǒyǐ nǐ zhēnde dǒng.
> *Because you listen slowly, you really understand.*

## The 因为……所以…… ladder 一句连一句

| 句子 | Pinyin | Step |
|---|---|---|
| 因为你慢慢听，所以你真的懂。 | Yīnwèi nǐ mànmàn tīng, suǒyǐ nǐ zhēnde dǒng. | slow → understand |
| 因为你真的懂，所以你记得住。 | Yīnwèi nǐ zhēnde dǒng, suǒyǐ nǐ jì de zhù. | understand → remember |
| 因为你记得住，所以你想继续听。 | Yīnwèi nǐ jì de zhù, suǒyǐ nǐ xiǎng jìxù tīng. | remember → keep going |

**Your turn:** the pair always lives in one sentence — 因为 states the why, 所以 states what
follows. Try your own: 因为我______，所以我______。

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**莎莎：**波哥，我先问你一个问题。
Bōgē, wǒ xiān wèn nǐ yí ge wèntí.
*Bo, let me ask you a question first.*

**波哥：**好，你问。
Hǎo, nǐ wèn.
*Sure, go ahead.*

**莎莎：**学中文，快一点好，还是慢一点好？
Xué Zhōngwén, kuài yìdiǎn hǎo, háishì màn yìdiǎn hǎo?
*Learning Chinese — is faster better, or slower?*

**波哥：**当然是快！大家都想快一点学会。
Dāngrán shì kuài! Dàjiā dōu xiǎng kuài yìdiǎn xuéhuì.
*Faster, of course! Everyone wants to learn quickly.*

**莎莎：**那我今天要说一句奇怪的话：慢，就是快。
Nà wǒ jīntiān yào shuō yí jù qíguài de huà: màn, jiùshì kuài.
*Then today I'll say something strange: slow IS fast.*

**波哥：**慢就是快？这句话听起来不对啊。
Màn jiùshì kuài? Zhè jù huà tīng qǐlái bú duì a.
*Slow is fast? That doesn't sound right.*

**莎莎：**对，听起来不对。可是，它是真的。
Duì, tīng qǐlái bú duì. Kěshì, tā shì zhēnde.
*Right, it doesn't sound right. But it's true.*

**波哥：**哈哈，"可是"，第六集的老朋友。好，你今天要让我信。
Hāhā, "kěshì", dì liù jí de lǎo péngyou. Hǎo, nǐ jīntiān yào ràng wǒ xìn.
*Haha — "kěshì", our old friend from episode six. Okay, today you'll have to convince me.*

**莎莎：**今天三件事：快的问题，慢的好处，还有一个问题的答案。
Jīntiān sān jiàn shì: kuài de wèntí, màn de hǎochu, háiyǒu yí ge wèntí de dá'àn.
*Three things today: the problem with fast, the good in slow, and the answer to one question.*

**波哥：**什么问题？
Shénme wèntí?
*What question?*

**莎莎：**很多人问：慢慢中文，为什么说得这么慢？
Hěnduō rén wèn: Mànmàn Zhōngwén, wèishénme shuō de zhème màn?
*Many people ask: why does Manman Zhongwen speak so slowly?*

**波哥：**对！这个问题，我也很想知道。
Duì! Zhège wèntí, wǒ yě hěn xiǎng zhīdào.
*Yes! I'd really like to know that too.*

**莎莎：**答案在最后。别走开。
Dá'àn zài zuìhòu. Bié zǒukāi.
*The answer comes at the end. Don't go away.*

### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。在这里，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Zài zhèlǐ, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*I'm Bo. Here, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**今天不学新场景，我们聊一个学习的问题：慢就是快。
Jīntiān bù xué xīn chǎngjǐng, wǒmen liáo yí ge xuéxí de wèntí: màn jiùshì kuài.
*No new scene today — we're talking about a learning question: slow is fast.*

**波哥：**好，开始吧。
Hǎo, kāishǐ ba.
*Great, let's start.*

### 听得快，忘得快 · Fast in, fast out

**莎莎：**波哥，你有没有这样的晚上：一个晚上看很多学中文的视频？
Bōgē, nǐ yǒu méiyǒu zhèyàng de wǎnshang: yí ge wǎnshang kàn hěnduō xué Zhōngwén de shìpín?
*Bo, have you ever had a night like this: watching lots of Chinese-learning videos in one evening?*

**波哥：**有！上个星期就有。一个晚上，我看了十个。
Yǒu! Shàng ge xīngqī jiù yǒu. Yí ge wǎnshang, wǒ kàn le shí ge.
*Yes! Just last week. Ten videos in one night.*

**莎莎：**看的时候，你觉得怎么样？都懂吗？
Kàn de shíhou, nǐ juéde zěnmeyàng? Dōu dǒng ma?
*How did it feel while watching? Did you understand it all?*

**波哥：**看的时候，好像都懂，感觉很好。
Kàn de shíhou, hǎoxiàng dōu dǒng, gǎnjué hěn hǎo.
*While watching, it seemed like I understood everything. Felt great.*

**莎莎：**那第二天呢？
Nà dì èr tiān ne?
*And the next day?*

**波哥：**第二天早上一想，差不多都忘了。
Dì èr tiān zǎoshang yì xiǎng, chàbuduō dōu wàng le.
*Next morning I tried to recall — almost all of it was gone.*

**莎莎：**这就是八个字：听得快，忘得快。
Zhè jiùshì bā ge zì: tīng de kuài, wàng de kuài.
*That's eight characters: listen fast, forget fast.*

**波哥：**听得快，忘得快……这八个字，说的就是我。
Tīng de kuài, wàng de kuài…… zhè bā ge zì, shuō de jiùshì wǒ.
*Fast in, fast out… those eight characters are literally me.*

**莎莎：**为什么会这样？我们用吃饭想一想。
Wèishénme huì zhèyàng? Wǒmen yòng chīfàn xiǎng yi xiǎng.
*Why does that happen? Let's think about it with food.*

**波哥：**吃饭？好啊，我最喜欢吃饭了。
Chīfàn? Hǎo a, wǒ zuì xǐhuan chīfàn le.
*Food? Great — eating is my favourite thing.*

**莎莎：**你吃饭吃得很快，五分钟吃完，舒服吗？
Nǐ chīfàn chī de hěn kuài, wǔ fēnzhōng chī wán, shūfu ma?
*If you eat very fast — done in five minutes — do you feel good?*

**波哥：**不舒服。吃得太快，肚子会不高兴。
Bù shūfu. Chī de tài kuài, dùzi huì bù gāoxìng.
*No. Eat too fast and your stomach gets unhappy.*

**莎莎：**对。饭要慢慢吃，身体才能用它。
Duì. Fàn yào mànmàn chī, shēntǐ cáinéng yòng tā.
*Right. Food has to be eaten slowly for the body to use it.*

**波哥：**那中文呢？
Nà Zhōngwén ne?
*And Chinese?*

**莎莎：**一样。词要慢慢听，脑子才能记住它。
Yíyàng. Cí yào mànmàn tīng, nǎozi cáinéng jìzhù tā.
*Same. Words have to be heard slowly for the brain to remember them.*

**波哥：**听得太快，就像吃得太快。
Tīng de tài kuài, jiù xiàng chī de tài kuài.
*Listening too fast is like eating too fast.*

**莎莎：**对。耳朵很忙，脑子跟不上。
Duì. Ěrduo hěn máng, nǎozi gēn bu shàng.
*Right. The ears are busy, but the brain can't keep up.*

**波哥：**词从左边进来，右边就出去了。
Cí cóng zuǒbiān jìnlái, yòubiān jiù chūqù le.
*Words come in one side and go straight out the other.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，对。东西进了耳朵，可是没有留下来。
Hāhā, duì. Dōngxi jìn le ěrduo, kěshì méiyǒu liúxiàlái.
*Haha, exactly. Things enter the ear — but they don't stay.*

**波哥：**所以我看了十个视频，等于没看？
Suǒyǐ wǒ kàn le shí ge shìpín, děngyú méi kàn?
*So my ten videos were the same as watching none?*

**莎莎：**不是没看，是留在脑子里的太少。
Búshì méi kàn, shì liú zài nǎozi lǐ de tài shǎo.
*Not none — but too little stayed in your brain.*

**波哥：**那慢慢听，留下来的就多？
Nà mànmàn tīng, liúxiàlái de jiù duō?
*So if I listen slowly, more of it stays?*

**莎莎：**对。留下来的，才是你的。所以还有八个字：听得慢，记得住。
Duì. Liúxiàlái de, cái shì nǐ de. Suǒyǐ háiyǒu bā ge zì: tīng de màn, jì de zhù.
*Yes. Only what stays is yours. So here are eight more characters: listen slow, and it sticks.*

**波哥：**听得快，忘得快；听得慢，记得住。
Tīng de kuài, wàng de kuài; tīng de màn, jì de zhù.
*Fast in, fast out; slow in, it stays.*

**莎莎：**说得真好。这两句话，请你记住。
Shuō de zhēn hǎo. Zhè liǎng jù huà, qǐng nǐ jìzhù.
*Well said. Please remember those two sentences.*

### 小任务 · Your turn

**波哥：**莎莎，是不是到评论区时间了？
Shāsha, shì bu shì dào pínglùnqū shíjiān le?

**莎莎：**对。今天的小事：用"慢慢"写一句话，放在评论区。

**波哥：**我先来：我慢慢吃饭，肚子很高兴。
Wǒ xiān lái: Wǒ mànmàn chīfàn, dùzi hěn gāoxìng.
*Me first: I eat slowly, and my stomach is happy.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，很好。再来一句？
Hāhā, hěn hǎo. Zài lái yí jù?
*Haha, nice. Another one?*

**波哥：**我每天慢慢听中文，一点都不着急。
Wǒ měitiān mànmàn tīng Zhōngwén, yìdiǎn dōu bù zháojí.

### 因为……所以…… · Because → therefore

**波哥：**好。可是我还有一个问题：为什么慢，就记得住？
Hǎo. Kěshì wǒ háiyǒu yí ge wèntí: wèishénme màn, jiù jì de zhù?
*Okay. But one more question: why does slow make it stick?*

**莎莎：**因为你慢慢听，所以你真的懂。
Yīnwèi nǐ mànmàn tīng, suǒyǐ nǐ zhēnde dǒng.
*Because you listen slowly, you really understand.*

**波哥：**因为你慢慢听，所以你真的懂……
Yīnwèi nǐ mànmàn tīng, suǒyǐ nǐ zhēnde dǒng……
*Because you listen slowly, you really understand…*

**莎莎：**对。"好像懂"和"真的懂"，是两件事。
Duì. "Hǎoxiàng dǒng" hé "zhēnde dǒng", shì liǎng jiàn shì.
*Right. "Seeming to understand" and "really understanding" are two different things.*

**波哥：**哪里不一样？
Nǎlǐ bù yíyàng?
*How are they different?*

**莎莎：**好像懂，是听见了。真的懂，是听懂了。
Hǎoxiàng dǒng, shì tīngjiàn le. Zhēnde dǒng, shì tīngdǒng le.
*Seeming to understand is hearing it. Really understanding is comprehending it.*

**波哥：**听见，不等于听懂。
Tīngjiàn, bù děngyú tīngdǒng.
*Hearing doesn't equal understanding.*

**莎莎：**对。快的时候，你只是听见。慢的时候，你才听懂。
Duì. Kuài de shíhou, nǐ zhǐshì tīngjiàn. Màn de shíhou, nǐ cái tīngdǒng.
*Right. At speed, you only hear. Slowly, you actually understand.*

**波哥：**懂了，才记得住。
Dǒng le, cái jì de zhù.
*And only what you understand sticks.*

**莎莎：**对。不懂的东西，脑子不想留它。
Duì. Bù dǒng de dōngxi, nǎozi bù xiǎng liú tā.
*Yes. The brain doesn't want to keep what it doesn't understand.*

**波哥：**懂了的东西，脑子才留下来。
Dǒng le de dōngxi, nǎozi cái liúxiàlái.
*What's understood is what the brain keeps.*

**莎莎：**就是这样。现在，我们认识一对新朋友：因为……所以……。
Jiùshì zhèyàng. Xiànzài, wǒmen rènshi yí duì xīn péngyou: yīnwèi…… suǒyǐ…….
*Exactly. Now, meet a new pair of friends: yīnwèi… suǒyǐ….*

**波哥：**因为……所以……。怎么用？
Yīnwèi…… suǒyǐ……. Zěnme yòng?
*Because… therefore…. How do we use it?*

**莎莎：**先听一句：因为天气冷，所以我穿得多。
Xiān tīng yí jù: Yīnwèi tiānqì lěng, suǒyǐ wǒ chuān de duō.
*Listen to one first: because the weather is cold, I wear more.*

**波哥：**哦，"因为"后面说为什么，"所以"后面说后来怎么了。
Ó, "yīnwèi" hòumiàn shuō wèishénme, "suǒyǐ" hòumiàn shuō hòulái zěnme le.
*Oh — after "yīnwèi" comes the why, after "suǒyǐ" comes what follows.*

**莎莎：**对。再听一句：因为我累了，所以我想睡觉。
Duì. Zài tīng yí jù: Yīnwèi wǒ lèi le, suǒyǐ wǒ xiǎng shuìjiào.
*Right. One more: because I'm tired, I want to sleep.*

**波哥：**我来试试：因为我饿了，所以我想吃饭。
Wǒ lái shìshi: Yīnwèi wǒ è le, suǒyǐ wǒ xiǎng chīfàn.
*Let me try: because I'm hungry, I want to eat.*

**莎莎：**很好，就是这样用！再来一句。
Hěn hǎo, jiùshì zhèyàng yòng! Zài lái yí jù.
*Great — that's exactly how! Another one.*

**波哥：**因为我每天听中文，所以我懂得更多了。
Yīnwèi wǒ měitiān tīng Zhōngwén, suǒyǐ wǒ dǒngde gèng duō le.
*Because I listen to Chinese every day, I understand more and more.*

**莎莎：**太好了。记住：这对朋友，要在同一句话里。
Tài hǎo le. Jìzhù: zhè duì péngyou, yào zài tóng yí jù huà lǐ.
*Excellent. Remember: this pair stays in the same sentence.*

**波哥：**就像"越……越……"，也是一对。
Jiù xiàng "yuè…… yuè……", yě shì yí duì.
*Like "yuè… yuè…" — also a pair.*

**莎莎：**对，第三集的老朋友。现在，把它用到今天的话上。
Duì, dì sān jí de lǎo péngyou. Xiànzài, bǎ tā yòng dào jīntiān de huà shàng.
*Right, our old friend from episode three. Now apply the new pair to today's idea.*

**波哥：**因为你慢慢听，所以你真的懂。
Yīnwèi nǐ mànmàn tīng, suǒyǐ nǐ zhēnde dǒng.
*Because you listen slowly, you really understand.*

**莎莎：**因为你真的懂，所以你记得住。
Yīnwèi nǐ zhēnde dǒng, suǒyǐ nǐ jì de zhù.
*Because you really understand, it sticks.*

**波哥：**因为你记得住，所以你想继续听。
Yīnwèi nǐ jì de zhù, suǒyǐ nǐ xiǎng jìxù tīng.
*Because it sticks, you want to keep listening.*

**莎莎：**你看，一句连一句，像走路，一步一步往前。
Nǐ kàn, yí jù lián yí jù, xiàng zǒulù, yí bù yí bù wǎng qián.
*See — sentence links to sentence, like walking, one step at a time.*

**波哥：**慢慢走的人，最后走得远。
Mànmàn zǒu de rén, zuìhòu zǒu de yuǎn.
*The one who walks slowly goes far in the end.*

**莎莎：**这就是"慢就是快"。
Zhè jiùshì "màn jiùshì kuài".
*And that is "slow is fast".*

### 我们为什么说得慢 · Why we speak slowly

**波哥：**好，现在到最后的答案了。
Hǎo, xiànzài dào zuìhòu de dá'àn le.
*Okay, time for the final answer.*

**莎莎：**对，慢慢中文为什么说得这么慢？
Duì, Mànmàn Zhōngwén wèishénme shuō de zhème màn?
*Yes — why does Manman Zhongwen speak so slowly?*

**波哥：**说实话，有人会觉得：你们说得太慢了，有点怪。
Shuō shíhuà, yǒu rén huì juéde: nǐmen shuō de tài màn le, yǒudiǎn guài.
*Honestly, some people think: you speak too slowly — it's a bit strange.*

**莎莎：**可能会。我们说实话：慢，是我们自己选的。
Kěnéng huì. Wǒmen shuō shíhuà: màn, shì wǒmen zìjǐ xuǎn de.
*Maybe so. Here's the honest truth: slow is our own choice.*

**波哥：**为什么选慢？
Wèishénme xuǎn màn?
*Why choose slow?*

**莎莎：**因为我们想让你真的懂，所以我们慢慢说。
Yīnwèi wǒmen xiǎng ràng nǐ zhēnde dǒng, suǒyǐ wǒmen mànmàn shuō.
*Because we want you to really understand, we speak slowly.*

**波哥：**一集听完真的懂，比十集"好像懂"好。
Yì jí tīng wán zhēnde dǒng, bǐ shí jí "hǎoxiàng dǒng" hǎo.
*One episode truly understood beats ten episodes "sort of understood".*

**莎莎：**对。说快很容易，说慢才难。
Duì. Shuō kuài hěn róngyì, shuō màn cái nán.
*Right. Speaking fast is easy — speaking slowly is the hard part.*

**波哥：**为什么说慢才难？
Wèishénme shuō màn cái nán?
*Why is slow harder?*

**莎莎：**因为慢的时候，每个字、每个声调，都要说清楚。
Yīnwèi màn de shíhou, měi ge zì, měi ge shēngdiào, dōu yào shuō qīngchu.
*Because when it's slow, every character and every tone has to be clear.*

**波哥：**像把每个词，慢慢放到我手里。
Xiàng bǎ měi ge cí, mànmàn fàngdào wǒ shǒu lǐ.
*Like placing each word slowly into my hands.*

**莎莎：**如果你也想每天慢慢听一点，就订阅慢慢中文。
Rúguǒ nǐ yě xiǎng měitiān mànmàn tīng yìdiǎn, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*If you'd also like a little slow listening every day, subscribe.*

**波哥：**每个星期都有新的一集。我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Měi ge xīngqī dōu yǒu xīn de yì jí. Wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*A new episode every week. We speak slowly, you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**还记得第一集吗？词像朋友。
Hái jìde dì yī jí ma? Cí xiàng péngyou.
*Remember episode one? Words are like friends.*

**波哥：**见得多了，就认识了。
Jiàn de duō le, jiù rènshi le.
*Meet them often enough, and you know them.*

**莎莎：**朋友要慢慢认识，词也要慢慢听。
Péngyou yào mànmàn rènshi, cí yě yào mànmàn tīng.
*Friends are made slowly — words are heard slowly.*

**波哥：**一天见一百个新朋友，一个也记不住。
Yì tiān jiàn yìbǎi ge xīn péngyou, yí ge yě jì bu zhù.
*Meet a hundred new people in one day and you remember none.*

**莎莎：**慢慢认识一个，就是真的朋友。
Mànmàn rènshi yí ge, jiùshì zhēnde péngyou.
*Get to know one slowly, and it's a real friend.*

**波哥：**所以词不用多，先跟十个词做朋友。
Suǒyǐ cí búyòng duō, xiān gēn shí ge cí zuò péngyou.
*So you don't need many words — befriend ten first.*

**莎莎：**对，这十个是真的朋友，一直都在。
Duì, zhè shí ge shì zhēnde péngyou, yìzhí dōu zài.
*Right — those ten are real friends, always there.*

**波哥：**第三集也说过：每天听一点，一年是一座小山。
Dì sān jí yě shuō guò: Měitiān tīng yìdiǎn, yì nián shì yí zuò xiǎoshān.
*Episode three said it too: a little every day is a small mountain in a year.*

**莎莎：**对。慢，可是每天都在，这就是快。
Duì. Màn, kěshì měitiān dōu zài, zhè jiùshì kuài.
*Yes. Slow — but there every day. That's what fast really is.*

**波哥：**所以在这里，请你慢慢来。
Suǒyǐ zài zhèlǐ, qǐng nǐ mànmàn lái.
*So here, please take it slow.*

### 快问快答 · Speed round

Answer out loud **before** Bo does.

**莎莎：**好，最后，快问快答！准备好了吗？
Hǎo, zuìhòu, kuài wèn kuài dá! Zhǔnbèi hǎo le ma?
*Okay, finally — speed round! Ready?*

**波哥：**准备好了，今天我来答！
Zhǔnbèi hǎo le, jīntiān wǒ lái dá!
*Ready — today I answer!*

**莎莎：**听得快，会怎么样？
Tīng de kuài, huì zěnmeyàng?
*Listen fast — what happens?*

**波哥：**听得快，忘得快。
Tīng de kuài, wàng de kuài.
*Fast in, fast out.*

**莎莎：**听得慢呢？
Tīng de màn ne?
*And listening slowly?*

**波哥：**听得慢，记得住。
Tīng de màn, jì de zhù.
*Slow in, it stays.*

**莎莎：**吃饭要快，还是要慢？
Chīfàn yào kuài, háishì yào màn?
*Should you eat fast or slow?*

**波哥：**要慢。慢慢吃，身体才能用它。
Yào màn. Mànmàn chī, shēntǐ cáinéng yòng tā.
*Slow. Eat slowly, and the body can use it.*

**莎莎：**一个晚上看十个视频，好吗？
Yí ge wǎnshang kàn shí ge shìpín, hǎo ma?
*Ten videos in one night — good idea?*

**波哥：**不好。留下来的太少。
Bù hǎo. Liúxiàlái de tài shǎo.
*No. Too little stays.*

**莎莎：**为什么慢就记得住？用那对新朋友说。
Wèishénme màn jiù jì de zhù? Yòng nà duì xīn péngyou shuō.
*Why does slow stick? Say it with the new pair.*

**波哥：**因为你慢慢听，所以你真的懂。
Yīnwèi nǐ mànmàn tīng, suǒyǐ nǐ zhēnde dǒng.
*Because you listen slowly, you really understand.*

**莎莎：**懂了以后呢？
Dǒng le yǐhòu ne?
*And after you understand?*

**波哥：**因为你真的懂，所以你记得住。
Yīnwèi nǐ zhēnde dǒng, suǒyǐ nǐ jì de zhù.
*Because you really understand, it sticks.*

**莎莎：**那，慢慢中文为什么说得这么慢？
Nà, Mànmàn Zhōngwén wèishénme shuō de zhème màn?
*So — why does Manman Zhongwen speak this slowly?*

**波哥：**因为你们想让我们真的懂，所以你们慢慢说。
Yīnwèi nǐmen xiǎng ràng wǒmen zhēnde dǒng, suǒyǐ nǐmen mànmàn shuō.
*Because you want us to really understand, you speak slowly.*

**莎莎：**学中文是跑步，还是走路？
Xué Zhōngwén shì pǎobù, háishì zǒulù?
*Is learning Chinese running or walking?*

**波哥：**是走路。慢慢走的人，走得远。
Shì zǒulù. Mànmàn zǒu de rén, zǒu de yuǎn.
*Walking. The one who walks slowly goes far.*

### 结尾 · Ending

**莎莎：**全对！好，今天我们说了：慢，就是快。
