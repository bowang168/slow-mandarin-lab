> **Pinyin note:** we write 一 and 不 with the tone you actually hear (yí ge, búshì).
> Neutral-tone syllables have no tone mark (méi guānxi, gùshi).

## In this episode 这一集

- One fictional workday where everything goes wrong: spilled coffee, the wrong number in a
  meeting, a dead computer.
- Beside every mishap, a small kindness — and the **虽然……但是……** pattern that holds
  both truths in one sentence.
- Two cups of coffee, and why Xiao Zhao smiles on the way home.

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **虽然今天很难，但是明天会好。**
> Suīrán jīntiān hěn nán, dànshì míngtiān huì hǎo.
> *Even though today was hard, tomorrow will be better.*

## The 虽然……但是…… sentences 难的事 + 好的事

| 句子 | Pinyin | Moment |
|---|---|---|
| 虽然咖啡打翻了，但是有人帮他。 | Suīrán kāfēi dǎfān le, dànshì yǒu rén bāng tā. | morning |
| 虽然我说错了话，但是没有人笑我。 | Suīrán wǒ shuō cuò le huà, dànshì méiyǒu rén xiào wǒ. | the meeting |
| 虽然电脑坏了，但是文件还在。 | Suīrán diànnǎo huài le, dànshì wénjiàn hái zài. | afternoon |
| 虽然今天很难，但是明天会好。 | Suīrán jīntiān hěn nán, dànshì míngtiān huì hǎo. | the whole day |

**Your turn:** 虽然 opens with the hard thing, 但是 answers with the good thing — always in
the same sentence. Try your own: 虽然今天______，但是______。

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**莎莎：**波哥，你有没有过很不顺的一天？
Bōgē, nǐ yǒu méiyǒu guò hěn bú shùn de yì tiān?
*Bo, have you ever had a day where nothing goes right?*

**波哥：**有啊。从早到晚，什么都不对的那种一天。
Yǒu a. Cóng zǎo dào wǎn, shénme dōu bú duì de nà zhǒng yì tiān.
*Sure. The kind where everything is off from morning to night.*

**莎莎：**今天的故事，就是这样的一天。
Jīntiān de gùshi, jiùshì zhèyàng de yì tiān.
*Today's story is exactly that kind of day.*

**波哥：**谁的一天？
Shéi de yì tiān?
*Whose day?*

**莎莎：**小赵的一天。早上，咖啡打翻了。
Xiǎozhào de yì tiān. Zǎoshang, kāfēi dǎfān le.
*Xiao Zhao's day. In the morning, the coffee gets knocked over.*

**波哥：**哎呀，不顺的开始。
Āiyā, bú shùn de kāishǐ.
*Ouch — a rough start.*

**莎莎：**上午，开会的时候，他说错了话。
Shàngwǔ, kāihuì de shíhou, tā shuō cuò le huà.
*Late morning, he says the wrong thing in a meeting.*

**波哥：**这就更糟了。
Zhè jiù gèng zāo le.
*That's even worse.*

**莎莎：**还没完。下午，电脑也坏了。
Hái méi wán. Xiàwǔ, diànnǎo yě huài le.
*Not done yet. In the afternoon, his computer dies too.*

**波哥：**一天三件事？这也太难了吧！
Yì tiān sān jiàn shì? Zhè yě tài nán le ba!
*Three things in one day? That's brutal!*

**莎莎：**可是，到了晚上，小赵笑了。
Kěshì, dào le wǎnshang, Xiǎozhào xiào le.
*And yet, by evening, Xiao Zhao is smiling.*

**波哥：**为什么？这么难的一天，他为什么笑？
Wèishénme? Zhème nán de yì tiān, tā wèishénme xiào?
*Why? After a day like that, why is he smiling?*

**莎莎：**答案在故事的最后。别走开。
Dá'àn zài gùshi de zuìhòu. Bié zǒukāi.
*The answer is at the end of the story. Don't go away.*

### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。今天，我们讲一个慢慢的故事。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Jīntiān, wǒmen jiǎng yí ge mànmàn de gùshi.
*I'm Bo. Today, a slow story.*

**莎莎：**小赵是一个虚构的人，这个故事也是虚构的。
Xiǎozhào shì yí ge xūgòu de rén, zhège gùshi yě shì xūgòu de.
*Xiao Zhao is a fictional person, and this story is fictional too.*

**波哥：**可是这样的一天，很多人都真的过过。
Kěshì zhèyàng de yì tiān, hěnduō rén dōu zhēnde guòguo.
*But plenty of us have genuinely lived a day like this.*

**莎莎：**对。好，故事开始了。
Duì. Hǎo, gùshi kāishǐ le.
*True. Okay — the story begins.*

### 打翻的咖啡 · The spilled coffee

**莎莎：**星期三早上八点半，小赵到了公司。
Xīngqīsān zǎoshang bā diǎn bàn, Xiǎozhào dào le gōngsī.
*Wednesday, 8:30 a.m. — Xiao Zhao arrives at the office.*

**波哥：**他今天来得早吗？
Tā jīntiān lái de zǎo ma?
*Is he early today?*

**莎莎：**不早也不晚。他先在楼下买了一杯热咖啡。
Bù zǎo yě bù wǎn. Tā xiān zài lóuxià mǎi le yì bēi rè kāfēi.
*Neither early nor late. First he buys a hot coffee downstairs.*

**波哥：**到这里，还是好的开始。
Dào zhèlǐ, háishì hǎo de kāishǐ.
*So far, still a good start.*

**莎莎：**他把咖啡放在桌子上，坐下来，打开电脑。
Tā bǎ kāfēi fàng zài zhuōzi shàng, zuò xiàlái, dǎkāi diànnǎo.
*He puts the coffee on the desk, sits down, opens his computer.*

**波哥：**然后呢？
Ránhòu ne?
*And then?*

**莎莎：**他一转身，手碰到了杯子。杯子倒了。
Tā yì zhuǎnshēn, shǒu pèngdào le bēizi. Bēizi dào le.
*He turns around — his hand catches the cup. The cup tips over.*

**波哥：**不会吧……
Bú huì ba……
*No way…*

**莎莎：**咖啡打翻了。桌子上都是咖啡，衬衫上也有。
Kāfēi dǎfān le. Zhuōzi shàng dōu shì kāfēi, chènshān shàng yě yǒu.
*The coffee spills everywhere — all over the desk, and on his shirt.*

**波哥：**哎呀。新的一天，先坏了一半。
Āiyā. Xīn de yì tiān, xiān huài le yíbàn.
*Ouch. The new day is half ruined already.*

**莎莎：**小赵站在那里，一下子不知道先做什么。
Xiǎozhào zhàn zài nàlǐ, yíxiàzi bù zhīdào xiān zuò shénme.
*Xiao Zhao just stands there, not knowing what to do first.*

**波哥：**我懂这种时候。脑子一下子空了。
Wǒ dǒng zhè zhǒng shíhou. Nǎozi yíxiàzi kōng le.
*I know that moment. Your brain just goes blank.*

**莎莎：**这时候，旁边的同事小李走了过来。
Zhèshíhou, pángbiān de tóngshì Xiǎo Lǐ zǒu le guòlái.
*Just then, his colleague Xiao Li comes over.*

**波哥：**他说什么了？
Tā shuō shénme le?
*What does he say?*

**莎莎：**他什么都没说，先给了小赵一包纸巾。
Tā shénme dōu méi shuō, xiān gěi le Xiǎozhào yì bāo zhǐjīn.
*He says nothing — he first hands Xiao Zhao a pack of tissues.*

**波哥：**先给纸巾，再说话。这个同事好。
Xiān gěi zhǐjīn, zài shuōhuà. Zhège tóngshì hǎo.
*Tissues first, words second. Good colleague.*

**莎莎：**然后他说：没关系，我帮你擦桌子。
Ránhòu tā shuō: Méi guānxi, wǒ bāng nǐ cā zhuōzi.
*Then he says: it's okay — I'll help you wipe the desk.*

**波哥：**没关系……第五集那句话！
Méi guānxi…… dì wǔ jí nà jù huà!
*"Méi guānxi"… the episode-five sentence!*

**莎莎：**对。这句"没关系"是说给别人的，一样很暖。
Duì. Zhè jù "méi guānxi" shì shuō gěi biéren de, yíyàng hěn nuǎn.
*Right — this "méi guānxi" is said to someone else, and it's just as warm.*

**波哥：**那衬衫呢？咖啡在衬衫上，一天都要穿着它。
Nà chènshān ne? Kāfēi zài chènshān shàng, yì tiān dōu yào chuānzhe tā.
*But the shirt? The coffee's on it, and he has to wear it all day.*

**莎莎：**小李说：我这里有一件干净的衬衫，你先穿。
Xiǎo Lǐ shuō: Wǒ zhèlǐ yǒu yí jiàn gānjìng de chènshān, nǐ xiān chuān.
*Xiao Li says: I have a clean shirt here — wear it for now.*

**波哥：**所以，虽然咖啡打翻了，但是有人帮他。
Suǒyǐ, suīrán kāfēi dǎfān le, dànshì yǒu rén bāng tā.
*So even though the coffee spilled, someone helped him.*

**莎莎：**这句说得好！"虽然……但是……"，一对朋友，在同一句话里。
Zhè jù shuō de hǎo! "Suīrán…… dànshì……", yí duì péngyou, zài tóng yí jù huà lǐ.
*Well said! "Suīrán… dànshì…" — a pair of friends, always in one sentence.*

**波哥：**前面说难的事，后面说好的事。
Qiánmiàn shuō nán de shì, hòumiàn shuō hǎo de shì.
*The hard thing goes first, the good thing follows.*

**莎莎：**对。难的事是真的，好的事也是真的。
Duì. Nán de shì shì zhēnde, hǎo de shì yě shì zhēnde.
*Right. The hard thing is real — and so is the good thing.*

### 小任务 · Your turn

**波哥：**莎莎，是不是到评论区时间了？
Shāsha, shì bu shì dào pínglùnqū shíjiān le?
*Sasha, is it comment-section time?*

**莎莎：**对。用"虽然……但是……"写一句话，放在评论区。
Duì. Yòng "suīrán…… dànshì……" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Yes. Write one sentence with "suīrán… dànshì…" in the comments.*

**波哥：**我先来：虽然今天下雨，但是我心情很好。
Wǒ xiān lái: Suīrán jīntiān xiàyǔ, dànshì wǒ xīnqíng hěn hǎo.
*Me first: even though it's raining today, I'm in a good mood.*

**莎莎：**很好。再来一句？
Hěn hǎo. Zài lái yí jù?
*Nice. Another?*

**波哥：**虽然中文有点难，但是我每天都在听。
Suīrán Zhōngwén yǒudiǎn nán, dànshì wǒ měitiān dōu zài tīng.
*Even though Chinese is a bit hard, I listen every day.*

**莎莎：**太好了。你也写一句，我们会看，也会回你。
Tài hǎo le. Nǐ yě xiě yí jù, wǒmen huì kàn, yě huì huí nǐ.
*Perfect. Write yours too — we read and reply.*

### 开会说错话 · The wrong number

**波哥：**好，回到故事。上午的会呢？
Hǎo, huídào gùshi. Shàngwǔ de huì ne?
*Okay, back to the story. What about the morning meeting?*

**莎莎：**十点，开会了。小赵穿着小李的衬衫，走进了会议室。
Shí diǎn, kāihuì le. Xiǎozhào chuānzhe Xiǎo Lǐ de chènshān, zǒujìn le huìyìshì.
*Ten o'clock, meeting time. Xiao Zhao walks in wearing Xiao Li's shirt.*

**波哥：**他要说话吗？
Tā yào shuōhuà ma?
*Does he have to speak?*

**莎莎：**要。经理请他说一说这个星期的工作。
Yào. Jīnglǐ qǐng tā shuō yi shuō zhège xīngqī de gōngzuò.
*Yes. The manager asks him to go over this week's work.*

**波哥：**他准备好了吗？
Tā zhǔnbèi hǎo le ma?
*Is he prepared?*

**莎莎：**准备好了。可是说到一半，他说错了一个数字。
Zhǔnbèi hǎo le. Kěshì shuō dào yíbàn, tā shuō cuò le yí ge shùzì.
*He is. But halfway through, he gets a number wrong.*

**波哥：**说错了什么？
Shuō cuò le shénme?
*What did he get wrong?*

**莎莎：**他把"十五号"说成了"五号"。
Tā bǎ "shíwǔ hào" shuō chéng le "wǔ hào".
*He said "the 5th" instead of "the 15th".*

**波哥：**差了十天！
Chà le shí tiān!
*Ten days off!*

**莎莎：**对。会议室里，一下子安静了。
Duì. Huìyìshì lǐ, yíxiàzi ānjìng le.
*Yes. The meeting room goes quiet all at once.*

**波哥：**我听着都紧张了。
Wǒ tīng zhe dōu jǐnzhāng le.
*I'm nervous just hearing this.*

**莎莎：**小赵的脸红了。他说：对不起，我说错了。是十五号。
Xiǎozhào de liǎn hóng le. Tā shuō: Duìbuqǐ, wǒ shuō cuò le. Shì shíwǔ hào.
*Xiao Zhao's face turns red. He says: sorry, I said that wrong — it's the 15th.*

**波哥：**然后呢？经理生气了吗？
Ránhòu ne? Jīnglǐ shēngqì le ma?
*And then? Was the manager angry?*

**莎莎：**没有。经理说：没关系，改过来就好。
Méiyǒu. Jīnglǐ shuō: Méi guānxi, gǎi guòlái jiù hǎo.
*No. The manager says: it's okay — just correct it.*

**波哥：**又是"没关系"。
Yòu shì "méi guānxi".
*"Méi guānxi" again.*

**莎莎：**对。然后大家继续开会，就像什么都没有发生过。
Duì. Ránhòu dàjiā jìxù kāihuì, jiù xiàng shénme dōu méiyǒu fāshēng guò.
*Yes. The meeting simply carries on, as if nothing had happened.*

**波哥：**原来说错了话，事情也没有那么可怕。
Yuánlái shuō cuò le huà, shìqing yě méiyǒu nàme kěpà.
*So saying the wrong thing isn't as scary as it feels.*

**莎莎：**对。第五集我们说过：说错了没关系，再说一次就好。
Duì. Dì wǔ jí wǒmen shuō guò: Shuō cuò le méi guānxi, zài shuō yí cì jiù hǎo.
*Right. As we said in episode five: getting it wrong is okay — just say it again.*

**波哥：**小赵再说了一次，就好了。
Xiǎozhào zài shuō le yí cì, jiù hǎo le.
*Xiao Zhao said it once more, and all was fine.*

**莎莎：**开完会，小赵回到桌子前，在本子上写了一句话。
Kāi wán huì, Xiǎozhào huídào zhuōzi qián, zài běnzi shàng xiě le yí jù huà.
*After the meeting, back at his desk, Xiao Zhao writes one line in his notebook.*

**波哥：**写了什么？
Xiě le shénme?
*What did he write?*

**莎莎：**虽然我说错了话，但是没有人笑我。
Suīrán wǒ shuō cuò le huà, dànshì méiyǒu rén xiào wǒ.
*Even though I said the wrong thing, nobody laughed at me.*

**波哥：**又是"虽然……但是……"。这一天，第二次了。
Yòu shì "suīrán…… dànshì……". Zhè yì tiān, dì èr cì le.
*"Suīrán… dànshì…" again — the second time today.*

### 电脑坏了 · The dead computer

**莎莎：**下午两点，第三件事来了。
Xiàwǔ liǎng diǎn, dì sān jiàn shì lái le.
*Two in the afternoon — the third thing arrives.*

**波哥：**我知道，是电脑。
Wǒ zhīdào, shì diànnǎo.
*I know — the computer.*

**莎莎：**对。小赵正在写一个很重要的文件，已经写了一半。
Duì. Xiǎozhào zhèngzài xiě yí ge hěn zhòngyào de wénjiàn, yǐjīng xiě le yíbàn.
*Right. Xiao Zhao is writing a very important document — already half done.*

**波哥：**然后电脑坏了？
Ránhòu diànnǎo huài le?
*And then the computer dies?*

**莎莎：**电脑一下子黑了，怎么按都没有用。
Diànnǎo yíxiàzi hēi le, zěnme àn dōu méiyǒu yòng.
*The screen goes black — no button does anything.*

**波哥：**文件呢？文件还在吗？
Wénjiàn ne? Wénjiàn hái zài ma?
*The document? Is the document still there?*

**莎莎：**小赵也在想这个。他心里想：完了，一下午的工作。
Xiǎozhào yě zài xiǎng zhège. Tā xīnlǐ xiǎng: Wán le, yí xiàwǔ de gōngzuò.
*That's what Xiao Zhao is thinking too. In his head: it's over — a whole afternoon's work.*

**波哥：**一天三件事，这是最大的一件。
Yì tiān sān jiàn shì, zhè shì zuì dà de yí jiàn.
*Of the three, this is the biggest.*

**莎莎：**他去找了公司里管电脑的同事，老王。
Tā qù zhǎo le gōngsī lǐ guǎn diànnǎo de tóngshì, Lǎo Wáng.
*He goes to find Lao Wang, the colleague who looks after the computers.*

**波哥：**老王怎么说？
Lǎo Wáng zěnme shuō?
*What does Lao Wang say?*

**莎莎：**老王看了看，说：别着急，先坐一下，我来修。
Lǎo Wáng kàn le kàn, shuō: Bié zháojí, xiān zuò yíxià, wǒ lái xiū.
*Lao Wang takes a look and says: don't worry — sit down first, I'll fix it.*

**波哥：**先坐一下，再修电脑。哈哈，"先……再……"！
Xiān zuò yíxià, zài xiū diànnǎo. Hāhā, "xiān…… zài……"!
*Sit first, fix later. Haha — the "xiān… zài…" pattern!*

**莎莎：**第二集的老朋友。十分钟以后，电脑好了。
Dì èr jí de lǎo péngyou. Shí fēnzhōng yǐhòu, diànnǎo hǎo le.
*Our old friend from episode two. Ten minutes later, the computer works.*

**波哥：**文件呢？
Wénjiàn ne?
*And the document?*

**莎莎：**文件还在，一个字都没少。
Wénjiàn hái zài, yí ge zì dōu méi shǎo.
*Still there — not a single character missing.*

**波哥：**太好了！虽然电脑坏了，但是文件还在。
Tài hǎo le! Suīrán diànnǎo huài le, dànshì wénjiàn hái zài.
*Wonderful! Even though the computer broke, the document survived.*

**莎莎：**对。小赵对老王说：太谢谢你了，帮了我大忙。
Duì. Xiǎozhào duì Lǎo Wáng shuō: Tài xièxie nǐ le, bāng le wǒ dà máng.
*Right. Xiao Zhao says to Lao Wang: thank you so much — you saved me.*

**波哥：**老王说什么？
Lǎo Wáng shuō shénme?
*And Lao Wang?*

**莎莎：**他笑着说：小事。下次请我喝杯咖啡就行。
Tā xiào zhe shuō: Xiǎo shì. Xiàcì qǐng wǒ hē bēi kāfēi jiù xíng.
*He smiles: no big deal. Buy me a coffee sometime.*

**波哥：**哈哈，咖啡又回来了。
Hāhā, kāfēi yòu huílái le.
*Haha — the coffee is back.*

**莎莎：**对，这次是好的咖啡。
Duì, zhè cì shì hǎo de kāfēi.
*Yes — the good kind this time.*

**波哥：**莎莎，听到这里，我心里暖暖的。
Shāsha, tīngdào zhèlǐ, wǒ xīnlǐ nuǎnnuǎn de.
*Sasha, hearing all this warms my heart.*

**莎莎：**如果你也喜欢这样能听懂的慢慢的故事，就订阅慢慢中文。
Rúguǒ nǐ yě xǐhuan zhèyàng néng tīngdǒng de mànmàn de gùshi, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*If you like slow stories you can actually follow, subscribe.*

**波哥：**每个星期，我们用简单的中文，讲真实生活里的感觉。
Měi ge xīngqī, wǒmen yòng jiǎndān de Zhōngwén, jiǎng zhēnshí shēnghuó lǐ de gǎnjué.
*Every week, simple Chinese about how real life actually feels.*

### 晚上的两杯咖啡 · Two cups of coffee

**莎莎：**晚上六点，小赵下班了。
Wǎnshang liù diǎn, Xiǎozhào xiàbān le.
*Six in the evening — Xiao Zhao finishes work.*

**波哥：**走出公司的时候，他在想什么？
Zǒuchū gōngsī de shíhou, tā zài xiǎng shénme?
*Walking out of the office, what is he thinking?*

**莎莎：**他想：今天真的很不顺。
Tā xiǎng: Jīntiān zhēnde hěn bú shùn.
*He thinks: today really did go badly.*

**波哥：**是真的。一天三件事，件件不顺。
Shì zhēnde. Yì tiān sān jiàn shì, jiàn jiàn bú shùn.
*True. Three things, every one of them wrong.*

**莎莎：**可是他又想：每一次不顺的时候，都有一个人帮了我一下。
Kěshì tā yòu xiǎng: Měi yí cì bú shùn de shíhou, dōu yǒu yí ge rén bāng le wǒ yíxià.
*But then he thinks: every time things went wrong, someone helped me a little.*

**波哥：**小李的纸巾和干净的衬衫。
Xiǎo Lǐ de zhǐjīn hé gānjìng de chènshān.
*Xiao Li's tissues and clean shirt.*

**莎莎：**经理的那句"没关系"。
Jīnglǐ de nà jù "méi guānxi".
*The manager's "méi guānxi".*

**波哥：**还有老王的十分钟。
Háiyǒu Lǎo Wáng de shí fēnzhōng.
*And Lao Wang's ten minutes.*

**莎莎：**想到这里，小赵在回家的路上买了两杯咖啡。
Xiǎngdào zhèlǐ, Xiǎozhào zài huíjiā de lù shàng mǎi le liǎng bēi kāfēi.
*Thinking of that, Xiao Zhao buys two coffees on the way home.*

**波哥：**两杯？为什么是两杯？
Liǎng bēi? Wèishénme shì liǎng bēi?
*Two? Why two?*

**莎莎：**一杯给自己。另一杯，明天早上放在老王的桌子上。
Yì bēi gěi zìjǐ. Lìng yì bēi, míngtiān zǎoshang fàng zài Lǎo Wáng de zhuōzi shàng.
*One for himself. The other goes on Lao Wang's desk tomorrow morning.*

**波哥：**啊，原来晚上他笑了，是因为这个。
À, yuánlái wǎnshang tā xiào le, shì yīnwèi zhège.
*Ah — so that's why he was smiling.*

**莎莎：**对。回家以前，他对自己说了一句话。
Duì. Huíjiā yǐqián, tā duì zìjǐ shuō le yí jù huà.
*Yes. Before going home, he says one sentence to himself.*

**波哥：**哪一句？
Nǎ yí jù?
*Which one?*

**莎莎：**虽然今天很难，但是明天会好。
Suīrán jīntiān hěn nán, dànshì míngtiān huì hǎo.
*Even though today was hard, tomorrow will be better.*

**波哥：**虽然今天很难，但是明天会好。
Suīrán jīntiān hěn nán, dànshì míngtiān huì hǎo.
*Even though today was hard, tomorrow will be better.*

**莎莎：**这就是今天的故事里，最重要的一句话。
Zhè jiùshì jīntiān de gùshi lǐ, zuì zhòngyào de yí jù huà.
*That is the most important sentence in today's story.*

### 回想 · Recall

Say the answers out loud **before** Bo does.

**波哥：**我们来回想一下小赵的一天。
Wǒmen lái huíxiǎng yíxià Xiǎozhào de yì tiān.
*Let's recall Xiao Zhao's day.*

**莎莎：**好。早上发生了什么？
Hǎo. Zǎoshang fāshēng le shénme?
*Okay. What happened in the morning?*

**波哥：**咖啡打翻了。可是小李给了他纸巾，还有一件干净的衬衫。
Kāfēi dǎfān le. Kěshì Xiǎo Lǐ gěi le tā zhǐjīn, háiyǒu yí jiàn gānjìng de chènshān.
*The coffee spilled. But Xiao Li gave him tissues and a clean shirt.*

**莎莎：**上午呢？
Shàngwǔ ne?
*Late morning?*

**波哥：**开会说错了数字。可是经理说：没关系，改过来就好。
Kāihuì shuō cuò le shùzì. Kěshì jīnglǐ shuō: Méi guānxi, gǎi guòlái jiù hǎo.
*He got a number wrong in the meeting. But the manager said: it's okay, just correct it.*

**莎莎：**下午呢？
Xiàwǔ ne?
*Afternoon?*

**波哥：**电脑坏了。可是老王十分钟就修好了，文件还在。
Diànnǎo huài le. Kěshì Lǎo Wáng shí fēnzhōng jiù xiū hǎo le, wénjiàn hái zài.
*The computer died. But Lao Wang fixed it in ten minutes, and the document survived.*

**莎莎：**三件不顺的事，三个帮忙的人。现在你来说：谢谢，怎么说得更暖？
Sān jiàn bú shùn de shì, sān ge bāngmáng de rén. Xiànzài nǐ lái shuō: Xièxie, zěnme shuō de gèng nuǎn?
*Three mishaps, three helpers. Now you: how do you say thank you more warmly?*

**波哥：**太谢谢你了，帮了我大忙。
Tài xièxie nǐ le, bāng le wǒ dà máng.
*Thank you so much — you really saved me.*

**莎莎：**最重要的那句呢？
Zuì zhòngyào de nà jù ne?
*And the most important sentence?*

**波哥：**虽然今天很难，但是明天会好。
Suīrán jīntiān hěn nán, dànshì míngtiān huì hǎo.
*Even though today was hard, tomorrow will be better.*

**莎莎：**全对。
Quán duì.
*All correct.*

### 结尾 · Ending

**波哥：**今天的故事说完了。"虽然……但是……"，请你带回家。
Jīntiān de gùshi shuō wán le. "Suīrán…… dànshì……", qǐng nǐ dài huí jiā.
*That's the story. Please take "suīrán… dànshì…" home with you.*

**莎莎：**难的事是真的，好的事也是真的。
Nán de shì shì zhēnde, hǎo de shì yě shì zhēnde.
*The hard things are real — and so are the good things.*

**波哥：**别忘了你的小事：用"虽然……但是……"写一句话，放在评论区。
Bié wàng le nǐ de xiǎo shì: yòng "suīrán…… dànshì……" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Don't forget your task: one "suīrán… dànshì…" sentence in the comments.*

**莎莎：**下一集，我们聊一件小赵今天做得很好的事。
Xià yì jí, wǒmen liáo yí jiàn Xiǎozhào jīntiān zuò de hěn hǎo de shì.
*Next episode, we talk about one thing Xiao Zhao did really well today.*

**波哥：**什么事？
Shénme shì?
*What's that?*

**莎莎：**请人帮忙。很多人不好意思开口，怕麻烦别人。
Qǐng rén bāngmáng. Hěnduō rén bù hǎoyìsi kāikǒu, pà máfan biéren.
*Asking for help. Many of us are too shy to ask — afraid of being a bother.*

**波哥：**下一集《怎么请人帮忙》，教你几句好用的话。
Xià yì jí "Zěnme qǐng rén bāngmáng", jiāo nǐ jǐ jù hǎoyòng de huà.
*Next episode, "How to Ask for Help" — a few sentences that really work.*

**莎莎：**谢谢你和我们在一起。我是莎莎。
Xièxie nǐ hé wǒmen zài yìqǐ. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Thanks for being with us. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。我们下一集见！
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Wǒmen xià yì jí jiàn!
*I'm Bo. See you next episode!*

**莎莎：**下次见。慢慢来。
Xiàcì jiàn. Mànmàn lái.
