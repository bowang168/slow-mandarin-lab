> **Pinyin note:** we write 一 and 不 with the tone you actually hear (yí ge, búshì).
> Neutral-tone syllables have no tone mark (méi guānxi, ěrduo).

## In this episode 这一集

- Tones are **not a hat on top of the word** — they are part of the word itself.
- Why a wrong tone almost never ruins you: the situation and the whole sentence help.
- The four-character trick that makes your tones clear — and the only drill you need.

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **说慢一点，声调就清楚了。**
> Shuō màn yìdiǎn, shēngdiào jiù qīngchǔ le.
> *Say it slower, and the tones come out clear.*

## The four tones on one sound 四个声调

| 声调 | 字 | Pinyin | English |
|---|---|---|---|
| 第一声（high, flat）| 妈 | mā | mother |
| 第二声（rising）| 麻 | má | numbing — as in 麻婆豆腐 |
| 第三声（down, then up）| 马 | mǎ | horse |
| 第四声（sharp fall）| 骂 | mà | to scold |

Then one real word pair: **我买，你卖。** Wǒ mǎi, nǐ mài. — *I buy, you sell.*

### 开场 · Cold open

**莎莎：**波哥，我说两个字，很多人一听就紧张。
Bōgē, wǒ shuō liǎng ge zì, hěn duō rén yì tīng jiù jǐnzhāng.
*Bo, I'll say two characters that make a lot of people tense up.*

**波哥：**两个字？你说。
Liǎng ge zì? Nǐ shuō.
*Two characters? Go on.*

**莎莎：**声调。
Shēngdiào.
*Tones.*

**波哥：**啊，声调……你说对了，我真的怕它。
À, shēngdiào…… nǐ shuō duì le, wǒ zhēnde pà tā.
*Ah, tones… you're right, I'm genuinely scared of them.*

**莎莎：**为什么怕？
Wèishénme pà?
*Why scared?*

**波哥：**老师说，声调错了，词就错了。妈妈会变成马。
Lǎoshī shuō, shēngdiào cuò le, cí jiù cuò le. Māma huì biànchéng mǎ.
*Teachers say: wrong tone, wrong word. Mum can turn into a horse.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，妈妈不能变成马。
Hāhā, māma bù néng biànchéng mǎ.
*Haha — we can't have Mum turning into a horse.*

**波哥：**对啊！所以我每次开口，都先想声调，越想越不敢说。
Duì a! Suǒyǐ wǒ měi cì kāikǒu, dōu xiān xiǎng shēngdiào, yuè xiǎng yuè bù gǎn shuō.
*Exactly! So every time I speak I think about tones first — and the more I think, the less I dare to speak.*

**莎莎：**很多朋友都是这样。别怕，今天没有表格，没有考试，只有三件事。
Hěn duō péngyou dōu shì zhèyàng. Bié pà, jīntiān méiyǒu biǎogé, méiyǒu kǎoshì, zhǐyǒu sān jiàn shì.
*Many of you are the same. Don't worry — no charts today, no tests, just three things.*

**波哥：**哪三件事？
Nǎ sān jiàn shì?
*Which three?*

**莎莎：**声调是什么，为什么不用那么怕，还有一个小办法。
Shēngdiào shì shénme, wèishénme búyòng nàme pà, háiyǒu yí ge xiǎo bànfǎ.
*What tones are, why you don't need to fear them — and one little trick.*

**波哥：**小办法？
Xiǎo bànfǎ?
*A little trick?*

**莎莎：**对。这个办法只有四个字，你今天就能用。别走开。
Duì. Zhège bànfǎ zhǐyǒu sì ge zì, nǐ jīntiān jiù néng yòng. Bié zǒukāi.
*Yes. It's only four characters, and you can use it today. Don't go away.*

### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。在这里，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Zài zhèlǐ, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*I'm Bo. Here, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**今天不学新场景，我们聊很多人心里的另一个怕：怕声调。
Jīntiān bù xué xīn chǎngjǐng, wǒmen liáo hěn duō rén xīnlǐ de lìng yí ge pà: pà shēngdiào.
*No new scene today — we're talking about another fear inside many of us: fear of tones.*

**波哥：**第五集我们聊了怕说错，今天聊它的兄弟。开始吧。
Dì wǔ jí wǒmen liáo le pà shuōcuò, jīntiān liáo tā de xiōngdì. Kāishǐ ba.
*Episode five was the fear of mistakes — today it's that fear's brother. Let's start.*

### 声调是词的一部分 · Tones are part of the word

**莎莎：**波哥，我先问你：你觉得声调是什么？
Bōgē, wǒ xiān wèn nǐ: nǐ juéde shēngdiào shì shénme?
*Bo, first question: what do you think a tone is?*

**波哥：**嗯……是拼音上面的小帽子。
Ǹg…… shì pīnyīn shàngmiàn de xiǎo màozi.
*Hmm… the little hat on top of the pinyin.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，很多人这么想：先有词，再给它一顶小帽子。
Hāhā, hěn duō rén zhème xiǎng: xiān yǒu cí, zài gěi tā yì dǐng xiǎo màozi.
*Haha — many people think that: first the word, then you put a little hat on it.*

**波哥：**不对吗？
Bú duì ma?
*Isn't that right?*

**莎莎：**不对。声调不是帽子，不是后来加上去的。
Bú duì. Shēngdiào búshì màozi, búshì hòulái jiā shàngqù de.
*No. A tone isn't a hat — it isn't added on afterwards.*

**波哥：**那它是什么？
Nà tā shì shénme?
*Then what is it?*

**莎莎：**声调是词的一部分。有了它，词才是词。
Shēngdiào shì cí de yí bùfen. Yǒu le tā, cí cái shì cí.
*The tone is part of the word. Only with it is the word complete.*

**波哥：**声调是词的一部分……
Shēngdiào shì cí de yí bùfen……
*Part of the word…*

**莎莎：**对。你听：妈，妈妈的妈。马，小马的马。
Duì. Nǐ tīng: mā, māma de mā. Mǎ, xiǎo mǎ de mǎ.
*Yes. Listen: mā, as in mother. Mǎ, as in horse.*

**波哥：**妈，马。真的不一样。
Mā, mǎ. Zhēnde bù yíyàng.
*Mā, mǎ. They really are different.*

**莎莎：**一样的音，不一样的声调，就是两个不一样的词。
Yíyàng de yīn, bù yíyàng de shēngdiào, jiùshì liǎng ge bù yíyàng de cí.
*Same sound, different tones — two different words.*

**波哥：**所以我说错声调，不是说错了一半，是说了另一个词？
Suǒyǐ wǒ shuōcuò shēngdiào, búshì shuōcuò le yíbàn, shì shuō le lìng yí ge cí?
*So a wrong tone isn't half a mistake — I've actually said a different word?*

**莎莎：**对。别怕，等一下你会看到，这没有那么可怕。
Duì. Bié pà, děng yíxià nǐ huì kàndào, zhè méiyǒu nàme kěpà.
*Right. But don't panic — in a moment you'll see it's not that scary.*

**波哥：**好。那我怎么记声调呢？
Hǎo. Nà wǒ zěnme jì shēngdiào ne?
*Okay. So how do I remember tones?*

**莎莎：**学新词的时候，把词和它的声调一起学，不分开。
Xué xīncí de shíhou, bǎ cí hé tā de shēngdiào yìqǐ xué, bù fēnkāi.
*When you learn a new word, learn the word and its tone together — never apart.*

**波哥：**词像朋友——记住朋友的脸，也记住他的声音。
Cí xiàng péngyou——jìzhù péngyou de liǎn, yě jìzhù tā de shēngyīn.
*Words are like friends — remember the friend's face and their voice.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，第一集的老朋友！对，就是这个意思。
Hāhā, dì yī jí de lǎo péngyou! Duì, jiùshì zhège yìsi.
*Haha, an old friend from episode one! Exactly.*

**波哥：**像唱歌一样，调子在歌里，不在纸上。
Xiàng chànggē yíyàng, diàozi zài gē lǐ, bù zài zhǐ shàng.
*Like singing — the tune lives in the song, not on the paper.*

**莎莎：**说得真好。声调在词里，不在表格里。
Shuō de zhēn hǎo. Shēngdiào zài cí lǐ, bù zài biǎogé lǐ.
*Beautifully put. Tones live in words, not in charts.*

### 为什么不用那么怕 · Why tones aren't so scary

**波哥：**莎莎，我还有一个大问题。
Shāsha, wǒ háiyǒu yí ge dà wèntí.
*Sasha, I still have one big question.*

**莎莎：**你说。
Nǐ shuō.
*Go on.*

**波哥：**如果我的声调错了，别人是不是就听不懂我了？
Rúguǒ wǒ de shēngdiào cuò le, biéren shì bu shì jiù tīng bu dǒng wǒ le?
*If my tone is wrong, does that mean nobody understands me?*

**莎莎：**我先问你：还记得第五集的水饺吗？
Wǒ xiān wèn nǐ: hái jìde dì wǔ jí de shuǐjiǎo ma?
*Let me ask you: remember the dumplings from episode five?*

**波哥：**哈哈，记得！那个学生想点水饺，说成了“我要睡觉”。
Hāhā, jìde! Nàge xuéshēng xiǎng diǎn shuǐjiǎo, shuō chéng le "wǒ yào shuìjiào".
*Haha, of course! The student who wanted dumplings and said "I want to sleep".*

**莎莎：**水饺和睡觉，差的就是一个声调。可是老板听懂了吗？
Shuǐjiǎo hé shuìjiào, chà de jiùshì yí ge shēngdiào. Kěshì lǎobǎn tīngdǒng le ma?
*Shuǐjiǎo and shuìjiào differ by exactly one tone. But did the owner understand?*

**波哥：**听懂了。因为他在饭馆，手里拿着菜单。
Tīngdǒng le. Yīnwèi tā zài fànguǎn, shǒulǐ ná zhe càidān.
*He did. Because they're in a restaurant, menu in hand.*

**莎莎：**对。没有人在饭馆要“睡觉”，所以老板知道是水饺。
Duì. Méiyǒu rén zài fànguǎn yào "shuìjiào", suǒyǐ lǎobǎn zhīdào shì shuǐjiǎo.
*Right. Nobody orders "sleep" at a restaurant, so the owner knew it was dumplings.*

**波哥：**场景在帮我说话。
Chǎngjǐng zài bāng wǒ shuōhuà.
*The situation speaks for me.*

**莎莎：**你的整句话也在帮你。人听的是整句话，不是一个字。
Nǐ de zhěng jù huà yě zài bāng nǐ. Rén tīng de shì zhěng jù huà, búshì yí ge zì.
*So does your whole sentence. People listen to sentences, not single characters.*

**波哥：**一个声调错了，句子还在，场景还在。
Yí ge shēngdiào cuò le, jùzi hái zài, chǎngjǐng hái zài.
*One tone goes wrong — the sentence is still there, the situation is still there.*

**莎莎：**对。声调错了，天不会塌。大部分时候，别人还是懂你。
Duì. Shēngdiào cuò le, tiān bú huì tā. Dàbùfen shíhou, biéren háishì dǒng nǐ.
*Right. A wrong tone won't make the sky fall. Most of the time, people still understand you.*

**波哥：**那……我是不是就不用管声调了？
Nà…… wǒ shì bu shì jiù búyòng guǎn shēngdiào le?
*So… can I just ignore tones then?*

**莎莎：**不是。声调重要，可是它不可怕。这是两件事。
Búshì. Shēngdiào zhòngyào, kěshì tā bù kěpà. Zhè shì liǎng jiàn shì.
*No. Tones matter — but they are not scary. Those are two different things.*

**波哥：**重要，可是不可怕。嗯，我慢慢明白了。
Zhòngyào, kěshì bù kěpà. Ǹg, wǒ mànmàn míngbai le.
*Important, but not scary. Hmm — it's sinking in.*

**莎莎：**还有一个好消息，你可能不知道。
Háiyǒu yí ge hǎo xiāoxi, nǐ kěnéng bù zhīdào.
*And there's good news you may not know.*

**波哥：**什么好消息？
Shénme hǎo xiāoxi?
*What good news?*

**莎莎：**你每次慢慢听中文，耳朵都在学声调。
Nǐ měi cì mànmàn tīng Zhōngwén, ěrduo dōu zài xué shēngdiào.
*Every time you listen to slow Chinese, your ears are learning tones.*

**波哥：**啊？可是我没有做声调练习啊。
À? Kěshì wǒ méiyǒu zuò shēngdiào liànxí a.
*What? But I don't do tone drills.*

**莎莎：**不用做。你听得多了，耳朵就懂了：哦，这个词是这个调子。
Búyòng zuò. Nǐ tīng de duō le, ěrduo jiù dǒng le: ó, zhège cí shì zhège diàozi.
*You don't need to. Listen enough, and your ear gets it: ah, this word carries this tune.*

**波哥：**小孩子学说话，好像也是这样。
Xiǎoháizi xué shuōhuà, hǎoxiàng yě shì zhèyàng.
*That's how children learn to speak, isn't it.*

**莎莎：**对。小孩子没有表格，就是天天听。你听这个节目，也一样，免费的。
Duì. Xiǎoháizi méiyǒu biǎogé, jiùshì tiāntiān tīng. Nǐ tīng zhège jiémù, yě yíyàng, miǎnfèi de.
*Right. Kids have no charts — they just listen every day. Listening to this show works the same way, for free.*

**波哥：**哈哈，太好了，我躺着也在学习。
Hāhā, tài hǎo le, wǒ tǎng zhe yě zài xuéxí.
*Haha, wonderful — I'm learning even lying down.*

### 说慢一点，声调就清楚了 · The four-character trick

**莎莎：**好，现在说那个四个字的小办法。波哥，你说话很快的时候，每个字有多少时间？
Hǎo, xiànzài shuō nàge sì ge zì de xiǎo bànfǎ. Bōgē, nǐ shuōhuà hěn kuài de shíhou, měi ge zì yǒu duōshao shíjiān?
*Now, the four-character trick. Bo — when you talk fast, how much time does each character get?*

**波哥：**嗯……很少。字和字都挤在一起了。
Ǹg…… hěn shǎo. Zì hé zì dōu jǐ zài yìqǐ le.
*Hmm… very little. The characters all get squeezed together.*

**莎莎：**对。字的时间太少，声调就出不来了。
Duì. Zì de shíjiān tài shǎo, shēngdiào jiù chū bu lái le.
*Right. Too little time per character, and the tone can't come out.*

**波哥：**哦！所以我说快的时候，声调就不见了。
Ó! Suǒyǐ wǒ shuō kuài de shíhou, shēngdiào jiù bújiàn le.
*Oh! So when I speak fast, my tones disappear.*

**莎莎：**对，声调需要一点时间。所以，办法就是四个字——
Duì, shēngdiào xūyào yìdiǎn shíjiān. Suǒyǐ, bànfǎ jiùshì sì ge zì——
*Yes — tones need a little time. So the trick is four characters—*

**波哥：**说慢一点！
Shuō màn yìdiǎn!
*Say it slower!*

**莎莎：**对。说慢一点，声调就清楚了。
Duì. Shuō màn yìdiǎn, shēngdiào jiù qīngchǔ le.
*Yes. Say it slower, and the tones come out clear.*

**波哥：**说慢一点，声调就清楚了。
Shuō màn yìdiǎn, shēngdiào jiù qīngchǔ le.
*Say it slower, and the tones come out clear.*

**莎莎：**这就是今天最重要的一句话：说慢一点，声调就清楚了。
Zhè jiùshì jīntiān zuì zhòngyào de yí jù huà: shuō màn yìdiǎn, shēngdiào jiù qīngchǔ le.
*That's today's most important sentence: say it slower, and the tones come out clear.*

**波哥：**可是说得慢，别人会不会觉得我笨？
Kěshì shuō de màn, biéren huì bú huì juéde wǒ bèn?
*But if I speak slowly, won't people think I'm slow-witted?*

**莎莎：**不会。说得慢、说得清楚的人，别人听得舒服。
Bú huì. Shuō de màn, shuō de qīngchǔ de rén, biéren tīng de shūfu.
*No. Someone slow and clear is comfortable to listen to.*

**波哥：**说得快、声调乱的人，别人反而听不懂。
Shuō de kuài, shēngdiào luàn de rén, biéren fǎn'ér tīng bu dǒng.
*Someone fast with messy tones is actually harder to understand.*

**莎莎：**对。慢，不是不会。慢，是清楚。
Duì. Màn, búshì bú huì. Màn, shì qīngchǔ.
*Right. Slow doesn't mean incapable. Slow means clear.*

**波哥：**慢，是清楚。我喜欢这句话。
Màn, shì qīngchǔ. Wǒ xǐhuan zhè jù huà.
*Slow means clear. I like that.*

**莎莎：**波哥，你发现没有，今天我们一直在说一件事。
Bōgē, nǐ fāxiàn méiyǒu, jīntiān wǒmen yìzhí zài shuō yí jiàn shì.
*Bo, have you noticed — today we keep coming back to one thing.*

**波哥：**什么事？
Shénme shì?
*What thing?*

**莎莎：**慢。慢慢听，慢慢说。这就是我们的名字：慢慢中文。
Màn. Mànmàn tīng, mànmàn shuō. Zhè jiùshì wǒmen de míngzi: Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*Slow. Listen slowly, speak slowly. That's literally our name.*

**波哥：**如果你也想每个星期慢慢练耳朵，就订阅慢慢中文。
Rúguǒ nǐ yě xiǎng měi ge xīngqī mànmàn liàn ěrduo, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*If you'd like to train your ears slowly every week, subscribe.*

**莎莎：**你不用做声调练习，每个星期来，慢慢听就好。
Nǐ búyòng zuò shēngdiào liànxí, měi ge xīngqī lái, mànmàn tīng jiù hǎo.
*No tone drills needed — just come every week and listen slowly.*

**波哥：**耳朵会替你记住声调。
Ěrduo huì tì nǐ jìzhù shēngdiào.
*Your ears will remember the tones for you.*

### 唯一的小练习 · The only drill

**莎莎：**现在，我们做今天唯一的小练习。四个声调，慢慢说。
Xiànzài, wǒmen zuò jīntiān wéiyī de xiǎo liànxí. Sì ge shēngdiào, mànmàn shuō.
*Now, today's one and only drill. The four tones — slowly.*

**波哥：**好，我准备好了。
Hǎo, wǒ zhǔnbèi hǎo le.
*Okay, I'm ready.*

**莎莎：**第一声，高高的，平平的：妈。妈妈的妈。
Dì yī shēng, gāogāo de, píngpíng de: mā. Māma de mā.
*First tone, high and flat: mā — as in mother.*

**波哥：**妈。
Mā.
*Mā.*

**莎莎：**第二声，往上走，像问问题：麻。麻婆豆腐的麻。
Dì èr shēng, wǎng shàng zǒu, xiàng wèn wèntí: má. Mápó dòufu de má.
*Second tone, rising like a question: má — as in mápó dòufu.*

**波哥：**麻。哈哈，我想吃麻婆豆腐了。
Má. Hāhā, wǒ xiǎng chī mápó dòufu le.
*Má. Haha, now I want mapo tofu.*

**莎莎：**第三声，先下去，再上来：马。小马的马。
Dì sān shēng, xiān xiàqù, zài shànglái: mǎ. Xiǎo mǎ de mǎ.
*Third tone, dipping down then up: mǎ — as in horse.*

**波哥：**马。
Mǎ.
*Mǎ.*

**莎莎：**第四声，往下走，短短的：骂。骂人的骂。
Dì sì shēng, wǎng xià zǒu, duǎnduǎn de: mà. Mà rén de mà.
*Fourth tone, falling sharply: mà — as in to scold.*

**波哥：**骂。没有人打你，没有人骂你——第五集的骂！
Mà. Méiyǒu rén dǎ nǐ, méiyǒu rén mà nǐ——dì wǔ jí de mà!
*Mà. "Nobody hits you, nobody scolds you" — the mà from episode five!*

**莎莎：**哈哈，对。现在慢慢说一次：妈、麻、马、骂。
Hāhā, duì. Xiànzài mànmàn shuō yí cì: mā, má, mǎ, mà.
*Haha, right. Now say them slowly: mā, má, mǎ, mà.*

**波哥：**妈、麻、马、骂。
Mā, má, mǎ, mà.
*Mā, má, mǎ, mà.*

**莎莎：**很好。在家的你，也请跟着说一次：妈、麻、马、骂。
Hěn hǎo. Zài jiā de nǐ, yě qǐng gēnzhe shuō yí cì: mā, má, mǎ, mà.
*Great. You at home — say it after us: mā, má, mǎ, mà.*

**波哥：**说慢一点，不着急。
Shuō màn yìdiǎn, bù zháojí.
*Slowly — no rush.*

**莎莎：**现在用两个真的词试试。买东西的买，第三声：买。
Xiànzài yòng liǎng ge zhēnde cí shìshi. Mǎi dōngxi de mǎi, dì sān shēng: mǎi.
*Now try two real words. Mǎi as in shopping — third tone: mǎi (buy).*

**波哥：**买。
Mǎi.
*Mǎi.*

**莎莎：**卖东西的卖，第四声：卖。
Mài dōngxi de mài, dì sì shēng: mài.
*Mài as in selling — fourth tone: mài (sell).*

**波哥：**卖。
Mài.
*Mài.*

**莎莎：**慢慢说：我买，你卖。
Mànmàn shuō: wǒ mǎi, nǐ mài.
*Slowly now: I buy, you sell.*

**波哥：**我买，你卖。哈哈，买和卖，就差一个声调。
Wǒ mǎi, nǐ mài. Hāhā, mǎi hé mài, jiù chà yí ge shēngdiào.
*Wǒ mǎi, nǐ mài. Haha — buy and sell, one tone apart.*

**莎莎：**对。说快了，买就变成卖了。所以呢？
Duì. Shuō kuài le, mǎi jiù biànchéng mài le. Suǒyǐ ne?
*Right. Say it too fast and buying turns into selling. So…?*

**波哥：**说慢一点，声调就清楚了。
Shuō màn yìdiǎn, shēngdiào jiù qīngchǔ le.
*Say it slower, and the tones come out clear.*

### 快问快答 · Speed round

Answer out loud **before** Bo does.

**莎莎：**好，最后，快问快答！准备好了吗？
Hǎo, zuìhòu, kuài wèn kuài dá! Zhǔnbèi hǎo le ma?
*Okay, speed round! Ready?*

**波哥：**准备好了！
Zhǔnbèi hǎo le!
*Ready!*

**莎莎：**声调是词的帽子吗？
Shēngdiào shì cí de màozi ma?
*Is a tone the word's hat?*

**波哥：**不是。声调是词的一部分。
Búshì. Shēngdiào shì cí de yí bùfen.
*No. The tone is part of the word.*

**莎莎：**声调错了，别人一定听不懂吗？
Shēngdiào cuò le, biéren yídìng tīng bu dǒng ma?
*Wrong tone — does that guarantee nobody understands?*

**波哥：**不一定。场景帮我，整句话也帮我。
Bù yídìng. Chǎngjǐng bāng wǒ, zhěng jù huà yě bāng wǒ.
*Not at all. The situation helps me, and so does the whole sentence.*

**莎莎：**让声调清楚的办法，四个字？
Ràng shēngdiào qīngchǔ de bànfǎ, sì ge zì?
*The four-character trick for clear tones?*

**波哥：**说慢一点！
Shuō màn yìdiǎn!
*Say it slower!*

**莎莎：**整句话呢？
Zhěng jù huà ne?
*And the full sentence?*

**波哥：**说慢一点，声调就清楚了。
Shuō màn yìdiǎn, shēngdiào jiù qīngchǔ le.
*Say it slower, and the tones come out clear.*

**莎莎：**四个声调，用“妈”的音，慢慢说一次？
Sì ge shēngdiào, yòng "mā" de yīn, mànmàn shuō yí cì?
*The four tones on the "ma" sound — slowly?*

**波哥：**妈、麻、马、骂。
Mā, má, mǎ, mà.
*Mā, má, mǎ, mà.*

**莎莎：**太好了，全对！
Tài hǎo le, quán duì!
*Perfect — all correct!*

### 结尾 · Ending

**波哥：**好，今天我们说了：声调不是帽子，是词的一部分。
Hǎo, jīntiān wǒmen shuō le: shēngdiào búshì màozi, shì cí de yí bùfen.
*So — today we learned: tones aren't hats, they're part of the word.*

**莎莎：**说错声调，天不会塌：场景帮你，句子帮你，别人也帮你。
Shuōcuò shēngdiào, tiān bú huì tā: chǎngjǐng bāng nǐ, jùzi bāng nǐ, biéren yě bāng nǐ.
*A wrong tone won't end the world: the situation helps, the sentence helps, people help.*

**波哥：**你慢慢听，耳朵就在免费学声调。
Nǐ mànmàn tīng, ěrduo jiù zài miǎnfèi xué shēngdiào.
*Listen slowly, and your ears learn tones for free.*

**莎莎：**最重要的一句话：说慢一点，声调就清楚了。
Zuì zhòngyào de yí jù huà: shuō màn yìdiǎn, shēngdiào jiù qīngchǔ le.
*The key sentence: say it slower, and the tones come out clear.*

**波哥：**别忘了你的小事：用“慢一点”写一句话，放在评论区。
Bié wàng le nǐ de xiǎo shì: yòng "màn yìdiǎn" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Don't forget your task: one "màn yìdiǎn" sentence in the comments.*

**莎莎：**下一集，我们聊一句很多人听不懂的话：你吃了吗？
Xià yì jí, wǒmen liáo yí jù hěn duō rén tīng bu dǒng de huà: nǐ chī le ma?
*Next episode, a sentence many learners misread: have you eaten?*

**波哥：**你吃了吗？这不是问我吃饭了没有吗？
Nǐ chī le ma? Zhè búshì wèn wǒ chīfàn le méiyǒu ma?
*"Have you eaten?" Isn't that just asking whether I've had a meal?*

**莎莎：**不一定哦。很多时候，它不是问饭。
Bù yídìng o. Hěn duō shíhou, tā búshì wèn fàn.
*Not necessarily. Often it isn't about the food at all.*

**波哥：**不是问饭？那是问什么？
Búshì wèn fàn? Nà shì wèn shénme?
*Not about food? Then what is it asking?*

**莎莎：**下一集告诉你。这句话，比你想的温暖。
Xià yì jí gàosu nǐ. Zhè jù huà, bǐ nǐ xiǎng de wēnnuǎn.
