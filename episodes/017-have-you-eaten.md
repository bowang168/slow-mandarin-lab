## In this episode 这一集

- Why a neighbour asks "have you eaten?" and then just walks away — and why that's friendly.
- The natural replies: **吃了，你呢？** and the soft **还没呢**。
- The honest picture: who says it, who doesn't — and its everyday cousins 下班了？回来了？

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **你吃了吗？——这是打招呼。**
> Nǐ chī le ma?——Zhè shì dǎ zhāohu.
> *"Have you eaten?" — it's a greeting.*

## How to reply 怎么回答

| 句子 | Pinyin | When |
|---|---|---|
| 吃了，你呢？ | Chī le, nǐ ne? | you've eaten — answer and ask back |
| 还没呢。 | Hái méi ne. | not yet — soft and friendly |
| 嗯，下班了。 | Ǹg, xiàbān le. | to "下班了？" — a smile does the rest |

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**波哥：**莎莎，我给你讲一件奇怪的事。
Shāsha, wǒ gěi nǐ jiǎng yí jiàn qíguài de shì.
*Sasha, let me tell you something strange.*

**莎莎：**好，你讲。
Hǎo, nǐ jiǎng.
*Go ahead.*

**波哥：**早上，楼下的阿姨看见我，问：你吃了吗？
Zǎoshang, lóuxià de āyí kànjiàn wǒ, wèn: nǐ chī le ma?
*This morning the auntie downstairs saw me and asked: have you eaten?*

**莎莎：**嗯，很正常啊。
Ǹg, hěn zhèngcháng a.
*Mm, perfectly normal.*

**波哥：**我说：还没有。然后你猜怎么了？
Wǒ shuō: hái méiyǒu. Ránhòu nǐ cāi zěnme le?
*I said: not yet. And guess what happened?*

**莎莎：**怎么了？
Zěnme le?
*What?*

**波哥：**她说了一声“哦”，然后就走了！
Tā shuō le yì shēng "ó", ránhòu jiù zǒu le!
*She said "oh" — and walked away!*

**莎莎：**哈哈哈！
Hāhāhā!
*Hahaha!*

**波哥：**她问我吃没吃，我说没吃，她也不请我吃饭！
Tā wèn wǒ chī méi chī, wǒ shuō méi chī, tā yě bù qǐng wǒ chīfàn!
*She asked if I'd eaten, I said no — and she didn't invite me to eat!*

**莎莎：**哈哈，她不是要请你吃饭。
Hāhā, tā búshì yào qǐng nǐ chīfàn.
*Haha — she was never going to invite you.*

**波哥：**那她为什么问？不想知道答案，为什么要问？
Nà tā wèishénme wèn? Bù xiǎng zhīdào dá'àn, wèishénme yào wèn?
*Then why ask? If you don't want the answer, why ask the question?*

**莎莎：**因为这句话的意思，不在“吃”上。
Yīnwèi zhè jù huà de yìsi, bù zài "chī" shàng.
*Because the meaning of this sentence isn't in the word "eat".*

**波哥：**不在“吃”上？那在哪儿？
Bù zài "chī" shàng? Nà zài nǎr?
*Not in "eat"? Then where?*

**莎莎：**今天我们就聊这句话：它是什么意思，怎么回答，还有哪些话和它一样。
Jīntiān wǒmen jiù liáo zhè jù huà: tā shì shénme yìsi, zěnme huídá, háiyǒu nǎxiē huà hé tā yíyàng.
*Today it's all about this sentence: what it means, how to answer, and its cousins.*

**波哥：**好，我一定要听懂这句话。别走开。
Hǎo, wǒ yídìng yào tīngdǒng zhè jù huà. Bié zǒukāi.
*I have to get to the bottom of this. Don't go away.*

### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。在这里，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Zài zhèlǐ, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*I'm Bo. Here, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**今天是一个文化小课：你吃了吗。
Jīntiān shì yí ge wénhuà xiǎokè: nǐ chī le ma.
*Today is a little culture lesson: "have you eaten?"*

**波哥：**为了楼下的阿姨，开始吧。
Wèile lóuxià de āyí, kāishǐ ba.
*For the auntie downstairs — let's start.*

### 这是打招呼 · A greeting, not a question

**莎莎：**波哥，我先问你：朋友见面，你们说什么？
Bōgē, wǒ xiān wèn nǐ: péngyou jiànmiàn, nǐmen shuō shénme?
*Bo, first: when friends meet, what do you say?*

**波哥：**说“你好”，或者“最近怎么样”。
Shuō "nǐ hǎo", huòzhě "zuìjìn zěnmeyàng".
*"Hello", or "how have you been?"*

**莎莎：**别人问你“最近怎么样”，你会讲一个小时吗？
Biéren wèn nǐ "zuìjìn zěnmeyàng", nǐ huì jiǎng yí ge xiǎoshí ma?
*When someone asks "how have you been", do you talk for an hour?*

**波哥：**不会。我就说“还好”，然后问回去。
Bú huì. Wǒ jiù shuō "hái hǎo", ránhòu wèn huíqù.
*No. I say "not bad" and ask back.*

**莎莎：**对。他真的想听你讲一个小时吗？
Duì. Tā zhēnde xiǎng tīng nǐ jiǎng yí ge xiǎoshí ma?
*Right. Do they actually want the hour-long version?*

**波哥：**哈哈，不想。就是打个招呼。
Hāhā, bù xiǎng. Jiùshì dǎ ge zhāohu.
*Haha, no. It's just saying hello.*

**莎莎：**打招呼，就是告诉对方：我看见你了，你好。
Dǎ zhāohu, jiùshì gàosu duìfāng: wǒ kànjiàn nǐ le, nǐ hǎo.
*A greeting just tells the other person: I see you — hello.*

**波哥：**打招呼。这个词我记住了。
Dǎ zhāohu. Zhège cí wǒ jìzhù le.
*Dǎ zhāohu. Got it.*

**莎莎：**你吃了吗，就是一样的。你吃了吗？——这是打招呼。
Nǐ chī le ma, jiùshì yíyàng de. Nǐ chī le ma?——Zhè shì dǎ zhāohu.
*"Have you eaten" works the same way. "Have you eaten?" — it's a greeting.*

**波哥：**你吃了吗？——这是打招呼。
Nǐ chī le ma?——Zhè shì dǎ zhāohu.
*"Have you eaten?" — it's a greeting.*

**莎莎：**阿姨问你“吃了吗”，意思是：我看见你了，你好。
Āyí wèn nǐ "chī le ma", yìsi shì: wǒ kànjiàn nǐ le, nǐ hǎo.
*When the auntie asks, she means: I see you — hello.*

**波哥：**不是真的问我的早饭。
Búshì zhēnde wèn wǒ de zǎofàn.
*Not actually asking about my breakfast.*

**莎莎：**对。就像“最近怎么样”，问的人不要一个长答案。
Duì. Jiù xiàng "zuìjìn zěnmeyàng", wèn de rén búyào yí ge cháng dá'àn.
*Right. Like "how have you been" — nobody wants a long answer.*

**波哥：**所以她听完就走，很正常。
Suǒyǐ tā tīng wán jiù zǒu, hěn zhèngcháng.
*So walking away after my answer is completely normal.*

**莎莎：**对，招呼打完了，就走了。她不是不喜欢你。
Duì, zhāohu dǎ wán le, jiù zǒu le. Tā búshì bù xǐhuan nǐ.
*Yes — greeting done, off she goes. It doesn't mean she dislikes you.*

**波哥：**好，我明白了。可是我还是想问：为什么用“吃”打招呼？
Hǎo, wǒ míngbai le. Kěshì wǒ háishì xiǎng wèn: wèishénme yòng "chī" dǎ zhāohu?
*Okay, I get it. But still — why greet people with "eat"?*

**莎莎：**这个问题问得好，等一下告诉你。先做一件小事。
Zhège wèntí wèn de hǎo, děng yíxià gàosu nǐ. Xiān zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì.
*Great question — answer coming up. First, one small thing.*

### 小任务 · Your turn

**波哥：**评论区时间到了。
Pínglùnqū shíjiān dào le.
*Comment-section time.*

**莎莎：**对。我现在问你，在家的你：你吃了吗？把回答写在评论区。
Duì. Wǒ xiànzài wèn nǐ, zài jiā de nǐ: nǐ chī le ma? Bǎ huídá xiě zài pínglùnqū.
*Yes. Now I'm asking you at home: have you eaten? Put your answer in the comments.*

**波哥：**可是怎么回答？我们还没学呢。
Kěshì zěnme huídá? Wǒmen hái méi xué ne.
*But how do we answer? We haven't learned that yet.*

**莎莎：**吃了，就写：吃了，你呢？还没吃，就写：还没呢。
Chī le, jiù xiě: chī le, nǐ ne? Hái méi chī, jiù xiě: hái méi ne.
*If you've eaten, write: chī le, nǐ ne? If not: hái méi ne.*

**波哥：**我先来：吃了，你呢？
Wǒ xiān lái: chī le, nǐ ne?
*Me first: I've eaten — and you?*

**莎莎：**我也吃了。你也来回答我们，我们会看，也会回你。
Wǒ yě chī le. Nǐ yě lái huídá wǒmen, wǒmen huì kàn, yě huì huí nǐ.
*Me too. Now answer us — we read and reply.*

### 为什么是“吃” · Why "eat"?

**波哥：**好，现在说说为什么是“吃”。
Hǎo, xiànzài shuōshuo wèishénme shì "chī".
*Okay — now, why "eat"?*

**莎莎：**你想，吃饭是不是每个人每天的大事？
Nǐ xiǎng, chīfàn shì bu shì měi ge rén měitiān de dàshì?
*Think about it: isn't eating everyone's big daily matter?*

**波哥：**是。吃好了，人就舒服，就有力气。
Shì. Chī hǎo le, rén jiù shūfu, jiù yǒu lìqi.
*It is. Eat well and you feel good, you have energy.*

**莎莎：**对。以前的日子，吃饱饭不容易，是每天最大的事。
Duì. Yǐqián de rìzi, chī bǎo fàn bù róngyì, shì měitiān zuì dà de shì.
*Right. In the old days, getting a full meal wasn't easy — it was the biggest thing each day.*

**波哥：**哦……所以问“你吃了吗”，就是问最要紧的那件事。
Ó…… suǒyǐ wèn "nǐ chī le ma", jiùshì wèn zuì yàojǐn de nà jiàn shì.
*Oh… so asking "have you eaten" is asking about the thing that matters most.*

**莎莎：**对。问你吃了吗，就是关心你。
Duì. Wèn nǐ chī le ma, jiùshì guānxīn nǐ.
*Yes. Asking whether you've eaten is caring about you.*

**波哥：**关心……就是把你放在心上。
Guānxīn…… jiùshì bǎ nǐ fàng zài xīn shàng.
*Guānxīn — keeping someone in your heart.*

**莎莎：**说得真好。你吃了吗，等于：我看见你了，我关心你。
Shuō de zhēn hǎo. Nǐ chī le ma, děngyú: wǒ kànjiàn nǐ le, wǒ guānxīn nǐ.
*Beautifully said. "Have you eaten" equals: I see you, and I care about you.*

**波哥：**你吃了吗？——这是打招呼，也是关心。这句话比我想的温暖。
Nǐ chī le ma?——Zhè shì dǎ zhāohu, yě shì guānxīn. Zhè jù huà bǐ wǒ xiǎng de wēnnuǎn.
*"Have you eaten?" — a greeting, and caring too. Warmer than I thought.*

**莎莎：**所以听到这句话，别奇怪，要开心。有人关心你。
Suǒyǐ tīngdào zhè jù huà, bié qíguài, yào kāixīn. Yǒurén guānxīn nǐ.
*So when you hear it, don't be puzzled — be happy. Someone cares about you.*

### 怎么回答 · How to reply

**波哥：**好，现在认真学回答。别人问：你吃了吗？
Hǎo, xiànzài rènzhēn xué huídá. Biéren wèn: nǐ chī le ma?
*Okay, now let's properly learn the replies. Someone asks: have you eaten?*

**莎莎：**最常用的回答，四个字：吃了，你呢？
Zuì chángyòng de huídá, sì ge zì: chī le, nǐ ne?
*The most common reply is four characters: eaten — and you?*

**波哥：**吃了，你呢？
Chī le, nǐ ne?
*Eaten — and you?*

**莎莎：**前面回答他，后面问回去。有来有往，招呼就打完了。
Qiánmiàn huídá tā, hòumiàn wèn huíqù. Yǒu lái yǒu wǎng, zhāohu jiù dǎ wán le.
*First half answers, second half asks back. Back and forth — greeting complete.*

**波哥：**像打球一样，你一下，我一下。
Xiàng dǎqiú yíyàng, nǐ yíxià, wǒ yíxià.
*Like a ball game — your turn, my turn.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，对。那还没吃呢？就说：还没呢。
Hāhā, duì. Nà hái méi chī ne? Jiù shuō: hái méi ne.
*Haha, exactly. Haven't eaten yet? Say: hái méi ne.*

**波哥：**还没呢。
Hái méi ne.
*Not yet.*

**莎莎：**这个“呢”很好用。声音软软的，人和人就近了。
Zhège "ne" hěn hǎo yòng. Shēngyīn ruǎnruǎn de, rén hé rén jiù jìn le.
*That little "ne" is wonderful. It softens the sound and brings people closer.*

**波哥：**还没呢——听起来真的很舒服。
Hái méi ne——tīng qǐlái zhēnde hěn shūfu.
*Hái méi ne — it really does sound cosy.*

**莎莎：**来，我们试一次。波哥，吃了吗？
Lái, wǒmen shì yí cì. Bōgē, chī le ma?
*Let's try. Bo — have you eaten?*

**波哥：**吃了，你呢？
Chī le, nǐ ne?
*Eaten — and you?*

**莎莎：**我也吃了。你看，招呼打完了，就这么简单。
Wǒ yě chī le. Nǐ kàn, zhāohu dǎ wán le, jiù zhème jiǎndān.
*Me too. See — greeting done, that simple.*

**波哥：**再来一次，这次我还没吃。
Zài lái yí cì, zhè cì wǒ hái méi chī.
*Again — this time I haven't eaten.*

**莎莎：**好。波哥，吃了吗？
Hǎo. Bōgē, chī le ma?
*Okay. Bo, have you eaten?*

**波哥：**还没呢。
Hái méi ne.
*Not yet.*

**莎莎：**那一会儿去吃点儿好的。你看，她还是在关心你。
Nà yíhuìr qù chī diǎnr hǎo de. Nǐ kàn, tā háishì zài guānxīn nǐ.
*"Then go get something nice later." See — still caring about you.*

**波哥：**可是，如果我说“还没呢”，她会不会觉得，要请我吃饭？
Kěshì, rúguǒ wǒ shuō "hái méi ne", tā huì bú huì juéde, yào qǐng wǒ chīfàn?
*But if I say "not yet", will she feel she has to invite me to eat?*

**莎莎：**一般不会。打招呼就是打招呼，大家都懂。
Yìbān bú huì. Dǎ zhāohu jiùshì dǎ zhāohu, dàjiā dōu dǒng.
*Usually not. A greeting is a greeting — everyone understands.*

**波哥：**那如果有人真的想请我吃饭呢？
Nà rúguǒ yǒurén zhēnde xiǎng qǐng wǒ chīfàn ne?
*And if someone genuinely wants to invite me?*

**莎莎：**那他会多说一句，比如：走，一起去吃点儿东西。
Nà tā huì duō shuō yí jù, bǐrú: zǒu, yìqǐ qù chī diǎnr dōngxi.
*Then they'll add a sentence, like: come on, let's go get something to eat.*

**波哥：**哦，多的那一句，才是真的请你。
Ó, duō de nà yí jù, cái shì zhēnde qǐng nǐ.
*Ah — the extra sentence is the real invitation.*

**莎莎：**对。记住这句话：你吃了吗？——这是打招呼。
Duì. Jìzhù zhè jù huà: nǐ chī le ma?——Zhè shì dǎ zhāohu.
*Right. Remember: "have you eaten?" — it's a greeting.*

### 谁常说，谁不常说 · The honest picture

**波哥：**莎莎，我还有一个问题。我的中国朋友，好像不常说“你吃了吗”。
Shāsha, wǒ háiyǒu yí ge wèntí. Wǒ de Zhōngguó péngyou, hǎoxiàng bù cháng shuō "nǐ chī le ma".
*Sasha, one more question. My Chinese friends don't seem to say it much.*

**莎莎：**好问题。这句话，不是每个人、每个地方都常说。
Hǎo wèntí. Zhè jù huà, búshì měi ge rén, měi ge dìfang dōu cháng shuō.
*Good question. Not everyone, and not everywhere, uses it often.*

**波哥：**那谁常说呢？
Nà shéi cháng shuō ne?
*So who does?*

**莎莎：**邻居见面常说，年纪大的人常说。
Línjū jiànmiàn cháng shuō, niánjì dà de rén cháng shuō.
*Neighbours say it when they meet, and older people say it a lot.*

**波哥：**邻居，就是住在你旁边的人。
Línjū, jiùshì zhù zài nǐ pángbiān de rén.
*Línjū — the people who live next to you.*

**莎莎：**对。在大城市，很多年轻人打招呼，就说“嗨”，或者“最近怎么样”。
Duì. Zài dàchéngshì, hěn duō niánqīngrén dǎ zhāohu, jiù shuō "hāi", huòzhě "zuìjìn zěnmeyàng".
*Right. In big cities, many young people just say "hi" or "how have you been".*

**波哥：**所以地方不一样，人不一样，习惯也不一样。
Suǒyǐ dìfang bù yíyàng, rén bù yíyàng, xíguàn yě bù yíyàng.
*So different places, different people, different habits.*

**莎莎：**对。可是没关系——你听懂这句话，就够了。
Duì. Kěshì méi guānxi——nǐ tīngdǒng zhè jù huà, jiù gòu le.
*Exactly. But no worries — understanding it is enough.*

**波哥：**哈哈，“没关系”，第五集的老朋友。
Hāhā, "méi guānxi", dì wǔ jí de lǎo péngyou.
*Haha — "méi guānxi", our old friend from episode five.*

**莎莎：**别人这么问，你会回答；别人不这么问，你也不奇怪。
Biéren zhème wèn, nǐ huì huídá; biéren bù zhème wèn, nǐ yě bù qíguài.
*If people ask, you can answer; if they don't, you won't be confused.*

**波哥：**懂了。这是真实的生活，不是书上的一句话。
Dǒng le. Zhè shì zhēnshí de shēnghuó, búshì shū shàng de yí jù huà.
*Got it. This is real life, not a textbook line.*

**莎莎：**波哥，学这样的话，是不是比背课本有意思？
Bōgē, xué zhèyàng de huà, shì bu shì bǐ bèi kèběn yǒu yìsi?
*Bo, isn't learning language like this more fun than memorising a textbook?*

**波哥：**有意思多了。因为这些话是活的，是人和人的关心。
Yǒu yìsi duō le. Yīnwèi zhèxiē huà shì huó de, shì rén hé rén de guānxīn.
*Much more fun. These sentences are alive — they're people caring about people.*

**莎莎：**如果你也想听懂中国人每天真的说的话，就订阅慢慢中文。
Rúguǒ nǐ yě xiǎng tīngdǒng Zhōngguórén měitiān zhēnde shuō de huà, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*If you want to understand what Chinese people actually say every day, subscribe.*

**波哥：**每个星期，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Měi ge xīngqī, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*Every week, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**书上的中文你已经有了，生活里的中文，我们这里有。
Shū shàng de Zhōngwén nǐ yǐjīng yǒu le, shēnghuó lǐ de Zhōngwén, wǒmen zhèlǐ yǒu.
*You already have textbook Chinese — the everyday kind lives here.*

### 下班了？回来了？ · Its everyday cousins

**波哥：**好，最后一部分之前，我要问：还有别的像“你吃了吗”的话吗？
Hǎo, zuìhòu yí bùfen zhīqián, wǒ yào wèn: háiyǒu biéde xiàng "nǐ chī le ma" de huà ma?
*Before the last part: are there other sentences like "have you eaten"?*

**莎莎：**有，我送你两句。晚上，邻居看见你回家，会说：下班了？
Yǒu, wǒ sòng nǐ liǎng jù. Wǎnshang, línjū kànjiàn nǐ huíjiā, huì shuō: xiàbān le?
*Yes — here are two. In the evening, a neighbour sees you coming home: finished work?*

**波哥：**下班了？——这也不是真的问题吧？
Xiàbān le?——Zhè yě búshì zhēnde wèntí ba?
*"Finished work?" — that's not a real question either, is it?*

**莎莎：**对。他看见你拿着包回家，当然知道你下班了。
Duì. Tā kànjiàn nǐ ná zhe bāo huíjiā, dāngrán zhīdào nǐ xiàbān le.
*Right. He can see you walking home with your bag — of course he knows.*

**波哥：**那他为什么还问？
Nà tā wèishénme hái wèn?
*Then why ask?*

**莎莎：**还是那个意思：我看见你了，我关心你。
Háishì nàge yìsi: wǒ kànjiàn nǐ le, wǒ guānxīn nǐ.
*Same meaning again: I see you, and I care about you.*

**波哥：**那我怎么回答？
Nà wǒ zěnme huídá?
*How do I answer?*

**莎莎：**很简单：嗯，下班了。再笑一笑。
Hěn jiǎndān: ǹg, xiàbān le. Zài xiào yi xiào.
*Easy: "mm, finished work" — plus a smile.*

**波哥：**嗯，下班了。
Ǹg, xiàbān le.
*Mm, finished work.*

**莎莎：**还有一句：回来了？
Háiyǒu yí jù: huílái le?
*And another one: you're back?*

**波哥：**回来了？——我人都到家门口了，当然回来了！
Huílái le?——Wǒ rén dōu dào jiā ménkǒu le, dāngrán huílái le!
*"You're back?" — I'm standing at my own front door, of course I'm back!*

**莎莎：**哈哈，对。你就说：嗯，回来了。
Hāhā, duì. Nǐ jiù shuō: ǹg, huílái le.
*Haha, right. You just say: mm, I'm back.*

**波哥：**这些话都一样：问的不是事情，说的是关心。
Zhèxiē huà dōu yíyàng: wèn de búshì shìqing, shuō de shì guānxīn.
*They all work the same: the question isn't the point — the caring is.*

**莎莎：**说得真好。关心，常常看起来像一个问题。
Shuō de zhēn hǎo. Guānxīn, chángcháng kàn qǐlái xiàng yí ge wèntí.
*Well said. Caring often comes dressed as a question.*

**波哥：**你吃了吗？下班了？回来了？都是关心。
Nǐ chī le ma? Xiàbān le? Huílái le? Dōu shì guānxīn.
*Have you eaten? Finished work? You're back? All of them are caring.*

### 快问快答 · Speed round

Answer out loud **before** Sasha does.

**莎莎：**好，最后，快问快答！准备好了吗？
Hǎo, zuìhòu, kuài wèn kuài dá! Zhǔnbèi hǎo le ma?
*Okay, speed round! Ready?*

**波哥：**这次我问你！准备好了吗？
Zhè cì wǒ wèn nǐ! Zhǔnbèi hǎo le ma?
*This time I ask you! Ready?*

**莎莎：**准备好了！
Zhǔnbèi hǎo le!
*Ready!*

**波哥：**邻居问“你吃了吗”，她想请我吃饭吗？
Línjū wèn "nǐ chī le ma", tā xiǎng qǐng wǒ chīfàn ma?
*A neighbour asks "have you eaten" — is she inviting me to eat?*

**莎莎：**不想。你吃了吗？——这是打招呼。
Bù xiǎng. Nǐ chī le ma?——Zhè shì dǎ zhāohu.
*No. "Have you eaten?" — it's a greeting.*

**波哥：**我吃了，怎么回答？
Wǒ chī le, zěnme huídá?
*I've eaten — how do I reply?*

**莎莎：**吃了，你呢？
Chī le, nǐ ne?
*Eaten — and you?*

**波哥：**还没吃呢？
Hái méi chī ne?
*And if I haven't?*

**莎莎：**还没呢。
Hái méi ne.
*Not yet.*

**波哥：**晚上邻居看见你回家，会说什么？
Wǎnshang línjū kànjiàn nǐ huíjiā, huì shuō shénme?
*Evening, a neighbour sees you coming home — what do they say?*

**莎莎：**下班了？或者：回来了？
Xiàbān le? Huòzhě: huílái le?
*Finished work? Or: you're back?*

**波哥：**这些话真正的意思是什么？
Zhèxiē huà zhēnzhèng de yìsi shì shénme?
*And what do they really mean?*

**莎莎：**我看见你了，我关心你。
Wǒ kànjiàn nǐ le, wǒ guānxīn nǐ.
*I see you, and I care about you.*

**波哥：**全对！阿姨们会很喜欢你。
Quán duì! Āyímen huì hěn xǐhuan nǐ.
*All correct! The aunties are going to love you.*

### 结尾 · Ending

**莎莎：**哈哈。好，今天我们说了：你吃了吗？——这是打招呼。
Hāhā. Hǎo, jīntiān wǒmen shuō le: nǐ chī le ma?——Zhè shì dǎ zhāohu.
*Haha. So — today we learned: "have you eaten?" is a greeting.*

**波哥：**里面有关心：我看见你了，我关心你。
Lǐmiàn yǒu guānxīn: wǒ kànjiàn nǐ le, wǒ guānxīn nǐ.
*With caring inside: I see you, and I care about you.*

**莎莎：**回答很简单：吃了，你呢？或者：还没呢。
Huídá hěn jiǎndān: chī le, nǐ ne? Huòzhě: hái méi ne.
*The replies are simple: eaten, and you? Or: not yet.*

**波哥：**还有它的朋友们：下班了？回来了？
Háiyǒu tā de péngyoumen: xiàbān le? Huílái le?
*Plus its friends: finished work? You're back?*

**莎莎：**别忘了你的小事：把你的回答写在评论区——吃了，你呢？还是还没呢？
Bié wàng le nǐ de xiǎo shì: bǎ nǐ de huídá xiě zài pínglùnqū——chī le, nǐ ne? Háishì hái méi ne?
*Don't forget your task: answer us in the comments — chī le, nǐ ne? Or hái méi ne?*

**波哥：**下一集，我们去一个地方。希望你用不到，可是你一定要会。
Xià yì jí, wǒmen qù yí ge dìfang. Xīwàng nǐ yòng bu dào, kěshì nǐ yídìng yào huì.
*Next episode we go somewhere I hope you never need — but you must know.*

**莎莎：**医院。用中文看医生。
Yīyuàn. Yòng Zhōngwén kàn yīshēng.
*The hospital. Seeing a doctor in Chinese.*

**波哥：**啊，生病的时候说不出话，最难受了。
À, shēngbìng de shíhou shuō bu chū huà, zuì nánshòu le.
*Ah — being sick and unable to say it is the worst.*

**莎莎：**下一集：怎么挂号，怎么说哪里不舒服，怎么听懂医生的话。
Xià yì jí: zěnme guàhào, zěnme shuō nǎlǐ bù shūfu, zěnme tīngdǒng yīshēng de huà.
*Next episode: how to register, how to say what's wrong, how to understand the doctor.*

**波哥：**这一集，请一定要听。我们下一集见！
Zhè yì jí, qǐng yídìng yào tīng. Wǒmen xià yì jí jiàn!

Xiàcì jiàn. Mànmàn lái.
