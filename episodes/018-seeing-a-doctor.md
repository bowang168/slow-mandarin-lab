Beginner-friendly slow Mandarin. Every line is shown three ways: **汉字 Hanzi**, pinyin,

> **Pinyin note:** we write 一 and 不 with the tone you actually hear (yí ge, búshì).
> Neutral-tone syllables have no tone mark (shūfu, xiūxi).

**How to use this page**

1. **Listen first** — play the episode once without reading.

3. **Speak** — walk the four steps out loud, then post your 不舒服 sentence.

## In this episode 这一集

- A doctor visit in **four steps**: register, say what's wrong, understand the doctor, buy the medicine.
- The nine-character sentence that tells the doctor **when it started**.
- The repair line for when the doctor talks too fast: **请说慢一点。**

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **我从昨天开始不舒服。**
> Wǒ cóng zuótiān kāishǐ bù shūfu.
> *I haven't been feeling well since yesterday.*

## The four steps 四步

| 步 | 你说 / 你听 | Pinyin |
|---|---|---|
| 1 挂号 | 我不舒服，我要看医生。 | Wǒ bù shūfu, wǒ yào kàn yīshēng. |
| 2 说清楚 | 我从昨天开始不舒服。 | Wǒ cóng zuótiān kāishǐ bù shūfu. |
| 3 听懂医生 | 多喝水，多休息。（听不懂→请说慢一点。）| Duō hē shuǐ, duō xiūxi. |
| 4 买药 | 一天几次？饭前吃，还是饭后吃？ | Yì tiān jǐ cì? Fàn qián chī, háishì fàn hòu chī? |

**从……开始** works everywhere: 我从今天早上开始头疼。 · 我从去年开始学中文。

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**莎莎：**波哥，今天这一集，我希望你用不到。
Bōgē, jīntiān zhè yì jí, wǒ xīwàng nǐ yòng bu dào.
*Bo, today's episode is one I hope you'll never need.*

**波哥：**啊？什么意思？你不想让我听吗？
À? Shénme yìsi? Nǐ bù xiǎng ràng wǒ tīng ma?
*What? You don't want me to listen?*

**莎莎：**不是。我希望你永远用不到它，可是你一定要会。
Búshì. Wǒ xīwàng nǐ yǒngyuǎn yòng bu dào tā, kěshì nǐ yídìng yào huì.
*No — I hope you never have to use it. But you absolutely must know it.*

**波哥：**哦……我知道了。是不是生病的事？
Ó…… wǒ zhīdào le. Shì bu shì shēngbìng de shì?
*Oh… I see. Is it about getting sick?*

**莎莎：**对。在国外生病，你觉得最难的是什么？
Duì. Zài guówài shēngbìng, nǐ juéde zuì nán de shì shénme?
*Yes. When you're sick abroad, what's the hardest part?*

**波哥：**不是难受，是说不出来。哪里不舒服，说不出来。
Búshì nánshòu, shì shuō bu chūlái. Nǎlǐ bù shūfu, shuō bu chūlái.
*Not the discomfort — it's not being able to say it. Where it hurts, and no words for it.*

**莎莎：**对。身体不舒服，话又说不出来，那个时候最想家。
Duì. Shēntǐ bù shūfu, huà yòu shuō bu chūlái, nàge shíhou zuì xiǎng jiā.
*Right. Feeling ill and unable to speak — that's when you miss home the most.*

**波哥：**所以这一集很重要。我们怎么练？
Suǒyǐ zhè yì jí hěn zhòngyào. Wǒmen zěnme liàn?
*So this episode matters. How do we practise?*

**莎莎：**我们把看医生这件事，一步一步慢慢走一遍。一共四步：挂号，说哪里不舒服，听懂医生的话，买药。
Wǒmen bǎ kàn yīshēng zhè jiàn shì, yí bù yí bù mànmàn zǒu yí biàn. Yígòng sì bù: guàhào, shuō nǎlǐ bù shūfu, tīngdǒng yīshēng de huà, mǎi yào.
*We'll walk through a doctor visit slowly, step by step. Four steps: register, say what's wrong, understand the doctor, buy the medicine.*

**波哥：**听起来好多啊。
Tīng qǐlái hǎo duō a.
*Sounds like a lot.*

**莎莎：**不多。最重要的一句话，只有九个字。
Bù duō. Zuì zhòngyào de yí jù huà, zhǐyǒu jiǔ ge zì.
*It isn't. The most important sentence is only nine characters.*

**波哥：**九个字？哪九个字？
Jiǔ ge zì? Nǎ jiǔ ge zì?
*Nine characters? Which nine?*

**莎莎：**等一下你就会说了。别走开。
Děng yíxià nǐ jiù huì shuō le. Bié zǒukāi.
*In a moment you'll be saying them yourself. Don't go away.*

### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。在这里，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Zài zhèlǐ, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*I'm Bo. Here, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**今天是一个真实场景：用中文看医生。
Jīntiān shì yí ge zhēnshí chǎngjǐng: yòng Zhōngwén kàn yīshēng.
*Today's a real-life scene: seeing a doctor in Chinese.*

**波哥：**希望你用不到，可是一定要会。开始吧。
Xīwàng nǐ yòng bu dào, kěshì yídìng yào huì. Kāishǐ ba.
*Hope you never need it — but you must know it. Let's start.*

### 第一步：挂号 · Step 1: Registering

**莎莎：**好。你现在不舒服，到了医院。第一步：挂号。
Hǎo. Nǐ xiànzài bù shūfu, dào le yīyuàn. Dì yī bù: guàhào.
*Okay. You're unwell and you've arrived at the hospital. Step one: guàhào.*

**波哥：**挂号？挂……什么？
Guàhào? Guà…… shénme?
*Guàhào? Hang… what?*

**莎莎：**挂号，就是告诉医院：我来了，我要看医生。然后拿一个号，等着。
Guàhào, jiùshì gàosu yīyuàn: wǒ lái le, wǒ yào kàn yīshēng. Ránhòu ná yí ge hào, děng zhe.
*Registering — telling the hospital: I'm here, I need a doctor. Then you take a number and wait.*

**波哥：**哦，像在饭馆门口拿号等位子一样。
Ó, xiàng zài fànguǎn ménkǒu ná hào děng wèizi yíyàng.
*Ah — like taking a number outside a restaurant to wait for a table.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，对，很像。进门的地方有人帮你。来，我们演一次，我是医院的人：你好，请问看什么？
Hāhā, duì, hěn xiàng. Jìnmén de dìfang yǒurén bāng nǐ. Lái, wǒmen yǎn yí cì, wǒ shì yīyuàn de rén: nǐ hǎo, qǐngwèn kàn shénme?
*Haha, very similar. Someone at the entrance will help you. Let's role-play — I'm the hospital staff: hello, what are you here for?*

**波哥：**啊……我……我不舒服。
À…… wǒ…… wǒ bù shūfu.
*Uh… I… I don't feel well.*

**莎莎：**可以！“我不舒服”，人人都懂。再加一句：我要看医生。
Kěyǐ! "Wǒ bù shūfu", rénrén dōu dǒng. Zài jiā yí jù: wǒ yào kàn yīshēng.
*That works! "I don't feel well" — everyone understands. Add one more: I need to see a doctor.*

**波哥：**我不舒服，我要看医生。
Wǒ bù shūfu, wǒ yào kàn yīshēng.
*I don't feel well — I need to see a doctor.*

**莎莎：**好的，先挂号。请问，第一次来吗？
Hǎo de, xiān guàhào. Qǐngwèn, dì yī cì lái ma?
*Sure — let's register you first. Is this your first visit?*

**波哥：**第一次来。
Dì yī cì lái.
*First visit.*

**莎莎：**好，这是你的号。请坐，等着叫你的名字。
Hǎo, zhè shì nǐ de hào. Qǐng zuò, děng zhe jiào nǐ de míngzi.
*Here's your number. Please sit and wait for your name.*

**波哥：**谢谢……哎，成功了？我挂号了？
Xièxie…… Āi, chénggōng le? Wǒ guàhào le?
*Thanks… wait, that worked? I'm registered?*

**莎莎：**成功了。你看，两句话就够了：我不舒服，我要看医生。
Chénggōng le. Nǐ kàn, liǎng jù huà jiù gòu le: wǒ bù shūfu, wǒ yào kàn yīshēng.
*You are. See — two sentences were enough: I don't feel well, I need to see a doctor.*

**波哥：**我不舒服，我要看医生。挂号原来不可怕。
Wǒ bù shūfu, wǒ yào kàn yīshēng. Guàhào yuánlái bù kěpà.
*I don't feel well, I need a doctor. Registering isn't scary after all.*

**莎莎：**对。不知道怎么办的时候，就说这两句，别人会帮你。
Duì. Bù zhīdào zěnme bàn de shíhou, jiù shuō zhè liǎng jù, biéren huì bāng nǐ.
*Right. Whenever you're lost, say those two sentences — people will help you.*

### 小任务 · Your turn

**莎莎：**现在，请你做一件小事。
Xiànzài, qǐng nǐ zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì.
*Now, one small thing.*

**波哥：**评论区时间到了。
Pínglùnqū shíjiān dào le.
*Comment-section time.*

**莎莎：**对。用“不舒服”写一句话，放在评论区。
Duì. Yòng "bù shūfu" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Yes. Write one sentence with "bù shūfu" in the comments.*

**波哥：**我先来：我今天有点不舒服，想早点睡。
Wǒ xiān lái: wǒ jīntiān yǒudiǎn bù shūfu, xiǎng zǎodiǎn shuì.
*Me first: I'm a bit under the weather today — early night for me.*

**莎莎：**很好。再来一句？
Hěn hǎo. Zài lái yí jù?
*Nice. Another?*

**波哥：**坐了一天，我的背不舒服。
Zuò le yì tiān, wǒ de bèi bù shūfu.
*After sitting all day, my back is sore.*

**莎莎：**好。你也写一句“不舒服”，我们会看，也会回你。
Hǎo. Nǐ yě xiě yí jù "bù shūfu", wǒmen huì kàn, yě huì huí nǐ.
*Good. Write yours too — we read and reply.*

### 第二步：哪里不舒服 · Step 2: Saying what's wrong

**莎莎：**第二步。里面叫你的名字了。
Dì èr bù. Lǐmiàn jiào nǐ de míngzi le.
*Step two. They're calling your name.*

**波哥：**到我了！我进去了。
Dào wǒ le! Wǒ jìnqù le.
*My turn! Going in.*

**莎莎：**医生会先问你：你哪里不舒服？
Yīshēng huì xiān wèn nǐ: nǐ nǎlǐ bù shūfu?
*The doctor will first ask: where does it hurt — what's wrong?*

**波哥：**这个我要好好学。嗯……我头疼。
Zhège wǒ yào hǎohǎo xué. Ǹg…… wǒ tóuténg.
*This part I really need. Hmm… I have a headache.*

**莎莎：**对，头疼，就是头不舒服，头很疼。
Duì, tóuténg, jiùshì tóu bù shūfu, tóu hěn téng.
*Right — tóuténg: your head hurts.*

**波哥：**我头疼。
Wǒ tóuténg.
*I have a headache.*

**莎莎：**还可以说：我发烧了。发烧，就是身体很热。
Hái kěyǐ shuō: wǒ fāshāo le. Fāshāo, jiùshì shēntǐ hěn rè.
*You can also say: I have a fever. Fāshāo — your body is hot.*

**波哥：**我发烧了。
Wǒ fāshāo le.
*I have a fever.*

**莎莎：**医生还会问一个重要的问题：什么时候开始的？
Yīshēng hái huì wèn yí ge zhòngyào de wèntí: shénme shíhou kāishǐ de?
*The doctor will also ask an important question: when did it start?*

**波哥：**啊，时间……这个怎么说？
À, shíjiān…… zhège zěnme shuō?
*Ah, the time… how do I say that?*

**莎莎：**今天最重要的一句话来了，九个字：我从昨天开始不舒服。
Jīntiān zuì zhòngyào de yí jù huà lái le, jiǔ ge zì: wǒ cóng zuótiān kāishǐ bù shūfu.
*Here comes today's key sentence — nine characters: I haven't felt well since yesterday.*

**波哥：**我从昨天开始不舒服。
Wǒ cóng zuótiān kāishǐ bù shūfu.
*I haven't felt well since yesterday.*

**莎莎：**从昨天开始——告诉医生，从什么时候开始的。
Cóng zuótiān kāishǐ——gàosu yīshēng, cóng shénme shíhou kāishǐ de.
*"Since yesterday" — it tells the doctor when it began.*

**波哥：**我从昨天开始不舒服。这句话真有用。
Wǒ cóng zuótiān kāishǐ bù shūfu. Zhè jù huà zhēn yǒuyòng.
*I haven't felt well since yesterday. Such a useful sentence.*

**莎莎：**这个“从……开始”，别的地方也能用。
Zhège "cóng…… kāishǐ", biéde dìfang yě néng yòng.
*And "cóng… kāishǐ" works in other places too.*

**波哥：**比如呢？
Bǐrú ne?
*Such as?*

**莎莎：**看病能用：我从今天早上开始头疼。学习也能用：我从去年开始学中文。
Kànbìng néng yòng: wǒ cóng jīntiān zǎoshang kāishǐ tóuténg. Xuéxí yě néng yòng: wǒ cóng qùnián kāishǐ xué Zhōngwén.
*At the doctor's: my head has hurt since this morning. In your studies: I've been learning Chinese since last year.*

**波哥：**哦！我从去年开始学中文。一个句子，到处能用。
Ó! Wǒ cóng qùnián kāishǐ xué Zhōngwén. Yí ge jùzi, dàochù néng yòng.
*Oh! "I've been learning Chinese since last year." One pattern, useful everywhere.*

**莎莎：**对。来，我们演一次。我是医生：你哪里不舒服？
Duì. Lái, wǒmen yǎn yí cì. Wǒ shì yīshēng: nǐ nǎlǐ bù shūfu?
*Right. Role-play time — I'm the doctor: what's wrong?*

**波哥：**医生，我头疼，我从昨天开始不舒服。
Yīshēng, wǒ tóuténg, wǒ cóng zuótiān kāishǐ bù shūfu.
*Doctor, I have a headache — I haven't felt well since yesterday.*

**莎莎：**发烧吗？
Fāshāo ma?
*Any fever?*

**波哥：**有一点。昨天晚上开始发烧。
Yǒu yìdiǎn. Zuótiān wǎnshang kāishǐ fāshāo.
*A little. The fever started last night.*

**莎莎：**好。你说得清清楚楚，医生就能帮你。
Hǎo. Nǐ shuō de qīngqīng chǔchǔ, yīshēng jiù néng bāng nǐ.
*Good. Say it clearly like that, and the doctor can help you.*

**波哥：**原来最重要的，是说清楚两件事：哪里不舒服，什么时候开始。
Yuánlái zuì zhòngyào de, shì shuō qīngchǔ liǎng jiàn shì: nǎlǐ bù shūfu, shénme shíhou kāishǐ.
*So the key is making two things clear: what's wrong, and when it started.*

**莎莎：**说得好！哪里，什么时候。记住这两个。
Shuō de hǎo! Nǎlǐ, shénme shíhou. Jìzhù zhè liǎng ge.
*Exactly! Where, and since when. Remember those two.*

### 听不懂医生怎么办 · The repair line

**莎莎：**可是波哥，现在有一个真的问题。
Kěshì Bōgē, xiànzài yǒu yí ge zhēnde wèntí.
*But Bo — now for a real problem.*

**波哥：**什么问题？
Shénme wèntí?
*What problem?*

**莎莎：**医生很忙，说话常常很快。
Yīshēng hěn máng, shuōhuà chángcháng hěn kuài.
*Doctors are busy — they often talk fast.*

**波哥：**对！我就怕这个。医生说了很长一段，我一个字都没听懂，怎么办？
Duì! Wǒ jiù pà zhège. Yīshēng shuō le hěn cháng yí duàn, wǒ yí ge zì dōu méi tīngdǒng, zěnme bàn?
*Yes! That's exactly my fear. The doctor gives a long speech and I catch nothing — then what?*

**莎莎：**先记住：听不懂的时候，千万别点头说“好”。
Xiān jìzhù: tīng bu dǒng de shíhou, qiānwàn bié diǎntóu shuō "hǎo".
*First rule: when you don't understand, never nod and say "okay".*

**波哥：**哈哈，我差一点就这样做过。
Hāhā, wǒ chà yìdiǎn jiù zhèyàng zuòguò.
*Haha — I very nearly did that once.*

**莎莎：**听不懂，就说：对不起，请说慢一点。
Tīng bu dǒng, jiù shuō: duìbuqǐ, qǐng shuō màn yìdiǎn.
*If you don't understand, say: sorry, please speak slower.*

**波哥：**请说慢一点——哎，这四个字，上上集刚说过！
Qǐng shuō màn yìdiǎn——āi, zhè sì ge zì, shàng shàng jí gāng shuō guò!
*Please speak slower — hey, those four characters, from two episodes ago!*

**莎莎：**对。上上集是你自己说慢一点，今天是请医生说慢一点。
Duì. Shàng shàng jí shì nǐ zìjǐ shuō màn yìdiǎn, jīntiān shì qǐng yīshēng shuō màn yìdiǎn.
*Right. Then it was you slowing down — today it's asking the doctor to.*

**波哥：**我说慢一点，声调就清楚了；医生说慢一点，我就听懂了。
Wǒ shuō màn yìdiǎn, shēngdiào jiù qīngchǔ le; yīshēng shuō màn yìdiǎn, wǒ jiù tīngdǒng le.
*When I slow down, my tones get clear; when the doctor slows down, I understand.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，对。还可以再加一句：请再说一遍。
Hāhā, duì. Hái kěyǐ zài jiā yí jù: qǐng zài shuō yí biàn.
*Haha, exactly. You can add one more: please say it again.*

**波哥：**请再说一遍。
Qǐng zài shuō yí biàn.
*Please say it again.*

**莎莎：**对。听懂了，再点头。医生也希望你听懂，他是来帮你的。
Duì. Tīngdǒng le, zài diǎntóu. Yīshēng yě xīwàng nǐ tīngdǒng, tā shì lái bāng nǐ de.
*Right. Understand first, then nod. The doctor wants you to understand — he's there to help.*

### 第三步：听懂医生的话 · Step 3: Understanding the doctor

**莎莎：**好，第三步：听懂医生的话。医生看完了，会告诉你怎么办。
Hǎo, dì sān bù: tīngdǒng yīshēng de huà. Yīshēng kàn wán le, huì gàosu nǐ zěnme bàn.
*Step three: understanding the doctor. After the check-up, they'll tell you what to do.*

**波哥：**这一部分最重要了。
Zhè yí bùfen zuì zhòngyào le.
*The most important part.*

**莎莎：**别怕，医生常说的话，就那么几句。第一句：问题不大。
Bié pà, yīshēng cháng shuō de huà, jiù nàme jǐ jù. Dì yī jù: wèntí bù dà.
*Don't worry — doctors mostly use the same few sentences. First: nothing serious.*

**波哥：**问题不大——听到这四个字，人一下就轻松了。
Wèntí bù dà——tīngdào zhè sì ge zì, rén yíxià jiù qīngsōng le.
*"Nothing serious" — four characters and the weight lifts instantly.*

**莎莎：**然后医生常常会说：多喝水，多休息。
Ránhòu yīshēng chángcháng huì shuō: duō hē shuǐ, duō xiūxi.
*Then the doctor often says: drink more water, rest more.*

**波哥：**多喝水，多休息。
Duō hē shuǐ, duō xiūxi.
*Drink more water, rest more.*

**莎莎：**多喝水，就是水要喝得比平时多。
Duō hē shuǐ, jiùshì shuǐ yào hē de bǐ píngshí duō.
*Duō hē shuǐ — drink more water than usual.*

**波哥：**多休息，就是好好睡觉，别工作。
Duō xiūxi, jiùshì hǎohǎo shuìjiào, bié gōngzuò.
*Duō xiūxi — sleep properly, no work.*

**莎莎：**对。有时候医生还会说：我给你开点药。开药，就是写下来你要吃什么药。
Duì. Yǒu shíhou yīshēng hái huì shuō: wǒ gěi nǐ kāi diǎn yào. Kāi yào, jiùshì xiě xiàlái nǐ yào chī shénme yào.
*Right. Sometimes the doctor adds: I'll prescribe you some medicine. Kāi yào — writing down what you should take.*

**波哥：**哦，医生开药，我去拿药。
Ó, yīshēng kāi yào, wǒ qù ná yào.
*Ah — the doctor prescribes, I go pick it up.*

**莎莎：**对。来试一次。我是医生：问题不大。多喝水，多休息。我给你开点药。
Duì. Lái shì yí cì. Wǒ shì yīshēng: wèntí bù dà. Duō hē shuǐ, duō xiūxi. Wǒ gěi nǐ kāi diǎn yào.
*Let's test it. I'm the doctor: nothing serious. Drink more water, rest more. I'll prescribe you something.*

**波哥：**好的，谢谢医生！——这次我全听懂了！
Hǎo de, xièxie yīshēng!——Zhè cì wǒ quán tīngdǒng le!
*Okay, thank you doctor! — I understood every word this time!*

**波哥：**莎莎，说真的，这一集，我想让每个在国外生活的朋友都听一遍。
Shāsha, shuō zhēnde, zhè yì jí, wǒ xiǎng ràng měi ge zài guówài shēnghuó de péngyou dōu tīng yí biàn.
*Sasha, honestly — I want everyone living abroad to hear this episode once.*

**莎莎：**因为这些话，平时不练，用的时候就说不出来。
Yīnwèi zhèxiē huà, píngshí bú liàn, yòng de shíhou jiù shuō bu chūlái.
*Because if you don't practise these sentences in calm times, they won't come when you need them.*

**波哥：**如果你想每个星期练一个真实场景，就订阅慢慢中文。
Rúguǒ nǐ xiǎng měi ge xīngqī liàn yí ge zhēnshí chǎngjǐng, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*If you'd like one real-life scene every week, subscribe.*

**莎莎：**饭馆我们练过了，医院今天练了，下次练别的地方。
Fànguǎn wǒmen liàn guò le, yīyuàn jīntiān liàn le, xiàcì liàn biéde dìfang.
*We've done the restaurant, today the hospital — next time somewhere new.*

**波哥：**先在这里练会，再到外面去用。
Xiān zài zhèlǐ liàn huì, zài dào wàimiàn qù yòng.
*Master it here first, then use it out there.*

### 第四步：买药 · Step 4: At the pharmacy

**莎莎：**好，最后一步：买药。你拿着医生开的单子，去拿药的地方。
Hǎo, zuìhòu yí bù: mǎi yào. Nǐ ná zhe yīshēng kāi de dānzi, qù ná yào de dìfang.
*Last step: buying the medicine. Take the doctor's slip to where the medicine is dispensed.*

**波哥：**医院里面有，外面街上也有药店，对吗？
Yīyuàn lǐmiàn yǒu, wàimiàn jiē shàng yě yǒu yàodiàn, duì ma?
*There's one inside the hospital, and pharmacies out on the street too, right?*

**莎莎：**对。拿到药，别急着走，先问清楚怎么吃。
Duì. Nádào yào, bié jí zhe zǒu, xiān wèn qīngchǔ zěnme chī.
*Right. Once you have the medicine, don't rush off — first ask exactly how to take it.*

**波哥：**对，盒子上都是字，我看不懂。
Duì, hézi shàng dōu shì zì, wǒ kàn bu dǒng.
*True — the box is covered in characters I can't read.*

**莎莎：**所以用嘴问，四个字：一天几次？
Suǒyǐ yòng zuǐ wèn, sì ge zì: yì tiān jǐ cì?
*So ask with your mouth — four characters: how many times a day?*

**波哥：**一天几次？
Yì tiān jǐ cì?
*How many times a day?*

**莎莎：**他们会告诉你：一天三次，一次一个。
Tāmen huì gàosu nǐ: yì tiān sān cì, yí cì yí ge.
*They'll tell you: three times a day, one each time.*

**波哥：**一天三次，一次一个。这个我听得懂！
Yì tiān sān cì, yí cì yí ge. Zhège wǒ tīng de dǒng!
*Three times a day, one each time. That I can understand!*

**莎莎：**再问一句：饭前吃，还是饭后吃？
Zài wèn yí jù: fàn qián chī, háishì fàn hòu chī?
*One more question: before meals, or after?*

**波哥：**饭前吃，还是饭后吃？
Fàn qián chī, háishì fàn hòu chī?
*Before meals, or after?*

**莎莎：**吃饭以前吃，还是吃饭以后吃。问清楚，再吃药。
Chīfàn yǐqián chī, háishì chīfàn yǐhòu chī. Wèn qīngchǔ, zài chī yào.
*Before eating, or after eating. Ask clearly first — then take the medicine.*

**波哥：**问清楚，再吃药。安全第一。
Wèn qīngchǔ, zài chī yào. Ānquán dì yī.
*Ask first, take it after. Safety first.*

### 快问快答 · Speed round

Answer out loud **before** Bo does.

**莎莎：**好，最后，快问快答！准备好了吗？
Hǎo, zuìhòu, kuài wèn kuài dá! Zhǔnbèi hǎo le ma?
*Okay, speed round! Ready?*

**波哥：**准备好了！
Zhǔnbèi hǎo le!
*Ready!*

**莎莎：**到了医院，第一步做什么？
Dào le yīyuàn, dì yī bù zuò shénme?
*You've arrived at the hospital — step one?*

**波哥：**挂号。我不舒服，我要看医生。
Guàhào. Wǒ bù shūfu, wǒ yào kàn yīshēng.
*Register. I don't feel well, I need to see a doctor.*

**莎莎：**医生问：你哪里不舒服？
Yīshēng wèn: nǐ nǎlǐ bù shūfu?
*The doctor asks: what's wrong?*

**波哥：**我头疼，我发烧了。
Wǒ tóuténg, wǒ fāshāo le.
*I have a headache and a fever.*

**莎莎：**什么时候开始的？
Shénme shíhou kāishǐ de?
*When did it start?*

**波哥：**我从昨天开始不舒服。
Wǒ cóng zuótiān kāishǐ bù shūfu.
*I haven't felt well since yesterday.*

**莎莎：**医生说话太快了，怎么办？
Yīshēng shuōhuà tài kuài le, zěnme bàn?
*The doctor talks too fast — what do you do?*

**波哥：**对不起，请说慢一点。请再说一遍。
Duìbuqǐ, qǐng shuō màn yìdiǎn. Qǐng zài shuō yí biàn.
*Sorry, please speak slower. Please say it again.*

**莎莎：**医生说“多喝水，多休息”，是什么意思？
Yīshēng shuō "duō hē shuǐ, duō xiūxi", shì shénme yìsi?
*The doctor says "duō hē shuǐ, duō xiūxi" — meaning?*

**波哥：**水多喝一点，好好休息，别工作。
Shuǐ duō hē yìdiǎn, hǎohǎo xiūxi, bié gōngzuò.
*Drink more water, rest well, no work.*

**莎莎：**拿了药，问什么？
Ná le yào, wèn shénme?
*You've got the medicine — what do you ask?*

**波哥：**一天几次？饭前吃，还是饭后吃？
Yì tiān jǐ cì? Fàn qián chī, háishì fàn hòu chī?
*How many times a day? Before meals or after?*

**莎莎：**太好了，全对！你可以照顾自己了。
Tài hǎo le, quán duì! Nǐ kěyǐ zhàogù zìjǐ le.
*Perfect — all correct! You can look after yourself now.*

### 结尾 · Ending

**波哥：**好，今天我们走完了看医生的四步。
Hǎo, jīntiān wǒmen zǒu wán le kàn yīshēng de sì bù.
*So — today we walked all four steps of a doctor visit.*

**莎莎：**第一步，挂号：我不舒服，我要看医生。
Dì yī bù, guàhào: wǒ bù shūfu, wǒ yào kàn yīshēng.
*Step one, register: I don't feel well, I need to see a doctor.*

**波哥：**第二步，说清楚：我从昨天开始不舒服。我头疼，我发烧了。
Dì èr bù, shuō qīngchǔ: wǒ cóng zuótiān kāishǐ bù shūfu. Wǒ tóuténg, wǒ fāshāo le.
*Step two, say it clearly: I haven't felt well since yesterday. Headache, fever.*

**莎莎：**第三步，听懂医生：问题不大。多喝水，多休息。听不懂就说：请说慢一点。
Dì sān bù, tīngdǒng yīshēng: wèntí bù dà. Duō hē shuǐ, duō xiūxi. Tīng bu dǒng jiù shuō: qǐng shuō màn yìdiǎn.
*Step three, understand the doctor: nothing serious; more water, more rest. Lost? Say: please speak slower.*

**波哥：**第四步，买药问清楚：一天几次？饭前吃，还是饭后吃？
Dì sì bù, mǎi yào wèn qīngchǔ: yì tiān jǐ cì? Fàn qián chī, háishì fàn hòu chī?
*Step four, ask before you take anything: how many times a day? Before or after meals?*

**莎莎：**别忘了你的小事：用“不舒服”写一句话，放在评论区。
Bié wàng le nǐ de xiǎo shì: yòng "bù shūfu" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Don't forget your task: one "bù shūfu" sentence in the comments.*

**波哥：**今天没有下集预告，因为我们想听你的。
Jīntiān méiyǒu xià jí yùgào, yīnwèi wǒmen xiǎng tīng nǐ de.
*No preview today — we want to hear from you.*

**莎莎：**你想让我们聊什么话题？写在评论区。
Nǐ xiǎng ràng wǒmen liáo shénme huàtí? Xiě zài pínglùnqū.
*What topic should we cover? Tell us in the comments.*

**波哥：**我们会先看话题适不适合频道，对学习有没有帮助，安不安全。
Wǒmen huì xiān kàn huàtí shì bu shìhé píndào, duì xuéxí yǒu méiyǒu bāngzhù, ān bu ānquán.
*We'll check fit, learning value and safety first.*

**莎莎：**合适的，我们放进节目单。不能保证每个都做，写一次就够了。
Héshì de, wǒmen fàng jìn jiémùdān. Bù néng bǎozhèng měi ge dōu zuò, xiě yí cì jiù gòu le.
*Suitable ones go on the list. No promises — one comment is enough.*

**波哥：**谢谢你和我们在一起。也祝你身体好，永远用不到今天的话。我是波哥。
Xièxie nǐ hé wǒmen zài yìqǐ. Yě zhù nǐ shēntǐ hǎo, yǒngyuǎn yòng bu dào jīntiān de huà. Wǒ shì Bōgē.
*Thanks for being with us. And here's to your health — may you never need today's sentences. I'm Bo.*

**莎莎：**我是莎莎。我们下期再见。
Wǒ shì Shāsha. Wǒmen xià qī zàijiàn.
*I'm Sasha. See you next time.*

**波哥：**下次见。慢慢来。
Xiàcì jiàn. Mànmàn lái.
*See you next time. Take it slow.*
