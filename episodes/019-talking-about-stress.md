## In this episode 这一集

- A story about Xiaoyu: work, study, family messages — and the heaviest thing of all,
  the three characters **我没事** ("I'm fine").
- Why a stone gets **heavier the longer you hide it** (越藏越重).
- The one sentence that made the evening lighter — and how to answer when a friend says it to you.

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **我压力很大，想休息一下。**
> Wǒ yālì hěn dà, xiǎng xiūxi yíxià.
> *I'm under a lot of stress — I'd like to rest a little.*

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**莎莎：**波哥，我先说一句话，你听听看：我压力很大。
Bōgē, wǒ xiān shuō yí jù huà, nǐ tīngting kàn: wǒ yālì hěn dà.
*Bo, let me say one sentence — listen: I'm under a lot of stress.*

**波哥：**这句话，我在心里说过很多次。
Zhè jù huà, wǒ zài xīnlǐ shuō guò hěn duō cì.
*I've said that sentence in my head many times.*

**莎莎：**在心里说过。那嘴里说过吗？
Zài xīnlǐ shuō guò. Nà zuǐ lǐ shuō guò ma?
*In your head. But has it ever come out of your mouth?*

**波哥：**很少。心里已经很累了，嘴里还是那三个字：我没事。
Hěn shǎo. Xīnlǐ yǐjīng hěn lèi le, zuǐ lǐ háishì nà sān ge zì: wǒ méishì.
*Rarely. Inside I'm exhausted, but out loud it's still those three characters: I'm fine.*

**莎莎：**对，很多人都是这样：心里一座山，嘴里"我没事"。
Duì, hěn duō rén dōu shì zhèyàng: xīnlǐ yí zuò shān, zuǐ lǐ "wǒ méishì".
*Right — so many people are like this: a mountain inside, "I'm fine" outside.*

**波哥：**为什么这句话，这么难说出来？
Wèishénme zhè jù huà, zhème nán shuō chūlái?
*Why is that sentence so hard to say?*

**莎莎：**今天的故事，就是关于这句话的。主角叫小雨。
Jīntiān de gùshi, jiùshì guānyú zhè jù huà de. Zhǔjué jiào Xiǎoyǔ.
*Today's story is about exactly that sentence. Our main character is Xiaoyu.*

**波哥：**小雨最近怎么了？
Xiǎoyǔ zuìjìn zěnme le?
*What's going on with Xiaoyu?*

**莎莎：**这个星期，她被三件事压住了：工作、学习，还有家里的消息。
Zhège xīngqī, tā bèi sān jiàn shì yāzhù le: gōngzuò, xuéxí, hái yǒu jiālǐ de xiāoxi.
*This week, three things are pressing down on her: work, study, and messages from family.*

**波哥：**听起来好重。哪一件最重？
Tīng qǐlái hǎo zhòng. Nǎ yí jiàn zuì zhòng?
*That sounds heavy. Which one is the heaviest?*

**莎莎：**都不是。最重的，是另一个东西。听到最后，你就知道了。
Dōu búshì. Zuì zhòng de, shì lìng yí ge dōngxi. Tīngdào zuìhòu, nǐ jiù zhīdào le.
*None of them. The heaviest thing is something else. Stay to the end and you'll see.*

**波哥：**好，别走开。
Hǎo, bié zǒukāi.
*Okay — don't go away.*

### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。在这里，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Zài zhèlǐ, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*I'm Bo. Here, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**今天是一个虚构的小故事。小雨和小云，都是我们写出来的人。
Jīntiān shì yí ge xūgòu de xiǎo gùshi. Xiǎoyǔ hé Xiǎoyún, dōu shì wǒmen xiě chūlái de rén.
*Today's story is fiction — Xiaoyu and Xiaoyun are characters we made up.*

**波哥：**放心听，我们开始吧。
Fàngxīn tīng, wǒmen kāishǐ ba.
*So listen with an easy heart. Let's start.*

### 三件事压住了小雨 · Three things pressing down

**莎莎：**小雨在一家公司上班，白天的工作很忙。
Xiǎoyǔ zài yì jiā gōngsī shàngbān, báitiān de gōngzuò hěn máng.
*Xiaoyu works at a company; her days are busy.*

**波哥：**晚上呢？晚上总可以休息了吧？
Wǎnshang ne? Wǎnshang zǒng kěyǐ xiūxi le ba?
*And at night? Surely she can rest at night?*

**莎莎：**晚上她还要学习，因为下个月有一个重要的考试。
Wǎnshang tā hái yào xuéxí, yīnwèi xià ge yuè yǒu yí ge zhòngyào de kǎoshì.
*At night she studies — there's an important exam next month.*

**波哥：**白天上班，晚上学习，真不容易。
Báitiān shàngbān, wǎnshang xuéxí, zhēn bù róngyì.
*Work by day, study by night — that's not easy.*

**莎莎：**周末也停不下来。家里人常常发消息给她。
Zhōumò yě tíng bu xiàlái. Jiālǐrén chángcháng fā xiāoxi gěi tā.
*Weekends don't stop either. Her family often sends her messages.*

**波哥：**家里的消息，不是好事吗？
Jiālǐ de xiāoxi, búshì hǎo shì ma?
*Messages from home — isn't that a good thing?*

**莎莎：**是好事。可是每一条，她都想马上回；回得慢了，她就觉得对不起家里人。
Shì hǎo shì. Kěshì měi yì tiáo, tā dōu xiǎng mǎshàng huí; huí de màn le, tā jiù juéde duìbuqǐ jiālǐrén.
*It is. But she wants to reply to every one right away — and if she's slow, she feels she's let them down.*

**波哥：**回消息也要时间，也要力气。
Huí xiāoxi yě yào shíjiān, yě yào lìqi.
*Replying takes time — and energy.*

**莎莎：**对。星期三晚上，小雨坐在桌子前，书打开了，可是一个字也看不进去。
Duì. Xīngqīsān wǎnshang, Xiǎoyǔ zuò zài zhuōzi qián, shū dǎkāi le, kěshì yí ge zì yě kàn bu jìnqù.
*Right. Wednesday night, Xiaoyu sits at her desk, book open — and can't take in a single word.*

**波哥：**为什么看不进去？
Wèishénme kàn bu jìnqù?
*Why can't she?*

**莎莎：**脑子里全是事：工作的事，考试的事，家里的事，全挤在一起。
Nǎozi lǐ quán shì shì: gōngzuò de shì, kǎoshì de shì, jiālǐ de shì, quán jǐ zài yìqǐ.
*Her head is full: work things, exam things, family things — all crowded together.*

**波哥：**我知道那种晚上。躺下以后，脑子还在转，越转越睡不着。
Wǒ zhīdào nà zhǒng wǎnshang. Tǎng xià yǐhòu, nǎozi hái zài zhuàn, yuè zhuàn yuè shuì bu zháo.
*I know that kind of night. You lie down, the mind keeps spinning — the more it spins, the less you sleep.*

**莎莎：**就是这样。她肩膀很紧，胸口很闷，睡也睡不好。
Jiùshì zhèyàng. Tā jiānbǎng hěn jǐn, xiōngkǒu hěn mèn, shuì yě shuì bu hǎo.
*Exactly. Her shoulders are tight, her chest feels heavy, and she sleeps badly.*

**波哥：**这种感觉，中文怎么说？
Zhè zhǒng gǎnjué, Zhōngwén zěnme shuō?
*What's this feeling called in Chinese?*

**莎莎：**压力。"压"，是重重地压住；"力"，是力气的力。
Yālì. "Yā", shì zhòngzhòng de yāzhù; "lì", shì lìqi de lì.
*Yālì. 压 means to press down hard; 力 is the 力 in "strength".*

**波哥：**压力大的时候，人就像被一块大石头压住。
Yālì dà de shíhou, rén jiù xiàng bèi yí kuài dà shítou yāzhù.
*When the pressure is big, it's like being pinned under a big stone.*

**莎莎：**对，做什么都做不动，连喜欢的事都不想做。
Duì, zuò shénme dōu zuò bu dòng, lián xǐhuan de shì dōu bù xiǎng zuò.
*Right — you can't get anything moving, not even the things you love.*

**波哥：**我压力很大——就是说，这块石头太重了。
Wǒ yālì hěn dà — jiùshì shuō, zhè kuài shítou tài zhòng le.
*"I'm under a lot of stress" — meaning: this stone is too heavy.*

### 小任务 · Your turn

**莎莎：**你看，这个词你已经会用了。现在，请你先做一件小事。
Nǐ kàn, zhège cí nǐ yǐjīng huì yòng le. Xiànzài, qǐng nǐ xiān zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì.
*See — you already know how to use the word. Now, one small thing first.*

**波哥：**评论区时间到了。
Pínglùnqū shíjiān dào le.
*Comment-section time.*

**莎莎：**对。用"压力"写一句话，放在评论区。
Duì. Yòng "yālì" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Yes. Write one sentence with "yālì" in the comments.*

**波哥：**我先来：考试以前，我的压力很大。
Wǒ xiān lái: kǎoshì yǐqián, wǒ de yālì hěn dà.
*Me first: before an exam, my stress is high.*

**莎莎：**很好。再来一句难一点的？
Hěn hǎo. Zài lái yí jù nán yìdiǎn de?
*Nice. One more, a bit harder?*

**波哥：**工作太多的时候，我压力很大，想休息一下。
Gōngzuò tài duō de shíhou, wǒ yālì hěn dà, xiǎng xiūxi yíxià.
*When there's too much work, I'm stressed and want a break.*

**莎莎：**太好了，这一句今天还会再见到。你也写一句，我们会看，也会回你。
Tài hǎo le, zhè yí jù jīntiān hái huì zài jiàndào. Nǐ yě xiě yí jù, wǒmen huì kàn, yě huì huí nǐ.
*Perfect — we'll meet that sentence again today. Write yours too; we read and reply.*

### 藏在心里的石头 · The hidden stone

**波哥：**回到故事。小雨跟别人说过她的压力吗？
Huídào gùshi. Xiǎoyǔ gēn biéren shuō guò tā de yālì ma?
*Back to the story. Has Xiaoyu told anyone about her stress?*

**莎莎：**一个人都没告诉过。同事不知道，家里人更不知道。
Yí ge rén dōu méi gàosu guò. Tóngshì bù zhīdào, jiālǐrén gèng bù zhīdào.
*Not a single person. Her colleagues don't know; her family knows even less.*

**波哥：**为什么不说？
Wèishénme bù shuō?
*Why not?*

**莎莎：**她怕家里人担心，也怕别人觉得她不行。
Tā pà jiālǐrén dānxīn, yě pà biéren juéde tā bùxíng.
*She's afraid her family will worry — and afraid people will think she can't cope.*

**波哥：**她是不是想：大家都很忙，别人都可以，为什么我不可以？
Tā shì bu shì xiǎng: dàjiā dōu hěn máng, biéren dōu kěyǐ, wèishénme wǒ bù kěyǐ?
*Is she thinking: everyone's busy, everyone else manages — why can't I?*

**莎莎：**对，她心里就是这么想的。
Duì, tā xīnlǐ jiùshì zhème xiǎng de.
*Yes, that's exactly what she tells herself.*

**波哥：**所以她把压力藏在心里。
Suǒyǐ tā bǎ yālì cáng zài xīnlǐ.
*So she hides the stress inside.*

**莎莎：**藏，就是放在别人看不见的地方。她笑着上班，笑着回消息。
Cáng, jiùshì fàng zài biéren kàn bu jiàn de dìfang. Tā xiào zhe shàngbān, xiào zhe huí xiāoxi.
*To hide — to put it where no one can see. She smiles at work, smiles while replying.*

**波哥：**可是石头藏起来，还是很重啊。
Kěshì shítou cáng qǐlái, háishì hěn zhòng a.
*But a hidden stone is still heavy.*

**莎莎：**而且越藏越重。
Érqiě yuè cáng yuè zhòng.
*And the longer you hide it, the heavier it gets.*

**波哥：**越藏越重……第三集的老朋友，今天听着有点疼。
Yuè cáng yuè zhòng… dì sān jí de lǎo péngyou, jīntiān tīng zhe yǒudiǎn téng.
*越…越… — our old friend from episode three. Today it stings a little.*

**莎莎：**星期五晚上，她的朋友小云打来电话。
Xīngqīwǔ wǎnshang, tā de péngyou Xiǎoyún dǎ lái diànhuà.
*Friday night, her friend Xiaoyun calls.*

**波哥：**小云发现了什么吗？
Xiǎoyún fāxiàn le shénme ma?
*Did Xiaoyun notice something?*

**莎莎：**小云说：你最近说话的声音，好像很累。
Xiǎoyún shuō: nǐ zuìjìn shuōhuà de shēngyīn, hǎoxiàng hěn lèi.
*Xiaoyun says: lately your voice sounds so tired.*

**波哥：**小雨怎么回答？
Xiǎoyǔ zěnme huídá?
*And Xiaoyu's answer?*

**莎莎：**她先说了那三个字：我没事。
Tā xiān shuō le nà sān ge zì: wǒ méishì.
*First, those three characters again: I'm fine.*

**波哥：**又是"我没事"。
Yòu shì "wǒ méishì".
*"I'm fine" — again.*

**莎莎：**小云安静了一下，然后轻轻地问：真的吗？
Xiǎoyún ānjìng le yíxià, ránhòu qīngqīng de wèn: zhēn de ma?
*Xiaoyun goes quiet for a moment, then asks softly: really?*

**波哥：**然后呢？小雨说真话了吗？
Ránhòu ne? Xiǎoyǔ shuō zhēnhuà le ma?
*And then? Did Xiaoyu tell the truth?*

**莎莎：**小雨拿着电话，很久没有说话。
Xiǎoyǔ ná zhe diànhuà, hěn jiǔ méiyǒu shuōhuà.
*Xiaoyu holds the phone and says nothing for a long time.*

**波哥：**她在想什么？
Tā zài xiǎng shénme?
*What is she thinking?*

**莎莎：**她在想：再说一次"我没事"，还是说一次真话？
Tā zài xiǎng: zài shuō yí cì "wǒ méishì", háishì shuō yí cì zhēnhuà?
*She's thinking: say "I'm fine" one more time — or say the truth once?*

**波哥：**说真话，比说"我没事"难多了。
Shuō zhēnhuà, bǐ shuō "wǒ méishì" nán duō le.
*The truth is much harder than "I'm fine".*

**莎莎：**最后，她慢慢地说：其实，我压力很大，想休息一下。
Zuìhòu, tā mànmàn de shuō: qíshí, wǒ yālì hěn dà, xiǎng xiūxi yíxià.
*Finally, slowly, she says: actually, I'm under a lot of stress — I'd like to rest a little.*

**波哥：**说出来了！
Shuō chūlái le!
*She said it!*

**莎莎：**对，就是这一句：我压力很大，想休息一下。十二个字，她用了一个星期才说出来。
Duì, jiùshì zhè yí jù: wǒ yālì hěn dà, xiǎng xiūxi yíxià. Shí'èr ge zì, tā yòng le yí ge xīngqī cái shuō chūlái.
*Yes — that one sentence. Twelve characters, and it took her a whole week to say them.*

**波哥：**小云听了，会不会担心？会不会觉得她不行？
Xiǎoyún tīng le, huì bu huì dānxīn? Huì bu huì juéde tā bùxíng?
*Did Xiaoyun worry? Did she think Xiaoyu couldn't cope?*

**莎莎：**都没有。小云只说：谢谢你告诉我。累了就休息，没关系。
Dōu méiyǒu. Xiǎoyún zhǐ shuō: xièxie nǐ gàosu wǒ. Lèi le jiù xiūxi, méi guānxi.
*Neither. Xiaoyun just said: thank you for telling me. If you're tired, rest — it's okay.*

**波哥：**没关系——第五集的老朋友。
Méi guānxi — dì wǔ jí de lǎo péngyou.
*"Méi guānxi" — our old friend from episode five.*

**莎莎：**对。说错了没关系；累了，也没关系。
Duì. Shuō cuò le méi guānxi; lèi le, yě méi guānxi.
*Right. Getting it wrong is okay — and being tired is okay too.*

**波哥：**小雨听到这句话，是什么感觉？
Xiǎoyǔ tīngdào zhè jù huà, shì shénme gǎnjué?
*How did that sentence feel to Xiaoyu?*

**莎莎：**她说，眼睛一下子就热了。
Tā shuō, yǎnjing yíxiàzi jiù rè le.
*She said her eyes suddenly went hot.*

**波哥：**我懂那种感觉。不是难过，是终于有人看见了。
Wǒ dǒng nà zhǒng gǎnjué. Búshì nánguò, shì zhōngyú yǒu rén kànjiàn le.
*I know that feeling. It's not sadness — it's finally being seen.*

**莎莎：**石头还在，可是好像轻了一半。
Shítou hái zài, kěshì hǎoxiàng qīng le yíbàn.
*The stone is still there, but it feels half as heavy.*

**波哥：**为什么会轻？事情一件也没变啊。
Wèishénme huì qīng? Shìqing yí jiàn yě méi biàn a.
*Why lighter? Not a single thing has changed.*

**莎莎：**因为压力说出来以后，就不用一个人搬了。
Yīnwèi yālì shuō chūlái yǐhòu, jiù búyòng yí ge rén bān le.
*Because once stress is spoken, you no longer carry it alone.*

**波哥：**两个人搬一块石头，比一个人搬轻多了。
Liǎng ge rén bān yí kuài shítou, bǐ yí ge rén bān qīng duō le.
*Two people carrying one stone — much lighter than one.*

**莎莎：**说得真好。这就是朋友最大的用处。
Shuō de zhēn hǎo. Zhè jiùshì péngyou zuì dà de yòngchu.
*Well said. That's what friends are for.*

### 先休息，再想办法 · Rest first, then plan

**波哥：**那个周末，小雨真的休息了吗？
Nàge zhōumò, Xiǎoyǔ zhēn de xiūxi le ma?
*So that weekend — did Xiaoyu really rest?*

**莎莎：**真的休息了一整天。什么都没做：睡觉，散步，喝茶。
Zhēn de xiūxi le yì zhěng tiān. Shénme dōu méi zuò: shuìjiào, sànbù, hē chá.
*A whole day. She did nothing: slept, walked, drank tea.*

**波哥：**不学习，她心里不会不安吗？
Bù xuéxí, tā xīnlǐ bú huì bù'ān ma?
*Not studying — didn't that make her uneasy?*

**莎莎：**一开始会。可是她对自己说：先休息，再想办法。
Yì kāishǐ huì. Kěshì tā duì zìjǐ shuō: xiān xiūxi, zài xiǎng bànfǎ.
*At first, yes. But she told herself: rest first, then find a way.*

**波哥：**先休息，再想办法。先……再……，又是老朋友！
Xiān xiūxi, zài xiǎng bànfǎ. Xiān… zài…, yòu shì lǎo péngyou!
*Rest first, then plan. The 先…再… pattern — another old friend!*

**莎莎：**对。休息不是不做事；休息，是把自己放回第一位。
Duì. Xiūxi búshì bú zuò shì; xiūxi, shì bǎ zìjǐ fàng huí dì yī wèi.
*Right. Resting isn't doing nothing — it's putting yourself first again.*

**波哥：**休息了一天，她感觉怎么样？
Xiūxi le yì tiān, tā gǎnjué zěnmeyàng?
*After a day of rest, how did she feel?*

**莎莎：**她说，肩膀放松了，呼吸也慢了下来。
Tā shuō, jiānbǎng fàngsōng le, hūxī yě màn le xiàlái.
*She said her shoulders relaxed and her breathing slowed down.*

**波哥：**放松，就是不再紧紧的。
Fàngsōng, jiùshì bú zài jǐnjǐn de.
*To relax — no longer wound tight.*

**莎莎：**对。人放松了，办法才进得来。
Duì. Rén fàngsōng le, bànfǎ cái jìn de lái.
*Right. Only a relaxed mind lets solutions in.*

**波哥：**莎莎，这样的故事，听着听着，心里也跟着轻了。
Shāsha, zhèyàng de gùshi, tīng zhe tīng zhe, xīnlǐ yě gēnzhe qīng le.
*Sasha, listening to a story like this, my own heart gets lighter.*

**莎莎：**这就是我们做慢慢中文的原因。
Zhè jiùshì wǒmen zuò Mànmàn Zhōngwén de yuányīn.
*That's why we make Manman Zhongwen.*

**波哥：**如果你也想每个星期，慢慢听一个真实生活的故事，就订阅慢慢中文。
Rúguǒ nǐ yě xiǎng měi ge xīngqī, mànmàn tīng yí ge zhēnshí shēnghuó de gùshi, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*If you'd like one slow, real-life story every week, subscribe.*

**莎莎：**我们慢慢说，你慢慢听——在这里，没有压力。
Wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng — zài zhèlǐ, méiyǒu yālì.
*We speak slowly, you listen slowly — no pressure here.*

### 星期一的小纸条 · Monday's little note

**波哥：**星期一呢？工作和考试还在啊。
Xīngqīyī ne? Gōngzuò hé kǎoshì hái zài a.
*And Monday? The work and the exam are still there.*

**莎莎：**还在。生活没有那么快。可是小雨变了一点点。
Hái zài. Shēnghuó méiyǒu nàme kuài. Kěshì Xiǎoyǔ biàn le yìdiǎndiǎn.
*Still there. Life doesn't move that fast. But Xiaoyu changed, a little.*

**波哥：**哪一点？我很想知道。
Nǎ yì diǎn? Wǒ hěn xiǎng zhīdào.
*Changed how? I really want to know.*

**莎莎：**她给自己写了一张小纸条：一天只做三件事，其他的，明天再说。
Tā gěi zìjǐ xiě le yì zhāng xiǎo zhǐtiáo: yì tiān zhǐ zuò sān jiàn shì, qítā de, míngtiān zài shuō.
*She wrote herself a little note: three things a day — the rest can wait till tomorrow.*

**波哥：**家里的消息呢？
Jiālǐ de xiāoxi ne?
*And the family messages?*

**莎莎：**她告诉家里人：我很好，就是最近有点忙，回得慢，别担心。
Tā gàosu jiālǐrén: wǒ hěn hǎo, jiùshì zuìjìn yǒudiǎn máng, huí de màn, bié dānxīn.
*She told her family: I'm well, just busy lately — if I reply slowly, don't worry.*

**波哥：**这句话很诚实。家里人生气了吗？
Zhè jù huà hěn chéngshí. Jiālǐrén shēngqì le ma?
*An honest sentence. Did the family get upset?*

**莎莎：**没有。妈妈只回了一句：好好吃饭，别太累。
Méiyǒu. Māma zhǐ huí le yí jù: hǎohǎo chīfàn, bié tài lèi.
*No. Her mum replied with one line: eat properly, don't tire yourself out.*

**波哥：**家里人比小雨想的，更容易懂她。
Jiālǐrén bǐ Xiǎoyǔ xiǎng de, gèng róngyì dǒng tā.
*Her family understood her more easily than she'd imagined.*

**莎莎：**对。很多时候，我们怕的事情，比真的事情大。
Duì. Hěn duō shíhou, wǒmen pà de shìqing, bǐ zhēn de shìqing dà.
*Right. So often, what we fear is bigger than what is real.*

**波哥：**所以，下次心里有石头，你也可以说：我压力很大，想休息一下。
Suǒyǐ, xiàcì xīnlǐ yǒu shítou, nǐ yě kěyǐ shuō: wǒ yālì hěn dà, xiǎng xiūxi yíxià.
*So next time there's a stone on your chest, you can say it too: I'm stressed — I need a break.*

**莎莎：**对。压力最怕的，就是被说出来。一说出来，它就开始变小。
Duì. Yālì zuì pà de, jiùshì bèi shuō chūlái. Yì shuō chūlái, tā jiù kāishǐ biàn xiǎo.
*Yes. What stress fears most is being spoken. Once spoken, it starts to shrink.*

### 快问快答 · Speed round

Answer out loud **before** Bo does.

**波哥：**好，最后，快问快答！这次我来答。
Hǎo, zuìhòu, kuài wèn kuài dá! Zhè cì wǒ lái dá.
*Okay — speed round! I'll answer this time.*

**莎莎：**心里像被石头压住，这种感觉叫什么？
Xīnlǐ xiàng bèi shítou yāzhù, zhè zhǒng gǎnjué jiào shénme?
*That pinned-under-a-stone feeling — what's it called?*

**波哥：**压力。
Yālì.
*Stress.*

**莎莎：**压力很大，想停一停，怎么说？
Yālì hěn dà, xiǎng tíng yi tíng, zěnme shuō?
*Stressed and needing to pause — how do you say it?*

**波哥：**我压力很大，想休息一下。
Wǒ yālì hěn dà, xiǎng xiūxi yíxià.
*I'm under a lot of stress; I'd like to rest a little.*

**莎莎：**朋友说他压力很大，你可以说什么？
Péngyou shuō tā yālì hěn dà, nǐ kěyǐ shuō shénme?
*A friend says they're stressed — what can you say?*

**波哥：**谢谢你告诉我。累了就休息，没关系。
Xièxie nǐ gàosu wǒ. Lèi le jiù xiūxi, méi guānxi.
*Thank you for telling me. If you're tired, rest — it's okay.*

**莎莎：**休息以后，用哪句话想下一步？
Xiūxi yǐhòu, yòng nǎ jù huà xiǎng xià yí bù?
*After resting, which sentence plans the next step?*

**波哥：**先休息，再想办法。
Xiān xiūxi, zài xiǎng bànfǎ.
*Rest first, then find a way.*

**莎莎：**肩膀不再紧紧的，人不再紧紧的，叫什么？
Jiānbǎng bú zài jǐnjǐn de, rén bú zài jǐnjǐn de, jiào shénme?
*Shoulders no longer tight, body no longer tight — what's that called?*

**波哥：**放松。
Fàngsōng.
*Relaxed.*

**莎莎：**全对！毕业！
Quán duì! Bìyè!
*All correct — you graduate!*

### 结尾 · Ending

**波哥：**今天的故事：小雨被工作、学习、家里的消息压住了。
Jīntiān de gùshi: Xiǎoyǔ bèi gōngzuò, xuéxí, jiālǐ de xiāoxi yāzhù le.
*Today's story: Xiaoyu, pressed down by work, study and family messages.*

**莎莎：**可是最重的，不是这三件事，是"我没事"这三个字。
Kěshì zuì zhòng de, búshì zhè sān jiàn shì, shì "wǒ méishì" zhè sān ge zì.
*But the heaviest thing wasn't those three — it was the three characters "I'm fine".*

**波哥：**对，这就是开头那个问题的答案：石头最重的部分，是一个人搬。
Duì, zhè jiùshì kāitóu nàge wèntí de dá'àn: shítou zuì zhòng de bùfen, shì yí ge rén bān.
*Right — the answer to the opening question: the heaviest part of the stone is carrying it alone.*

**莎莎：**她说出来的那一句是：我压力很大，想休息一下。
Tā shuō chūlái de nà yí jù shì: wǒ yālì hěn dà, xiǎng xiūxi yíxià.
*The sentence she finally said: I'm under a lot of stress — I'd like to rest.*

**波哥：**压力说出来，就不用一个人搬了。
Yālì shuō chūlái, jiù búyòng yí ge rén bān le.
*Speak the stress, and you stop carrying it alone.*

**莎莎：**别忘了你的小事：用"压力"写一句话，放在评论区。
Bié wàng le nǐ de xiǎo shì: yòng "yālì" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Don't forget your task: one "yālì" sentence in the comments.*

**波哥：**下一集呢？
Xià yì jí ne?
*And next episode?*

**莎莎：**下一集，说一个你天天在做、可是常常做错的事：听懂别人的话。
Xià yì jí, shuō yí ge nǐ tiāntiān zài zuò, kěshì chángcháng zuò cuò de shì: tīngdǒng biéren de huà.
*Next time: something you do every day and often get wrong — understanding what people say.*

**波哥：**听懂还能做错？啊——懂错了！约了明天，他说的是后天。
Tīngdǒng hái néng zuò cuò? À — dǒng cuò le! Yuē le míngtiān, tā shuō de shì hòutiān.
*How can understanding go wrong? Ah — understanding it WRONG! You agree on tomorrow; he meant the day after.*

**莎莎：**对。我们学一句好用的话：你的意思是……吗？一句话，能救你很多次。
Duì. Wǒmen xué yí jù hǎoyòng de huà: nǐ de yìsi shì… ma? Yí jù huà, néng jiù nǐ hěn duō cì.
*Right. We'll learn one useful sentence: "So you mean…?" — it will save you many times.*

**波哥：**《你是什么意思？》，我们下一集见！
"Nǐ shì shénme yìsi?", wǒmen xià yì jí jiàn!
*"What do you mean?" — see you next episode!*

**莎莎：**谢谢你和我们在一起。我是莎莎。
Xièxie nǐ hé wǒmen zài yìqǐ. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Thanks for being with us. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。我们下期再见。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Wǒmen xià qī zàijiàn.
*I'm Bo. See you next time.*

**莎莎：**下次见。慢慢来。
Xiàcì jiàn. Mànmàn lái.
*See you next time. Take it slow.*
