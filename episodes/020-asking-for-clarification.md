Slow, clear Mandarin for adult learners. Every line is shown three ways: **汉字 Hanzi**, pinyin,
and *a short English gloss* — the same three layers you see on screen in the video.

> **Pinyin note:** we write 一 and 不 with the tone you actually hear (yí ge, búshì).
> Neutral-tone syllables have no tone mark (yìsi, dìfang). 差不多 = chàbuduō.

**How to use this page**

1. **Listen first** — play the episode once without reading.
2. **Read along** — replay it with this page open.
3. **Speak** — say the three tools out loud, then post your 你的意思是……吗？ sentence.

## In this episode 这一集

- The invisible trap: **not** understanding makes you ask — understanding it **wrong** doesn't.
- The repeat-back trick that saves you from waiting 40 minutes in the rain at the wrong place.
- Why asking one more question is not stupid — it's polite (and one four-character
  question you should be careful with).

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **你的意思是，我们明天再去吗？**
> Nǐ de yìsi shì, wǒmen míngtiān zài qù ma?
> *So you mean we'll go tomorrow instead?*

## The three tools 三个确认工具

| 句子 | Pinyin | When |
|---|---|---|
| 你的意思是，……吗？ | Nǐ de yìsi shì, … ma? | repeat back what you heard |
| 是不是……？ | Shì bu shì …? | check the key word: time, place, person |
| 我没听错吧？ | Wǒ méi tīng cuò ba? | for the surprising ones |

**Your turn:** write one 你的意思是……吗？ sentence in the comments — we read and reply.

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**波哥：**莎莎，上个星期六，我一个人站在雨里，等了四十分钟。
Shāsha, shàng ge xīngqīliù, wǒ yí ge rén zhàn zài yǔ lǐ, děng le sìshí fēnzhōng.
*Sasha, last Saturday I stood alone in the rain for forty minutes.*

**莎莎：**啊？等谁？
À? Děng shéi?
*What? Waiting for whom?*

**波哥：**等我朋友。我们说好三点见，我三点就到了。
Děng wǒ péngyou. Wǒmen shuōhǎo sān diǎn jiàn, wǒ sān diǎn jiù dào le.
*My friend. We agreed on three o'clock, and I was there at three.*

**莎莎：**他没来？
Tā méi lái?
*He didn't come?*

**波哥：**他来了——在另一个地方。
Tā lái le — zài lìng yí ge dìfang.
*He came — to a different place.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，你们两个人，去了两个地方？
Hāhā, nǐmen liǎng ge rén, qù le liǎng ge dìfang?
*Haha — two people, two places?*

**波哥：**对。他说"老地方见"。我以为是咖啡店，他说的是书店。
Duì. Tā shuō "lǎo dìfang jiàn". Wǒ yǐwéi shì kāfēidiàn, tā shuō de shì shūdiàn.
*Yes. He said "see you at the usual place". I thought the café; he meant the bookshop.*

**莎莎：**"老地方"这三个字，可能是世界上最危险的三个字。
"Lǎo dìfang" zhè sān ge zì, kěnéng shì shìjiè shàng zuì wēixiǎn de sān ge zì.
*"The usual place" — possibly the three most dangerous characters in the world.*

**波哥：**四十分钟啊，莎莎。还下着雨，我连伞都没带。
Sìshí fēnzhōng a, Shāsha. Hái xià zhe yǔ, wǒ lián sǎn dōu méi dài.
*Forty minutes, Sasha. In the rain. I didn't even have an umbrella.*

**莎莎：**其实，只要多问一句话，那四十分钟就不会有。
Qíshí, zhǐyào duō wèn yí jù huà, nà sìshí fēnzhōng jiù bú huì yǒu.
*Actually, one more question and those forty minutes would never have happened.*

**波哥：**一句话？哪句话？
Yí jù huà? Nǎ jù huà?
*One question? Which one?*

**莎莎：**今天我们就学这句话，还有它的两个好朋友。
Jīntiān wǒmen jiù xué zhè jù huà, hái yǒu tā de liǎng ge hǎo péngyou.
*Today we learn that sentence — and its two good friends.*

**波哥：**早十年学会就好了。别走开。
Zǎo shí nián xuéhuì jiù hǎo le. Bié zǒukāi.
*If only I'd learned it ten years earlier. Don't go away.*

### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。在这里，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Zài zhèlǐ, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*I'm Bo. Here, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**今天我们学"确认"：把你听到的，说回去，问一问。
Jīntiān wǒmen xué "quèrèn": bǎ nǐ tīngdào de, shuō huíqù, wèn yi wèn.
*Today's skill is "confirming": take what you heard, say it back, and ask.*

**波哥：**就是把话问清楚，对吗？我们开始吧。
Jiùshì bǎ huà wèn qīngchu, duì ma? Wǒmen kāishǐ ba.
*Getting things clear, right? Let's start.*

### 懂错了比没听懂更危险 · The invisible trap

**莎莎：**波哥，我先问你：别人说的话，你都听得懂吗？
Bōgē, wǒ xiān wèn nǐ: biéren shuō de huà, nǐ dōu tīng de dǒng ma?
*Bo, first question: do you understand everything people say?*

**波哥：**说得慢的时候，差不多都懂。
Shuō de màn de shíhou, chàbuduō dōu dǒng.
*When they speak slowly — I understand pretty much everything.*

**莎莎：**那有没有这种时候：你觉得你懂了，其实懂错了？
Nà yǒu méiyǒu zhè zhǒng shíhou: nǐ juéde nǐ dǒng le, qíshí dǒng cuò le?
*And are there times you think you understood — but understood wrong?*

**波哥：**太多了，一个星期总有一两次。而且这个最可怕。
Tài duō le, yí ge xīngqī zǒng yǒu yì liǎng cì. Érqiě zhège zuì kěpà.
*Too many. Once or twice a week. And it's the scariest kind.*

**莎莎：**为什么最可怕？
Wèishénme zuì kěpà?
*Why the scariest?*

**波哥：**不懂的时候，我会问；懂错的时候，我以为我懂了，就不会问。
Bù dǒng de shíhou, wǒ huì wèn; dǒng cuò de shíhou, wǒ yǐwéi wǒ dǒng le, jiù bú huì wèn.
*When I don't understand, I ask. When I understand wrong, I think I've got it — so I don't ask.*

**莎莎：**说得太对了。误会，就是这么来的。
Shuō de tài duì le. Wùhuì, jiùshì zhème lái de.
*Exactly right. That's where misunderstandings come from.*

**波哥：**误会？这个词我见过，可是说不清楚。
Wùhuì? Zhège cí wǒ jiàn guò, kěshì shuō bu qīngchu.
*误会? I've seen the word but can't quite explain it.*

**莎莎：**误会，就是你懂的，和他说的，不一样。两个人都没错，可是事情错了。
Wùhuì, jiùshì nǐ dǒng de, hé tā shuō de, bù yíyàng. Liǎng ge rén dōu méi cuò, kěshì shìqing cuò le.
*A misunderstanding: what you understood isn't what they said. Neither person is wrong — but the thing goes wrong.*

**波哥：**老地方事件就是：他没说错，我也没听错，可是我们在两个地方。
Lǎo dìfang shìjiàn jiùshì: tā méi shuō cuò, wǒ yě méi tīng cuò, kěshì wǒmen zài liǎng ge dìfang.
*Exactly the usual-place incident: he said nothing wrong, I heard nothing wrong — and we were in two places.*

**莎莎：**对。所以今天的第一个工具是：把你听到的，说回去。
Duì. Suǒyǐ jīntiān de dì yī ge gōngjù shì: bǎ nǐ tīngdào de, shuō huíqù.
*Right. So tool number one: say back what you heard.*

**波哥：**怎么说回去？
Zěnme shuō huíqù?
*Say it back — how?*

**莎莎：**用这个句子：你的意思是，……吗？把你听懂的意思放进去，问一问。
Yòng zhège jùzi: nǐ de yìsi shì, … ma? Bǎ nǐ tīngdǒng de yìsi fàng jìnqù, wèn yi wèn.
*With this sentence: "So you mean … ?" Put in the meaning you understood, and ask.*

**波哥：**给我一个例子。
Gěi wǒ yí ge lìzi.
*Give me an example.*

**莎莎：**好。朋友对你说：今天人太多了，明天再去吧。你怎么确认？
Hǎo. Péngyou duì nǐ shuō: jīntiān rén tài duō le, míngtiān zài qù ba. Nǐ zěnme quèrèn?
*Okay. A friend says: too crowded today, let's go tomorrow instead. How do you confirm?*

**波哥：**你的意思是，我们明天再去吗？
Nǐ de yìsi shì, wǒmen míngtiān zài qù ma?
*So you mean we'll go tomorrow instead?*

**莎莎：**对！他说"是"，你们就说好了；他说"不是"，误会当场就没有了。
Duì! Tā shuō "shì", nǐmen jiù shuōhǎo le; tā shuō "búshì", wùhuì dāngchǎng jiù méiyǒu le.
*Yes! If he says "right", it's settled; if he says "no", the misunderstanding dies on the spot.*

**波哥：**问一句，两个答案都赢。
Wèn yí jù, liǎng ge dá'àn dōu yíng.
*One question — and you win either way.*

### 小任务 · Your turn

**莎莎：**好，第一个工具到手了。现在轮到你了。
Hǎo, dì yī ge gōngjù dào shǒu le. Xiànzài lúndào nǐ le.
*Tool one is yours. Now it's your turn.*

**波哥：**评论区时间！
Pínglùnqū shíjiān!
*Comment-section time!*

**莎莎：**用"你的意思是……吗？"写一句话，放在评论区。
Yòng "nǐ de yìsi shì… ma?" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Write one sentence with "So you mean…?" in the comments.*

**波哥：**我先来：你的意思是，我们不吃辣的吗？
Wǒ xiān lái: nǐ de yìsi shì, wǒmen bù chī là de ma?
*Me first: so you mean we're not having anything spicy?*

**莎莎：**很好。你也写一句，我们会看，也会回你。
Hěn hǎo. Nǐ yě xiě yí jù, wǒmen huì kàn, yě huì huí nǐ.
*Nice. Write yours too — we read and reply.*

### 演一次：明天再去 · Role-play one

**波哥：**写完以后呢？
Xiě wán yǐhòu ne?
*And after you write it?*

**莎莎：**我们来演一次完整的。场景：你和朋友说好，去新开的那家店。
Wǒmen lái yǎn yí cì wánzhěng de. Chǎngjǐng: nǐ hé péngyou shuōhǎo, qù xīn kāi de nà jiā diàn.
*We act out a full round. Scene: you and a friend planned to visit that newly opened shop.*

**波哥：**好，你演我的朋友。
Hǎo, nǐ yǎn wǒ de péngyou.
*Okay — you play my friend.*

**莎莎：**波哥，今天雨太大了，明天再去吧。
Bōgē, jīntiān yǔ tài dà le, míngtiān zài qù ba.
*Bo, the rain's too heavy today — let's go tomorrow instead.*

**波哥：**你的意思是，我们明天再去吗？
Nǐ de yìsi shì, wǒmen míngtiān zài qù ma?
*So you mean we'll go tomorrow instead?*

**莎莎：**对，明天下午两点，老时间。
Duì, míngtiān xiàwǔ liǎng diǎn, lǎo shíjiān.
*Yes — tomorrow at two, the usual time.*

**波哥：**好，明天下午两点见。
Hǎo, míngtiān xiàwǔ liǎng diǎn jiàn.
*Great — see you tomorrow at two.*

**莎莎：**你看，多问一句，误会就没有了。
Nǐ kàn, duō wèn yí jù, wùhuì jiù méiyǒu le.
*See — one extra question, and the misunderstanding is gone.*

**波哥：**要是我没确认呢？
Yàoshi wǒ méi quèrèn ne?
*And if I hadn't confirmed?*

**莎莎：**那你今天下午，可能又一个人站在店门口，等一个不会来的人。
Nà nǐ jīntiān xiàwǔ, kěnéng yòu yí ge rén zhàn zài diàn ménkǒu, děng yí ge bú huì lái de rén.
*Then this afternoon you'd be standing alone at the shop door again, waiting for someone who isn't coming.*

**波哥：**又是四十分钟……我懂了。
Yòu shì sìshí fēnzhōng… wǒ dǒng le.
*Another forty minutes… I get it.*

**莎莎：**这就是确认：多问一句，省四十分钟。
Zhè jiùshì quèrèn: duō wèn yí jù, shěng sìshí fēnzhōng.
*That's confirming: one extra question saves forty minutes.*

**波哥：**多问一句，省四十分钟。这句我要写在手机上。
Duō wèn yí jù, shěng sìshí fēnzhōng. Zhè jù wǒ yào xiě zài shǒujī shàng.
*One extra question, forty minutes saved. I'm putting that on my phone.*

**莎莎：**那句确认的话，再说一遍，让耳朵记住它。
Nà jù quèrèn de huà, zài shuō yí biàn, ràng ěrduo jìzhù tā.
*Say the confirming sentence once more — let your ears remember it.*

**波哥：**你的意思是，我们明天再去吗？
Nǐ de yìsi shì, wǒmen míngtiān zài qù ma?
*So you mean we'll go tomorrow instead?*

### 两个更短的工具 · Two shorter tools

**莎莎：**很好。接下来是第二个工具，更短。
Hěn hǎo. Jiē xiàlái shì dì èr ge gōngjù, gèng duǎn.
*Good. Next comes tool two — even shorter.*

**波哥：**"你的意思是……吗"确实有点长。
"Nǐ de yìsi shì… ma" quèshí yǒudiǎn cháng.
*"So you mean…?" is indeed a bit long.*

**莎莎：**第二个工具只有三个字开头：是不是……？
Dì èr ge gōngjù zhǐ yǒu sān ge zì kāitóu: shì bu shì…?
*Tool two starts with just three characters: 是不是…?*

**波哥：**是不是……？这个我天天都能用。
Shì bu shì…? Zhège wǒ tiāntiān dōu néng yòng.
*"Is it…?" — I could use that every day.*

**莎莎：**对。是不是明天？是不是三点？是不是在门口？
Duì. Shì bu shì míngtiān? Shì bu shì sān diǎn? Shì bu shì zài ménkǒu?
*Right. Is it tomorrow? Is it three o'clock? Is it at the entrance?*

**波哥：**哦，把最容易错的那个词，放进去问。
Ó, bǎ zuì róngyì cuò de nàge cí, fàng jìnqù wèn.
*Ah — you drop in the word most likely to be wrong.*

**莎莎：**对。时间、地点、人——误会最喜欢住在这三个词里。
Duì. Shíjiān, dìdiǎn, rén — wùhuì zuì xǐhuan zhù zài zhè sān ge cí lǐ.
*Yes. Time, place, person — misunderstandings love living in those three words.*

**波哥：**那第三个工具呢？
Nà dì sān ge gōngjù ne?
*And tool three?*

**莎莎：**第三个最好玩：我没听错吧？
Dì sān ge zuì hǎowán: wǒ méi tīng cuò ba?
*The most fun one: did I hear that right?*

**波哥：**我没听错吧？这句什么时候用？
Wǒ méi tīng cuò ba? Zhè jù shénme shíhou yòng?
*"Did I hear that right?" — when do I use it?*

**莎莎：**听到一件让你很吃惊的事情的时候。
Tīngdào yí jiàn ràng nǐ hěn chījīng de shìqing de shíhou.
*When you hear something that really surprises you.*

**波哥：**比如：明天不用上班？我没听错吧？
Bǐrú: míngtiān búyòng shàngbān? Wǒ méi tīng cuò ba?
*Like: no work tomorrow? Did I hear that right?*

**莎莎：**哈哈，对，就这么用。它一半是确认，一半是开心。
Hāhā, duì, jiù zhème yòng. Tā yíbàn shì quèrèn, yíbàn shì kāixīn.
*Haha, exactly. It's half confirmation, half joy.*

### 回到那个星期六 · Replay the Saturday

**波哥：**好，三个工具都有了。再演一次吧。
Hǎo, sān ge gōngjù dōu yǒu le. Zài yǎn yí cì ba.
*All three tools in hand. Let's act it out again.*

**莎莎：**好。这次，我们回到那个下雨的星期六，给你一次重来的机会。
Hǎo. Zhè cì, wǒmen huídào nàge xiàyǔ de xīngqīliù, gěi nǐ yí cì chóng lái de jīhuì.
*Okay. This time we go back to that rainy Saturday — you get a do-over.*

**波哥：**这次我不会再站在雨里了。
Zhè cì wǒ bú huì zài zhàn zài yǔ lǐ le.
*This time I won't be standing in the rain.*

**莎莎：**波哥，明天老地方见，三点。
Bōgē, míngtiān lǎo dìfang jiàn, sān diǎn.
*Bo, see you at the usual place tomorrow, three o'clock.*

**波哥：**等等——老地方，是不是书店旁边那家咖啡店？
Děngdeng — lǎo dìfang, shì bu shì shūdiàn pángbiān nà jiā kāfēidiàn?
*Wait — the usual place: is that the café next to the bookshop?*

**莎莎：**不是，是书店里面的小桌子，我们上次坐过的那张。
Búshì, shì shūdiàn lǐmiàn de xiǎo zhuōzi, wǒmen shàng cì zuò guò de nà zhāng.
*No — the little table inside the bookshop, the one we sat at last time.*

**波哥：**你看，又不一样！我没听错吧？书店里面？
Nǐ kàn, yòu bù yíyàng! Wǒ méi tīng cuò ba? Shūdiàn lǐmiàn?
*See — different again! Did I hear that right? Inside the bookshop?*

**莎莎：**对，书店里面，最里面那张桌子。
Duì, shūdiàn lǐmiàn, zuì lǐmiàn nà zhāng zhuōzi.
*Yes — inside, the table right at the back.*

**波哥：**好，明天三点，书店里面见。
Hǎo, míngtiān sān diǎn, shūdiàn lǐmiàn jiàn.
*Great — tomorrow at three, inside the bookshop.*

**莎莎：**这一次，两个人坐在一起喝咖啡，没有人站在雨里。
Zhè yí cì, liǎng ge rén zuò zài yìqǐ hē kāfēi, méiyǒu rén zhàn zài yǔ lǐ.
*This time, two people drink coffee together — nobody stands in the rain.*

**波哥：**多问了两句，救了一个下午。
Duō wèn le liǎng jù, jiù le yí ge xiàwǔ.
*Two extra questions saved a whole afternoon.*

**莎莎：**对。确认不花钱，误会才贵。
Duì. Quèrèn bù huā qián, wùhuì cái guì.
*Right. Confirming costs nothing — it's the misunderstanding that's expensive.*

### 问清楚是礼貌 · Asking is polite

**波哥：**可是莎莎，我有一个担心。
Kěshì Shāsha, wǒ yǒu yí ge dānxīn.
*But Sasha, one worry.*

**莎莎：**你说，我听着。
Nǐ shuō, wǒ tīng zhe.
*Go on, I'm listening.*

**波哥：**问这么多，别人会不会觉得我笨？会不会觉得我烦？
Wèn zhème duō, biéren huì bu huì juéde wǒ bèn? Huì bu huì juéde wǒ fán?
*If I ask so much, will people think I'm slow? Or annoying?*

**莎莎：**不会，正好相反。你想想：一个认真听你说话的人，和一个随便点头的人，你喜欢哪个？
Bú huì, zhènghǎo xiāngfǎn. Nǐ xiǎngxiang: yí ge rènzhēn tīng nǐ shuōhuà de rén, hé yí ge suíbiàn diǎntóu de rén, nǐ xǐhuan nǎge?
*No — the opposite. Think: someone who really listens to you, or someone who just nods along — which do you prefer?*

**波哥：**当然是认真的那个。随便点头的人，其实没在听。
Dāngrán shì rènzhēn de nàge. Suíbiàn diǎntóu de rén, qíshí méi zài tīng.
*The attentive one, of course. The nodder isn't really listening.*

**莎莎：**确认，就是告诉对方：你的话，对我很重要。
Quèrèn, jiùshì gàosu duìfāng: nǐ de huà, duì wǒ hěn zhòngyào.
*Confirming tells the other person: your words matter to me.*

**波哥：**哦，原来问清楚，不是笨，是一种礼貌。
Ó, yuánlái wèn qīngchu, búshì bèn, shì yì zhǒng lǐmào.
*Ah — so asking clearly isn't slow. It's a kind of politeness.*

**莎莎：**对。中国人自己也天天这么问，你在电话里就能听到：你是说星期五吗？
Duì. Zhōngguórén zìjǐ yě tiāntiān zhème wèn, nǐ zài diànhuà lǐ jiù néng tīngdào: nǐ shì shuō xīngqīwǔ ma?
*Right. Chinese speakers do it every day — listen to any phone call: "You mean Friday?"*

**波哥：**原来大家都在用，只有课本不教。
Yuánlái dàjiā dōu zài yòng, zhǐ yǒu kèběn bù jiāo.
*So everyone uses it — only the textbooks skip it.*

**莎莎：**所以别怕问。慢慢地问，笑着问，谁都喜欢。
Suǒyǐ bié pà wèn. Mànmàn de wèn, xiào zhe wèn, shéi dōu xǐhuan.
*So don't be afraid to ask. Ask slowly, ask with a smile — everyone likes that.*

**波哥：**这种马上能用的中文，为什么以前没有人教我？
Zhè zhǒng mǎshàng néng yòng de Zhōngwén, wèishénme yǐqián méiyǒu rén jiāo wǒ?
*Why did nobody teach me this instantly usable Chinese before?*

**莎莎：**课本教句子，我们练真实的对话。
Kèběn jiāo jùzi, wǒmen liàn zhēnshí de duìhuà.
*Textbooks teach sentences; we practise real conversations.*

**波哥：**如果你也想每个星期，练这种一开口就有用的中文，就订阅慢慢中文。
Rúguǒ nǐ yě xiǎng měi ge xīngqī, liàn zhè zhǒng yì kāikǒu jiù yǒuyòng de Zhōngwén, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*If you'd like this kind of instantly useful Chinese every week, subscribe.*

**莎莎：**下次别人一开口，你就知道怎么接，也知道怎么问。
Xiàcì biéren yì kāikǒu, nǐ jiù zhīdào zěnme jiē, yě zhīdào zěnme wèn.
*Next time someone speaks, you'll know how to respond — and how to ask.*

### 小提醒："你什么意思？" · One warning

**波哥：**说到接话，我还有一个问题。
Shuō dào jiē huà, wǒ hái yǒu yí ge wèntí.
*Speaking of responding — one more question.*

**莎莎：**你说。
Nǐ shuō.
*Go ahead.*

**波哥：**我可以直接问"你什么意思？"吗？意思差不多啊。
Wǒ kěyǐ zhíjiē wèn "nǐ shénme yìsi?" ma? Yìsi chàbuduō a.
*Can I just ask "What do you mean?" — it means about the same, right?*

**莎莎：**小心！字差不多，语气完全不一样。"你什么意思？"听起来像生气了。
Xiǎoxīn! Zì chàbuduō, yǔqì wánquán bù yíyàng. "Nǐ shénme yìsi?" tīng qǐlái xiàng shēngqì le.
*Careful! Almost the same characters, completely different tone. "What do you mean?!" sounds angry.*

**波哥：**啊，对！朋友这样问我，我会以为他不高兴了。
À, duì! Péngyou zhèyàng wèn wǒ, wǒ huì yǐwéi tā bù gāoxìng le.
*Ah, true! If a friend asked me that, I'd think they were upset.*

**莎莎：**语气，就是说话的样子。同一句话，可以是问，也可以是生气。
Yǔqì, jiùshì shuōhuà de yàngzi. Tóng yí jù huà, kěyǐ shì wèn, yě kěyǐ shì shēngqì.
*Tone is how a sentence is carried. The same words can be a question — or anger.*

**波哥：**那怎么问最安全？
Nà zěnme wèn zuì ānquán?
*So what's the safest way to ask?*

**莎莎：**把你听到的说出来，最后加一个"吗"，再加一点笑。
Bǎ nǐ tīngdào de shuō chūlái, zuìhòu jiā yí ge "ma", zài jiā yìdiǎn xiào.
*Say what you heard, add a "ma" at the end — and add a little smile.*

**波哥：**学会了：问要问清楚，脸要笑一笑。
Xuéhuì le: wèn yào wèn qīngchu, liǎn yào xiào yi xiào.
*Got it: ask clearly, and let the face smile.*

### 快问快答 · Speed round

Answer out loud **before** Bo does.

**莎莎：**好，最后，快问快答！准备好了吗？
Hǎo, zuìhòu, kuài wèn kuài dá! Zhǔnbèi hǎo le ma?
*Okay, speed round! Ready?*

**波哥：**准备好了！
Zhǔnbèi hǎo le!
*Ready!*

**莎莎：**朋友说"明天再去吧"，你怎么确认？
Péngyou shuō "míngtiān zài qù ba", nǐ zěnme quèrèn?
*A friend says "let's go tomorrow instead" — how do you confirm?*

**波哥：**你的意思是，我们明天再去吗？
Nǐ de yìsi shì, wǒmen míngtiān zài qù ma?
*So you mean we'll go tomorrow instead?*

**莎莎：**想确认时间，短一点怎么问？
Xiǎng quèrèn shíjiān, duǎn yìdiǎn zěnme wèn?
*To check the time, the short way?*

**波哥：**是不是三点？
Shì bu shì sān diǎn?
*Is it three o'clock?*

**莎莎：**听到很吃惊的事，怎么说？
Tīngdào hěn chījīng de shì, zěnme shuō?
*And for surprising news?*

**波哥：**我没听错吧？
Wǒ méi tīng cuò ba?
*Did I hear that right?*

**莎莎：**"你什么意思？"这四个字要小心，为什么？
"Nǐ shénme yìsi?" zhè sì ge zì yào xiǎoxīn, wèishénme?
*Why be careful with "What do you mean?!"*

**波哥：**因为语气不对，听起来像生气了。
Yīnwèi yǔqì bú duì, tīng qǐlái xiàng shēngqì le.
*Because with the wrong tone, it sounds angry.*

**莎莎：**确认是笨，还是礼貌？
Quèrèn shì bèn, háishì lǐmào?
*Is confirming slow-witted — or polite?*

**波哥：**是礼貌。认真听的人，才会问。
Shì lǐmào. Rènzhēn tīng de rén, cái huì wèn.
*Polite. Only real listeners ask.*

**莎莎：**多问一句，省什么？
Duō wèn yí jù, shěng shénme?
*One extra question saves…?*

**波哥：**省四十分钟！
Shěng sìshí fēnzhōng!
*Forty minutes!*

### 结尾 · Ending

**莎莎：**全对，毕业！今天我们学了三个确认工具。
Quán duì, bìyè! Jīntiān wǒmen xué le sān ge quèrèn gōngjù.
*All correct — you graduate! Three confirming tools today.*

**波哥：**第一个：你的意思是……吗？朋友说"明天再去吧"，我就问：你的意思是，我们明天再去吗？
Dì yī ge: nǐ de yìsi shì… ma? Péngyou shuō "míngtiān zài qù ba", wǒ jiù wèn: nǐ de yìsi shì, wǒmen míngtiān zài qù ma?
*One: "So you mean…?" — friend says "tomorrow instead", I ask: so you mean we'll go tomorrow?*

**莎莎：**第二个：是不是……？问最容易错的那个词——时间、地点、人。
Dì èr ge: shì bu shì…? Wèn zuì róngyì cuò de nàge cí — shíjiān, dìdiǎn, rén.
*Two: "Is it…?" — check the riskiest word: time, place, person.*

**波哥：**第三个：我没听错吧？留给吃惊的时候。
Dì sān ge: wǒ méi tīng cuò ba? Liú gěi chījīng de shíhou.
*Three: "Did I hear that right?" — saved for the surprises.*

**莎莎：**懂错了不可怕，不问才可怕。
Dǒng cuò le bù kěpà, bú wèn cái kěpà.
*Understanding wrong isn't scary. Not asking is.*

**波哥：**别忘了你的小事：用"你的意思是……吗？"写一句话，放在评论区。
Bié wàng le nǐ de xiǎo shì: yòng "nǐ de yìsi shì… ma?" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Don't forget your task: one "So you mean…?" sentence in the comments.*

**莎莎：**对了波哥，今天你说"差不多都懂"的时候，我笑了一下，你发现了吗？
Duì le Bōgē, jīntiān nǐ shuō "chàbuduō dōu dǒng" de shíhou, wǒ xiào le yíxià, nǐ fāxiàn le ma?
*By the way, Bo — when you said "I understand chàbuduō everything", I smiled. Did you notice?*

**波哥：**发现了。你笑什么？我说错了吗？
Fāxiàn le. Nǐ xiào shénme? Wǒ shuō cuò le ma?
*I did. What's funny? Did I say it wrong?*

**莎莎：**因为"差不多"这个词，太有意思了，值得讲一整集。
Yīnwèi "chàbuduō" zhège cí, tài yǒu yìsi le, zhíde jiǎng yì zhěng jí.
*Because 差不多 is such a fascinating word, it deserves a whole episode.*

**波哥：**一个词，讲一整集？
Yí ge cí, jiǎng yì zhěng jí?
*One word — one whole episode?*

**莎莎：**有人用它说时间，有人用它说钱，还有人用它过日子。
Yǒu rén yòng tā shuō shíjiān, yǒu rén yòng tā shuō qián, hái yǒu rén yòng tā guò rìzi.
*Some use it for time, some for money — and some live their whole life by it.*

**波哥：**过日子？！好，下一集《差不多》，我们下一集见！
Guò rìzi?! Hǎo, xià yì jí "chàbuduō", wǒmen xià yì jí jiàn!
*Live by it?! Next episode: 差不多 — see you then!*

**莎莎：**谢谢你和我们在一起。我是莎莎。
Xièxie nǐ hé wǒmen zài yìqǐ. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Thanks for being with us. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。我们下期再见。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Wǒmen xià qī zàijiàn.
*I'm Bo. See you next time.*

**莎莎：**下次见。慢慢来。
Xiàcì jiàn. Mànmàn lái.
*See you next time. Take it slow.*
