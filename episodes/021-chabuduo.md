> **Pinyin note:** we write 一 and 不 with the tone you actually hear (yí ge, búshì).
> Neutral-tone syllables have no tone mark (tàidu, dāying). 差不多 = chàbuduō.

## In this episode 这一集

- Take the word apart: 差 (what's missing) + 不多 (is not much) → **almost**.
- Its three everyday uses — and why 差不多 is **not** the same as 一样.
- The honest part: when "good enough" is a kindness, and the **two places you should
  never use it** (numbers, and things you promised).

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **差不多，就是还差一点。**
> Chàbuduō, jiùshì hái chà yìdiǎn.
> *"Chàbuduō" means: what's missing is only a little.*

## The three uses 三个用法

| 句子 | Pinyin | Use |
|---|---|---|
| 时间差不多了。 | Shíjiān chàbuduō le. | almost — it's about time |
| 我们俩差不多高。 | Wǒmen liǎ chàbuduō gāo. | about the same |
| 差不多就行。 | Chàbuduō jiù xíng. | good enough — but not for numbers or promises |

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**莎莎：**波哥，我们玩一个游戏。我问，你快快地答，别想太久。
Bōgē, wǒmen wán yí ge yóuxì. Wǒ wèn, nǐ kuàikuài de dá, bié xiǎng tài jiǔ.
*Bo, let's play a game. I ask, you answer fast — no thinking too long.*

**波哥：**好，来！我最喜欢玩游戏了。
Hǎo, lái! Wǒ zuì xǐhuan wán yóuxì le.
*Great, go! I love games.*

**莎莎：**饭好了吗？
Fàn hǎo le ma?
*Is dinner ready?*

**波哥：**差不多了。
Chàbuduō le.
*Almost.*

**莎莎：**你和你哥哥，谁高？
Nǐ hé nǐ gēge, shéi gāo?
*You or your brother — who's taller?*

**波哥：**我们俩差不多。
Wǒmen liǎ chàbuduō.
*We're about the same.*

**莎莎：**你的作业写完了吗？
Nǐ de zuòyè xiě wán le ma?
*Is your homework finished?*

**波哥：**嗯……差不多就行了吧。
Ǹg… chàbuduō jiù xíng le ba.
*Hmm… it's good enough, I guess.*

**莎莎：**停！波哥，你刚才说了三次"差不多"。
Tíng! Bōgē, nǐ gāngcái shuō le sān cì "chàbuduō".
*Stop! Bo, you just said "chàbuduō" three times.*

**波哥：**啊？有吗？我自己都没发现。
À? Yǒu ma? Wǒ zìjǐ dōu méi fāxiàn.
*Did I? I didn't even notice.*

**莎莎：**有。而且这三次的意思，一次和一次不一样。
Yǒu. Érqiě zhè sān cì de yìsi, yí cì hé yí cì bù yíyàng.
*You did. And each time it meant something different.*

**波哥：**不一样？差不多就是差不多啊。
Bù yíyàng? Chàbuduō jiùshì chàbuduō a.
*Different? Chàbuduō is just… chàbuduō.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，你看，第四次。今天我们就拆开这个词。
Hāhā, nǐ kàn, dì sì cì. Jīntiān wǒmen jiù chāikāi zhège cí.
*Haha — see, that's four. Today we take this word apart.*

**波哥：**好啊。拆完我就懂了吗？
Hǎo a. Chāi wán wǒ jiù dǒng le ma?
*Fine. And once it's apart, I'll understand it?*

**莎莎：**懂。最后我还要告诉你：有两个地方，千万不要用它。
Dǒng. Zuìhòu wǒ hái yào gàosu nǐ: yǒu liǎng ge dìfang, qiānwàn búyào yòng tā.
*You will. And at the end I'll tell you: there are two places you must never use it.*

**波哥：**哪两个地方？
Nǎ liǎng ge dìfang?
*Which two?*

**莎莎：**先不说，说了你就不听到最后了。别走开。
Xiān bù shuō, shuō le nǐ jiù bù tīngdào zuìhòu le. Bié zǒukāi.
*Not yet — or you won't stay to the end. Don't go away.*

### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。在这里，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Zài zhèlǐ, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*I'm Bo. Here, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**今天只讲一个词：差不多。
Jīntiān zhǐ jiǎng yí ge cí: chàbuduō.
*Today, just one word: chàbuduō.*

**波哥：**一个词，讲一集？值得吗？
Yí ge cí, jiǎng yì jí? Zhíde ma?
*One word, one whole episode? Is it worth it?*

### 拆开这个词 · Taking the word apart

**莎莎：**听完你就知道，它可能是中文里最有意思的一个词。我们先把它拆开：差、不、多，三个字。
Tīng wán nǐ jiù zhīdào, tā kěnéng shì Zhōngwén lǐ zuì yǒu yìsi de yí ge cí. Wǒmen xiān bǎ tā chāikāi: chà, bù, duō, sān ge zì.
*You'll see — it may be the most interesting word in Chinese. First, take it apart: 差, 不, 多 — three characters.*

**波哥：**差，是什么意思？
Chà, shì shénme yìsi?
*What does 差 mean?*

**莎莎：**差，就是还少一点，还不够。
Chà, jiùshì hái shǎo yìdiǎn, hái bú gòu.
*差 means a bit is still missing — not quite enough yet.*

**波哥：**比如呢？给我一个看得见的例子。
Bǐrú ne? Gěi wǒ yí ge kàn de jiàn de lìzi.
*For example? Give me one I can see.*

**莎莎：**好。你倒一杯水，倒到快满的时候停下来——这杯水，差一点就满了。
Hǎo. Nǐ dào yì bēi shuǐ, dào dào kuài mǎn de shíhou tíng xiàlái — zhè bēi shuǐ, chà yìdiǎn jiù mǎn le.
*Okay. Pour a glass of water and stop just before the top — that glass is one tiny bit short of full.*

**波哥：**哦，就是还没满，还少一点点。
Ó, jiùshì hái méi mǎn, hái shǎo yìdiǎndiǎn.
*Ah — not full yet, just a tiny bit missing.*

**莎莎：**对。那"不多"呢？
Duì. Nà "bù duō" ne?
*Right. And "bù duō"?*

**波哥：**不多，就是很少，只有一点点。
Bù duō, jiùshì hěn shǎo, zhǐ yǒu yìdiǎndiǎn.
*"Not much" — very little, only a tiny bit.*

**莎莎：**对。所以三个字放在一起：差的，不多。
Duì. Suǒyǐ sān ge zì fàng zài yìqǐ: chà de, bù duō.
*Right. So the three characters together: what's missing — is not much.*

**波哥：**差的不多……少的那部分，很少……
Chà de bù duō… shǎo de nà bùfen, hěn shǎo…
*What's missing is not much… the missing part is tiny…*

**莎莎：**所以今天最重要的一句是：差不多，就是还差一点。
Suǒyǐ jīntiān zuì zhòngyào de yí jù shì: chàbuduō, jiùshì hái chà yìdiǎn.
*So today's key sentence: chàbuduō means — what's missing is only a little.*

**波哥：**差不多，就是还差一点。哦，我明白了！
Chàbuduō, jiùshì hái chà yìdiǎn. Ó, wǒ míngbai le!
*Chàbuduō — only a little missing. Now I get it!*

**莎莎：**那我考你：饭差不多好了，是什么意思？
Nà wǒ kǎo nǐ: fàn chàbuduō hǎo le, shì shénme yìsi?
*Quiz time: "fàn chàbuduō hǎo le" means…?*

**波哥：**饭还差一点就好了。快好了！
Fàn hái chà yìdiǎn jiù hǎo le. Kuài hǎo le!
*The food is just a little short of ready. Almost done!*

**莎莎：**时间差不多了呢？
Shíjiān chàbuduō le ne?
*And "shíjiān chàbuduō le"?*

**波哥：**时间快到了，我们该走了。
Shíjiān kuài dào le, wǒmen gāi zǒu le.
*Time's almost up — we should go.*

**莎莎：**都对。你看，一句话就够用了：差不多，就是还差一点。
Dōu duì. Nǐ kàn, yí jù huà jiù gòu yòng le: chàbuduō, jiùshì hái chà yìdiǎn.
*All correct. See — one sentence is all you need: chàbuduō means only a little missing.*

**波哥：**那我去中国朋友家吃饭，主人说"菜差不多了"——
Nà wǒ qù Zhōngguó péngyou jiā chīfàn, zhǔrén shuō "cài chàbuduō le" —
*So at a Chinese friend's house, when the host says "cài chàbuduō le" —*

**莎莎：**你就可以准备吃饭了。这是第一个用法：快……了。
Nǐ jiù kěyǐ zhǔnbèi chīfàn le. Zhè shì dì yī ge yòngfǎ: kuài… le.
*— you can get ready to eat. That's use number one: almost… / about to.*

**波哥：**第一个用法，学会了。
Dì yī ge yòngfǎ, xuéhuì le.
*Use one — learned.*

**莎莎：**好，第一个用法会了，轮到你了。

Pínglùnqū shíjiān!

Yòng "chàbuduō" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.

Wǒ xiān lái: wǒ de Zhōngwén, chàbuduō néng diǎn cài le.
*Me first: my Chinese is almost good enough to order food.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，很好，也很诚实。你也写一句，我们会看，也会回你。

### 差别很小 · About the same

**波哥：**那游戏里第二句呢？我说"我和我哥哥差不多"。
Nà yóuxì lǐ dì èr jù ne? Wǒ shuō "wǒ hé wǒ gēge chàbuduō".
*And my second answer in the game? "My brother and I are chàbuduō."*

**莎莎：**这是第二个用法：两个东西，差别很小。
Zhè shì dì èr ge yòngfǎ: liǎng ge dōngxi, chābié hěn xiǎo.
*Use two: two things with only a small difference.*

**波哥：**差别很小，就是几乎一样。
Chābié hěn xiǎo, jiùshì jīhū yíyàng.
*A small difference — almost the same.*

**莎莎：**对。你们俩差不多高，这两个手机差不多贵。
Duì. Nǐmen liǎ chàbuduō gāo, zhè liǎng ge shǒujī chàbuduō guì.
*Right. You two are about the same height; these two phones cost about the same.*

**波哥：**这家店和那家店，味道也差不多。
Zhè jiā diàn hé nà jiā diàn, wèidao yě chàbuduō.
*This place and that place taste about the same too.*

**莎莎：**对。不过要注意："差不多"不是"一样"。
Duì. Búguò yào zhùyì: "chàbuduō" búshì "yíyàng".
*Right. But careful: chàbuduō is not 一样 ("identical").*

**波哥：**它们有什么不一样？
Tāmen yǒu shénme bù yíyàng?
*What's the difference between them?*

**莎莎：**"一样"是完全相同；"差不多"是还差一点点。
"Yíyàng" shì wánquán xiāngtóng; "chàbuduō" shì hái chà yìdiǎndiǎn.
*一样 means exactly the same; chàbuduō means there's still a tiny gap.*

**波哥：**又回到那句话了：差不多，就是还差一点。
Yòu huídào nà jù huà le: chàbuduō, jiùshì hái chà yìdiǎn.
*Back to the key sentence again: chàbuduō — only a little missing.*

**莎莎：**对。比较的时候也是：有一点差别，可是差得不多。
Duì. Bǐjiào de shíhou yě shì: yǒu yìdiǎn chābié, kěshì chà de bù duō.
*Right. Same when comparing: there is a difference — just not much of one.*

**波哥：**第二个用法也学会了。那第三个呢？
Dì èr ge yòngfǎ yě xuéhuì le. Nà dì sān ge ne?
*Use two — learned. And use three?*

**莎莎：**第三个，最有中国味道，也最容易出事。
Dì sān ge, zuì yǒu Zhōngguó wèidao, yě zuì róngyì chūshì.
*Use three is the most Chinese — and the one that causes the most trouble.*

### 差不多就行？ · The "good enough" attitude

**波哥：**说吧，我准备好了。
Shuō ba, wǒ zhǔnbèi hǎo le.
*Go on, I'm ready.*

**莎莎：**你写作业的时候说："差不多就行了吧。"
Nǐ xiě zuòyè de shíhou shuō: "chàbuduō jiù xíng le ba."
*About your homework you said: "good enough, I guess."*

**波哥：**对。就是不用做到最好，这样就可以了。
Duì. Jiùshì búyòng zuòdào zuì hǎo, zhèyàng jiù kěyǐ le.
*Right — no need to make it perfect; this will do.*

**莎莎：**这就是第三个用法：够好了，可以了。
Zhè jiùshì dì sān ge yòngfǎ: gòu hǎo le, kěyǐ le.
*That's use three: good enough — that'll do.*

**波哥：**差不多就行，就是不用完美。
Chàbuduō jiù xíng, jiùshì búyòng wánměi.
*"Chàbuduō jiù xíng" — it doesn't have to be perfect.*

**莎莎：**对。这个用法里，有一种生活的态度。
Duì. Zhège yòngfǎ lǐ, yǒu yì zhǒng shēnghuó de tàidu.
*Right. Inside this use lives a whole attitude to life.*

**波哥：**态度？什么态度？
Tàidu? Shénme tàidu?
*Attitude? What attitude?*

**莎莎：**就是你看事情、做事情的样子。很多事情，真的不用完美。
Jiùshì nǐ kàn shìqing, zuò shìqing de yàngzi. Hěn duō shìqing, zhēn de búyòng wánměi.
*The way you see and do things. Truly, many things don't need to be perfect.*

**波哥：**比如呢？
Bǐrú ne?
*Such as?*

**莎莎：**饭好吃就行，房间干净就行，周末的计划，差不多就行。
Fàn hǎochī jiù xíng, fángjiān gānjìng jiù xíng, zhōumò de jìhuà, chàbuduō jiù xíng.
*Dinner tastes good — fine. Room is clean — fine. Weekend plans — good enough is fine.*

**波哥：**等等，"差不多就行"，听起来有点懒啊。
Děngdeng, "chàbuduō jiù xíng", tīng qǐlái yǒudiǎn lǎn a.
*Wait — "good enough" sounds a bit lazy.*

**莎莎：**有时候是懒，有时候是聪明。事情有大有小，小事放过自己。
Yǒu shíhou shì lǎn, yǒu shíhou shì cōngmíng. Shìqing yǒu dà yǒu xiǎo, xiǎo shì fàngguò zìjǐ.
*Sometimes lazy, sometimes smart. Things come big and small — on the small ones, let yourself off.*

**波哥：**哦，大事认真做，小事差不多就行。
Ó, dà shì rènzhēn zuò, xiǎo shì chàbuduō jiù xíng.
*Ah — big things done properly, small things good enough.*

**莎莎：**对。有时候它是一种温柔：对自己说，差不多就行，别太累。
Duì. Yǒu shíhou tā shì yì zhǒng wēnróu: duì zìjǐ shuō, chàbuduō jiù xíng, bié tài lèi.
*Right. Sometimes it's a kindness: telling yourself, good enough — don't wear yourself out.*

**波哥：**学中文也可以这样吗？
Xué Zhōngwén yě kěyǐ zhèyàng ma?
*Does that work for learning Chinese too?*

**莎莎：**可以。今天听懂了差不多，就很好，不用每个字都懂。
Kěyǐ. Jīntiān tīngdǒng le chàbuduō, jiù hěn hǎo, búyòng měi ge zì dōu dǒng.
*It does. Understanding most of today is great — you don't need every single word.*

**波哥：**太好了！那我以后什么都差不多：作业差不多，工作差不多，做饭差不多……
Tài hǎo le! Nà wǒ yǐhòu shénme dōu chàbuduō: zuòyè chàbuduō, gōngzuò chàbuduō, zuòfàn chàbuduō…
*Excellent! From now on everything's chàbuduō: homework, work, cooking…*

**莎莎：**停停停。什么都差不多的人，生活会给他一个大惊喜。
Tíng tíng tíng. Shénme dōu chàbuduō de rén, shēnghuó huì gěi tā yí ge dà jīngxǐ.
*Stop, stop. For people who are chàbuduō about everything, life prepares a big surprise.*

**波哥：**什么惊喜？听起来不像好事。
Shénme jīngxǐ? Tīng qǐlái bú xiàng hǎo shì.
*What surprise? That doesn't sound like a good one.*

**莎莎：**你想想：做饭的时候，盐放"差不多"，菜会怎么样？
Nǐ xiǎngxiang: zuòfàn de shíhou, yán fàng "chàbuduō", cài huì zěnmeyàng?
*Think: if you add salt "chàbuduō-style", how does the dish turn out?*

**波哥：**可能太咸，也可能没味道，只能再做一次。
Kěnéng tài xián, yě kěnéng méi wèidao, zhǐ néng zài zuò yí cì.
*Maybe too salty, maybe tasteless — and you cook it all over again.*

**莎莎：**去机场的时候，时间算得"差不多"，会怎么样？
Qù jīchǎng de shíhou, shíjiān suàn de "chàbuduō", huì zěnmeyàng?
*And if your timing to the airport is only "chàbuduō"?*

**波哥：**飞机差不多等我一下？
Fēijī chàbuduō děng wǒ yíxià?
*Maybe the plane will chàbuduō wait for me?*

**莎莎：**飞机从来不等"差不多"的人，它只看时间。
Fēijī cónglái bù děng "chàbuduō" de rén, tā zhǐ kàn shíjiān.
*Planes never wait for chàbuduō people. They only look at the clock.*

### 两个不能用的地方 · Numbers and promises

**波哥：**好吧，我明白了。那到底哪两个地方不能用？
Hǎo ba, wǒ míngbai le. Nà dàodǐ nǎ liǎng ge dìfang bù néng yòng?
*Okay, I get it. So which are the two forbidden places?*

**莎莎：**马上说第一个：数字。钱、时间、号码。
Mǎshàng shuō dì yī ge: shùzì. Qián, shíjiān, hàomǎ.
*Number one, right away: numbers. Money, time, phone numbers.*

**波哥：**可是别人问多少钱，我说"差不多一百块"，不行吗？
Kěshì biéren wèn duōshǎo qián, wǒ shuō "chàbuduō yì bǎi kuài", bùxíng ma?
*But if someone asks the price and I say "about a hundred kuai" — is that wrong?*

**莎莎：**说自己的事，可以。还别人钱的时候，不行。
Shuō zìjǐ de shì, kěyǐ. Huán biéren qián de shíhou, bùxíng.
*Talking about your own things — fine. Paying someone back — no.*

**波哥：**哦，一百就是一百，九十八不行，一百零二也不行。
Ó, yì bǎi jiùshì yì bǎi, jiǔshíbā bùxíng, yì bǎi líng èr yě bùxíng.
*Ah — a hundred means a hundred. Not ninety-eight, not a hundred and two.*

**莎莎：**对。吃药的时候呢？医生说一天两次——
Duì. Chī yào de shíhou ne? Yīshēng shuō yì tiān liǎng cì —
*Right. And medicine? The doctor says twice a day —*

**波哥：**不能"差不多三次"！
Bù néng "chàbuduō sān cì"!
*You can't take it "about three times"!*

**莎莎：**对。数字后面，是别人的信任；信任，一点都不能差。
Duì. Shùzì hòumiàn, shì biéren de xìnrèn; xìnrèn, yìdiǎn dōu bù néng chà.
*Right. Behind a number is someone's trust — and trust can't be short by even a little.*

**波哥：**明白。那第二个地方呢？
Míngbai. Nà dì èr ge dìfang ne?
*Understood. And the second place?*

**莎莎：**先等三十秒。像这样把一个词拆开、慢慢讲，是我们每个星期都在做的事。
Xiān děng sānshí miǎo. Xiàng zhèyàng bǎ yí ge cí chāikāi, mànmàn jiǎng, shì wǒmen měi ge xīngqī dōu zài zuò de shì.
*Give me thirty seconds first. Taking a word apart and explaining it slowly — that's what we do every week.*

**波哥：**如果你也想一个词一个词地，真的懂中文，就订阅慢慢中文。
Rúguǒ nǐ yě xiǎng yí ge cí yí ge cí de, zhēn de dǒng Zhōngwén, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*If you'd like to truly understand Chinese one word at a time, subscribe.*

**莎莎：**好，回来。第二个不能用的地方：答应别人的事。
Hǎo, huílái. Dì èr ge bù néng yòng de dìfang: dāying biéren de shì.
*Okay, back to it. Forbidden place two: things you promised someone.*

**波哥：**答应，就是我说了"我会做"。
Dāying, jiùshì wǒ shuō le "wǒ huì zuò".
*To promise — I said "I will do it".*

**莎莎：**对。你答应朋友三点到，就是三点，不是"差不多三点"。
Duì. Nǐ dāying péngyou sān diǎn dào, jiùshì sān diǎn, búshì "chàbuduō sān diǎn".
*Right. If you promised to arrive at three, it's three — not "around three".*

**波哥：**可是路上会堵车啊。
Kěshì lùshang huì dǔchē a.
*But there might be traffic.*

**莎莎：**会晚，就早点说：我可能晚十分钟。这比"差不多"好一百倍。
Huì wǎn, jiù zǎodiǎn shuō: wǒ kěnéng wǎn shí fēnzhōng. Zhè bǐ "chàbuduō" hǎo yì bǎi bèi.
*If you'll be late, say so early: I might be ten minutes late. A hundred times better than "chàbuduō".*

**波哥：**哦……我想到一句话：对事情可以差不多，对人不能差不多。
Ó… wǒ xiǎngdào yí jù huà: duì shìqing kěyǐ chàbuduō, duì rén bù néng chàbuduō.
*Oh… here's a thought: with things, chàbuduō is fine — with people, never.*

**莎莎：**波哥！这是你今年说得最好的一句。
Bōgē! Zhè shì nǐ jīnnián shuō de zuì hǎo de yí jù.
*Bo! That's your best sentence of the year.*

**波哥：**哈哈，我差不多是个天才。
Hāhā, wǒ chàbuduō shì ge tiāncái.
*Haha — I'm pretty much a genius.*

**莎莎：**这个用法，完全正确。
Zhège yòngfǎ, wánquán zhèngquè.
*And that usage is completely correct.*

**波哥：**一个词里有这么多东西，课本里只写了一行。
Yí ge cí lǐ yǒu zhème duō dōngxi, kèběn lǐ zhǐ xiě le yì háng.
*So much inside one word — and the textbook gives it a single line.*

**莎莎：**因为词不只是意思，词里面有生活。
Yīnwèi cí bù zhǐshì yìsi, cí lǐmiàn yǒu shēnghuó.
*Because a word is more than a meaning — there's life inside it.*

### 快问快答 · Speed round

Answer out loud **before** Bo does.

**波哥：**好，最后，快问快答！我准备好了。
Hǎo, zuìhòu, kuài wèn kuài dá! Wǒ zhǔnbèi hǎo le.
*Okay, speed round! I'm ready.*

**莎莎：**先热身。"差不多"拆开，是什么意思？
Xiān rèshēn. "Chàbuduō" chāikāi, shì shénme yìsi?
*Warm-up first. Taken apart, what does chàbuduō mean?*

**波哥：**差不多，就是还差一点。
Chàbuduō, jiùshì hái chà yìdiǎn.
*Chàbuduō — what's missing is only a little.*

**莎莎：**饭差不多好了——饭好了没有？
Fàn chàbuduō hǎo le — fàn hǎo le méiyǒu?
*"The food is chàbuduō ready" — is it ready or not?*

**波哥：**还没好，可是快了。
Hái méi hǎo, kěshì kuài le.
*Not yet — but very soon.*

**莎莎：**这两个手机差不多——它们一样吗？
Zhè liǎng ge shǒujī chàbuduō — tāmen yíyàng ma?
*"These two phones are chàbuduō" — are they identical?*

**波哥：**不一样，可是差别很小。
Bù yíyàng, kěshì chābié hěn xiǎo.
*Not identical — but the difference is tiny.*

**莎莎：**朋友问你到哪儿了，你快到了，怎么说？
Péngyou wèn nǐ dào nǎr le, nǐ kuài dào le, zěnme shuō?
*A friend asks where you are; you're nearly there — what do you say?*

**波哥：**差不多到了，再等我五分钟。
Chàbuduō dào le, zài děng wǒ wǔ fēnzhōng.
*Almost there — give me five more minutes.*

**莎莎：**很好。那哪两个地方不能用"差不多"？
Hěn hǎo. Nà nǎ liǎng ge dìfang bù néng yòng "chàbuduō"?
*Good. And the two places you can't use it?*

**波哥：**数字，还有答应别人的事。
Shùzì, hái yǒu dāying biéren de shì.
*Numbers — and things you promised.*

**莎莎：**今天你自己说的那句好话呢？
Jīntiān nǐ zìjǐ shuō de nà jù hǎo huà ne?
*And your own good sentence from today?*

**波哥：**对事情可以差不多，对人不能差不多。
Duì shìqing kěyǐ chàbuduō, duì rén bù néng chàbuduō.
*With things, chàbuduō is fine — with people, never.*

**莎莎：**全对！老师问你：毕业了吗？
Quán duì! Lǎoshī wèn nǐ: bìyè le ma?
*All correct! Teacher asks: have you graduated?*

**波哥：**差不多了！
Chàbuduō le!
*Just about!*

**莎莎：**哈哈，这里可以用。毕业！
Hāhā, zhèlǐ kěyǐ yòng. Bìyè!
*Haha — that's a legal use. You graduate!*

### 结尾 · Ending

**波哥：**好，我们一起回头看看，今天到底学了什么。
Hǎo, wǒmen yìqǐ huítóu kànkan, jīntiān dàodǐ xué le shénme.
*Let's look back at what we actually learned today.*

**莎莎：**差不多，就是还差一点。
Chàbuduō, jiùshì hái chà yìdiǎn.
*Chàbuduō — what's missing is only a little.*

**波哥：**第一个用法：快……了。时间差不多了。
Dì yī ge yòngfǎ: kuài… le. Shíjiān chàbuduō le.
*Use one: almost. Time's about up.*

**莎莎：**第二个用法：差别很小。我们俩差不多高。
Dì èr ge yòngfǎ: chābié hěn xiǎo. Wǒmen liǎ chàbuduō gāo.
*Use two: about the same. We're about the same height.*

**波哥：**第三个用法：够好了。差不多就行。
Dì sān ge yòngfǎ: gòu hǎo le. Chàbuduō jiù xíng.
*Use three: good enough. That'll do.*

**莎莎：**对自己，它可以是温柔；对数字和答应的事，要放下它。
Duì zìjǐ, tā kěyǐ shì wēnróu; duì shùzì hé dāying de shì, yào fàngxià tā.
*Toward yourself it can be a kindness; with numbers and promises, put it down.*

**波哥：**第一集我们说过，词像朋友。今天这个朋友，很特别。
Dì yī jí wǒmen shuō guò, cí xiàng péngyou. Jīntiān zhège péngyou, hěn tèbié.
*In episode one we said words are like friends. Today's friend is a special one.*

**莎莎：**对，它会陪你很多年。别忘了你的小事：用"差不多"写一句话，放在评论区。
Duì, tā huì péi nǐ hěn duō nián. Bié wàng le nǐ de xiǎo shì: yòng "chàbuduō" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Yes — it will stay with you for years. Don't forget your task: one chàbuduō sentence in the comments.*

**波哥：**今天没有下集预告，因为我们想听你的。
Jīntiān méiyǒu xià jí yùgào, yīnwèi wǒmen xiǎng tīng nǐ de.
*No preview today — we want to hear from you.*

**莎莎：**你想让我们聊什么？也许是一个词，也许是一个场景。写在评论区。
Nǐ xiǎng ràng wǒmen liáo shénme? Yěxǔ shì yí ge cí, yěxǔ shì yí ge chǎngjǐng. Xiě zài pínglùnqū.
*What should we talk about? Maybe a word, maybe a real-life scene. Tell us in the comments.*

**波哥：**我们会先看话题适不适合频道，对学习有没有帮助，安不安全。
Wǒmen huì xiān kàn huàtí shì bu shìhé píndào, duì xuéxí yǒu méiyǒu bāngzhù, ān bu ānquán.
*We'll check fit, learning value and safety first.*

**莎莎：**合适的，我们放进节目单。不能保证每个都做，写一次就够了。
Héshì de, wǒmen fàng jìn jiémùdān. Bù néng bǎozhèng měi ge dōu zuò, xiě yí cì jiù gòu le.
*Suitable ones go on the list. No promises — one comment is enough.*

**波哥：**谢谢你和我们在一起。我是波哥。
Xièxie nǐ hé wǒmen zài yìqǐ. Wǒ shì Bōgē.
*Thanks for being with us. I'm Bo.*

**莎莎：**我是莎莎。我们下期再见。
Wǒ shì Shāsha. Wǒmen xià qī zàijiàn.
*I'm Sasha. See you next time.*

**波哥：**下次见。慢慢来。
Xiàcì jiàn. Mànmàn lái.
*See you next time. Take it slow.*
