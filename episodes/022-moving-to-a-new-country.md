## In this episode 这一集

- A story about the hardest week: Xiaohe's first days in a new country.
- Homesickness, named honestly — 想家很正常，不是错 (missing home is normal, not a mistake).
- The sentence that carries the whole story: settle in first, get used to it slowly.

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **新的地方，先住下来，再慢慢习惯。**
> Xīn de dìfang, xiān zhù xiàlái, zài mànmàn xíguàn.
> *In a new place, settle in first — then get used to it, slowly.*

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**莎莎：**一个行李箱，一张机票。
Yí ge xínglixiāng, yì zhāng jīpiào.
*One suitcase, one plane ticket.*

**波哥：**小何的东西，就这么多。
Xiǎohé de dōngxi, jiù zhème duō.
*That's everything Xiaohe owns.*

**莎莎：**他要搬到一个新的国家。
Tā yào bān dào yí ge xīn de guójiā.
*He's moving to a new country.*

**波哥：**新的城市，新的语言，一个认识的人都没有。
Xīn de chéngshì, xīn de yǔyán, yí ge rènshi de rén dōu méiyǒu.
*New city, new language — and not one person he knows.*

**莎莎：**第一个晚上，小何在空空的房间里，给妈妈打了一个电话。
Dì yī ge wǎnshang, Xiǎohé zài kōngkōng de fángjiān lǐ, gěi māma dǎ le yí ge diànhuà.
*On the first night, in an empty room, Xiaohe called his mum.*

**波哥：**电话里，他只说了三个字。
Diànhuà lǐ, tā zhǐ shuō le sān ge zì.
*On the phone, he only said three characters.*

**莎莎：**哪三个字？
Nǎ sān ge zì?
*Which three?*

**波哥：**我想家。
Wǒ xiǎng jiā.
*I miss home.*

**莎莎：**可是三个月以后，小何在电话里说了另一句话。
Kěshì sān ge yuè yǐhòu, Xiǎohé zài diànhuà lǐ shuō le lìng yí jù huà.
*But three months later, he said a different sentence on the phone.*

**波哥：**哪一句？
Nǎ yí jù?
*Which one?*

**莎莎：**先别急。听完今天的故事，你就知道了。
Xiān bié jí. Tīng wán jīntiān de gùshi, nǐ jiù zhīdào le.
*Patience. Hear today's story and you'll know.*

**波哥：**好，我想听。
Hǎo, wǒ xiǎng tīng.
*Okay, I want to hear it.*

### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。在这里，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Zài zhèlǐ, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*I'm Bo. Here, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**今天是一个虚构的小故事。小何搬到了一个新的国家。
Jīntiān shì yí ge xūgòu de xiǎo gùshi. Xiǎohé bān dào le yí ge xīn de guójiā.
*Today's story is fictional: Xiaohe has moved to a new country.*

**波哥：**搬家已经不容易了，搬到新的国家更难。开始吧。
Bānjiā yǐjīng bù róngyì le, bān dào xīn de guójiā gèng nán. Kāishǐ ba.
*Moving house is hard enough — moving countries is harder. Let's start.*

### 机场：没有人认识我 · The airport

**莎莎：**飞机是下午到的。小何走出机场。
Fēijī shì xiàwǔ dào de. Xiǎohé zǒuchū jīchǎng.
*The plane landed in the afternoon. Xiaohe walked out of the airport.*

**波哥：**外面是什么样子？
Wàimiàn shì shénme yàngzi?
*What was it like outside?*

**莎莎：**天很蓝，风有点儿大。街上的字，他很多看不懂。
Tiān hěn lán, fēng yǒudiǎnr dà. Jiē shàng de zì, tā hěn duō kàn bu dǒng.
*Blue sky, a bit windy. Many of the street signs, he couldn't read.*

**波哥：**人多吗？
Rén duō ma?
*Were there many people?*

**莎莎：**人很多。可是没有一个人认识他。
Rén hěn duō. Kěshì méiyǒu yí ge rén rènshi tā.
*Many. But not a single one knew him.*

**波哥：**这种感觉我懂。热闹是别人的。
Zhè zhǒng gǎnjué wǒ dǒng. Rènao shì biéren de.
*I know that feeling. The buzz belongs to everyone else.*

**莎莎：**小何拉着行李箱，要去找他的新家。
Xiǎohé lā zhe xínglixiāng, yào qù zhǎo tā de xīn jiā.
*Pulling his suitcase, Xiaohe went to find his new home.*

**波哥：**他会说这里的话吗？
Tā huì shuō zhèlǐ de huà ma?
*Can he speak the local language?*

**莎莎：**会一点儿，说得很慢。他把新家的地址给司机看。
Huì yìdiǎnr, shuō de hěn màn. Tā bǎ xīn jiā de dìzhǐ gěi sījī kàn.
*A little, very slowly. He showed the driver the address.*

**波哥：**说不清楚的时候，就给别人看。
Shuō bu qīngchu de shíhou, jiù gěi biéren kàn.
*When you can't say it clearly, show it.*

**莎莎：**对。手指是最好的翻译，还记得吗？
Duì. Shǒuzhǐ shì zuì hǎo de fānyì, hái jìde ma?
*Right. The finger is the best translator — remember?*

**波哥：**哈哈，第四集的老朋友。
Hāhā, dì sì jí de lǎo péngyou.
*Haha, an old friend from episode four.*

**莎莎：**车开了很久。小何一直看着窗外。
Chē kāi le hěn jiǔ. Xiǎohé yìzhí kàn zhe chuāng wài.
*It was a long drive. Xiaohe watched out the window the whole way.*

**波哥：**他在想什么？
Tā zài xiǎng shénme?
*What was he thinking?*

**莎莎：**他在想：从今天开始，我就住在这里了。
Tā zài xiǎng: Cóng jīntiān kāishǐ, wǒ jiù zhù zài zhèlǐ le.
*He was thinking: from today, this is where I live.*

**波哥：**这句话，一半是开心，一半是害怕。
Zhè jù huà, yíbàn shì kāixīn, yíbàn shì hàipà.
*A sentence that's half excitement, half fear.*

### 小任务 · Your turn

**莎莎：**说到这里，请你做一件小事。
Shuō dào zhèlǐ, qǐng nǐ zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì.
*Before we go on, one small thing.*

**波哥：**评论区时间到了。
Pínglùnqū shíjiān dào le.
*Comment-section time.*

**莎莎：**对。用"第一天"写一句话，放在评论区。
Duì. Yòng "dì yī tiān" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Yes. Write one sentence with "dì yī tiān" (the first day) in the comments.*

**波哥：**我先来：我学中文的第一天，只会说"你好"。
Wǒ xiān lái: Wǒ xué Zhōngwén de dì yī tiān, zhǐ huì shuō "nǐ hǎo".
*Me first: on my first day of Chinese, I could only say "nǐ hǎo".*

**莎莎：**很好。新工作、新学校、新城市，都可以写。
Hěn hǎo. Xīn gōngzuò, xīn xuéxiào, xīn chéngshì, dōu kěyǐ xiě.
*Nice. A new job, new school, new city — all fine.*

**波哥：**写好放在评论区，我们会看，也会回你。
Xiě hǎo fàng zài pínglùnqū, wǒmen huì kàn, yě huì huí nǐ.
*Post it in the comments — we read and reply.*

### 空房间里的电话 · The phone call in the empty room

**莎莎：**好，回到故事。小何打开新家的门。
Hǎo, huídào gùshi. Xiǎohé dǎkāi xīn jiā de mén.
*Back to the story. Xiaohe opened the door of his new home.*

**波哥：**新家是什么样子？
Xīn jiā shì shénme yàngzi?
*What was it like?*

**莎莎：**一个小房间，空空的。一张床，一张桌子。
Yí ge xiǎo fángjiān, kōngkōng de. Yì zhāng chuáng, yì zhāng zhuōzi.
*A small room, bare. One bed, one table.*

**波哥：**别的什么都没有？
Biéde shénme dōu méiyǒu?
*Nothing else at all?*

**莎莎：**没有。墙是白的，房间安静得有点儿可怕。
Méiyǒu. Qiáng shì bái de, fángjiān ānjìng de yǒudiǎnr kěpà.
*Nothing. White walls, and a silence that felt a little scary.*

**波哥：**天慢慢黑了。小何坐在床上，没有开灯。
Tiān mànmàn hēi le. Xiǎohé zuò zài chuáng shàng, méiyǒu kāi dēng.
*It slowly got dark. Xiaohe sat on the bed without turning on the light.*

**莎莎：**然后，他拿出了手机。就是开头的那个电话。妈妈很快就接了。
Ránhòu, tā náchū le shǒujī. Jiùshì kāitóu de nàge diànhuà. Māma hěn kuài jiù jiē le.
*Then he took out his phone — the call from the beginning. His mum picked up right away.*

**波哥：**妈妈问什么？
Māma wèn shénme?
*What did she ask?*

**莎莎：**妈妈问：到了吗？吃饭了没有？
Māma wèn: Dào le ma? Chīfàn le méiyǒu?
*She asked: have you arrived? Have you eaten?*

**波哥：**小何说：到了，还没吃。
Xiǎohé shuō: Dào le, hái méi chī.
*Xiaohe said: I've arrived. Haven't eaten yet.*

**莎莎：**然后他就说不出话了。过了一会儿，他说：妈，我想家。
Ránhòu tā jiù shuō bu chū huà le. Guò le yíhuìr, tā shuō: Mā, wǒ xiǎng jiā.
*Then the words stopped coming. After a moment he said: Mum, I miss home.*

**波哥：**才第一天，就想家了。
Cái dì yī tiān, jiù xiǎng jiā le.
*Homesick on day one.*

**莎莎：**想家不用等很多天。有时候，一分钟就够了。
Xiǎng jiā búyòng děng hěn duō tiān. Yǒushíhou, yì fēnzhōng jiù gòu le.
*Homesickness doesn't wait for days. Sometimes one minute is enough.*

**波哥：**想家，是不是小何做错了什么？
Xiǎng jiā, shì bu shì Xiǎohé zuò cuò le shénme?
*Is missing home something Xiaohe did wrong?*

**莎莎：**不是。想家很正常，不是错。
Búshì. Xiǎng jiā hěn zhèngcháng, búshì cuò.
*No. Missing home is normal — not a mistake.*

**波哥：**那妈妈说了什么？
Nà māma shuō le shénme?
*So what did his mum say?*

**莎莎：**妈妈安静了一下，然后说：新的地方，先住下来，再慢慢习惯。
Māma ānjìng le yíxià, ránhòu shuō: Xīn de dìfang, xiān zhù xiàlái, zài mànmàn xíguàn.
*She was quiet for a moment, then said: in a new place, settle in first — then get used to it slowly.*

**波哥：**新的地方，先住下来，再慢慢习惯。
Xīn de dìfang, xiān zhù xiàlái, zài mànmàn xíguàn.
*In a new place, settle in first — then get used to it slowly.*

**莎莎：**先……再……，第二集的老朋友又来了。
Xiān… zài…, dì èr jí de lǎo péngyou yòu lái le.
*The 先…再… pattern — our old friend from episode two is back.*

**波哥：**先做第一件事，再做后面的事。
Xiān zuò dì yī jiàn shì, zài zuò hòumiàn de shì.
*Do the first thing first, then whatever comes after.*

**莎莎：**对。今天不用习惯，今天只要住下来。
Duì. Jīntiān búyòng xíguàn, jīntiān zhǐyào zhù xiàlái.
*Right. You don't have to adjust today — today you only have to settle in.*

**波哥：**住下来，就是先吃一碗饭，先睡一觉，先把今天过完。
Zhù xiàlái, jiùshì xiān chī yì wǎn fàn, xiān shuì yí jiào, xiān bǎ jīntiān guò wán.
*Settling in means: eat a meal first, sleep first, get through today first.*

**莎莎：**小何听完，哭了一小会儿。然后他给自己做了一碗面。
Xiǎohé tīng wán, kū le yì xiǎo huìr. Ránhòu tā gěi zìjǐ zuò le yì wǎn miàn.
*Xiaohe cried a little after that. Then he made himself a bowl of noodles.*

**波哥：**新家里的第一顿饭。
Xīn jiā lǐ de dì yī dùn fàn.
*The first meal in the new home.*

**莎莎：**吃完面，他把行李箱打开，把衣服一件一件放好。
Chī wán miàn, tā bǎ xínglixiāng dǎkāi, bǎ yīfu yí jiàn yí jiàn fàng hǎo.
*After the noodles, he opened the suitcase and put his clothes away, piece by piece.*

**波哥：**这就是"住下来"的开始。
Zhè jiùshì "zhù xiàlái" de kāishǐ.
*And that's how settling in begins.*

### 第一次开口的超市 · The supermarket

**莎莎：**第二天早上，小何去了家旁边的超市。
Dì èr tiān zǎoshang, Xiǎohé qù le jiā pángbiān de chāoshì.
*The next morning, Xiaohe went to the supermarket next door.*

**波哥：**买东西，应该不难吧？
Mǎi dōngxi, yīnggāi bù nán ba?
*Shopping shouldn't be hard, right?*

**莎莎：**他也是这么想的。可是一进门，他就站住了。
Tā yě shì zhème xiǎng de. Kěshì yí jìn mén, tā jiù zhànzhù le.
*That's what he thought. But one step inside, he froze.*

**波哥：**为什么？
Wèishénme?
*Why?*

**莎莎：**东西很多，字都看不懂。牛奶在哪里？他找不到。
Dōngxi hěn duō, zì dōu kàn bu dǒng. Niúnǎi zài nǎlǐ? Tā zhǎo bu dào.
*So many things, and he couldn't read any labels. Where's the milk? He couldn't find it.*

**波哥：**包装上的字，一个都不认识。
Bāozhuāng shàng de zì, yí ge dōu bú rènshi.
*Not one character on the packaging looked familiar.*

**莎莎：**他拿起一瓶白色的，看了很久。是牛奶吗？他不确定。
Tā ná qǐ yì píng báisè de, kàn le hěn jiǔ. Shì niúnǎi ma? Tā bú quèdìng.
*He picked up a white bottle and stared at it. Milk? He wasn't sure.*

**波哥：**问人啊。
Wèn rén a.
*Just ask someone.*

**莎莎：**他想问，可是他怕说错。
Tā xiǎng wèn, kěshì tā pà shuō cuò.
*He wanted to — but he was afraid of saying it wrong.*

**波哥：**怕说错——第五集我们聊过这个。
Pà shuō cuò — dì wǔ jí wǒmen liáo guò zhège.
*Fear of mistakes — episode five was all about that.*

**莎莎：**对。小何对自己说：说错了没关系，再说一次就好。
Duì. Xiǎohé duì zìjǐ shuō: Shuō cuò le méi guānxi, zài shuō yí cì jiù hǎo.
*Right. Xiaohe told himself: getting it wrong is okay — just say it again.*

**波哥：**然后他真的开口了？
Ránhòu tā zhēn de kāikǒu le?
*And then he actually spoke?*

**莎莎：**他问旁边的一位阿姨：请问，这是牛奶吗？
Tā wèn pángbiān de yí wèi āyí: Qǐngwèn, zhè shì niúnǎi ma?
*He asked the lady next to him: excuse me, is this milk?*

**波哥：**说得怎么样？
Shuō de zěnmeyàng?
*How did it come out?*

**莎莎：**很慢，可是阿姨听懂了。她笑着帮他拿了另一瓶。
Hěn màn, kěshì āyí tīngdǒng le. Tā xiào zhe bāng tā ná le lìng yì píng.
*Slowly — but she understood. She smiled and handed him a different bottle.*

**波哥：**原来他拿错了。
Yuánlái tā ná cuò le.
*So he'd picked the wrong one.*

**莎莎：**对。阿姨说：这个才是牛奶。小何说：谢谢！
Duì. Āyí shuō: Zhège cái shì niúnǎi. Xiǎohé shuō: Xièxie!
*Yes. She said: this one is the milk. Xiaohe said: thank you!*

**波哥：**一句很小的话，可是他做到了。
Yí jù hěn xiǎo de huà, kěshì tā zuòdào le.
*A tiny sentence — but he did it.*

**莎莎：**走出超市的时候，小何有一点儿开心。
Zǒuchū chāoshì de shíhou, Xiǎohé yǒu yìdiǎnr kāixīn.
*Walking out of the supermarket, Xiaohe felt a little happy.*

**波哥：**因为他今天在超市做到了一件小事。
Yīnwèi tā jīntiān zài chāoshì zuòdào le yí jiàn xiǎo shì.
*Because today, in the supermarket, he managed one small thing.*

**莎莎：**对。先住下来，再慢慢习惯。习惯，就是从小事开始的。
Duì. Xiān zhù xiàlái, zài mànmàn xíguàn. Xíguàn, jiùshì cóng xiǎo shì kāishǐ de.
*Right. Settle in first, adjust slowly — and adjusting starts with small things.*

### 敲门的邻居 · The neighbour at the door

**波哥：**如果你也喜欢这种慢慢的、真实的中文故事，就订阅慢慢中文。
Rúguǒ nǐ yě xǐhuan zhè zhǒng mànmàn de, zhēnshí de Zhōngwén gùshi, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*If you like slow, true-to-life Chinese stories like this, subscribe.*

**莎莎：**每个星期，我们用一个故事，陪你练耳朵。
Měi ge xīngqī, wǒmen yòng yí ge gùshi, péi nǐ liàn ěrduo.
*Every week, one story to train your ears with you.*

**波哥：**你不用去很远的地方，坐在家里就能听。
Nǐ búyòng qù hěn yuǎn de dìfang, zuò zài jiālǐ jiù néng tīng.
*No need to travel far — you can listen from home.*

**莎莎：**好，回到故事。还有一个人，我们还没说。
Hǎo, huídào gùshi. Hái yǒu yí ge rén, wǒmen hái méi shuō.
*Back to the story. There's one more person we haven't mentioned.*

**波哥：**谁？
Shéi?
*Who?*

**莎莎：**第三天晚上，有人敲小何的门。
Dì sān tiān wǎnshang, yǒu rén qiāo Xiǎohé de mén.
*On the third evening, someone knocked on Xiaohe's door.*

**波哥：**他认识的人？他一个人都不认识啊。
Tā rènshi de rén? Tā yí ge rén dōu bú rènshi a.
*Someone he knows? He doesn't know anyone!*

**莎莎：**门口站着一位老先生，手里拿着一盘水果。
Ménkǒu zhàn zhe yí wèi lǎo xiānsheng, shǒu lǐ ná zhe yì pán shuǐguǒ.
*An old gentleman stood at the door, holding a plate of fruit.*

**波哥：**他是谁？
Tā shì shéi?
*Who was he?*

**莎莎：**他说：你好，我是你的邻居，住在你旁边。
Tā shuō: Nǐ hǎo, wǒ shì nǐ de línjū, zhù zài nǐ pángbiān.
*He said: hello, I'm your neighbour — I live next door.*

**波哥：**邻居来看新来的人。家人很远的时候，邻居就在旁边。
Línjū lái kàn xīnlái de rén. Jiārén hěn yuǎn de shíhou, línjū jiù zài pángbiān.
*A neighbour visiting the newcomer. When family is far away, a neighbour is near.*

**莎莎：**对。他说：刚搬来吧？有事，可以来找我。
Duì. Tā shuō: Gāng bān lái ba? Yǒu shì, kěyǐ lái zhǎo wǒ.
*Yes. He said: just moved in? If you need anything, come find me.*

**波哥：**小何说什么？
Xiǎohé shuō shénme?
*What did Xiaohe say?*

**莎莎：**他说得很慢：谢谢你。我叫小何。
Tā shuō de hěn màn: Xièxie nǐ. Wǒ jiào Xiǎohé.
*Very slowly, he said: thank you. My name is Xiaohe.*

**波哥：**一位邻居，两句话，一盘水果。
Yí wèi línjū, liǎng jù huà, yì pán shuǐguǒ.
*One neighbour, two sentences, one plate of fruit.*

**莎莎：**那天晚上，房间还是那个房间。可是感觉不一样了。
Nàtiān wǎnshang, fángjiān háishì nàge fángjiān. Kěshì gǎnjué bù yíyàng le.
*That night, the room was still the same room. But it felt different.*

**波哥：**因为这个城市里，有一个人知道他的名字了。
Yīnwèi zhège chéngshì lǐ, yǒu yí ge rén zhīdào tā de míngzi le.
*Because now one person in this city knew his name.*

### 三个月以后 · Three months later

**莎莎：**说得真好。时间过得很快，三个月过去了。
Shuō de zhēn hǎo. Shíjiān guò de hěn kuài, sān ge yuè guòqù le.
*Well said. Time moved fast — three months went by.*

**波哥：**现在的小何，是什么样子？
Xiànzài de Xiǎohé, shì shénme yàngzi?
*What's Xiaohe like now?*

**莎莎：**他习惯了那家超市，知道牛奶放在哪里。
Tā xíguàn le nà jiā chāoshì, zhīdào niúnǎi fàng zài nǎlǐ.
*He knows that supermarket now — he knows where the milk is.*

**波哥：**现在去超市，不怕开口了吧？
Xiànzài qù chāoshì, bú pà kāikǒu le ba?
*And he's not afraid to speak there any more?*

**莎莎：**不怕了。路也认识了。他常常和邻居坐在门口，一句一句地聊天。
Bú pà le. Lù yě rènshi le. Tā chángcháng hé línjū zuò zài ménkǒu, yí jù yí jù de liáotiān.
*Not any more. He knows the streets too, and often sits at the door chatting with his neighbour, sentence by sentence.*

**波哥：**那他还想家吗？
Nà tā hái xiǎng jiā ma?
*Does he still miss home?*

**莎莎：**想。想家没有不见，只是变小了。
Xiǎng. Xiǎng jiā méiyǒu bújiàn, zhǐshì biàn xiǎo le.
*He does. The homesickness didn't disappear — it just got smaller.*

**波哥：**所以，三个月后的那个电话，他说了什么？
Suǒyǐ, sān ge yuè hòu de nàge diànhuà, tā shuō le shénme?
*So — that phone call three months later. What did he say?*

**莎莎：**他对妈妈说：妈，你放心。我住下来了，也慢慢习惯了。
Tā duì māma shuō: Mā, nǐ fàngxīn. Wǒ zhù xiàlái le, yě mànmàn xíguàn le.
*He told his mum: Mum, don't worry. I've settled in — and slowly, I'm getting used to it.*

**波哥：**从"我想家"，到"我习惯了"。
Cóng "wǒ xiǎng jiā", dào "wǒ xíguàn le".
*From "I miss home" to "I'm used to it now".*

**莎莎：**中间没有大事，只有一件一件的小事。
Zhōngjiān méiyǒu dàshì, zhǐyǒu yí jiàn yí jiàn de xiǎo shì.
*Nothing big happened in between — only one small thing after another.*

### 回想 · Recap

Answer out loud **before** Sasha does.

**波哥：**我们来回想一下今天的句子。
Wǒmen lái huíxiǎng yíxià jīntiān de jùzi.
*Let's recall today's sentences.*

**莎莎：**好。第一句：想家很正常，不是错。
Hǎo. Dì yī jù: Xiǎng jiā hěn zhèngcháng, búshì cuò.
*Okay. One: missing home is normal, not a mistake.*

**波哥：**第二句：说错了没关系，再说一次就好。
Dì èr jù: Shuō cuò le méi guānxi, zài shuō yí cì jiù hǎo.
*Two: getting it wrong is okay — just say it again.*

**莎莎：**第三句，最重要的：新的地方，先住下来，再慢慢习惯。
Dì sān jù, zuì zhòngyào de: Xīn de dìfang, xiān zhù xiàlái, zài mànmàn xíguàn.
*Three, the big one: in a new place, settle in first, then adjust slowly.*

**波哥：**现在你来说。第一天到一个新地方，很想家，怎么办？
Xiànzài nǐ lái shuō. Dì yī tiān dào yí ge xīn dìfang, hěn xiǎng jiā, zěnme bàn?
*Now you. Day one in a new place, badly homesick — what do you do?*

**莎莎：**先住下来。先吃一碗饭，先睡一觉。
Xiān zhù xiàlái. Xiān chī yì wǎn fàn, xiān shuì yí jiào.
*Settle in first. Eat a meal first, sleep first.*

**波哥：**不敢开口问，怎么办？
Bù gǎn kāikǒu wèn, zěnme bàn?
*Too scared to ask — what then?*

**莎莎：**说错了没关系，再说一次就好。
Shuō cuò le méi guānxi, zài shuō yí cì jiù hǎo.
*Getting it wrong is okay — just say it again.*

**波哥：**习惯，从哪里开始？
Xíguàn, cóng nǎlǐ kāishǐ?
*Where does getting used to it start?*

**莎莎：**从小事开始。一家超市，一条路，一个邻居。
Cóng xiǎo shì kāishǐ. Yì jiā chāoshì, yì tiáo lù, yí ge línjū.
*With small things. One supermarket, one street, one neighbour.*

**波哥：**全对！
Quán duì!
*All correct!*

### 结尾 · Ending

**莎莎：**今天的故事就到这里。小何的三个月，从一个行李箱开始。
Jīntiān de gùshi jiù dào zhèlǐ. Xiǎohé de sān ge yuè, cóng yí ge xínglixiāng kāishǐ.
*That's today's story. Xiaohe's three months began with one suitcase.*

**波哥：**从"我想家"，到"我慢慢习惯了"。
Cóng "wǒ xiǎng jiā", dào "wǒ mànmàn xíguàn le".
*From "I miss home" to "slowly, I'm getting used to it".*

**莎莎：**别忘了你的小事：用"第一天"写一句话，放在评论区。
Bié wàng le nǐ de xiǎo shì: yòng "dì yī tiān" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Don't forget your task: one "dì yī tiān" sentence in the comments.*

**波哥：**下一集，我们聊一个很难开口的问题。
Xià yì jí, wǒmen liáo yí ge hěn nán kāikǒu de wèntí.
*Next episode: a question that's hard to say out loud.*

**莎莎：**朋友请你吃饭，你不想去。直接说"不"，好像太硬了。
Péngyou qǐng nǐ chīfàn, nǐ bù xiǎng qù. Zhíjiē shuō "bù", hǎoxiàng tài yìng le.
*A friend invites you to dinner and you don't want to go. A flat "no" feels too hard.*

**波哥：**怎么说，别人不难过，你也不难过？
Zěnme shuō, biéren bù nánguò, nǐ yě bù nánguò?
*How do you say it so nobody ends up hurt?*

**莎莎：**有几句很软的话，下一集教你。
Yǒu jǐ jù hěn ruǎn de huà, xià yì jí jiāo nǐ.
*There are some very soft sentences — we'll teach you next episode.*

**波哥：**谢谢你和我们在一起。我是波哥。
Xièxie nǐ hé wǒmen zài yìqǐ. Wǒ shì Bōgē.
*Thanks for being with us. I'm Bo.*

**莎莎：**我是莎莎。我们下一集见！
Wǒ shì Shāsha. Wǒmen xià yì jí jiàn!
*I'm Sasha. See you next episode!*

**波哥：**下次见。慢慢来。
Xiàcì jiàn. Mànmàn lái.
*See you next time. Take it slow.*
