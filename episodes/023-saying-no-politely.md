## In this episode 这一集

- Why a flat 不行 feels like a door slamming shut — and what to say instead.
- The **three soft sentences** that refuse without hurting anyone.
- The honest rule: soft is not fake — 软，是好好地说真话 (soft means saying the truth kindly).

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **我再想一想，好吗？**
> Wǒ zài xiǎng yi xiǎng, hǎo ma?
> *Let me think about it a bit more, okay?*

## The three soft sentences 三句软软的话

| 句子 | Pinyin | When |
|---|---|---|
| 我再想一想，好吗？ | Wǒ zài xiǎng yi xiǎng, hǎo ma? | when you need time to decide |
| 我可能去不了，下次吧。 | Wǒ kěnéng qù bu liǎo, xiàcì ba. | declining an invitation warmly |
| 这几天有点儿不方便。 | Zhè jǐ tiān yǒudiǎnr bù fāngbiàn. | when you'd rather not explain |

## Key vocabulary 重点词

| 词 | Pinyin | English |
|---|---|---|
| 拒绝 | jùjué | to refuse, turn down |
| 软 | ruǎn | soft, gentle |
| 硬 | yìng | hard, blunt |
| 直接 | zhíjiē | direct(ly) |
| 照顾 | zhàogù | to look after, take care of |
| 可能 | kěnéng | maybe, possibly |
| 下次 | xiàcì | next time |
| 方便 | fāngbiàn | convenient |
| 不好意思 | bù hǎoyìsi | sorry / excuse me (mild) |
| 同事 | tóngshì | colleague |
| 办法 | bànfǎ | way out, solution |
| 假话 | jiǎhuà | a lie, false words |
| 真话 | zhēnhuà | the truth |

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**莎莎：**波哥，周末来我家吃饭吧！
Bōgē, zhōumò lái wǒ jiā chīfàn ba!
*Bo, come to my place for dinner this weekend!*

**波哥：**啊……好，好啊。
À… hǎo, hǎo a.
*Uh… sure, okay.*

**莎莎：**你看，你又说"好"了。
Nǐ kàn, nǐ yòu shuō "hǎo" le.
*See? You said "yes" again.*

**波哥：**其实我这个周末很累，不太想出门。
Qíshí wǒ zhège zhōumò hěn lèi, bú tài xiǎng chūmén.
*Honestly, I'm tired this weekend and don't really want to go out.*

**莎莎：**那你为什么不说"不"？
Nà nǐ wèishénme bù shuō "bù"?
*Then why didn't you say no?*

**波哥：**说"不"太难了！我怕朋友不高兴。
Shuō "bù" tài nán le! Wǒ pà péngyou bù gāoxìng.
*Saying no is too hard! I'm afraid my friend will be upset.*

**莎莎：**很多人都是这样。心里想说"不"，嘴里说了"好"。
Hěn duō rén dōu shì zhèyàng. Xīnlǐ xiǎng shuō "bù", zuǐ lǐ shuō le "hǎo".
*So many of us are like this: "no" in the heart, "yes" out of the mouth.*

**波哥：**然后回家的路上，一直不开心。
Ránhòu huí jiā de lùshang, yìzhí bù kāixīn.
*And then you're unhappy all the way home.*

**莎莎：**今天，我们就聊这件事：怎么说"不"，说得软一点儿。
Jīntiān, wǒmen jiù liáo zhè jiàn shì: zěnme shuō "bù", shuō de ruǎn yìdiǎnr.
*Today we talk about exactly this: how to say no — softly.*

**波哥：**软一点儿的"不"？能学会吗？
Ruǎn yìdiǎnr de "bù"? Néng xuéhuì ma?
*A softer "no"? Can I learn that?*

**莎莎：**能。今天有三句话。最软的一句，只有七个字。
Néng. Jīntiān yǒu sān jù huà. Zuì ruǎn de yí jù, zhǐyǒu qī ge zì.
*You can. Three sentences today — the softest one is only seven characters.*

**波哥：**七个字？好，我不走开。
Qī ge zì? Hǎo, wǒ bù zǒukāi.
*Seven characters? Okay, I'm not going anywhere.*

### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。在这里，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Zài zhèlǐ, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*I'm Bo. Here, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**今天不讲故事，我们聊一个想法：拒绝。
Jīntiān bù jiǎng gùshi, wǒmen liáo yí ge xiǎngfǎ: jùjué.
*No story today — we're talking about an idea: refusing.*

**波哥：**拒绝，就是对别人说"不"。开始吧。
Jùjué, jiùshì duì biéren shuō "bù". Kāishǐ ba.
*Refusing means saying no to someone. Let's start.*

### 为什么"不行"太硬 · Why a flat no feels hard

**莎莎：**波哥，我先问你。朋友请你吃饭，你直接说"不行"，可以吗？
Bōgē, wǒ xiān wèn nǐ. Péngyou qǐng nǐ chīfàn, nǐ zhíjiē shuō "bùxíng", kěyǐ ma?
*First question, Bo. A friend invites you to dinner — can you just say "no way"?*

**波哥：**可以是可以……可是听起来有点儿硬。
Kěyǐ shì kěyǐ… kěshì tīng qǐlái yǒudiǎnr yìng.
*You can… but it sounds a bit harsh.*

**莎莎：**对。"不行"两个字，像一扇门，一下就关上了。
Duì. "Bùxíng" liǎng ge zì, xiàng yí shàn mén, yíxià jiù guānshàng le.
*Right. "Bùxíng" is like a door slamming shut.*

**波哥：**门关上了，人也不好意思再说话了。
Mén guānshàng le, rén yě bù hǎoyìsi zài shuōhuà le.
*Once the door shuts, the other person feels awkward saying anything more.*

**莎莎：**在中文里，很多人说"不"的时候，会说得软一点儿。
Zài Zhōngwén lǐ, hěn duō rén shuō "bù" de shíhou, huì shuō de ruǎn yìdiǎnr.
*In Chinese, many people soften their "no".*

**波哥：**为什么呢？
Wèishénme ne?
*Why is that?*

**莎莎：**因为说"不"的时候，我们也在照顾别人的心。
Yīnwèi shuō "bù" de shíhou, wǒmen yě zài zhàogù biéren de xīn.
*Because while saying no, we're also looking after the other person's feelings.*

**波哥：**不想让请你的人难过。
Bù xiǎng ràng qǐng nǐ de rén nánguò.
*You don't want the person who invited you to feel hurt.*

**莎莎：**对。他请你，是因为他喜欢你。
Duì. Tā qǐng nǐ, shì yīnwèi tā xǐhuan nǐ.
*Exactly. They invited you because they like you.*

**波哥：**难怪不能把"不"说得太硬。
Nánguài bùnéng bǎ"bù"shuō de tài yìng.
*No wonder you can't make the "no" too hard.*

**莎莎：**在新西兰，很多人会直接说：不了，谢谢。
Zài Xīnxīlán, hěn duō rén huì zhíjiē shuō: Bù le, xièxie.
*In New Zealand, many people just say directly: no, thanks.*

**波哥：**对，说得很自然，也没有人不高兴。
Duì, shuō de hěn zìrán, yě méiyǒu rén bù gāoxìng.
*Right — it's natural there, and nobody minds.*

**莎莎：**这不是谁对谁错，只是习惯不一样。
Zhè búshì shéi duì shéi cuò, zhǐshì xíguàn bù yíyàng.
*Neither way is right or wrong — the habits are just different.*

**波哥：**在中文里，软一点儿的"不"，更常见。
Zài Zhōngwén lǐ, ruǎn yìdiǎnr de "bù", gèng chángjiàn.
*In Chinese, the softer "no" is simply more common.*

**莎莎：**对。你把"不"说软了，朋友还是你的朋友。
Duì. Nǐ bǎ "bù" shuō ruǎn le, péngyou háishì nǐ de péngyou.
*Yes. Soften the no, and your friend is still your friend.*

**波哥：**那软的"不"，是不是假话？
Nà ruǎn de "bù", shì bu shì jiǎhuà?
*But is a soft "no" a lie?*

**莎莎：**问得好！这个问题很重要，我们等一下认真说。
Wèn de hǎo! Zhège wèntí hěn zhòngyào, wǒmen děng yíxià rènzhēn shuō.
*Great question! It matters — we'll take it seriously in a moment.*

**波哥：**好，先学怎么说。
Hǎo, xiān xué zěnme shuō.
*Okay — first, teach me what to say.*

### 第一句：我再想一想，好吗？· The softest no

**莎莎：**第一句，也是最软的一句：我再想一想，好吗？
Dì yī jù, yě shì zuì ruǎn de yí jù: Wǒ zài xiǎng yi xiǎng, hǎo ma?
*Sentence one, the softest: let me think about it a bit more, okay?*

**波哥：**我再想一想，好吗？
Wǒ zài xiǎng yi xiǎng, hǎo ma?
*Let me think about it a bit more, okay?*

**莎莎：**数一数，正好七个字。它不说"要"，也不说"不要"。
Shǔ yi shǔ, zhènghǎo qī ge zì. Tā bù shuō "yào", yě bù shuō "búyào".
*Count them — exactly seven characters. It says neither yes nor no.*

**波哥：**它说的是：请给我一点儿时间。
Tā shuō de shì: qǐng gěi wǒ yìdiǎnr shíjiān.
*What it says is: please give me a little time.*

**莎莎：**对。你不用马上回答，别人也不难过。
Duì. Nǐ búyòng mǎshàng huídá, biéren yě bù nánguò.
*Right. You don't have to answer at once, and nobody gets hurt.*

**波哥：**最后的"好吗"，也很重要吧？
Zuìhòu de "hǎo ma", yě hěn zhòngyào ba?
*The "hǎo ma" at the end matters too, right?*

**莎莎：**很重要。"好吗"两个字，让这句话更软。
Hěn zhòngyào. "Hǎo ma" liǎng ge zì, ràng zhè jù huà gèng ruǎn.
*Very much. Those two characters make the whole sentence softer.*

**波哥：**我再想一想，好吗？——真的很软。
Wǒ zài xiǎng yi xiǎng, hǎo ma? — Zhēn de hěn ruǎn.
*"Let me think about it, okay?" — genuinely soft.*

### 小任务 · Your turn

**莎莎：**说到这里，请你做一件小事。
Shuō dào zhèlǐ, qǐng nǐ zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì.
*Before we go on, one small thing.*

**波哥：**评论区时间到了。
Pínglùnqū shíjiān dào le.
*Comment-section time.*

**莎莎：**对。用"我再想一想"写一句话，放在评论区。
Duì. Yòng "wǒ zài xiǎng yi xiǎng" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Yes. Write one sentence with "wǒ zài xiǎng yi xiǎng" in the comments.*

**波哥：**我先来：这个东西有点儿贵，我再想一想，好吗？
Wǒ xiān lái: Zhège dōngxi yǒudiǎnr guì, wǒ zài xiǎng yi xiǎng, hǎo ma?
*Me first: this is a bit expensive — let me think about it, okay?*

**莎莎：**很好。你也写一句，我们会看，也会回你。
Hěn hǎo. Nǐ yě xiě yí jù, wǒmen huì kàn, yě huì huí nǐ.
*Nice. Write yours too — we read and reply.*

### 第二句、第三句 · Two more soft sentences

**波哥：**好。第二句是什么？
Hǎo. Dì èr jù shì shénme?
*Okay. What's sentence two?*

**莎莎：**第二句：我可能去不了，下次吧。
Dì èr jù: Wǒ kěnéng qù bu liǎo, xiàcì ba.
*Sentence two: I probably can't make it — next time.*

**波哥：**我可能去不了，下次吧。
Wǒ kěnéng qù bu liǎo, xiàcì ba.
*I probably can't make it — next time.*

**莎莎：**"可能"和"下次"，都在帮你。
"Kěnéng" hé "xiàcì", dōu zài bāng nǐ.
*"Maybe" and "next time" are both working for you.*

**波哥：**"可能"让话变软，"下次"让人知道：我还想见你。
"Kěnéng" ràng huà biàn ruǎn, "xiàcì" ràng rén zhīdào: wǒ hái xiǎng jiàn nǐ.
*"Maybe" softens it; "next time" says: I still want to see you.*

**莎莎：**对。"下次吧"三个字，门没有关上。
Duì. "Xiàcì ba" sān ge zì, mén méiyǒu guānshàng.
*Right. With "next time", the door never closes.*

**波哥：**不是不见，只是这次不行。
Búshì bújiàn, zhǐshì zhècì bùxíng.
*It's not "never" — just not this time.*

**莎莎：**第三句：这几天有点儿不方便。
Dì sān jù: Zhè jǐ tiān yǒudiǎnr bù fāngbiàn.
*Sentence three: these days are a bit inconvenient for me.*

**波哥：**这几天有点儿不方便。
Zhè jǐ tiān yǒudiǎnr bù fāngbiàn.
*These days are a bit inconvenient for me.*

**莎莎：**方便，就是做一件事很容易，没有问题。
Fāngbiàn, jiùshì zuò yí jiàn shì hěn róngyì, méiyǒu wèntí.
*"Fāngbiàn" means something is easy to do, no problem.*

**波哥：**不方便，就是现在做，有点儿难。
Bù fāngbiàn, jiùshì xiànzài zuò, yǒudiǎnr nán.
*"Bù fāngbiàn" means right now it's a bit difficult.*

**莎莎：**对。你不用说为什么，说"不方便"就够了。
Duì. Nǐ búyòng shuō wèishénme, shuō "bù fāngbiàn" jiù gòu le.
*Right. No reason needed — "bù fāngbiàn" is enough.*

**波哥：**那"不方便"可以用在很多地方吧？
Nà"bù fāngbiàn"kěyǐ yòng zài hěnduō dìfāng ba?
*So "bu fangbian" works in lots of places?*

**莎莎：**对。吃饭、帮忙、借东西，都可以用它。
Duì. Chīfàn, bāngmáng, jiè dōngxi, dōu kěyǐ yòng tā.
*Right. Meals, favours, borrowing things, it works for all of them.*

**波哥：**好，三句话我都记下来了。
Hǎo, sān jù huà wǒ dōu jì xiàlái le.
*Great — I've got all three.*

### 场景一：朋友请吃饭 · Declining an invitation

**莎莎：**光记住还不够，我们来用一用。第一个场景：朋友请你吃饭。
Guāng jìzhù hái búgòu, wǒmen lái yòng yi yòng. Dì yī ge chǎngjǐng: péngyou qǐng nǐ chīfàn.
*Remembering isn't enough — let's use them. Scene one: a dinner invitation.*

**波哥：**好，你来请我。
Hǎo, nǐ lái qǐng wǒ.
*Okay, you invite me.*

**莎莎：**波哥，这个星期六，来我家吃饭吧！
Bōgē, zhège xīngqīliù, lái wǒ jiā chīfàn ba!
*Bo, come to my place for dinner this Saturday!*

**波哥：**谢谢你！不过这个星期六我可能去不了，下次吧。
Xièxie nǐ! Búguò zhège xīngqīliù wǒ kěnéng qù bu liǎo, xiàcì ba.
*Thank you! But this Saturday I probably can't make it — next time.*

**莎莎：**好呀，那下次。你有空的时候告诉我。
Hǎo ya, nà xiàcì. Nǐ yǒu kòng de shíhou gàosu wǒ.
*Sure, next time then. Tell me when you're free.*

**波哥：**一定！
Yídìng!
*Definitely!*

**莎莎：**你看，拒绝完了，可是没有人不高兴。
Nǐ kàn, jùjué wán le, kěshì méiyǒu rén bù gāoxìng.
*See? The refusal is done, and nobody's upset.*

**波哥：**因为我说了"下次"，你知道我还想去。
Yīnwèi wǒ shuō le "xiàcì", nǐ zhīdào wǒ hái xiǎng qù.
*Because I said "next time", you know I still want to come.*

**莎莎：**对。再试一个：这次你很想去，可是要先看时间。
Duì. Zài shì yí ge: zhècì nǐ hěn xiǎng qù, kěshì yào xiān kàn shíjiān.
*Right. One more: this time you really want to go, but need to check your schedule.*

**波哥：**那我说：我很想去！我再想一想时间，好吗？明天告诉你。
Nà wǒ shuō: Wǒ hěn xiǎng qù! Wǒ zài xiǎng yi xiǎng shíjiān, hǎo ma? Míngtiān gàosu nǐ.
*Then I'd say: I'd love to! Let me think about the timing, okay? I'll tell you tomorrow.*

**莎莎：**太好了。先说"很想去"，再说"再想一想"。
Tài hǎo le. Xiān shuō "hěn xiǎng qù", zài shuō "zài xiǎng yi xiǎng".
*Perfect. First say "I'd love to", then "let me think".*

**波哥：**先给一块糖，再说难的话。
Xiān gěi yí kuài táng, zài shuō nán de huà.
*Hand over a sweet first, then say the hard part.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，可以这么说。这就是"软"的意思。
Hāhā, kěyǐ zhème shuō. Zhè jiùshì "ruǎn" de yìsi.
*Haha — you could put it that way. That's what "soft" means.*

**波哥：**软话的顺序，原来这么重要。
Ruǎnhuà de shùnxù, yuánlái zhème zhòngyào.
*So the order of the soft words really matters.*

**莎莎：**对。先暖，再说"不"，话就不伤人。
Duì. Xiānnuǎn, zàishuō"bú", huà jiù bù shāngrén.
*Right. Warmth first, then the "no", and nobody gets hurt.*

### 场景二：同事加活 · Declining extra work

**波哥：**第二个场景呢？
Dì èr ge chǎngjǐng ne?
*And scene two?*

**莎莎：**在公司。同事问你：你今天能帮我做一个东西吗？
Zài gōngsī. Tóngshì wèn nǐ: Nǐ jīntiān néng bāng wǒ zuò yí ge dōngxi ma?
*At the office. A colleague asks: can you help me with something today?*

**波哥：**可是我自己的工作已经很多了。
Kěshì wǒ zìjǐ de gōngzuò yǐjīng hěn duō le.
*But my own plate is already full.*

**莎莎：**对。这个时候，很多人不好意思说"不"。
Duì. Zhège shíhou, hěn duō rén bù hǎoyìsi shuō "bù".
*Right. This is when many people feel too awkward to say no.*

**波哥：**然后工作越接越多，人越来越累。
Ránhòu gōngzuò yuè jiē yuè duō, rén yuèláiyuè lèi.
*And then the more work you take on, the more exhausted you get.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，越……越……，第三集的老朋友。
Hāhā, yuè… yuè…, dì sān jí de lǎo péngyou.
*Haha — the 越…越… pattern, our old friend from episode three.*

**波哥：**那到底怎么说？
Nà dàodǐ zěnme shuō?
*So what exactly do you say?*

**莎莎：**你可以说：不好意思，我今天有点儿不方便，明天可以吗？
Nǐ kěyǐ shuō: Bù hǎoyìsi, wǒ jīntiān yǒudiǎnr bù fāngbiàn, míngtiān kěyǐ ma?
*You can say: sorry, today's a bit difficult for me — would tomorrow work?*

**波哥：**不好意思，我今天有点儿不方便，明天可以吗？
Bù hǎoyìsi, wǒ jīntiān yǒudiǎnr bù fāngbiàn, míngtiān kěyǐ ma?
*Sorry, today's a bit difficult for me — would tomorrow work?*

**莎莎：**你没有说"我不帮你"，你说的是"今天不行"。
Nǐ méiyǒu shuō "wǒ bù bāng nǐ", nǐ shuō de shì "jīntiān bùxíng".
*You didn't say "I won't help you" — you said "not today".*

**波哥：**还给了他一个办法：明天。
Hái gěi le tā yí ge bànfǎ: míngtiān.
*And you offered a way out: tomorrow.*

**莎莎：**对。工作里的"不"，最好带一个办法。
Duì. Gōngzuò lǐ de "bù", zuìhǎo dài yí ge bànfǎ.
*Exactly. A "no" at work is best served with an alternative.*

**波哥：**这句我要多练几次。
Zhè jù wǒ yào duō liàn jǐ cì.
*I need to practise that one a few more times.*

### 软不是假话 · Soft is not dishonest

**莎莎：**如果你喜欢这种能马上用的中文，就订阅慢慢中文。
Rúguǒ nǐ xǐhuan zhè zhǒng néng mǎshàng yòng de Zhōngwén, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*If you like Chinese you can use right away, subscribe.*

**波哥：**每个星期一集，我们把难开口的话，一句一句变容易。
Měi ge xīngqī yì jí, wǒmen bǎ nán kāikǒu de huà, yí jù yí jù biàn róngyì.
*One episode a week — we make the hard-to-say sentences easy, one by one.*

**莎莎：**好，现在回到那个重要的问题：软的话，是不是假话？
Hǎo, xiànzài huídào nàge zhòngyào de wèntí: ruǎn de huà, shì bu shì jiǎhuà?
*Now back to the big question: are soft words lies?*

**波哥：**对，我一直想问。"我再想一想"——如果我根本不想想呢？
Duì, wǒ yìzhí xiǎng wèn. "Wǒ zài xiǎng yi xiǎng" — rúguǒ wǒ gēnběn bù xiǎngxiang ne?
*Yes, I've been wanting to ask. "Let me think about it" — what if I have no intention of thinking?*

**莎莎：**那就别用这句。软的话，不是假话。
Nà jiù bié yòng zhè jù. Ruǎn de huà, búshì jiǎhuà.
*Then don't use it. Soft words are not false words.*

**波哥：**这话怎么说？
Zhè huà zěnme shuō?
*What do you mean?*

**莎莎：**你说"我再想一想"，就要真的想一想，想好了告诉别人。
Nǐ shuō "wǒ zài xiǎng yi xiǎng", jiù yào zhēn de xiǎng yi xiǎng, xiǎng hǎo le gàosu biéren.
*If you say "let me think", actually think — and then tell them.*

**波哥：**那我已经知道去不了呢？
Nà wǒ yǐjīng zhīdào qù bùliǎo ne?
*And if I already know I can't go?*

**莎莎：**那就说"我可能去不了，下次吧"，别让朋友一直等。
Nà jiù shuō "wǒ kěnéng qù bu liǎo, xiàcì ba", bié ràng péngyou yìzhí děng.
*Then say "I probably can't make it — next time", and don't keep your friend waiting.*

**波哥：**哦，我懂了。软，不是不说；软，是好好地说。
Ó, wǒ dǒng le. Ruǎn, búshì bù shuō; ruǎn, shì hǎohǎo de shuō.
*Oh, I get it. Soft doesn't mean not saying it — soft means saying it well.*

**莎莎：**对。软软的拒绝，是把真话说得让人舒服。
Duì. Ruǎnruǎn de jùjué, shì bǎ zhēnhuà shuō de ràng rén shūfu.
*Right. A gentle refusal is the truth, said comfortably.*

**波哥：**话软，心不软；话轻，事不轻。
Huà ruǎn, xīnbùruǎn; huà qīng, shìbùqīng.
*Soft words, firm heart; light words, real matters.*

**莎莎：**说得好，这十二个字，可以写下来了。
Shuōdehǎo, zhè shíèrge zì, kěyǐ xiě xiàlái le.
*Well said. Those twelve characters are worth writing down.*

**波哥：**第五集我们说过，开口会怕。说"不"，很多人更怕。
Dì wǔ jí wǒmen shuō guò, kāikǒu huì pà. Shuō "bù", hěn duō rén gèng pà.
*Episode five was about the fear of speaking. Saying no scares people even more.*

**莎莎：**是啊。可是一句软软的"不"，比一句假假的"好"更好。
Shì a. Kěshì yí jù ruǎnruǎn de "bù", bǐ yí jù jiǎjiǎ de "hǎo" gèng hǎo.
*True. But a soft "no" beats a fake "yes".*

**波哥：**因为说了假的"好"，最后难过的是两个人。
Yīnwèi shuō le jiǎ de "hǎo", zuìhòu nánguò de shì liǎng ge rén.
*Because a fake "yes" ends up hurting both people.*

**莎莎：**所以说"不"以前，先想一想：我是真的去不了，还是只要一点儿时间？
Suǒyǐ shuō"bù"yǐqián, xiān xiǎngyìxiǎng: wǒ shì zhēnde qù bùliǎo, háishì zhǐyào yìdiǎnēr shíjiān?
*So before saying "no", ask yourself: can I really not go, or do I just need a little time?*

**波哥：**想清楚了，再选那句最合适的话。
Xiǎng qīngchǔ le, zàixuǎn nàjù zuìhéshì dehuà.
*Think it through, then pick the line that fits best.*

**莎莎：**这句说得真好。好，快问快答的时间到了！
Zhè jù shuō de zhēn hǎo. Hǎo, kuài wèn kuài dá de shíjiān dào le!
*Beautifully said. Okay — speed round time!*

### 快问快答 · Speed round

Answer out loud **before** Bo does.

**波哥：**来！你问我答。
Lái! Nǐ wèn wǒ dá.
*Bring it on! You ask, I answer.*

**莎莎：**朋友请你吃饭，你去不了，怎么说？
Péngyou qǐng nǐ chīfàn, nǐ qù bu liǎo, zěnme shuō?
*A friend invites you to dinner and you can't go — what do you say?*

**波哥：**我可能去不了，下次吧。
Wǒ kěnéng qù bu liǎo, xiàcì ba.
*I probably can't make it — next time.*

**莎莎：**你需要时间想一想，怎么说？
Nǐ xūyào shíjiān xiǎng yi xiǎng, zěnme shuō?
*You need time to think — what do you say?*

**波哥：**我再想一想，好吗？
Wǒ zài xiǎng yi xiǎng, hǎo ma?
*Let me think about it a bit more, okay?*

**莎莎：**同事让你今天帮忙，你今天太忙，怎么说？
Tóngshì ràng nǐ jīntiān bāngmáng, nǐ jīntiān tài máng, zěnme shuō?
*A colleague needs help today, but you're swamped — what do you say?*

**波哥：**不好意思，我今天有点儿不方便，明天可以吗？
Bù hǎoyìsi, wǒ jīntiān yǒudiǎnr bù fāngbiàn, míngtiān kěyǐ ma?
*Sorry, today's a bit difficult — would tomorrow work?*

**莎莎：**软的话是假话吗？
Ruǎn de huà shì jiǎhuà ma?
*Are soft words lies?*

**波哥：**不是。软，是好好地说真话。
Búshì. Ruǎn, shì hǎohǎo de shuō zhēnhuà.
*No. Soft means saying the truth kindly.*

**莎莎：**"不行"两个字，为什么太硬？
"Bùxíng"liǎngge zì, wèishénme tàiyìng?
*Why does "bu xing" sound so hard?*

**波哥：**像一扇门，一下就关上了。
Xiàng yíshànmén, yíxià jiù guānshàng le.
*Like a door slamming shut in one go.*

**莎莎：**工作里的"不"，最好带什么？
Gōngzuò lǐ de"bù", zuìhǎo dài shénme?
*A "no" at work is best served with what?*

**波哥：**带一个办法，比如"明天可以吗"。
Dài yí ge bànfǎ, bǐrú"míngtiān kěyǐ ma".
*With a plan B, like "how about tomorrow?"*

**莎莎：**说"下次吧"的时候，门关上了吗？
Shuō "xiàcì ba" de shíhou, mén guānshàng le ma?
*When you say "next time", does the door close?*

**波哥：**没有，门还开着。
Méiyǒu, mén hái kāi zhe.
*No — it stays open.*

**莎莎：**拒绝以后，朋友还是朋友吗？
Jùjué yǐhòu, péngyou háishì péngyou ma?
*After a refusal, is your friend still your friend?*

**波哥：**还是朋友。
Háishì péngyou.
*Still your friend.*

**莎莎：**拒绝了一次，下次还能一起吃饭吗？
Jùjué le yícì, xiàcì hái néng yìqǐ chīfàn ma?
*After turning someone down once, can you still have dinner together?*

**波哥：**当然能。门开着，人就还在。
Dāngrán néng. Ménkāi zhe, rén jiù hái zài.
*Of course. The door is open, so the friendship stays.*

**莎莎：**全对！毕业了。
Quán duì! Bìyè le.
*All correct! You graduate.*

### 结尾 · Ending

**波哥：**好，今天我们学了三句软软的话。
Hǎo, jīntiān wǒmen xué le sān jù ruǎnruǎn de huà.
*So — three soft sentences today.*

**莎莎：**我再想一想，好吗？
Wǒ zài xiǎng yi xiǎng, hǎo ma?
*Let me think about it a bit more, okay?*

**波哥：**我可能去不了，下次吧。
Wǒ kěnéng qù bu liǎo, xiàcì ba.
*I probably can't make it — next time.*

**莎莎：**这几天有点儿不方便。
Zhè jǐ tiān yǒudiǎnr bù fāngbiàn.
*These days are a bit inconvenient for me.*

**波哥：**三句话都不硬，可是都是真话。
Sānjùhuà dōu bú yìng, kěshì dōu shì zhēnhuà.
*Three lines, none of them hard, and all of them true.*

**莎莎：**对。软软地说"不"，两个人都舒服。
Duì. Ruǎnruǎn dìshuō"bù", liǎngge rén dōu shūfu.
*Right. Say "no" softly and both of you feel fine.*

**波哥：**拒绝，不是不喜欢别人。是照顾自己，也照顾别人。
Jùjué, búshì bù xǐhuan biéren. Shì zhàogù zìjǐ, yě zhàogù biéren.
*Refusing doesn't mean disliking someone. It's caring for yourself — and for them.*

**莎莎：**会说"好"，也会说"不"，朋友才做得长。
Huì shuō"hǎo", yě huì shuō"bù", péngyou cái zuò de cháng.
*Know how to say "yes" and how to say "no", and friendships last.*

**波哥：**这句话，我记住了。
Zhèjù huà, wǒ jìzhù le.
*That line, I'm keeping it.*

**莎莎：**别忘了你的小事：用"我再想一想"写一句话，放在评论区。
Bié wàng le nǐ de xiǎo shì: yòng "wǒ zài xiǎng yi xiǎng" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Don't forget your task: one "wǒ zài xiǎng yi xiǎng" sentence in the comments.*

**波哥：**下一集，我们聊一个很有名的中国习惯。
Xià yì jí, wǒmen liáo yí ge hěn yǒumíng de Zhōngguó xíguàn.
*Next episode: a very famous Chinese habit.*

**莎莎：**你感冒了，中国朋友会对你说什么？
Nǐ gǎnmào le, Zhōngguó péngyou huì duì nǐ shuō shénme?
*You catch a cold — what will your Chinese friend say?*

**波哥：**我知道！多喝热水！
Wǒ zhīdào! Duō hē rèshuǐ!
*I know this one! Drink more hot water!*

**莎莎：**为什么是热水？这句话是关心，还是别的什么？
Wèishénme shì rèshuǐ? Zhè jù huà shì guānxīn, háishì biéde shénme?
*Why hot water? Is it caring — or something else?*

**波哥：**下一集，慢慢说。
Xià yì jí, mànmàn shuō.
*Next episode — slowly.*

**莎莎：**谢谢你和我们在一起。我是莎莎。
Xièxie nǐ hé wǒmen zài yìqǐ. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Thanks for being with us. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。我们下一集见！
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Wǒmen xià yì jí jiàn!
*I'm Bo. See you next episode!*

**莎莎：**下次见。慢慢来。
Xiàcì jiàn. Mànmàn lái.
*See you next time. Take it slow.*
