## In this episode 这一集

- Where you hear 多喝热水 — a sick friend, a cold day, a parent on the phone.
- Where the habit comes from — boiled water, big cups, and centuries of tea (habits, not medicine).
- The heart of it: it's care, not a command — and how to answer so the care lands.

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **多喝热水——是关心，不是命令。**
> Duō hē rèshuǐ — shì guānxīn, búshì mìnglìng.
> *"Drink more hot water" is care — not a command.*

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**波哥：**莎莎，我感冒了，头有点儿疼。
Shāsha, wǒ gǎnmào le, tóu yǒudiǎnr téng.
*Sasha, I've caught a cold — my head hurts a bit.*

**莎莎：**啊，那你多喝热水。
À, nà nǐ duō hē rèshuǐ.
*Oh — then drink more hot water.*

**波哥：**……又是这句话。
… Yòu shì zhè jù huà.
*…That sentence again.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，怎么了？
Hāhā, zěnme le?
*Haha, what's wrong?*

**波哥：**我的中国朋友都这么说。感冒了？多喝热水。累了？多喝热水。
Wǒ de Zhōngguó péngyou dōu zhème shuō. Gǎnmào le? Duō hē rèshuǐ. Lèi le? Duō hē rèshuǐ.
*All my Chinese friends say it. A cold? Hot water. Tired? Hot water.*

**莎莎：**不开心了？也是多喝热水。
Bù kāixīn le? Yě shì duō hē rèshuǐ.
*Feeling down? Also hot water.*

**波哥：**可是我头疼，热水有用吗？
Kěshì wǒ tóuténg, rèshuǐ yǒuyòng ma?
*But my head hurts — does hot water even help?*

**莎莎：**问得好。今天我们就聊这四个字：多喝热水。
Wèn de hǎo. Jīntiān wǒmen jiù liáo zhè sì ge zì: duō hē rèshuǐ.
*Good question. Today we talk about exactly these four characters.*

**波哥：**一集聊四个字？够吗？
Yì jí liáo sì ge zì? Gòu ma?
*A whole episode on four characters? Is that enough?*

**莎莎：**够。因为这四个字里面，有一样东西，很多人没听出来。
Gòu. Yīnwèi zhè sì ge zì lǐmiàn, yǒu yí yàng dōngxi, hěn duō rén méi tīng chūlái.
*Plenty. Because inside these four characters is something most people never hear.*

**波哥：**什么东西？
Shénme dōngxi?
*What is it?*

**莎莎：**听到最后你就知道了。别走开。
Tīngdào zuìhòu nǐ jiù zhīdào le. Bié zǒukāi.
*Listen to the end and you'll know. Don't go away.*

### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。在这里，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Zài zhèlǐ, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*I'm Bo. Here, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**今天聊一个中国人的习惯：爱喝热水。
Jīntiān liáo yí ge Zhōngguórén de xíguàn: ài hē rèshuǐ.
*Today's topic: a very Chinese habit — loving hot water.*

**波哥：**这个习惯，我天天都能见到。开始吧。
Zhège xíguàn, wǒ tiāntiān dōu néng jiàndào. Kāishǐ ba.
*A habit I see every single day. Let's start.*

### 哪里会听到 · Where you hear it

**莎莎：**波哥，你在哪些时候听过"多喝热水"？
Bōgē, nǐ zài nǎxiē shíhou tīng guò "duō hē rèshuǐ"?
*Bo, when have you heard "drink more hot water"?*

**波哥：**太多了。第一次，我感冒了。中国同学对我说：多喝热水，早点儿睡。
Tài duō le. Dì yī cì, wǒ gǎnmào le. Zhōngguó tóngxué duì wǒ shuō: Duō hē rèshuǐ, zǎodiǎnr shuì.
*Too many times. First: I had a cold, and a Chinese classmate said: drink hot water, sleep early.*

**莎莎：**这是最常见的。有人感冒了，就会听到这句话。
Zhè shì zuì chángjiàn de. Yǒu rén gǎnmào le, jiù huì tīngdào zhè jù huà.
*The classic one. Catch a cold, and this sentence arrives.*

**波哥：**感冒，就是身体不舒服，头疼，有时候发烧。
Gǎnmào, jiùshì shēntǐ bù shūfu, tóuténg, yǒushíhou fāshāo.
*A cold: you feel unwell, your head hurts, sometimes there's a fever.*

**莎莎：**对。第二次呢？
Duì. Dì èr cì ne?
*Right. And the second time?*

**波哥：**冬天，天很冷。朋友看我的手很凉，说：喝点儿热水吧。他还把自己的杯子给了我。
Dōngtiān, tiān hěn lěng. Péngyou kàn wǒ de shǒu hěn liáng, shuō: Hē diǎnr rèshuǐ ba. Tā hái bǎ zìjǐ de bēizi gěi le wǒ.
*Winter, freezing day. A friend saw my cold hands and said: have some hot water — and handed me his own cup.*

**莎莎：**天一冷，这句话就到处都是。
Tiān yì lěng, zhè jù huà jiù dàochù dōu shì.
*The moment it gets cold, the sentence is everywhere.*

**波哥：**还有一次，最有意思。我朋友的妈妈打电话来。
Hái yǒu yí cì, zuì yǒu yìsi. Wǒ péngyou de māma dǎ diànhuà lái.
*And one more — my favourite. My friend's mum called.*

**莎莎：**电话里说什么？
Diànhuà lǐ shuō shénme?
*What did she say?*

**波哥：**妈妈问：吃饭了吗？天冷了，多穿一点儿，多喝热水。
Māma wèn: Chīfàn le ma? Tiān lěng le, duō chuān yìdiǎnr, duō hē rèshuǐ.
*She asked: have you eaten? It's cold now — dress warmly, drink more hot water.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，很多妈妈都会说这三句。
Hāhā, hěn duō māma dōu huì shuō zhè sān jù.
*Haha — many mums know all three lines.*

**波哥：**可是莎莎，为什么是热水？为什么不是别的？
Kěshì Shāsha, wèishénme shì rèshuǐ? Wèishénme búshì biéde?
*But Sasha — why hot water? Why not something else?*

**莎莎：**好问题。我们先说习惯，再说心。
Hǎo wèntí. Wǒmen xiān shuō xíguàn, zài shuō xīn.
*Good question. First the habit, then the heart.*

**波哥：**先说习惯，再说心。好。
Xiān shuō xíguàn, zài shuō xīn. Hǎo.
*Habit first, heart second. Okay.*

### 这个习惯从哪里来 · Where the habit comes from

**莎莎：**很多中国人，从小喝的就是热水。
Hěn duō Zhōngguórén, cóng xiǎo hē de jiùshì rèshuǐ.
*Many Chinese people grow up drinking hot water.*

**波哥：**从小？
Cóng xiǎo?
*From childhood?*

**莎莎：**对。家里每天烧水。水开了，凉一会儿，再喝。
Duì. Jiālǐ měitiān shāo shuǐ. Shuǐ kāi le, liáng yíhuìr, zài hē.
*Yes. Water is boiled at home every day — boiled, cooled a little, then drunk.*

**波哥：**学校呢？
Xuéxiào ne?
*What about school?*

**莎莎：**学校里也有热水，办公室里也有。很多人天天带着自己的杯子。
Xuéxiào lǐ yě yǒu rèshuǐ, bàngōngshì lǐ yě yǒu. Hěn duō rén tiāntiān dài zhe zìjǐ de bēizi.
*Hot water at school, hot water at the office. Many people carry their own cup every day.*

**波哥：**难怪我的中国同学，人人都有一个大杯子！
Nánguài wǒ de Zhōngguó tóngxué, rénrén dōu yǒu yí ge dà bēizi!
*No wonder every one of my Chinese classmates has a big cup!*

**莎莎：**对。还有，中国人喝茶，喝了很多很多年。
Duì. Hái yǒu, Zhōngguórén hē chá, hē le hěn duō hěn duō nián.
*Right. And Chinese people have been drinking tea for a very, very long time.*

**波哥：**茶也是热的。
Chá yě shì rè de.
*Tea is hot too.*

**莎莎：**热茶、热水、热汤——很多中国人觉得，热的东西让身体舒服。
Rè chá, rèshuǐ, rè tāng — hěn duō Zhōngguórén juéde, rè de dōngxi ràng shēntǐ shūfu.
*Hot tea, hot water, hot soup — many Chinese people feel that warm things make the body comfortable.*

**波哥：**这是真的吗？热水真的对身体好吗？
Zhè shì zhēn de ma? Rèshuǐ zhēn de duì shēntǐ hǎo ma?
*Is that true, though? Is hot water really good for you?*

**莎莎：**我们不是医生，今天不说这个。我们说的是习惯。
Wǒmen búshì yīshēng, jīntiān bù shuō zhège. Wǒmen shuō de shì xíguàn.
*We're not doctors — that's not today's topic. We're talking about the habit.*

**波哥：**对，习惯没有对错。
Duì, xíguàn méiyǒu duì cuò.
*Right — habits aren't right or wrong.*

**莎莎：**在很多国家，你一坐下，饭馆就先给你一杯冰水。
Zài hěn duō guójiā, nǐ yí zuòxià, fànguǎn jiù xiān gěi nǐ yì bēi bīngshuǐ.
*In many countries, the moment you sit down, the restaurant brings ice water.*

**波哥：**在中国，很多饭馆给你热茶，或者热水。
Zài Zhōngguó, hěn duō fànguǎn gěi nǐ rè chá, huòzhě rèshuǐ.
*In China, many restaurants bring hot tea — or hot water.*

**莎莎：**你到了别的国家，就会看到别的习惯。
Nǐ dào le biéde guójiā, jiù huì kàndào biéde xíguàn.
*Go to another country and you'll see another habit.*

**波哥：**习惯不一样，没有谁对谁错。
Xíguàn bù yíyàng, méiyǒu shéi duì shéi cuò.
*Different habits — nobody's right or wrong.*

### 小任务 · Your turn

**莎莎：**说到这里，请你做一件小事。
Shuō dào zhèlǐ, qǐng nǐ zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì.
*Before we go on, one small thing.*

**波哥：**评论区时间到了。
Pínglùnqū shíjiān dào le.
*Comment-section time.*

**莎莎：**对。用"习惯"写一句话，放在评论区。
Duì. Yòng "xíguàn" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Yes. Write one sentence with "xíguàn" in the comments.*

**波哥：**我先来：我习惯早上喝一杯咖啡。
Wǒ xiān lái: Wǒ xíguàn zǎoshang hē yì bēi kāfēi.
*Me first: I'm used to a cup of coffee in the morning.*

**莎莎：**很好。你们国家喝水的习惯，也可以写。我们会看，也会回你。
Hěn hǎo. Nǐmen guójiā hē shuǐ de xíguàn, yě kěyǐ xiě. Wǒmen huì kàn, yě huì huí nǐ.
*Nice. Your own country's drinking habits work too — we read and reply.*

### 是关心，不是命令 · Care, not a command

**波哥：**好。习惯我懂了。可是我还有一个问题。
Hǎo. Xíguàn wǒ dǒng le. Kěshì wǒ hái yǒu yí ge wèntí.
*Okay, I get the habit now. But I still have one question.*

**莎莎：**你说。
Nǐ shuō.
*Go ahead.*

**波哥：**我头疼的时候，听到"多喝热水"，有时候有点儿……不高兴。
Wǒ tóuténg de shíhou, tīngdào "duō hē rèshuǐ", yǒushíhou yǒudiǎnr… bù gāoxìng.
*When my head hurts and I hear "drink more hot water", sometimes I get a little… annoyed.*

**莎莎：**哦？为什么？
Ó? Wèishénme?
*Oh? Why?*

**波哥：**因为它听起来像命令：你去喝水！好像我的头疼不重要。
Yīnwèi tā tīng qǐlái xiàng mìnglìng: Nǐ qù hē shuǐ! Hǎoxiàng wǒ de tóuténg bú zhòngyào.
*Because it sounds like an order: go drink water! As if my headache doesn't matter.*

**莎莎：**命令，就是让你"必须做"的话。
Mìnglìng, jiùshì ràng nǐ "bìxū zuò" de huà.
*A command is a sentence that says you "must do" something.*

**波哥：**对。"多喝热水"，听起来就像四个字的命令。
Duì. "Duō hē rèshuǐ", tīng qǐlái jiù xiàng sì ge zì de mìnglìng.
*Right. "Drink more hot water" can sound like a four-character order.*

**莎莎：**波哥，现在我要说今天最重要的一句话了。
Bōgē, xiànzài wǒ yào shuō jīntiān zuì zhòngyào de yí jù huà le.
*Bo, here comes today's most important sentence.*

**波哥：**你说。
Nǐ shuō.
*Say it.*

**莎莎：**多喝热水——是关心，不是命令。
Duō hē rèshuǐ — shì guānxīn, búshì mìnglìng.
*"Drink more hot water" is care — not a command.*

**波哥：**是关心，不是命令。
Shì guānxīn, búshì mìnglìng.
*Care, not a command.*

**莎莎：**关心，就是把你放在心里，想让你好。
Guānxīn, jiùshì bǎ nǐ fàng zài xīnlǐ, xiǎng ràng nǐ hǎo.
*"Guānxīn" means holding you in their heart and wanting you well.*

**波哥：**那为什么关心，要说成这个样子？
Nà wèishénme guānxīn, yào shuō chéng zhège yàngzi?
*Then why does care come out sounding like this?*

**莎莎：**因为很多中国人，不习惯说"我爱你""我想你"。
Yīnwèi hěn duō Zhōngguórén, bù xíguàn shuō "wǒ ài nǐ" "wǒ xiǎng nǐ".
*Because many Chinese people aren't used to saying "I love you" or "I miss you".*

**波哥：**心里有，嘴里不说。
Xīnlǐ yǒu, zuǐ lǐ bù shuō.
*It's in the heart, but not on the lips.*

**莎莎：**对。心里的话，就变成了别的样子。
Duì. Xīnlǐ de huà, jiù biànchéng le biéde yàngzi.
*Right. So the words of the heart take another shape.*

**波哥：**变成了"多喝热水"。
Biànchéng le "duō hē rèshuǐ".
*They become "drink more hot water".*

**莎莎：**变成了"吃饭了吗"，变成了"多穿一点儿"。
Biànchéng le "chīfàn le ma", biànchéng le "duō chuān yìdiǎnr".
*They become "have you eaten", "dress warmly".*

**波哥：**哦……原来这些话，都是一个意思。
Ó… yuánlái zhèxiē huà, dōu shì yí ge yìsi.
*Oh… so all these sentences mean the same thing.*

**莎莎：**什么意思？
Shénme yìsi?
*Which is?*

**波哥：**我关心你。
Wǒ guānxīn nǐ.
*I care about you.*

**莎莎：**对。你越听，越能听出里面的关心。
Duì. Nǐ yuè tīng, yuè néng tīng chū lǐmiàn de guānxīn.
*Right. The more you listen, the more care you hear inside.*

**波哥：**越……越……，第三集的老朋友。
Yuè… yuè…, dì sān jí de lǎo péngyou.
*The 越…越… pattern — our old friend from episode three.*

**莎莎：**所以，下次朋友对你说"多喝热水"，你怎么回答？
Suǒyǐ, xiàcì péngyou duì nǐ shuō "duō hē rèshuǐ", nǐ zěnme huídá?
*So next time a friend says it to you — how do you reply?*

**波哥：**嗯……好，我会的？
Ǹg… hǎo, wǒ huì de?
*Um… "okay, I will"?*

**莎莎：**很好。还可以加一句：谢谢你关心我。
Hěn hǎo. Hái kěyǐ jiā yí jù: Xièxie nǐ guānxīn wǒ.
*Good. And you can add: thank you for caring about me.*

**波哥：**好，我会的。谢谢你关心我。
Hǎo, wǒ huì de. Xièxie nǐ guānxīn wǒ.
*Okay, I will. Thank you for caring about me.*

**莎莎：**他听了，心里会很暖。关心被听懂了，是很开心的事。
Tā tīng le, xīnlǐ huì hěn nuǎn. Guānxīn bèi tīngdǒng le, shì hěn kāixīn de shì.
*That will warm their heart. Having your care understood is a happy thing.*

**波哥：**这句话，比热水还暖。
Zhè jù huà, bǐ rèshuǐ hái nuǎn.
*That reply is warmer than the water.*

### 话要加上小事 · Words plus one small thing

**莎莎：**如果你喜欢这种"听懂话里的心"的中文，就订阅慢慢中文。
Rúguǒ nǐ xǐhuan zhè zhǒng "tīngdǒng huà lǐ de xīn" de Zhōngwén, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*If you like Chinese that lets you hear the heart inside the words, subscribe.*

**波哥：**每个星期一集，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Měi ge xīngqī yì jí, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*One episode a week — we speak slowly, you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**好。不过波哥，我知道你还有话想说。
Hǎo. Búguò Bōgē, wǒ zhīdào nǐ hái yǒu huà xiǎng shuō.
*Now, Bo — I know you have something else to say.*

**波哥：**对，我也听过别的说法。
Duì, wǒ yě tīng guò biéde shuōfǎ.
*Yes. I've heard another take on this.*

**莎莎：**你说。
Nǐ shuō.
*Go on.*

**波哥：**有的年轻人说，"多喝热水"这句话，太容易了。
Yǒu de niánqīngrén shuō, "duō hē rèshuǐ" zhè jù huà, tài róngyì le.
*Some young people say this sentence is too easy.*

**莎莎：**怎么说？
Zěnme shuō?
*How so?*

**波哥：**朋友生病了，只说这四个字，别的什么都不做。
Péngyou shēngbìng le, zhǐ shuō zhè sì ge zì, biéde shénme dōu bú zuò.
*A friend is sick, you say the four characters — and do nothing else.*

**莎莎：**对，这是真的。只有话，没有心，别人听得出来。
Duì, zhè shì zhēn de. Zhǐyǒu huà, méiyǒu xīn, biéren tīng de chūlái.
*True. Words without heart — people can hear the difference.*

**波哥：**所以有的人听了，会不高兴。
Suǒyǐ yǒu de rén tīng le, huì bù gāoxìng.
*Which is why the sentence sometimes annoys people.*

**莎莎：**是的。那怎么让这句话真的暖呢？
Shì de. Nà zěnme ràng zhè jù huà zhēn de nuǎn ne?
*Yes. So how do you make it genuinely warm?*

**波哥：**怎么做？
Zěnme zuò?
*How?*

**莎莎：**说了以后，再做一件小事。倒一杯热水，或者问一句：好点儿了吗？
Shuō le yǐhòu, zài zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì. Dào yì bēi rèshuǐ, huòzhě wèn yí jù: Hǎo diǎnr le ma?
*Say it — then do one small thing. Pour a cup of hot water, or ask: feeling any better?*

**波哥：**话加上小事，关心才是真的。
Huà jiāshàng xiǎo shì, guānxīn cái shì zhēn de.
*Words plus a small action — that's when the care is real.*

**莎莎：**对。话是心的开始，不是心的全部。
Duì. Huà shì xīn de kāishǐ, búshì xīn de quánbù.
*Right. Words are where the heart starts — not the whole of it.*

### 一家人的关心话 · A family of caring phrases

**波哥：**除了"多喝热水"，还有别的这样的话吗？
Chúle "duō hē rèshuǐ", hái yǒu biéde zhèyàng de huà ma?
*Besides "drink more hot water", are there other sentences like it?*

**莎莎：**有很多。天冷了——多穿一点儿。
Yǒu hěn duō. Tiān lěng le — duō chuān yìdiǎnr.
*Many. It's cold — dress a bit warmer.*

**波哥：**多穿一点儿。
Duō chuān yìdiǎnr.
*Dress a bit warmer.*

**莎莎：**晚上很晚了——早点儿睡。
Wǎnshang hěn wǎn le — zǎodiǎnr shuì.
*It's late — go to bed early.*

**波哥：**早点儿睡。
Zǎodiǎnr shuì.
*Go to bed early.*

**莎莎：**你要出门了——路上小心。
Nǐ yào chūmén le — lùshang xiǎoxīn.
*You're heading out — take care on the road.*

**波哥：**路上小心。
Lùshang xiǎoxīn.
*Take care on the road.*

**莎莎：**你也可以把这些话送回去。
Nǐ yě kěyǐ bǎ zhèxiē huà sòng huíqù.
*You can also send these sentences back.*

**波哥：**怎么送？
Zěnme sòng?
*How?*

**莎莎：**妈妈说"多穿一点儿"，你就说：你也多穿一点儿。
Māma shuō "duō chuān yìdiǎnr", nǐ jiù shuō: Nǐ yě duō chuān yìdiǎnr.
*Mum says "dress warmly" — you say: you dress warmly too.*

**波哥：**朋友说"多喝热水"，我就说：你也是，多喝热水。
Péngyou shuō "duō hē rèshuǐ", wǒ jiù shuō: Nǐ yě shì, duō hē rèshuǐ.
*A friend says "drink hot water" — I say: you too, drink hot water.*

**莎莎：**对。这些话都很短，也都是同一个意思。
Duì. Zhèxiē huà dōu hěn duǎn, yě dōu shì tóng yí ge yìsi.
*Right. All short sentences — all with the same meaning.*

**波哥：**我关心你。
Wǒ guānxīn nǐ.
*I care about you.*

**莎莎：**对。学中文，学到这里，就学到心里了。
Duì. Xué Zhōngwén, xuédào zhèlǐ, jiù xuédào xīnlǐ le.
*Yes. Learn Chinese this far, and you're learning it by heart.*

### 快问快答 · Speed round

Answer out loud **before** Bo does.

**波哥：**好，快问快答！这次你问我。
Hǎo, kuài wèn kuài dá! Zhè cì nǐ wèn wǒ.
*Okay, speed round! You ask, I answer.*

**莎莎：**好。朋友感冒了，你说什么？
Hǎo. Péngyou gǎnmào le, nǐ shuō shénme?
*Okay. Your friend has a cold — what do you say?*

**波哥：**多喝热水，早点儿睡。
Duō hē rèshuǐ, zǎodiǎnr shuì.
*Drink more hot water, sleep early.*

**莎莎：**朋友对你说"多喝热水"，你怎么回答？
Péngyou duì nǐ shuō "duō hē rèshuǐ", nǐ zěnme huídá?
*A friend says it to you — how do you reply?*

**波哥：**好，我会的。谢谢你关心我。
Hǎo, wǒ huì de. Xièxie nǐ guānxīn wǒ.
*Okay, I will. Thank you for caring about me.*

**莎莎：**"多喝热水"是命令吗？
"Duō hē rèshuǐ" shì mìnglìng ma?
*Is "drink more hot water" a command?*

**波哥：**不是，是关心。
Búshì, shì guānxīn.
*No — it's care.*

**莎莎：**怎么让这句话更暖？
Zěnme ràng zhè jù huà gèng nuǎn?
*How do you make it warmer?*

**波哥：**说了以后，再做一件小事。
Shuō le yǐhòu, zài zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì.
*Say it — then do one small thing.*

**莎莎：**天冷了，怎么关心朋友？
Tiān lěng le, zěnme guānxīn péngyou?
*It's cold — how do you show a friend you care?*

**波哥：**多穿一点儿。
Duō chuān yìdiǎnr.
*Dress a bit warmer.*

**莎莎：**全对！毕业了。
Quán duì! Bìyè le.
*All correct! You graduate.*

### 结尾 · Ending

**波哥：**好，今天我们聊了四个字：多喝热水。
Hǎo, jīntiān wǒmen liáo le sì ge zì: duō hē rèshuǐ.
*So — today was four characters: drink more hot water.*

**莎莎：**多喝热水——是关心，不是命令。
Duō hē rèshuǐ — shì guānxīn, búshì mìnglìng.
*"Drink more hot water" is care — not a command.*

**波哥：**心里的话不好说出口，就变成了热水、衣服和"路上小心"。
Xīnlǐ de huà bù hǎo shuō chūkǒu, jiù biànchéng le rèshuǐ, yīfu hé "lùshang xiǎoxīn".
*When the heart's words are hard to say, they turn into hot water, warm clothes, and "take care".*

**莎莎：**别忘了你的小事：用"习惯"写一句话，放在评论区。
Bié wàng le nǐ de xiǎo shì: yòng "xíguàn" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Don't forget your task: one "xíguàn" sentence in the comments.*

**波哥：**今天没有下集预告，因为我们想听你的。
Jīntiān méiyǒu xià jí yùgào, yīnwèi wǒmen xiǎng tīng nǐ de.
*No preview today — we want to hear from you.*

**莎莎：**你想让我们聊什么话题？写在评论区。
Nǐ xiǎng ràng wǒmen liáo shénme huàtí? Xiě zài pínglùnqū.
*What topic should we cover? Tell us in the comments.*

**波哥：**我们会先看话题适不适合频道，对学习有没有帮助，安不安全。
Wǒmen huì xiān kàn huàtí shì bu shìhé píndào, duì xuéxí yǒu méiyǒu bāngzhù, ān bu ānquán.
*We'll check fit, learning value and safety first.*

**莎莎：**合适的，我们放进节目单。不能保证每个都做，写一次就够了。
Héshì de, wǒmen fàng jìn jiémùdān. Bù néng bǎozhèng měi ge dōu zuò, xiě yí cì jiù gòu le.
*Suitable ones go on the list. No promises — one comment is enough.*

**波哥：**谢谢你和我们在一起。我是波哥。
Xièxie nǐ hé wǒmen zài yìqǐ. Wǒ shì Bōgē.
*Thanks for being with us. I'm Bo.*

**莎莎：**我是莎莎。我们下期再见。
Wǒ shì Shāsha. Wǒmen xià qī zàijiàn.
*I'm Sasha. See you next time.*

**波哥：**下次见。多喝热水！
Xiàcì jiàn. Duō hē rèshuǐ!
