## In this episode 这一集

- An old box, a university photo, and a friend who went quiet two years ago.
- The **fear of reaching out**: typing a message, deleting it, typing again.
- The five characters that reopen a friendship — and the line to keep: 好朋友，不怕晚.

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **他很久没有消息了。**
> Tā hěn jiǔ méiyǒu xiāoxi le.
> *There's been no news from him for a long time.*

## Pattern of the day 今日句型：很久没有……了

很久没有 A 了 = *it's been a long time since A*. The 了 at the end marks the situation "up to now".

1. 他很久没有消息了。 — *No news from him for ages.*
2. 我很久没有看电影了。 — *I haven't seen a film in ages.*
3. 我很久没有给妈妈打电话了。 — *I haven't called my mum in ages.*
4. 我们很久没有见面了。 — *We haven't met for a long time.*

behind it, send that person a 最近怎么样？ today.

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**莎莎：**波哥，我问你一个问题：你有没有一个朋友，以前天天说话，现在很久不联系了？
Bōgē, wǒ wèn nǐ yí ge wèntí: nǐ yǒu méiyǒu yí ge péngyou, yǐqián tiāntiān shuōhuà, xiànzài hěn jiǔ bù liánxì le?
*Bo, a question: do you have a friend you once talked to daily — and now haven't contacted in ages?*

**波哥：**……有。你怎么知道？
… Yǒu. Nǐ zěnme zhīdào?
*…I do. How did you know?*

**莎莎：**因为几乎每个人的心里，都有这样一个人。
Yīnwèi jīhū měi ge rén de xīnlǐ, dōu yǒu zhèyàng yí ge rén.
*Because almost everyone carries someone like that.*

**波哥：**想给他发消息，又不敢发。
Xiǎng gěi tā fā xiāoxi, yòu bù gǎn fā.
*You want to message them — and don't dare.*

**莎莎：**为什么不敢？
Wèishénme bù gǎn?
*Why not?*

**波哥：**这么久没联系，突然发一句话，会不会很奇怪？
Zhème jiǔ méi liánxì, tūrán fā yí jù huà, huì búhuì hěn qíguài?
*After so long, wouldn't a sudden message be weird?*

**莎莎：**今天的故事，就是关于这个"不敢"。
Jīntiān de gùshi, jiùshì guānyú zhège "bù gǎn".
*Today's story is about exactly that "don't dare".*

**波哥：**故事的名字呢？
Gùshi de míngzi ne?
*And its name?*

**莎莎：**不见了的朋友。
Bújiàn le de péngyou.
*The friend who disappeared.*

**波哥：**朋友不见了……最后找回来了吗？
Péngyou bújiàn le… zuìhòu zhǎo huílái le ma?
*A friend disappears… do they get him back?*

**莎莎：**答案在故事的最后，在一句很短的话里。
Dá'àn zài gùshi de zuìhòu, zài yí jù hěn duǎn de huà lǐ.
*The answer comes at the end — in one very short sentence.*

**波哥：**好，我想听。
Hǎo, wǒ xiǎng tīng.
*Okay, I want to hear it.*

### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。在这里，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Zài zhèlǐ, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*I'm Bo. Here, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**这是一个虚构的小故事。故事里的人，叫小文。
Zhè shì yí ge xūgòu de xiǎo gùshi. Gùshi lǐ de rén, jiào Xiǎowén.
*This is a fictional little story. Its hero is called Xiaowen.*

**波哥：**好，故事开始了。
Hǎo, gùshi kāishǐ le.
*Okay — the story begins.*

### 一个旧盒子 · The old box

**莎莎：**星期六下午，天气很好，太阳照进房间里。
Xīngqīliù xiàwǔ, tiānqì hěn hǎo, tàiyáng zhào jìn fángjiān lǐ.
*Saturday afternoon, fine weather, sunlight falling into the room.*

**波哥：**小文在做什么？
Xiǎowén zài zuò shénme?
*What's Xiaowen doing?*

**莎莎：**他在家里收拾东西，找到了一个旧盒子。
Tā zài jiālǐ shōushi dōngxi, zhǎodào le yí ge jiù hézi.
*Tidying up at home — and he finds an old box.*

**波哥：**盒子里有什么？
Hézi lǐ yǒu shénme?
*What's inside?*

**莎莎：**一些旧东西：几张车票，几张卡片，还有一些照片。
Yìxiē jiù dōngxi: jǐ zhāng chēpiào, jǐ zhāng kǎpiàn, háiyǒu yìxiē zhàopiàn.
*Old things: train tickets, cards, and some photos.*

**波哥：**照片里是谁？
Zhàopiàn lǐ shì shéi?
*Who's in the photos?*

**莎莎：**小文和一个朋友，站在大学门口。两个人都很年轻，笑得很开心。
Xiǎowén hé yí ge péngyou, zhàn zài dàxué ménkǒu. Liǎng ge rén dōu hěn niánqīng, xiào de hěn kāixīn.
*Xiaowen and a friend at the university gate — both young, both laughing.*

**波哥：**这个朋友是谁？
Zhège péngyou shì shéi?
*Who's the friend?*

**莎莎：**他叫大伟。大学四年，他们天天在一起。
Tā jiào Dàwěi. Dàxué sì nián, tāmen tiāntiān zài yìqǐ.
*Dawei. Four years of university, together every day.*

**波哥：**一起吃饭，一起上课，一起考试。
Yìqǐ chīfàn, yìqǐ shàngkè, yìqǐ kǎoshì.
*Eating together, classes together, exams together.*

**莎莎：**对。那时候，小文有什么事，第一个告诉的就是大伟。
Duì. Nà shíhou, Xiǎowén yǒu shénme shì, dì yī ge gàosu de jiùshì Dàwěi.
*Right. Back then, whatever happened, Dawei was the first to know.*

**波哥：**真正的好朋友。后来呢？
Zhēnzhèng de hǎo péngyou. Hòulái ne?
*A true friend. And then?*

**莎莎：**后来，他们离开学校了。小文去了南方，大伟去了北方，离得很远。
Hòulái, tāmen líkāi xuéxiào le. Xiǎowén qù le nánfāng, Dàwěi qù le běifāng, lí de hěn yuǎn.
*Then they left school. Xiaowen went south, Dawei went north — far apart.*

**波哥：**然后呢？
Ránhòu ne?
*Then what?*

**莎莎：**一开始，他们常常打电话。
Yì kāishǐ, tāmen chángcháng dǎ diànhuà.
*At first they phoned all the time.*

**波哥：**后来呢？
Hòulái ne?
*And later?*

**莎莎：**后来，电话少了，变成了消息。
Hòulái, diànhuà shǎo le, biànchéng le xiāoxi.
*Later the calls thinned out and became messages.*

**波哥：**再后来，消息也少了。
Zài hòulái, xiāoxi yě shǎo le.
*And later still, even the messages thinned out.*

**莎莎：**对。新年的时候发一句"新年快乐"，就没有别的了。
Duì. Xīnnián de shíhou fā yí jù "xīnnián kuàilè", jiù méiyǒu biéde le.
*Right. One "Happy New Year" a year — and nothing else.*

**波哥：**不是不想联系，就是……时间过得太快了。
Búshì bù xiǎng liánxì, jiùshì… shíjiān guò de tài kuài le.
*It's not that you don't want to stay in touch — time just moves too fast.*

**莎莎：**对。没有人做错什么。日子一天一天过，话就一年比一年少。
Duì. Méiyǒu rén zuò cuò shénme. Rìzi yì tiān yì tiān guò, huà jiù yì nián bǐ yì nián shǎo.
*Right. Nobody did anything wrong. The days pass, and each year there are fewer words.*

**波哥：**这个我太懂了……
Zhège wǒ tài dǒng le…
*I know this feeling all too well…*

**莎莎：**小文拿着照片，看了很久，说了一句话。
Xiǎowén ná zhe zhàopiàn, kàn le hěn jiǔ, shuō le yí jù huà.
*Xiaowen holds the photo, looks at it a long time, and says one sentence.*

**波哥：**什么话？
Shénme huà?
*Which sentence?*

**莎莎：**他很久没有消息了。
Tā hěn jiǔ méiyǒu xiāoxi le.
*There's been no news from him for a long time.*

**波哥：**他很久没有消息了……是多久？
Tā hěn jiǔ méiyǒu xiāoxi le… shì duōjiǔ?
*A long time… how long exactly?*

**莎莎：**小文想了想：上一次说话，是两年前。
Xiǎowén xiǎng le xiǎng: shàng yí cì shuōhuà, shì liǎng nián qián.
*Xiaowen thinks: the last time they talked was two years ago.*

**波哥：**两年！
Liǎng nián!
*Two years!*

### 小任务 · Your turn

**莎莎：**对。说到这里，请你做一件小事。
Duì. Shuō dào zhèlǐ, qǐng nǐ zuò yí jiàn xiǎoshì.
*Yes. And right here — one small thing for you.*

**波哥：**评论区时间到了。
Pínglùnqū shíjiān dào le.
*Comment-section time.*

**莎莎：**对。用"很久没有……了"写一句话，放在评论区。
Duì. Yòng "hěn jiǔ méiyǒu… le" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Yes. Write one "hěn jiǔ méiyǒu… le" sentence in the comments.*

**波哥：**我先来：我很久没有看电影了。
Wǒ xiān lái: wǒ hěn jiǔ méiyǒu kàn diànyǐng le.
*Me first: I haven't seen a film in ages.*

**莎莎：**很好。再来一句？
Hěn hǎo. Zài lái yí jù?
*Nice. Another?*

**波哥：**我很久没有给妈妈打电话了……哎呀，今天就打。
Wǒ hěn jiǔ méiyǒu gěi māma dǎ diànhuà le… āiyā, jīntiān jiù dǎ.
*I haven't called my mum in ages… oh no — I'm calling today.*

**莎莎：**哈哈，太好了。你也写一句，我们会看，也会回你。
Hāhā, tài hǎo le. Nǐ yě xiě yí jù, wǒmen huì kàn, yě huì huí nǐ.
*Haha, perfect. Write yours too — we read and reply.*

### 打了字，又删了 · Typed, then deleted

**波哥：**好，回到故事。小文想大伟了，对吗？
Hǎo, huídào gùshi. Xiǎowén xiǎng Dàwěi le, duì ma?
*Back to the story. Xiaowen misses Dawei, right?*

**莎莎：**对。他拿起手机，找到了大伟的名字。
Duì. Tā ná qǐ shǒujī, zhǎodào le Dàwěi de míngzi.
*Right. He picks up his phone and finds Dawei's name.*

**波哥：**要发消息了？
Yào fā xiāoxi le?
*Time to send a message?*

**莎莎：**他打了几个字，又删了。
Tā dǎ le jǐ ge zì, yòu shān le.
*He types a few characters — then deletes them.*

**波哥：**删了？为什么？
Shān le? Wèishénme?
*Deleted? Why?*

**莎莎：**删，就是把打好的字去掉。他心里想：这么久没联系，突然发消息，会不会很奇怪？
Shān, jiùshì bǎ dǎ hǎo de zì qùdiào. Tā xīnlǐ xiǎng: zhème jiǔ méi liánxì, tūrán fā xiāoxi, huì búhuì hěn qíguài?
*To delete is to erase what you typed. He's thinking: after so long, would a sudden message be weird?*

**波哥：**他怕打扰大伟。
Tā pà dǎrǎo Dàwěi.
*He's afraid of bothering Dawei.*

**莎莎：**对。大伟现在一定很忙吧？他还记得我吗？
Duì. Dàwěi xiànzài yídìng hěn máng ba? Tā hái jìde wǒ ma?
*Right. Dawei must be so busy now. Does he even remember me?*

**波哥：**越想，越不敢发。
Yuè xiǎng, yuè bù gǎn fā.
*The more he thinks, the less he dares.*

**莎莎：**越想越不敢，越等越难。"越……越……"，第三集的老朋友。
Yuè xiǎng yuè bù gǎn, yuè děng yuè nán. "Yuè… yuè…", dì sān jí de lǎo péngyou.
*The more you think, the less you dare; the longer you wait, the harder it gets. The 越…越… pattern — an old friend from episode three.*

**波哥：**结果呢？
Jiéguǒ ne?
*So what happens?*

**莎莎：**小文把手机放下了。
Xiǎowén bǎ shǒujī fàngxià le.
*Xiaowen puts the phone down.*

**波哥：**唉，就这样算了吗？
Āi, jiù zhèyàng suàn le ma?
*Ah — he just gives up?*

**莎莎：**那个下午，他去洗了衣服，又看了一会儿电视，可是什么都没看进去。
Nàge xiàwǔ, tā qù xǐ le yīfu, yòu kàn le yíhuìr diànshì, kěshì shénme dōu méi kàn jìnqù.
*That afternoon he does the laundry, watches some TV — and takes none of it in.*

**波哥：**可是心里一直想着这件事。
Kěshì xīnlǐ yìzhí xiǎng zhe zhè jiàn shì.
*Because his mind keeps circling back.*

**莎莎：**对。照片还在桌子上，朋友的笑还在照片里。
Duì. Zhàopiàn hái zài zhuōzi shàng, péngyou de xiào hái zài zhàopiàn lǐ.
*Right. The photo is still on the table, the friend's smile still in it.*

**波哥：**其实他很想大伟。
Qíshí tā hěn xiǎng Dàwěi.
*He really misses Dawei.*

**莎莎：**很想。可是"想"在心里，"怕"在手上。
Hěn xiǎng. Kěshì "xiǎng" zài xīnlǐ, "pà" zài shǒu shàng.
*Very much. But the missing lives in his heart — and the fear lives in his hands.*

**波哥：**想在心里，怕在手上……这句我记住了。
Xiǎng zài xīnlǐ, pà zài shǒu shàng… zhè jù wǒ jìzhù le.
*Missing in the heart, fear in the hands… I'm keeping that line.*

### 五个字 · Five characters

**莎莎：**到了晚上，小文又拿起了手机。
Dào le wǎnshang, Xiǎowén yòu ná qǐ le shǒujī.
*That evening, Xiaowen picks up the phone again.*

**波哥：**这次发了吗？
Zhè cì fā le ma?
*Does he send it this time?*

**莎莎：**他想了很多开头。
Tā xiǎng le hěn duō kāitóu.
*He tries out many openings.*

**波哥：**比如？
Bǐrú?
*Such as?*

**莎莎：**"好久不见，你还记得我吗？"——太奇怪了，删掉。
"Hǎojiǔ bú jiàn, nǐ hái jìde wǒ ma?" — tài qíguài le, shāndiào.
*"Long time no see — do you still remember me?" Too weird. Delete.*

**波哥：**哈哈，像电视剧。
Hāhā, xiàng diànshìjù.
*Haha — like a TV drama.*

**莎莎：**"对不起，这么久没联系。"——上来就道歉，也不对，删掉。
"Duìbuqǐ, zhème jiǔ méi liánxì." — shànglái jiù dàoqiàn, yě bù duì, shāndiào.
*"Sorry for not being in touch." Opening with an apology — also wrong. Delete.*

**波哥：**上一集刚学的：这里没有真的错，不用道歉。
Shàng yì jí gāng xué de: zhèlǐ méiyǒu zhēnde cuò, búyòng dàoqiàn.
*Just like last episode: no real mistake here, so no apology needed.*

**莎莎：**说得好！最后，小文只写了五个字。
Shuō de hǎo! Zuìhòu, Xiǎowén zhǐ xiě le wǔ ge zì.
*Well said! In the end, Xiaowen writes just five characters.*

**波哥：**哪五个字？
Nǎ wǔ ge zì?
*Which five?*

**莎莎：**最近怎么样？
Zuìjìn zěnmeyàng?
*How have you been lately?*

**波哥：**最近怎么样……就这么简单？
Zuìjìn zěnmeyàng… jiù zhème jiǎndān?
*"How have you been"… that simple?*

**莎莎：**就这么简单。第八集我们说过，聊天的门，用它打开。
Jiù zhème jiǎndān. Dì bā jí wǒmen shuō guò, liáotiān de mén, yòng tā dǎkāi.
*That simple. As we said in episode eight — it's the door-opener of conversations.*

**波哥：**老朋友之间，也用它？
Lǎo péngyou zhījiān, yě yòng tā?
*Even between old friends?*

**莎莎：**越是老朋友，越不用想太多。
Yuè shì lǎo péngyou, yuè búyòng xiǎng tài duō.
*The older the friend, the less you need to overthink.*

**波哥：**然后呢？他发了吗？
Ránhòu ne? Tā fā le ma?
*And then? Did he send it?*

**莎莎：**他闭上眼睛，把消息发了出去。
Tā bì shàng yǎnjing, bǎ xiāoxi fā le chūqù.
*He closes his eyes and sends the message.*

**波哥：**然后呢？！
Ránhòu ne?!
*And then?!*

**莎莎：**然后，他把手机放在桌子上，不敢看。
Ránhòu, tā bǎ shǒujī fàng zài zhuōzi shàng, bù gǎn kàn.
*Then he puts the phone face-up on the table — and can't look at it.*

**波哥：**等的时间，最长了。
Děng de shíjiān, zuì cháng le.
*Waiting is the longest time there is.*

### 手机响了 · The reply

**莎莎：**等的时候，我们说一句话。
Děng de shíhou, wǒmen shuō yí jù huà.
*While we wait, one quick word.*

**波哥：**如果你喜欢这种慢慢的、真实生活的故事，就订阅慢慢中文。
Rúguǒ nǐ xǐhuan zhè zhǒng mànmàn de, zhēnshí shēnghuó de gùshi, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*If you like slow, real-life stories like this one, subscribe.*

**莎莎：**每个星期，我们用一个故事，陪你练中文。
Měi ge xīngqī, wǒmen yòng yí ge gùshi, péi nǐ liàn Zhōngwén.
*Every week, one story to practise your Chinese with.*

**波哥：**好，回到故事。手机响了没有？
Hǎo, huídào gùshi. Shǒujī xiǎng le méiyǒu?
*Back to the story. Did the phone ring or not?*

**莎莎：**五分钟以后，桌子上的手机响了一声。
Wǔ fēnzhōng yǐhòu, zhuōzi shàng de shǒujī xiǎng le yì shēng.
*Five minutes later, the phone on the table chimes once.*

**波哥：**是大伟？！
Shì Dàwěi?!
*It's Dawei?!*

**莎莎：**是大伟。他回了很长很长的一段话。
Shì Dàwěi. Tā huí le hěn cháng hěn cháng de yí duàn huà.
*It's Dawei. And he replies with a long, long message.*

**波哥：**他说什么？
Tā shuō shénme?
*What does he say?*

**莎莎：**他说：小文！我正想你呢！上个星期，我还翻到我们的照片！你还好吗？
Tā shuō: Xiǎowén! Wǒ zhèng xiǎng nǐ ne! Shàng ge xīngqī, wǒ hái fāndào wǒmen de zhàopiàn! Nǐ hái hǎo ma?
*He says: Xiaowen! I was just thinking of you! Last week I came across our photos! Are you well?*

**波哥：**等等……大伟也在想他？
Děngdeng… Dàwěi yě zài xiǎng tā?
*Wait… Dawei was thinking of him too?*

**莎莎：**对。大伟也很多次想发消息。
Duì. Dàwěi yě hěn duō cì xiǎng fā xiāoxi.
*Yes. Dawei had also wanted to message — many times.*

**波哥：**也是打了字，又删了？
Yě shì dǎ le zì, yòu shān le?
*Typed and deleted too?*

**莎莎：**一样的。他也怕：这么久了，会不会很奇怪？
Yíyàng de. Tā yě pà: zhème jiǔ le, huì búhuì hěn qíguài?
*Exactly the same. He too was afraid: after so long, wouldn't it be weird?*

**波哥：**原来两个人都在等。
Yuánlái liǎng ge rén dōu zài děng.
*So both of them were waiting.*

**莎莎：**两个人都怕打扰，所以两年没有一句话。
Liǎng ge rén dōu pà dǎrǎo, suǒyǐ liǎng nián méiyǒu yí jù huà.
*Both afraid of intruding — so two years passed without a word.*

**波哥：**其实谁先开口，谁就把门打开了。
Qíshí shéi xiān kāikǒu, shéi jiù bǎ mén dǎkāi le.
*Whoever speaks first opens the door.*

**莎莎：**那天晚上，他们你一句，我一句，聊到了很晚。
Nàtiān wǎnshang, tāmen nǐ yí jù, wǒ yí jù, liáo dào le hěn wǎn.
*That night, message after message, they talk late into the evening.*

**波哥：**聊什么？
Liáo shénme?
*About what?*

**莎莎：**新的城市，新的工作，还有大学时候的那些老日子。
Xīn de chéngshì, xīn de gōngzuò, háiyǒu dàxué shíhou de nàxiē lǎo rìzi.
*New cities, new jobs — and the old university days.*

**波哥：**像回到了以前。
Xiàng huídào le yǐqián.
*Like going back in time.*

**莎莎：**小文说：我们两年没说话，可是一开口，还是老样子。
Xiǎowén shuō: wǒmen liǎng nián méi shuōhuà, kěshì yì kāikǒu, háishì lǎo yàngzi.
*Xiaowen says: two years of silence — and the moment we speak, we're the same as always.*

**波哥：**好朋友就是这样。"可是"，第六集的老朋友。
Hǎo péngyou jiùshì zhèyàng. "Kěshì", dì liù jí de lǎo péngyou.
*That's good friends for you. And kěshì — an old friend from episode six.*

**莎莎：**对。最后，大伟说：下个月我去南方看你，我们见一面吧。
Duì. Zuìhòu, Dàwěi shuō: xià ge yuè wǒ qù nánfāng kàn nǐ, wǒmen jiàn yí miàn ba.
*Right. Finally Dawei says: next month I'll come south to see you — let's meet.*

**波哥：**见面！从五个字开始的见面。
Jiànmiàn! Cóng wǔ ge zì kāishǐ de jiànmiàn.
*A reunion! And it started with five characters.*

### 回想 · Recall

Answer out loud **before** Bo does.

**莎莎：**故事讲完了。我们来回想几个句子。你来答：小文看着照片，说了哪句话？
Gùshi jiǎng wán le. Wǒmen lái huíxiǎng jǐ ge jùzi. Nǐ lái dá: Xiǎowén kànzhe zhàopiàn, shuō le nǎ jù huà?
*Story's over — let's recall a few sentences. You answer: what did Xiaowen say, looking at the photo?*

**波哥：**他很久没有消息了。
Tā hěn jiǔ méiyǒu xiāoxi le.
*There's been no news from him for a long time.*

**莎莎：**小文怕什么？
Xiǎowén pà shénme?
*What was Xiaowen afraid of?*

**波哥：**怕打扰。这么久没联系，突然发消息，会不会很奇怪？
Pà dǎrǎo. Zhème jiǔ méi liánxì, tūrán fā xiāoxi, huì búhuì hěn qíguài?
*Of intruding. After so long, wouldn't a sudden message be weird?*

**莎莎：**最后他发的五个字是——
Zuìhòu tā fā de wǔ ge zì shì——
*And the five characters he finally sent were—*

**波哥：**最近怎么样？
Zuìjìn zěnmeyàng?
*How have you been lately?*

**莎莎：**大伟的回答，告诉了我们什么？
Dàwěi de huídá, gàosu le wǒmen shénme?

**波哥：**他也在想小文。两个人都在等。

### 结尾 · Ending

**莎莎：**全对。所以，今天的故事，想送你一句话。
Quán duì. Suǒyǐ, jīntiān de gùshi, xiǎng sòng nǐ yí jù huà.
*All correct. So today's story leaves you one line.*

**波哥：**什么话？
Shénme huà?
*Which line?*

**莎莎：**好朋友，不怕晚。
Hǎo péngyou, bú pà wǎn.
*A good friendship isn't afraid of "late".*

**波哥：**好朋友，不怕晚。两年不说话，一句话就回来了。
Hǎo péngyou, bú pà wǎn. Liǎng nián bù shuōhuà, yí jù huà jiù huílái le.
*Not afraid of late. Two years of silence — one sentence brings it back.*

**莎莎：**如果你心里也有一个"他很久没有消息了"的人，今天就发那五个字吧。
Rúguǒ nǐ xīnlǐ yě yǒu yí ge "tā hěn jiǔ méiyǒu xiāoxi le" de rén, jīntiān jiù fā nà wǔ ge zì ba.

**波哥：**最近怎么样？
Zuìjìn zěnmeyàng?
*How have you been lately?*

**莎莎：**别忘了你的小事：用"很久没有……了"写一句话，放在评论区。
Bié wàng le nǐ de xiǎoshì: yòng "hěn jiǔ méiyǒu… le" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.

**波哥：**下一集，我们去上班。
Xià yì jí, wǒmen qù shàngbān.
*Next episode, we go to work.*

**莎莎：**开会的时候，一句话让别人放心。
Kāihuì de shíhou, yí jù huà ràng biéren fàngxīn.

**波哥：**这件事我来跟进，有消息告诉你。
Zhè jiàn shì wǒ lái gēnjìn, yǒu xiāoxi gàosu nǐ.
