## In this episode 这一集

- One bad conversation is not a verdict: 太快的中文，不是你的水平 — too-fast Chinese was never your level.
- One tiny win can restart everything: understanding a stranger's 现在几点了.
- Old friends return: 词像朋友 (episode 1) and 说错了没关系 (episode 5).

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **今天不行，不等于明天不行。**
> Jīntiān bù xíng, bù děngyú míngtiān bù xíng.
> *Not today doesn't mean not tomorrow.*

## Pattern of the day 今日句型：不等于

A 不等于 B = *A doesn't equal B* — one fact doesn't get to decide everything.

1. 今天不行，不等于明天不行。 — *Not today doesn't mean not tomorrow.*
2. 跑不动，不等于不会走。 — *Not being able to run doesn't mean you can't walk.*
3. 说错了一句，不等于不会说。 — *One wrong sentence doesn't mean you can't speak.* (episode 5)
4. 听不懂一次，不等于学不会。 — *Not understanding once doesn't mean you can't learn.*

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**莎莎：**波哥，今天的故事，从一句话开始。
Bōgē, jīntiān de gùshi, cóng yí jù huà kāishǐ.
*Bo, today's story starts with one sentence.*

**波哥：**哪一句？
Nǎ yí jù?
*Which one?*

**莎莎：**小丁说："我不学了。"
Xiǎodīng shuō: "wǒ bù xué le."
*Xiao Ding said: "I'm done studying."*

**波哥：**不学了？不学中文了？
Bù xué le? Bù xué Zhōngwén le?
*Done? Done with Chinese?*

**莎莎：**对。他把书合上了，也不听中文了。
Duì. Tā bǎ shū héshàng le, yě bù tīng Zhōngwén le.
*Yes. He closed his book and stopped listening to Chinese.*

**波哥：**可是他学了很久吧？
Kěshì tā xué le hěn jiǔ ba?
*But he'd been learning for a long time, right?*

**莎莎：**六个月。每天都学。
Liù ge yuè. Měitiān dōu xué.
*Six months. Every single day.*

**波哥：**六个月，然后一天就不学了？
Liù ge yuè, ránhòu yì tiān jiù bù xué le?
*Six months — and then one day he just quit?*

**莎莎：**对。因为一次聊天，一次很短的聊天。
Duì. Yīnwèi yí cì liáotiān, yí cì hěn duǎn de liáotiān.
*Yes. Because of one conversation. One very short conversation.*

**波哥：**一次聊天，就让人想放弃？
Yí cì liáotiān, jiù ràng rén xiǎng fàngqì?
*One conversation can make someone want to give up?*

**莎莎：**对。可是故事没有在这里结束。
Duì. Kěshì gùshi méiyǒu zài zhèlǐ jiéshù.
*Yes. But the story doesn't end there.*

**波哥：**后来呢？
Hòulái ne?
*What happened next?*

**莎莎：**后来，一个陌生人问了他一个问题。
Hòulái, yí ge mòshēngrén wèn le tā yí ge wèntí.
*Later, a stranger asked him a question.*

**波哥：**一个问题，就把他问回来了？
Yí ge wèntí, jiù bǎ tā wèn huílái le?
*One question brought him back?*

**莎莎：**对。是什么问题？别走开。
Duì. Shì shénme wèntí? Bié zǒukāi.
*Yes. What question? Don't go away.*


### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。在这里，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Zài zhèlǐ, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*I'm Bo. Here, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**今天讲一个虚构的小故事，名字叫：失去信心的时候。
Jīntiān jiǎng yí ge xūgòu de xiǎo gùshi, míngzi jiào: shīqù xìnxīn de shíhou.
*Today, a little fictional story called: when you lose confidence.*

**波哥：**小丁不是真的人，可是他的感觉，很多人都有过。开始吧。
Xiǎodīng búshì zhēnde rén, kěshì tā de gǎnjué, hěnduō rén dōu yǒu guò. Kāishǐ ba.
*Xiao Ding isn't real, but many of us know exactly how he feels. Let's start.*


### 六个月的信心 · Six months of confidence

**莎莎：**小丁学中文六个月了。
Xiǎodīng xué Zhōngwén liù ge yuè le.
*Xiao Ding had been learning Chinese for six months.*

**波哥：**他学得怎么样？
Tā xué de zěnmeyàng?
*How was he doing?*

**莎莎：**不错。他每天听一点，也每天说几句。
Búcuò. Tā měitiān tīng yìdiǎn, yě měitiān shuō jǐ jù.
*Not bad. A little listening every day, a few spoken sentences every day.*

**波哥：**每天听一点——老朋友的办法。
Měitiān tīng yìdiǎn——lǎo péngyou de bànfǎ.
*A little every day — our old friend of a method.*

**莎莎：**对。他觉得自己越来越好。
Duì. Tā juéde zìjǐ yuè lái yuè hǎo.
*Right. He felt he was getting better and better.*

**波哥：**越听越懂，越懂越想学。
Yuè tīng yuè dǒng, yuè dǒng yuè xiǎng xué.
*The more you listen, the more you understand — and the more you want to learn.*

**莎莎：**所以他心里有了一点信心。
Suǒyǐ tā xīnlǐ yǒu le yìdiǎn xìnxīn.
*So he had a little confidence now.*

**波哥：**信心，就是心里觉得"我可以"。
Xìnxīn, jiùshì xīnlǐ juéde "wǒ kěyǐ".
*Confidence — that feeling inside of "I can do this".*

**莎莎：**对。有信心的时候，人想开口。
Duì. Yǒu xìnxīn de shíhou, rén xiǎng kāikǒu.
*Yes. With confidence, you want to speak.*

**波哥：**没有信心的时候，嘴就关上了。
Méiyǒu xìnxīn de shíhou, zuǐ jiù guānshàng le.
*Without it, the mouth stays shut.*


### 小任务 · Your turn

**莎莎：**说得好。故事讲下去以前，请你先做一件小事。
Shuō de hǎo. Gùshi jiǎng xiàqù yǐqián, qǐng nǐ xiān zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì.
*Well said. Before the story goes on, one small thing first.*

**波哥：**评论区时间到了。
Pínglùnqū shíjiān dào le.
*Comment-section time.*

**莎莎：**对。用"信心"写一句话，放在评论区。
Duì. Yòng "xìnxīn" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Yes. Write one sentence with "xìnxīn" — confidence — in the comments.*

**波哥：**我先来：我对我的中文，有一点信心了。
Wǒ xiān lái: wǒ duì wǒ de Zhōngwén, yǒu yìdiǎn xìnxīn le.
*Me first: I have a little confidence in my Chinese now.*

**莎莎：**很好。你也写一句，我们会看，也会回你。
Hěn hǎo. Nǐ yě xiě yí jù, wǒmen huì kàn, yě huì huí nǐ.
*Nice. Write yours too — we read and reply.*


### 那次聚会 · The party

**波哥：**好，回到故事。那天发生了什么？
Hǎo, huídào gùshi. Nàtiān fāshēng le shénme?
*Okay, back to the story. What happened that day?*

**莎莎：**那天，朋友带小丁去一个聚会，那里有几个中国人。
Nàtiān, péngyou dài Xiǎodīng qù yí ge jùhuì, nàlǐ yǒu jǐ ge Zhōngguórén.
*That day, a friend took Xiao Ding to a get-together, and there were several Chinese people there.*

**波哥：**练习的好机会啊！
Liànxí de hǎo jīhuì a!
*A great chance to practise!*

**莎莎：**小丁也这么想。他走过去，说：你好！
Xiǎodīng yě zhème xiǎng. Tā zǒu guòqù, shuō: nǐ hǎo!
*That's what he thought. He walked over and said: hello!*

**波哥：**开始得很好啊。
Kāishǐ de hěn hǎo a.
*A good start.*

**莎莎：**对方很高兴，就跟他聊了起来。可是，说得很快。
Duìfāng hěn gāoxìng, jiù gēn tā liáo le qǐlái. Kěshì, shuō de hěn kuài.
*They were delighted and started chatting with him. But — fast.*

**波哥：**多快？
Duō kuài?
*How fast?*

**莎莎：**小丁一个词都抓不住。他听到的不是词，是一条很快的河。
Xiǎodīng yí ge cí dōu zhuā bu zhù. Tā tīngdào de búshì cí, shì yì tiáo hěn kuài de hé.
*He couldn't catch a single word. He didn't hear words — he heard a fast river.*

**波哥：**词都在水里，抓不住。这个感觉，我太知道了。
Cí dōu zài shuǐlǐ, zhuā bu zhù. Zhège gǎnjué, wǒ tài zhīdào le.
*Words in the water, impossible to catch. I know that feeling all too well.*

**莎莎：**小丁想说"请说慢一点"，可是他太紧张了，说不出来。
Xiǎodīng xiǎng shuō "qǐng shuō màn yìdiǎn", kěshì tā tài jǐnzhāng le, shuō bu chūlái.
*He wanted to say "please speak slower", but he was too nervous to get it out.*

**波哥：**越紧张，越说不出来。
Yuè jǐnzhāng, yuè shuō bu chūlái.
*The more nervous you are, the less comes out.*

**莎莎：**对方看他没听懂，笑了笑，换成了英文。
Duìfāng kàn tā méi tīngdǒng, xiào le xiào, huànchéng le Yīngwén.
*Seeing he didn't understand, they smiled and switched to English.*

**波哥：**哦……换成英文了。
Ó…… huànchéng Yīngwén le.
*Oh… switched to English.*

**莎莎：**对方不是坏意思，他只是想帮忙。
Duìfāng búshì huài yìsi, tā zhǐshì xiǎng bāngmáng.
*They meant nothing bad — they just wanted to help.*

**波哥：**可是对小丁来说，这句英文很重。
Kěshì duì Xiǎodīng lái shuō, zhè jù Yīngwén hěn zhòng.
*But to Xiao Ding, that English sentence felt heavy.*

**莎莎：**很重。小丁心里想：六个月，白学了。
Hěn zhòng. Xiǎodīng xīnlǐ xiǎng: liù ge yuè, bái xué le.
*Very heavy. He thought: six months, all for nothing.*

**波哥：**白学了，就是学了很久，好像没有用。
Bái xué le, jiùshì xué le hěn jiǔ, hǎoxiàng méiyǒu yòng.
*"Bái xué le" — you studied a long time, and it seems useless.*

**莎莎：**那天晚上，小丁回到家，把书合上了。他说：我不行，我不学了。
Nàtiān wǎnshang, Xiǎodīng huídào jiā, bǎ shū héshàng le. Tā shuō: wǒ bù xíng, wǒ bù xué le.
*That night he went home and closed the book. He said: I can't do this. I quit.*


### 不碰中文的一个星期 · The empty week

**波哥：**然后呢？他真的放弃了？
Ránhòu ne? Tā zhēnde fàngqì le?
*And then? Did he really give up?*

**莎莎：**一个星期，他没有碰中文。不听，也不说。
Yí ge xīngqī, tā méiyǒu pèng Zhōngwén. Bù tīng, yě bù shuō.
*For one week he didn't touch Chinese. No listening, no speaking.*

**波哥：**放弃的时候，人先觉得轻松，然后觉得难过。
Fàngqì de shíhou, rén xiān juéde qīngsōng, ránhòu juéde nánguò.
*When you give up, first you feel relieved — then you feel sad.*

**莎莎：**对。他轻松了两天，难过了五天。
Duì. Tā qīngsōng le liǎng tiān, nánguò le wǔ tiān.
*Exactly. Two days of relief, five days of sadness.*

**波哥：**为什么难过？因为他其实很喜欢中文吧。
Wèishénme nánguò? Yīnwèi tā qíshí hěn xǐhuan Zhōngwén ba.
*Why sad? Because he actually loves Chinese, right?*

**莎莎：**对。放弃一个不喜欢的东西很容易；放弃一个喜欢的东西，很难。
Duì. Fàngqì yí ge bù xǐhuan de dōngxi hěn róngyì; fàngqì yí ge xǐhuan de dōngxi, hěn nán.
*Yes. Quitting something you don't love is easy; quitting something you love is hard.*

**波哥：**他有没有想起那句老话：说错了没关系？
Tā yǒu méiyǒu xiǎngqǐ nà jù lǎohuà: shuōcuò le méi guānxi?
*Did he remember the old line: it's okay to say it wrong?*

**莎莎：**想起了。可是他对自己说：我不是说错，我是全都听不懂。
Xiǎngqǐ le. Kěshì tā duì zìjǐ shuō: wǒ búshì shuōcuò, wǒ shì quán dōu tīng bu dǒng.
*He did. But he told himself: I didn't say it wrong — I understood nothing.*

**波哥：**嗯，这次他觉得问题更大。
Ǹg, zhè cì tā juéde wèntí gèng dà.
*Mm, this time the problem felt bigger.*

**莎莎：**他还想：别人三个月就会说了，我六个月还不行。
Tā hái xiǎng: biéren sān ge yuè jiù huì shuō le, wǒ liù ge yuè hái bù xíng.
*He also thought: others can speak after three months; I can't after six.*

**波哥：**又开始和别人比较了。越比较，心里越难受。
Yòu kāishǐ hé biéren bǐjiào le. Yuè bǐjiào, xīnlǐ yuè nánshòu.
*Comparing himself with others again. The more you compare, the worse it feels.*

**莎莎：**第十集我们说过：别人的三个月，不是你的三个月。
Dìshíjí wǒmen shuō guò: biéren de sānge yuè, búshì nǐ de sānge yuè.
*Episode 10 said it: someone else's three months are not your three months.*

**波哥：**对，每个人有自己的路。可是难过的时候，人会忘。
Duì, měige rényǒu zìjǐ de lù. Kěshì nánguò de shíhou, rénhuì wàng.
*Right, everyone has their own road. But when you're down, you forget.*

**莎莎：**对。可是晚上走路的时候，他还是会想起几个中文词。
Duì. Kěshì wǎnshang zǒulù de shíhou, tā háishì huì xiǎngqǐ jǐ ge Zhōngwén cí.
*Yes. But at night, out walking, a few Chinese words would still come to him.*

**波哥：**词像朋友。他不找它们，它们也没有走。
Cí xiàng péngyou. Tā bù zhǎo tāmen, tāmen yě méiyǒu zǒu.
*Words are like friends. He wasn't looking for them, but they never left.*

**莎莎：**说得真好，朋友还在。好，现在，那个陌生人来了。
Shuō de zhēn hǎo, péngyou hái zài. Hǎo, xiànzài, nàge mòshēngrén lái le.
*Beautifully said — the friends were still there. And now, here comes the stranger.*


### 超市里的一句话 · One sentence in the supermarket

**莎莎：**一个星期以后，小丁去超市买东西。
Yí ge xīngqī yǐhòu, Xiǎodīng qù chāoshì mǎi dōngxi.
*A week later, Xiao Ding went to the supermarket.*

**波哥：**然后呢？
Ránhòu ne?
*And then?*

**莎莎：**他前面有一位陌生的老人，转过来问他：现在几点了？
Tā qiánmiàn yǒu yí wèi mòshēng de lǎorén, zhuǎn guòlái wèn tā: xiànzài jǐ diǎn le?
*An elderly stranger in front of him turned around and asked: what time is it?*

**波哥：**用中文问的？
Yòng Zhōngwén wèn de?
*In Chinese?*

**莎莎：**对，说得很自然，也不快。
Duì, shuō de hěn zìrán, yě bú kuài.
*Yes — very naturally, and not fast.*

**波哥：**小丁听懂了吗？
Xiǎodīng tīngdǒng le ma?
*Did he understand?*

**莎莎：**他没有想"我要听懂"，他直接说了：三点半。
Tā méiyǒu xiǎng "wǒ yào tīngdǒng", tā zhíjiē shuō le: sān diǎn bàn.
*He didn't think "I must understand". He simply said: half past three.*

**波哥：**等等——他直接就答了？
Děngdeng——tā zhíjiē jiù dá le?
*Wait — he just answered?*

**莎莎：**对。先答了，然后才发现：我听懂了，我还答对了。
Duì. Xiān dá le, ránhòu cái fāxiàn: wǒ tīngdǒng le, wǒ hái dá duì le.
*Yes. Answered first, realised after: I understood — and I got it right.*

**波哥：**哈哈，嘴比脑子快。
Hāhā, zuǐ bǐ nǎozi kuài.
*Haha, mouth faster than brain.*

**莎莎：**对。六个月的中文，都在他身体里。
Duì. Liù ge yuè de Zhōngwén, dōu zài tā shēntǐ lǐ.
*Right. Six months of Chinese, all still inside him.*

**波哥：**它们没有走，只是在等一个说话的机会。
Tāmen méiyǒu zǒu, zhǐshì zài děng yí ge shuōhuà de jīhuì.
*It never left — it was just waiting for a chance to speak.*

**莎莎：**老人说了声谢谢，就走了。她不知道，这句话对小丁有多重要。
Lǎorén shuō le shēng xièxie, jiù zǒu le. Tā bù zhīdào, zhè jù huà duì Xiǎodīng yǒu duō zhòngyào.
*She said thanks and left. She'll never know how much that one sentence meant to him.*

**波哥：**一件很小很小的事。
Yí jiàn hěn xiǎo hěn xiǎo de shì.
*Such a small, small thing.*

**莎莎：**很小。可是小丁站在那里，想了很久。
Hěn xiǎo. Kěshì Xiǎodīng zhàn zài nàlǐ, xiǎng le hěn jiǔ.
*Tiny. But Xiao Ding stood there and thought for a long time.*

**波哥：**他想明白了什么？
Tā xiǎng míngbai le shénme?
*What did he figure out?*

**莎莎：**他想：聚会上的中文太快了，那不是我的水平。
Tā xiǎng: jùhuì shàng de Zhōngwén tài kuài le, nà búshì wǒ de shuǐpíng.
*He thought: the Chinese at that party was too fast. That was never my level.*

**波哥：**就像让刚学走路的人去跑步。
Jiù xiàng ràng gāng xué zǒulù de rén qù pǎobù.
*Like asking someone who just learned to walk to go running.*

**莎莎：**对。跑不动，不等于不会走。
Duì. Pǎo bu dòng, bù děngyú bú huì zǒu.
*Right. Not being able to run doesn't mean you can't walk.*

**波哥：**这句话，像第五集说过的：说错了一句，不等于不会说。
Zhè jù huà, xiàng dì wǔ jí shuō guò de: shuōcuò le yí jù, bù děngyú bú huì shuō.
*Like episode five said: one wrong sentence doesn't mean you can't speak.*

**莎莎：**对。今天，小丁给自己写了一句新的。
Duì. Jīntiān, Xiǎodīng gěi zìjǐ xiě le yí jù xīn de.
*Yes. And that day, Xiao Ding wrote himself a new one.*


### 今天不行，不等于明天不行 · Not today, not never

**波哥：**哪一句？
Nǎ yí jù?
*Which one?*

**莎莎：**今天不行，不等于明天不行。
Jīntiān bù xíng, bù děngyú míngtiān bù xíng.
*Not today doesn't mean not tomorrow.*

**波哥：**今天不行，不等于明天不行。
Jīntiān bù xíng, bù děngyú míngtiān bù xíng.
*Not today doesn't mean not tomorrow.*

**莎莎：**前面是"今天不行"——那次聚会，是真的难，不用假装不难。
Qiánmiàn shì "jīntiān bù xíng"——nà cì jùhuì, shì zhēnde nán, búyòng jiǎzhuāng bù nán.
*First half: "not today" — that party really was hard, and there's no need to pretend it wasn't.*

**波哥：**后面是"不等于明天不行"——明天还在。
Hòumiàn shì "bù děngyú míngtiān bù xíng"——míngtiān hái zài.
*Second half: "doesn't mean not tomorrow" — tomorrow is still there.*

**莎莎：**小丁最需要的，其实就是这样一句听得懂的中文。
Xiǎodīng zuì xūyào de, qíshí jiùshì zhèyàng yí jù tīng de dǒng de Zhōngwén.
*What Xiao Ding needed most was exactly this: one sentence of Chinese he could understand.*

**波哥：**对。太快的中文让人想放弃，听得懂的中文让人想继续。
Duì. Tài kuài de Zhōngwén ràng rén xiǎng fàngqì, tīng de dǒng de Zhōngwén ràng rén xiǎng jìxù.
*Right. Chinese that's too fast makes you want to quit; Chinese you understand makes you want to continue.*

**莎莎：**如果你也想每个星期都有听得懂的中文，就订阅慢慢中文。
Rúguǒ nǐ yě xiǎng měi ge xīngqī dōu yǒu tīng de dǒng de Zhōngwén, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*If you'd like Chinese you can understand every week too, subscribe to Manman Zhongwen.*

**波哥：**我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。信心，就是这样，一点一点回来的。
Wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng. Xìnxīn, jiùshì zhèyàng, yìdiǎn yìdiǎn huílái de.
*We speak slowly, you listen slowly. That's how confidence comes back — bit by bit.*

**莎莎：**好，回到故事的最后。那天晚上，小丁把书打开了。
Hǎo, huídào gùshi de zuìhòu. Nàtiān wǎnshang, Xiǎodīng bǎ shū dǎkāi le.
*Okay — the end of the story. That night, Xiao Ding opened the book again.*

**波哥：**他做了什么？
Tā zuò le shénme?
*What did he do?*

**莎莎：**他把那句话写在本子的第一页：今天不行，不等于明天不行。
Tā bǎ nà jù huà xiě zài běnzi de dì yī yè: jīntiān bù xíng, bù děngyú míngtiān bù xíng.
*He wrote that sentence on the first page of his notebook: not today doesn't mean not tomorrow.*

**波哥：**第二天呢？
Dì èr tiān ne?
*And the next day?*

**莎莎：**第二天，他又开始每天听一点。慢慢来，不跟别人比。
Dì èr tiān, tā yòu kāishǐ měitiān tīng yìdiǎn. Mànmàn lái, bù gēn biéren bǐ.
*The next day he went back to listening a little every day. Slowly — and no comparing.*

**波哥：**还是那个老办法。
Háishì nàge lǎobànfǎ.
*The same old method.*

**莎莎：**对，好办法不用换新的。
Duì, hǎo bànfǎ búyòng huàn xīn de.
*Right. A good method doesn't need replacing.*

**波哥：**信心全回来了吗？
Xìnxīn quán huílái le ma?
*Did his confidence all come back?*

**莎莎：**回来了一点。不是全部，是一点。
Huílái le yìdiǎn. Búshì quánbù, shì yìdiǎn.
*A little of it. Not all — a little.*

**波哥：**一点就够了。信心像天气，会走，也会回来。
Yìdiǎn jiù gòu le. Xìnxīn xiàng tiānqì, huì zǒu, yě huì huílái.
*A little is enough. Confidence is like weather: it leaves, and it comes back.*

**莎莎：**对。信心走了的时候，别把门关上，明天它认识路。
Duì. Xìnxīn zǒu le de shíhou, bié bǎ mén guānshàng, míngtiān tā rènshi lù.
*Yes. When confidence leaves, don't lock the door — tomorrow it knows the way home.*


### 快问快答 · Speed round

Answer out loud **before** Bo does.

**波哥：**好，最后，快问快答！这次都是故事里的问题。
Hǎo, zuìhòu, kuài wèn kuài dá! Zhè cì dōu shì gùshi lǐ de wèntí.
*Okay, finally: speed round! All questions from the story.*

**莎莎：**这次你来答！我问。
Zhè cì nǐ lái dá! Wǒ wèn.
*You answer this time! I'll ask.*

**波哥：**好，你问。
Hǎo, nǐ wèn.
*Fine — ask away.*

**莎莎：**小丁学了多久的中文？
Xiǎodīng xué le duōjiǔ de Zhōngwén?
*How long had Xiao Ding been learning Chinese?*

**波哥：**六个月。
Liù ge yuè.
*Six months.*

**莎莎：**聚会上，他为什么听不懂？
Jùhuì shàng, tā wèishénme tīng bu dǒng?
*At the party, why couldn't he understand?*

**波哥：**因为对方说得太快。那不是他的水平。
Yīnwèi duìfāng shuō de tài kuài. Nà búshì tā de shuǐpíng.
*Because they spoke too fast. That wasn't his level.*

**莎莎：**那位陌生的老人，问了他什么？
Nà wèi mòshēng de lǎorén, wèn le tā shénme?
*What did that stranger ask him?*

**波哥：**现在几点了？
Xiànzài jǐ diǎn le?
*What time is it?*

**莎莎：**他答了什么？
Tā dá le shénme?
*And he answered?*

**波哥：**三点半！
Sān diǎn bàn!
*Half past three!*

**莎莎：**六个月的中文，白学了吗？
Liùgeyuè de Zhōngwén, báixué le ma?
*Six months of Chinese, all wasted?*

**波哥：**没有白学，它们都在身体里。
Méiyǒu báixué, tāmen dōu zài shēntǐ lǐ.
*Not wasted. It's all still in you.*

**莎莎：**跑不动，等于不会走吗？
Pǎo bu dòng, děngyú bú huì zǒu ma?
*Not being able to run — does that mean you can't walk?*

**波哥：**不等于！
Bù děngyú!
*It doesn't!*

**莎莎：**那，今天不行呢？
Nà, jīntiān bù xíng ne?
*And "not today"?*

**波哥：**今天不行，不等于明天不行！
Jīntiān bù xíng, bù děngyú míngtiān bù xíng!
*Not today doesn't mean not tomorrow!*

**莎莎：**全对！这句话，现在也是你的了。
Quán duì! Zhè jù huà, xiànzài yě shì nǐ de le.
*All correct! That sentence is yours now too.*


### 结尾 · Ending

**波哥：**好，今天的故事说了：信心会走，也会回来。
Hǎo, jīntiān de gùshi shuō le: xìnxīn huì zǒu, yě huì huílái.
*So — today's story said: confidence leaves, and it comes back.*

**莎莎：**太快太难的中文，不是你的失败，只是那不是你今天的路。
Tài kuài tài nán de Zhōngwén, búshì nǐ de shībài, zhǐshì nà búshì nǐ jīntiān de lù.
*Chinese that's too fast and too hard isn't your failure — it's just not your road today.*

**波哥：**去找一句你听得懂的话，那才是你真实的水平。
Qù zhǎo yí jù nǐ tīng de dǒng de huà, nà cái shì nǐ zhēnshí de shuǐpíng.
*Go find one sentence you do understand. That's your real level.*

**莎莎：**还有最重要的一句：今天不行，不等于明天不行。
Háiyǒu zuì zhòngyào de yí jù: jīntiān bù xíng, bù děngyú míngtiān bù xíng.
*And the sentence that matters most: not today doesn't mean not tomorrow.*

**波哥：**别忘了你的小事：用"信心"写一句话，放在评论区。
Bié wàng le nǐ de xiǎo shì: yòng "xìnxīn" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Don't forget your small task: one "xìnxīn" sentence in the comments.*

**莎莎：**下一集，我们去一个很热闹的地方。
Xià yì jí, wǒmen qù yí ge hěn rènao de dìfang.
*Next episode, we're going somewhere very lively.*

**波哥：**什么地方？
Shénme dìfang?
*Where?*

**莎莎：**中国人的家庭聚餐。一张大桌子，很多菜，还有一句你一定会听到的话。
Zhōngguórén de jiātíng jùcān. Yì zhāng dà zhuōzi, hěnduō cài, háiyǒu yí jù nǐ yídìng huì tīngdào de huà.
*A Chinese family dinner. One big table, lots of dishes — and one sentence you're guaranteed to hear.*

**波哥：**什么话？
Shénme huà?
*What sentence?*

**莎莎：**多吃一点，别客气！
Duō chī yìdiǎn, bié kèqi!
*Eat a little more — don't be polite!*

**波哥：**哈哈，我已经饿了。我们下一集见！
Hāhā, wǒ yǐjīng è le. Wǒmen xià yì jí jiàn!
*Haha, I'm already hungry. See you next episode!*

**莎莎：**下次见。慢慢来。
Xiàcì jiàn. Mànmàn lái.
*See you next time. Take it slow.*
