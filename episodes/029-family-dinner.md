## In this episode 这一集

- 我的碗，我们的菜 — dishes are shared from the middle; the rice bowl is your own.
- 多吃一点，别客气 is a love language, not a command — and 夹菜 is an unspoken 我爱你.
- Three small guest things, and the two replies that always work: 太好吃了 / 够了够了，我吃饱了.

**Target sentence 目标句**

> **多吃一点，别客气。**
> Duō chī yìdiǎn, bié kèqi.
> *Eat a little more — make yourself at home.*

| 随便 | suíbiàn | relaxed, casual |

## Pattern of the day 今日句型：别……

别 + verb = *don't…* — soft, friendly, spoken every day.

1. 别客气。 — *Don't be polite — make yourself at home.*
2. 别紧张。 — *Don't be nervous.*
3. 别走开。 — *Don't go away.*
4. 别忘了你的小事。 — *Don't forget your small task.*

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**莎莎：**波哥，问你一件事。你去中国朋友的家里吃过饭吗？
Bōgē, wèn nǐ yí jiàn shì. Nǐ qù Zhōngguó péngyou de jiālǐ chī guò fàn ma?
*Bo, a question: have you ever eaten at a Chinese friend's home?*

**波哥：**去过一次。那天我又开心，又紧张。
Qùguò yí cì. Nàtiān wǒ yòu kāixīn, yòu jǐnzhāng.
*Once. That day I was happy and nervous at the same time.*

**莎莎：**紧张什么？
Jǐnzhāng shénme?
*Nervous about what?*

**波哥：**桌子上有十个菜，我不知道该做什么。
Zhuōzi shàng yǒu shí ge cài, wǒ bù zhīdào gāi zuò shénme.
*Ten dishes on the table, and I had no idea what to do.*

**莎莎：**坐哪里，先吃什么，都不知道？
Zuò nǎlǐ, xiān chī shénme, dōu bù zhīdào?
*Where to sit, what to eat first — no idea?*

**波哥：**对！还有，他们一直对我说一句话。
Duì! Háiyǒu, tāmen yìzhí duì wǒ shuō yí jù huà.
*Exactly! And they kept saying one sentence to me.*

**莎莎：**哪一句？
Nǎ yí jù?
*Which one?*

**波哥：**多吃一点，别客气！
Duō chī yìdiǎn, bié kèqi!
*Eat a little more — don't be polite!*

**莎莎：**哈哈，这句话，今天我们就好好聊一聊。
Hāhā, zhè jù huà, jīntiān wǒmen jiù hǎohǎo liáo yi liáo.
*Haha — that's the sentence we're unpacking today.*

**波哥：**它不就是让我多吃吗？
Tā bú jiùshì ràng wǒ duō chī ma?
*Doesn't it just mean "eat more"?*

**莎莎：**不只是。它的意思，比"吃"多得多。
Bù zhǐshì. Tā de yìsi, bǐ "chī" duō de duō.
*Not only. It means much more than "eat".*

**波哥：**比"吃"多得多？
Bǐ "chī" duō de duō?
*Much more than "eat"?*

**莎莎：**对。听懂了这句话，你就听懂了中国人的饭桌。
Duì. Tīngdǒng le zhè jù huà, nǐ jiù tīngdǒng le Zhōngguórén de fànzhuō.
*Yes. Understand this sentence, and you understand the Chinese dinner table.*

**波哥：**那怎么做一个让人喜欢的客人呢？
Nà zěnme zuò yí ge ràng rén xǐhuan de kèrén ne?
*And how do I become a guest people love?*

**莎莎：**三件小事，就够了。今天也教你。
Sān jiàn xiǎo shì, jiù gòu le. Jīntiān yě jiào nǐ.
*Three small things — that's all. We'll teach you today.*

**波哥：**好，我要学。别走开。
Hǎo, wǒ yào xué. Bié zǒukāi.
*Great, I want to learn. Don't go away.*


### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。在这里，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Zài zhèlǐ, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*I'm Bo. Here, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**今天聊一个文化话题：中国人的家庭聚餐。
Jīntiān liáo yí ge wénhuà huàtí: Zhōngguórén de jiātíng jùcān.
*Today, a culture topic: the Chinese family dinner.*

**波哥：**聚餐，就是一家人或者朋友，一起吃一顿大饭。开始吧。
Jùcān, jiùshì yì jiā rén huòzhě péngyou, yìqǐ chī yí dùn dà fàn. Kāishǐ ba.
*Jùcān — family or friends eating one big meal together. Let's start.*


### 我的碗，我们的菜 · My bowl, our dishes

**莎莎：**先说桌子。家庭聚餐的菜，都放在桌子的中间。
Xiān shuō zhuōzi. Jiātíng jùcān de cài, dōu fàng zài zhuōzi de zhōngjiān.
*First, the table. At a family dinner, the dishes all go in the middle.*

**波哥：**不是一人一个盘子？
Búshì yì rén yí ge pánzi?
*Not one plate per person?*

**莎莎：**不是。菜是大家的，饭碗是自己的。
Búshì. Cài shì dàjiā de, fànwǎn shì zìjǐ de.
*No. The dishes belong to everyone; the rice bowl is your own.*

**波哥：**我的碗，我们的菜。
Wǒ de wǎn, wǒmen de cài.
*My bowl, our dishes.*

**莎莎：**说得好。还有，常常有人用筷子拿菜，放进你的碗里。
Shuō de hǎo. Háiyǒu, chángcháng yǒu rén yòng kuàizi ná cài, fàng jìn nǐ de wǎn lǐ.
*Well put. And often, someone picks up food with their chopsticks and puts it in your bowl.*

**波哥：**这个我见过！这个动作叫什么？
Zhège wǒ jiàn guò! Zhège dòngzuò jiào shénme?
*I've seen that! What's that move called?*

**莎莎：**叫夹菜。夹，就是用筷子拿。
Jiào jiācài. Jiā, jiùshì yòng kuàizi ná.
*Jiā cài. Jiā means picking something up with chopsticks.*

**波哥：**夹菜。可是，为什么不让我自己拿呢？
Jiācài. Kěshì, wèishénme bú ràng wǒ zìjǐ ná ne?
*Jiā cài. But why not let me take it myself?*

**莎莎：**因为在很多家庭，夹菜不只是夹菜，是"我爱你"。
Yīnwèi zài hěnduō jiātíng, jiācài bù zhǐshì jiācài, shì "wǒ ài nǐ".
*Because in many families, jiā cài isn't just serving food — it's "I love you".*

**波哥：**哦……很多人不好意思说"我爱你"，就用菜来说。
Ó…… hěnduō rén bù hǎoyìsi shuō "wǒ ài nǐ", jiù yòng cài lái shuō.
*Oh… people too shy to say "I love you" say it with food.*

**莎莎：**对。所以"多吃一点，别客气"，也不只是让你吃。
Duì. Suǒyǐ "duō chī yìdiǎn, bié kèqi", yě bù zhǐshì ràng nǐ chī.
*Right. So "eat a little more, don't be polite" isn't only about eating either.*

**波哥：**它的意思是：你来了，我很高兴。
Tā de yìsi shì: nǐ lái le, wǒ hěn gāoxìng.
*It means: you came, and I'm happy.*


### 小任务 · Your turn

**莎莎：**对，是关心，不是命令。现在，请你做一件小事。
Duì, shì guānxīn, búshì mìnglìng. Xiànzài, qǐng nǐ zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì.
*Exactly — it's care, not a command. Now, one small thing for you.*

**波哥：**评论区时间到了。
Pínglùnqū shíjiān dào le.
*Comment-section time.*

**莎莎：**对。用"别客气"写一句话，放在评论区。
Duì. Yòng "bié kèqi" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Yes. Write one sentence with "bié kèqi" in the comments.*

**波哥：**我先来：来我家吃饭，别客气！
Wǒ xiān lái: lái wǒ jiā chīfàn, bié kèqi!
*Me first: come eat at my place — don't be polite!*

**莎莎：**很好。你也写一句，我们会看，也会回你。
Hěn hǎo. Nǐ yě xiě yí jù, wǒmen huì kàn, yě huì huí nǐ.
*Nice. Write yours too — we read and reply.*


### 做客三件小事 · Three small guest things

**波哥：**好。那我去做客的时候，应该做什么？
Hǎo. Nà wǒ qù zuòkè de shíhou, yīnggāi zuò shénme?
*Okay. So when I go as a guest, what should I do?*

**莎莎：**三件小事，就够了。第一件：手里带一点东西。
Sān jiàn xiǎo shì, jiù gòu le. Dì yī jiàn: shǒulǐ dài yìdiǎn dōngxi.
*Three small things, that's all. First: bring a little something.*

**波哥：**带什么好？
Dài shénme hǎo?
*What's good to bring?*

**莎莎：**不用贵的。带一点水果，就很好。
Búyòng guì de. Dài yìdiǎn shuǐguǒ, jiù hěn hǎo.
*Nothing expensive. A little fruit is perfect.*

**波哥：**一盒苹果，可以吗？
Yì hé píngguǒ, kěyǐ ma?
*A box of apples — okay?*

**莎莎：**很好。意思是：谢谢你请我来。
Hěn hǎo. Yìsi shì: xièxie nǐ qǐng wǒ lái.
*Perfect. It says: thank you for having me.*

**波哥：**要不要带很贵的礼物？
Yào búyào dài hěn guì de lǐwù?
*Should I bring a really expensive gift?*

**莎莎：**不用。家庭聚餐不是看礼物，是看你来不来。人到了，心就到了。
Búyòng. Jiātíng jùcān búshì kàn lǐwù, shì kàn nǐ lái bu lái. Rén dào le, xīn jiù dào le.
*No need. A family dinner isn't about the gift — it's about whether you come. When you arrive, your heart arrives.*

**波哥：**东西小，意思大。
Dōngxi xiǎo, yìsi dà.
*Small thing, big meaning.*

**莎莎：**对。第二件：吃的时候，说"好吃"。
Duì. Dì èr jiàn: chī de shíhou, shuō "hǎochī".
*Right. Second: while you eat, say "hǎochī" — delicious.*

**波哥：**就这么简单？
Jiù zhème jiǎndān?
*That simple?*

**莎莎：**对。做饭的人最想听的，就是这一句。
Duì. Zuòfàn de rén zuì xiǎng tīng de, jiùshì zhè yí jù.
*Yes. It's the one sentence the cook most wants to hear.*

**波哥：**太好吃了！——这样说可以吗？
Tài hǎochī le!——Zhèyàng shuō kěyǐ ma?
*太好吃了! — said like that?*

**莎莎：**太可以了。你说"太好吃了"，做饭的人一个晚上都开心。
Tài kěyǐ le. Nǐ shuō "tài hǎochī le", zuòfàn de rén yí ge wǎnshang dōu kāixīn.
*Exactly like that. Say it, and the cook is happy all evening.*

**波哥：**第三件呢？
Dì sān jiàn ne?
*And the third thing?*

**莎莎：**第三件最小：碗里的饭，尽量吃完。
Dì sān jiàn zuì xiǎo: wǎn lǐ de fàn, jǐnliàng chī wán.
*The smallest one: try to finish the rice in your bowl.*

**波哥：**为什么呢？
Wèishénme ne?
*Why?*

**莎莎：**很多长辈觉得，饭很重要，剩下太多，有一点可惜。
Hěnduō zhǎngbèi juéde, fàn hěn zhòngyào, shèngxià tài duō, yǒu yìdiǎn kěxī.
*Many elders feel rice really matters; leaving too much behind feels a little wasteful.*

**波哥：**长辈，就是家里年纪大的人。
Zhǎngbèi, jiùshì jiālǐ niánjì dà de rén.
*Elders — the older people in the family.*

**莎莎：**对。你可能想问：吃不下怎么办？
Duì. Nǐ kěnéng xiǎng wèn: chī bu xià zěnme bàn?
*Right. Now you may want to ask: what if I can't finish?*

**波哥：**对啊，我饭量小。
Duì a, wǒ fànliàng xiǎo.
*Yes — I have a small appetite.*

**莎莎：**办法很简单：一开始少拿一点。吃多少，拿多少。
Bànfǎ hěn jiǎndān: yì kāishǐ shǎo ná yìdiǎn. Chī duōshao, ná duōshao.
*Easy: take a little at first. Take only as much as you'll eat.*

**波哥：**吃完了再拿，就不会剩。
Chī wán le zài ná, jiù bú huì shèng.
*Finish it, then take more — nothing gets left behind.*

**莎莎：**对。记住，这不是很大的规矩，别紧张。
Duì. Jìzhù, zhè búshì hěn dà de guīju, bié jǐnzhāng.
*Exactly. And remember: this isn't some big rule. Don't stress.*

**波哥：**对了，还有一个问题：我该坐哪里？
Duì le, háiyǒu yí ge wèntí: wǒ gāi zuò nǎlǐ?
*Oh — one more question: where do I sit?*

**莎莎：**好问题。不同的家不一样：有的家很在意，有的家随便坐。
Hǎo wèntí. Bùtóng de jiā bù yíyàng: yǒu de jiā hěn zàiyì, yǒu de jiā suíbiàn zuò.
*Good question. Families differ: some care about it, some sit wherever.*

**波哥：**那我怎么办？
Nà wǒ zěnme bàn?
*So what do I do?*

**莎莎：**最简单的办法：等一等，让主人告诉你。
Zuì jiǎndān de bànfǎ: děng yi děng, ràng zhǔrén gàosu nǐ.
*The simplest way: wait a moment, and let the host tell you.*

**波哥：**主人说"你坐这里"，我就坐这里。
Zhǔrén shuō "nǐ zuò zhèlǐ", wǒ jiù zuò zhèlǐ.
*The host says "sit here", and I sit there.*

**莎莎：**对。一般来说，最好的位子，会留给长辈。你等一等，就不会错。
Duì. Yìbān lái shuō, zuìhǎo de wèizi, huì liú gěi zhǎngbèi. Nǐ děng yi děng, jiù bú huì cuò.
*Yes. Usually the best seat is kept for the elders. Wait a moment and you can't go wrong.*


### 够了够了，我吃饱了 · The friendly dance

**波哥：**好。现在，最重要的问题来了。
Hǎo. Xiànzài, zuì zhòngyào de wèntí lái le.
*Okay. Now the most important question.*

**莎莎：**你说。
Nǐ shuō.
*Go ahead.*

**波哥：**他们一直给我夹菜，一直说"多吃一点"。我真的吃不下了，怎么办？
Tāmen yìzhí gěi wǒ jiācài, yìzhí shuō "duō chī yìdiǎn". Wǒ zhēnde chī bu xià le, zěnme bàn?
*They keep serving me food and saying "eat a little more". I truly can't eat any more — what do I do?*

**莎莎：**哈哈，这个问题，每个客人都遇到过。
Hāhā, zhège wèntí, měi ge kèrén dōu yùdào guò.
*Haha — every guest has met this problem.*

**波哥：**我不想没有礼貌，可是我的肚子说：不行了。
Wǒ bù xiǎng méiyǒu lǐmào, kěshì wǒ de dùzi shuō: bù xíng le.
*I don't want to be rude, but my stomach says: no more.*

**莎莎：**教你一句话：够了够了，我吃饱了。
Jiào nǐ yí jù huà: gòu le gòu le, wǒ chībǎo le.
*Here's your sentence: enough, enough — I'm full.*

**波哥：**够了够了，我吃饱了。
Gòu le gòu le, wǒ chībǎo le.
*Gòu le gòu le, wǒ chībǎo le.*

**莎莎：**饱，就是吃好了，不能再吃了。
Bǎo, jiùshì chī hǎo le, bù néng zài chī le.
*Bǎo means you've eaten well and can't eat any more.*

**波哥：**为什么"够了"要说两次？
Wèishénme "gòu le" yào shuō liǎng cì?
*Why say "gòu le" twice?*

**莎莎：**说两次，听起来更亲切，像家里人说话。
Shuō liǎng cì, tīng qǐlái gèng qīnqiè, xiàng jiālǐ rén shuōhuà.
*Said twice, it sounds warmer — like family talking to family.*

**波哥：**够了够了——真的像家里人。
Gòu le gòu le——zhēnde xiàng jiālǐ rén.
*Gòu le gòu le — it really does sound like family.*

**莎莎：**你还可以先加一句：太好吃了，我吃了很多。
Nǐ hái kěyǐ xiān jiā yí jù: tài hǎochī le, wǒ chī le hěnduō.
*You can also add one first: it was delicious — I ate a lot.*

**波哥：**先说好吃，再说吃饱。对方听了就开心。
Xiān shuō hǎochī, zài shuō chībǎo. Duìfāng tīng le jiù kāixīn.
*Compliment first, then "I'm full". They'll be glad to hear it.*

**莎莎：**对。可是我要告诉你一个小秘密。
Duì. Kěshì wǒ yào gàosu nǐ yí ge xiǎo mìmì.
*Right. But let me tell you a little secret.*

**波哥：**什么秘密？
Shénme mìmì?
*What secret?*

**莎莎：**你说了"我吃饱了"，对方可能还会再说一次：多吃一点，别客气！
Nǐ shuō le "wǒ chībǎo le", duìfāng kěnéng hái huì zài shuō yí cì: duō chī yìdiǎn, bié kèqi!
*After you say "I'm full", they may well say it one more time: eat a little more, don't be polite!*

**波哥：**啊？为什么？
À? Wèishénme?
*What? Why?*

**莎莎：**因为这像一个小小的舞。你退一步，我进一步。
Yīnwèi zhè xiàng yí ge xiǎoxiǎo de wǔ. Nǐ tuì yí bù, wǒ jìn yí bù.
*Because it's like a tiny dance. You step back, I step forward.*

**波哥：**这不是真的要我吃，是在说关心。
Zhè búshì zhēnde yào wǒ chī, shì zài shuō guānxīn.
*It's not really about making me eat — it's a way of showing care.*

**莎莎：**对。一句话的意思，有时候不在词里，在人心里。
Duì. Yí jù huà de yìsi, yǒushíhou bù zài cí lǐ, zài rén xīnlǐ.
*Exactly. Sometimes a sentence's meaning isn't in the words — it's in the heart.*

**波哥：**所以我不用怕，也不用真的一直吃。
Suǒyǐ wǒ búyòng pà, yě búyòng zhēnde yìzhí chī.
*So I don't need to be scared, and I don't need to actually keep eating.*

**莎莎：**对。你笑着再说一次"真的吃饱了"，就可以了。没有人会生气。
Duì. Nǐ xiào zhe zài shuō yí cì "zhēnde chībǎo le", jiù kěyǐ le. Méiyǒu rén huì shēngqì.
*Right. Smile and say "really, I'm full" once more, and that's it. Nobody will be upset.*

**波哥：**中国人的饭桌，原来这么多话在菜里。
Zhōngguórén de fànzhuō, yuánlái zhème duō huà zài cài lǐ.
*The Chinese dinner table — so much is being said inside the dishes.*

**莎莎：**对。如果你想在坐上饭桌以前，先听懂这些话，就订阅慢慢中文。
Duì. Rúguǒ nǐ xiǎng zài zuò shàng fànzhuō yǐqián, xiān tīngdǒng zhèxiē huà, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*Yes. If you'd like to understand these words before you sit at that table, subscribe to Manman Zhongwen.*

**波哥：**每个星期，我们把真实生活里的中文，慢慢讲给你听。
Měi ge xīngqī, wǒmen bǎ zhēnshí shēnghuó lǐ de Zhōngwén, mànmàn jiǎng gěi nǐ tīng.
*Every week, we take the Chinese of real life and explain it slowly.*

**莎莎：**先在这里听懂，再到饭桌上用。
Xiān zài zhèlǐ tīngdǒng, zài dào fànzhuō shàng yòng.
*Understand it here first, then use it at the table.*

**波哥：**哈哈，先……再……，第二集的老朋友！
Hāhā, xiān…… zài……, dì èr jí de lǎo péngyou!
*Haha — 先……再……, our old friend from episode two!*


### 每个家都不一样 · Every family is different

**莎莎：**最后，还有一句重要的话。
Zuìhòu, háiyǒu yí jù zhòngyào de huà.
*Finally, one more important thing.*

**波哥：**你说。
Nǐ shuō.
*Go ahead.*

**莎莎：**每个家都不一样。有的家热闹，有的家安静；有的规矩多，有的很随便。
Měi ge jiā dōu bù yíyàng. Yǒu de jiā rènao, yǒu de jiā ānjìng; yǒu de guīju duō, yǒu de hěn suíbiàn.
*Every family is different. Some are lively, some are quiet; some keep many customs, some are relaxed.*

**波哥：**所以今天说的，不是"必须这样"？
Suǒyǐ jīntiān shuō de, búshì "bìxū zhèyàng"?
*So what we said today isn't "you must do this"?*

**莎莎：**对，是常见的样子。你到了那个家，用眼睛看，跟着做，就好。
Duì, shì chángjiàn de yàngzi. Nǐ dào le nàge jiā, yòng yǎnjing kàn, gēnzhe zuò, jiù hǎo.
*Right — it's what's common. In that home, watch with your eyes, follow along, and you'll be fine.*

**波哥：**看别人怎么做，我就怎么做。这个办法哪里都能用。
Kàn biéren zěnme zuò, wǒ jiù zěnme zuò. Zhège bànfǎ nǎlǐ dōu néng yòng.
*Watch what others do and do the same. That method works everywhere.*


### 快问快答 · Speed round

Answer out loud **before** Bo does.

**莎莎：**好，最后，快问快答！准备好了吗？
Hǎo, zuìhòu, kuài wèn kuài dá! Zhǔnbèi hǎo le ma?
*Okay, finally: speed round! Ready?*

**波哥：**准备好了！
Zhǔnbèi hǎo le!
*Ready!*

**莎莎：**菜放在哪里？
Cài fàng zài nǎlǐ?
*Where do the dishes go?*

**波哥：**桌子中间。菜是大家的，饭碗是自己的。
Zhuōzi zhōngjiān. Cài shì dàjiā de, fànwǎn shì zìjǐ de.
*The middle of the table. Dishes are everyone's; the rice bowl is your own.*

**莎莎：**去做客，手里带什么？
Qù zuòkè, shǒulǐ dài shénme?
*Going as a guest — what's in your hand?*

**波哥：**带一点水果。
Dài yìdiǎn shuǐguǒ.
*A little fruit.*

**莎莎：**吃到好吃的菜，说什么？
Chī dào hǎochī de cài, shuō shénme?
*The food is delicious — what do you say?*

**波哥：**太好吃了！
Tài hǎochī le!
*太好吃了!*

**莎莎：**真的吃不下了，说什么？
Zhēnde chī bu xià le, shuō shénme?
*Truly can't eat more — what do you say?*

**波哥：**够了够了，我吃饱了。
Gòu le gòu le, wǒ chībǎo le.
*Gòu le gòu le, wǒ chībǎo le.*

**莎莎：**有人给你夹菜，是什么意思？
Yǒu rén gěi nǐ jiācài, shì shénme yìsi?
*Someone puts food in your bowl — what does it mean?*

**波哥：**是关心，是没有说出来的"我爱你"。
Shì guānxīn, shì méiyǒu shuō chūlái de "wǒ ài nǐ".
*Care — an unspoken "I love you".*

**莎莎：**多吃一点，别客气——真正的意思是？
Duō chī yìdiǎn, bié kèqi——zhēnzhèng de yìsi shì?
*"Eat a little more, don't be polite" — the real meaning?*

**波哥：**你来了，我很高兴。
Nǐ lái le, wǒ hěn gāoxìng.
*You came, and I'm happy.*

**莎莎：**全对！你可以去做客了。
Quán duì! Nǐ kěyǐ qù zuòkè le.
*All correct! You're ready to be a guest.*


### 结尾 · Ending

**波哥：**好，今天我们说了中国人的家庭聚餐。
Hǎo, jīntiān wǒmen shuō le Zhōngguórén de jiātíng jùcān.
*So — today we talked about the Chinese family dinner.*

**莎莎：**菜在中间，大家一起吃。夹菜和"多吃一点"，都是爱的话。
Cài zài zhōngjiān, dàjiā yìqǐ chī. Jiācài hé "duō chī yìdiǎn", dōu shì ài de huà.
*Dishes in the middle, everyone eats together. Serving food and "eat a little more" are both words of love.*

**波哥：**做客三件小事：带一点水果，说"太好吃了"，吃多少拿多少。
Zuòkè sān jiàn xiǎo shì: dài yìdiǎn shuǐguǒ, shuō "tài hǎochī le", chī duōshao ná duōshao.
*Three small guest things: bring a little fruit, say "太好吃了", take only what you'll eat.*

**莎莎：**吃不下了，就笑着说：够了够了，我吃饱了。
Chī bu xià le, jiù xiào zhe shuō: gòu le gòu le, wǒ chībǎo le.
*Can't eat more? Smile and say: enough, enough — I'm full.*

**波哥：**还有那句最重要的：多吃一点，别客气。现在你听懂它了。
Háiyǒu nà jù zuì zhòngyào de: duō chī yìdiǎn, bié kèqi. Xiànzài nǐ tīngdǒng tā le.
*And the sentence that matters most: eat a little more, don't be polite. Now you truly understand it.*

**莎莎：**别忘了你的小事：用"别客气"写一句话，放在评论区。
Bié wàng le nǐ de xiǎo shì: yòng "bié kèqi" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Don't forget your small task: one "bié kèqi" sentence in the comments.*

**波哥：**下一集，有一点特别。
Xià yì jí, yǒu yìdiǎn tèbié.
*Next episode is a little special.*

**莎莎：**对。下一集是第三十集，我们这一季的最后一集。
Duì. Xià yì jí shì dì sānshí jí, wǒmen zhè yí jì de zuìhòu yì jí.
*Yes. The next one is episode thirty — the last episode of this season.*

**波哥：**最后一集？这么快？
Zuìhòu yì jí? Zhème kuài?
*The last one? Already?*

**莎莎：**对。我们想和你一起，回头看看这三个月的路。
Duì. Wǒmen xiǎng hé nǐ yìqǐ, huítóu kànkan zhè sān ge yuè de lù.
*Yes. We want to look back with you at these three months of road.*

**波哥：**三个月前你听不懂的话，现在呢？
Sān ge yuè qián nǐ tīng bu dǒng de huà, xiànzài ne?
*The sentences you couldn't understand three months ago — what about now?*

**莎莎：**现在，你比你想的听得懂。下一集见。
Xiànzài, nǐ bǐ nǐ xiǎng de tīng de dǒng. Xià yì jí jiàn.
*Now, you understand more than you think. See you next episode.*

**波哥：**我们下一集见！
Wǒmen xià yì jí jiàn!
*See you next episode!*

**莎莎：**下次见。慢慢来。
Xiàcì jiàn. Mànmàn lái.
*See you next time. Take it slow.*
