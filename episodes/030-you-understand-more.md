## Phrase of the day 今日句型：现在我会……了

现在我会 + skill + 了 = *now I can…* — the sentence that measures three months.

1. 现在我会用中文点菜了。 — *Now I can order food in Chinese.*
2. 现在我听得懂一点中文了。 — *Now I can understand a little Chinese.*
3. 现在我会对自己说"没关系"了。 — *Now I can tell myself "it's okay".*

**Your turn:** write your own 现在我会……了 sentence and post it in the comments — it's your three-month gift.

## Full transcript 全文

### 开场 · Cold open

**莎莎：**波哥，今天这一集，和以前的都不一样。
Bōgē, jīntiān zhè yì jí, hé yǐqián de dōu bù yíyàng.
*Bo, today's episode is different from all the ones before.*

**波哥：**哪里不一样？
Nǎlǐ bù yíyàng?
*Different how?*

**莎莎：**今天，我们不学新的东西。
Jīntiān, wǒmen bù xué xīn de dōngxi.
*Today, we're not learning anything new.*

**波哥：**不学新词，也不学新句子？
Bù xué xīn cí, yě bù xué xīn jùzi?
*No new words, and no new sentences?*

**莎莎：**都不学。今天我们做一件事：数一数。
Dōu bù xué. Jīntiān wǒmen zuò yí jiàn shì: shǔ yi shǔ.
*None. Today we do one thing: count.*

**波哥：**数一数？数什么？
Shǔ yi shǔ? Shǔ shénme?
*Count? Count what?*

**莎莎：**数一数，这三个月，你已经会了多少。
Shǔ yi shǔ, zhè sān ge yuè, nǐ yǐjīng huì le duōshao.
*Count how much you've come to know in these three months.*

**波哥：**啊，对了——今天是我们这一季的最后一集。
À, duì le——jīntiān shì wǒmen zhè yí jì de zuìhòu yì jí.
*Ah, that's right — today is the last episode of our season.*

**莎莎：**对，第三十集。
Duì, dì sānshí jí.
*Yes. Episode thirty.*

**波哥：**三个月前，我们说了第一句"大家好"。
Sān ge yuè qián, wǒmen shuō le dì yī jù "dàjiā hǎo".
*Three months ago, we said our very first "dàjiā hǎo".*

**莎莎：**那时候在听的你，可能一句都听不懂。
Nà shíhou zài tīng de nǐ, kěnéng yí jù dōu tīng bu dǒng.
*The you listening back then may not have understood a single sentence.*

**波哥：**可是现在，你在听，你也在懂。
Kěshì xiànzài, nǐ zài tīng, nǐ yě zài dǒng.
*But right now, you're listening — and you're understanding.*

**莎莎：**所以今天有一句最重要的话。它是真的，不是好听的话。
Suǒyǐ jīntiān yǒu yí jù zuì zhòngyào de huà. Tā shì zhēnde, búshì hǎotīng de huà.
*So today there's one sentence that matters most. It's true — not just nice-sounding.*

**波哥：**哪一句？
Nǎ yí jù?
*Which one?*

**莎莎：**等一下你就会听到。别走开。
Děng yíxià nǐ jiù huì tīngdào. Bié zǒukāi.
*You'll hear it in a moment. Don't go away.*


### 欢迎 · Welcome

**莎莎：**大家好，欢迎来到慢慢中文。我是莎莎。
Dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng láidào Mànmàn Zhōngwén. Wǒ shì Shāsha.
*Hello everyone, welcome to Manman Zhongwen. I'm Sasha.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。在这里，我们慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Wǒ shì Bōgē. Zài zhèlǐ, wǒmen mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*I'm Bo. Here, we speak slowly and you listen slowly.*

**莎莎：**今天是第三十集，这一季的最后一集。
Jīntiān shì dì sānshí jí, zhè yí jì de zuìhòu yì jí.
*Today is episode thirty — the last episode of the season.*

**波哥：**我们一起回头看看，这条走了三个月的路。开始吧。
Wǒmen yìqǐ huítóu kànkan, zhè tiáo zǒu le sān ge yuè de lù. Kāishǐ ba.
*Let's look back together at this road we've walked for three months. Let's start.*


### 回到第一集 · Back to episode one

**莎莎：**波哥，你还记得第一集吗？
Bōgē, nǐ hái jìde dì yī jí ma?
*Bo, do you remember episode one?*

**波哥：**记得。第一集我们问：为什么你记不住中文词？
Jìde. Dì yī jí wǒmen wèn: wèishénme nǐ jì bu zhù Zhōngwén cí?
*I do. Episode one asked: why can't you remember Chinese words?*

**莎莎：**那时候我们说了一句话：词像朋友。
Nà shíhou wǒmen shuō le yí jù huà: cí xiàng péngyou.
*Back then we said one sentence: words are like friends.*

**波哥：**见一次，会忘；常常见，就记住了。
Jiàn yí cì, huì wàng; chángcháng jiàn, jiù jìzhù le.
*Meet once, you forget; meet often, you remember.*

**莎莎：**三个月过去了。你听我说这些词：听，说，慢，懂，朋友。
Sān ge yuè guòqù le. Nǐ tīng wǒ shuō zhèxiē cí: tīng, shuō, màn, dǒng, péngyou.
*Three months have passed. Listen to these words: tīng, shuō, màn, dǒng, péngyou.*

**波哥：**这些词，现在都是老朋友了。
Zhèxiē cí, xiànzài dōu shì lǎo péngyou le.
*These words are all old friends now.*

**莎莎：**对。你没有坐在桌子前面背它们。
Duì. Nǐ méiyǒu zuò zài zhuōzi qiánmiàn bèi tāmen.
*Right. And you never sat at a desk memorising them.*

**波哥：**你只是常常见它们。每天听一点。
Nǐ zhǐshì chángcháng jiàn tāmen. Měitiān tīng yìdiǎn.
*You just kept meeting them. A little listening every day.*

**莎莎：**第一集还有一句话：如果你每天听一点，就会记住更多。
Dì yī jí háiyǒu yí jù huà: rúguǒ nǐ měitiān tīng yìdiǎn, jiù huì jìzhù gèng duō.
*Episode one had another sentence: if you listen a little every day, you'll remember more.*

**波哥：**三个月了，这句话是真的吗？
Sān ge yuè le, zhè jù huà shì zhēnde ma?
*Three months later — is it true?*

**莎莎：**你自己就是答案。你还在这里，你听懂的，比那时候多得多。
Nǐ zìjǐ jiùshì dá'àn. Nǐ hái zài zhèlǐ, nǐ tīngdǒng de, bǐ nà shíhou duō de duō.
*You yourself are the answer. You're still here, and you understand far more than back then.*


### 小任务 · Your turn

**波哥：**说到这里，请你做一件小事。
Shuō dào zhèlǐ, qǐng nǐ zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì.
*Speaking of which — one small thing for you.*

**莎莎：**评论区时间到了？这次你来说？
Pínglùnqū shíjiān dào le? Zhè cì nǐ lái shuō?
*Comment-section time? Your turn to set the task?*

**波哥：**对，这次我来。用"现在我会……了"写一句话。
Duì, zhè cì wǒ lái. Yòng "xiànzài wǒ huì…… le" xiě yí jù huà.
*Yes, my turn. Write one sentence with "现在我会……了" — now I can….*

**莎莎：**我先来一个例子：现在我会用中文点菜了。
Wǒ xiān lái yí ge lìzi: xiànzài wǒ huì yòng Zhōngwén diǎn cài le.
*An example from me: now I can order food in Chinese.*

**波哥：**或者简单一点：现在我听得懂一点中文了。
Huòzhě jiǎndān yìdiǎn: xiànzài wǒ tīng de dǒng yìdiǎn Zhōngwén le.
*Or simpler: now I can understand a little Chinese.*

**莎莎：**把你的那一句，写在评论区。
Bǎ nǐ de nà yí jù, xiě zài pínglùnqū.
*Write your sentence in the comments.*

**波哥：**这一句话，就是你三个月的礼物。
Zhè yí jù huà, jiùshì nǐ sān ge yuè de lǐwù.
*That one sentence is your three-month gift.*

**莎莎：**我们会看，也会回你。每一句我们都想看。
Wǒmen huì kàn, yě huì huí nǐ. Měi yí jù wǒmen dōu xiǎng kàn.
*We read and reply. We want to see every single one.*


### 数一数你会的 · Count what you can do

**波哥：**好，现在开始数一数吧。
Hǎo, xiànzài kāishǐ shǔ yi shǔ ba.
*Okay — let's start counting.*

**莎莎：**数一数，就是一个一个地数。今天数的，是你会的东西。
Shǔ yi shǔ, jiùshì yí ge yí ge de shǔ. Jīntiān shǔ de, shì nǐ huì de dōngxi.
*Shǔ yi shǔ means counting one by one. Today we count the things you can do.*

**波哥：**第一个！去饭馆，会点菜吗？你先想一想，再说出来。
Dì yī ge! Qù fànguǎn, huì diǎn cài ma? Nǐ xiān xiǎng yi xiǎng, zài shuō chūlái.
*Number one! At a restaurant — can you order? Think first, then say it out loud.*

**莎莎：**我先小声说答案：我要一个这个，不要辣。
Wǒ xiān xiǎoshēng shuō dá'àn: wǒ yào yí ge zhège, búyào là.
*I'll whisper the answer: I'll have this one, not spicy.*

**波哥：**对！第四集的句子。那听不懂菜名，怎么办？
Duì! Dì sì jí de jùzi. Nà tīng bu dǒng càimíng, zěnme bàn?
*Yes! Episode four's sentence. And if you can't understand a dish's name?*

**莎莎：**用手指菜单。手指是最好的翻译！
Yòng shǒu zhǐ càidān. Shǒuzhǐ shì zuìhǎo de fānyì!
*Point at the menu. The finger is the best translator!*

**波哥：**哈哈，老朋友又来了。第二个：别人问你一件事，你不想马上说"好"。
Hāhā, lǎo péngyou yòu lái le. Dì èr ge: biéren wèn nǐ yí jiàn shì, nǐ bù xiǎng mǎshàng shuō "hǎo".
*Haha, another old friend. Number two: someone asks you something, and you don't want to say "yes" right away.*

**莎莎：**有一句很客气的话：我再想一想，好吗？
Yǒu yí jù hěn kèqi de huà: wǒ zài xiǎng yi xiǎng, hǎo ma?
*There's a very gracious line: let me think about it, okay?*

**波哥：**对。不用说"不"，也不让人难过。第二十三集。
Duì. Búyòng shuō "bù", yě bú ràng rén nánguò. Dì èrshísān jí.
*Right. No "no" needed, and nobody feels hurt. Episode twenty-three.*

**莎莎：**第三个：你说错了一句话，心里不舒服。你对自己说什么？
Dì sān ge: nǐ shuōcuò le yí jù huà, xīnlǐ bù shūfu. Nǐ duì zìjǐ shuō shénme?
*Number three: you said something wrong and it stings. What do you tell yourself?*

**波哥：**说错了没关系，再说一次就好。
Shuōcuò le méi guānxi, zài shuō yí cì jiù hǎo.
*Getting it wrong is okay — just say it again.*

**莎莎：**对。这句话，是说给自己听的。第五集。
Duì. Zhè jù huà, shì shuō gěi zìjǐ tīng de. Dì wǔ jí.
*Yes. That sentence is said to yourself. Episode five.*

**波哥：**那时候我们还说：说错，是学说话的一部分。
Nà shíhou wǒmen hái shuō: shuōcuò, shì xué shuōhuà de yí bùfen.
*Back then we also said: mistakes are part of learning to speak.*

**莎莎：**现在你敢开口了，就是最好的样子。
Xiànzài nǐ gǎn kāikǒu le, jiùshì zuìhǎo de yàngzi.
*Now you dare to speak. That's the best look on you.*

**波哥：**对，敢说，比说得对更重要。
Duì, gǎn shuō, bǐ shuōdeduì gèng zhòngyào.
*Right. Daring to speak beats saying it perfectly.*

**莎莎：**第四个：一件事很大，你不知道从哪里开始。
Dì sì ge: yí jiàn shì hěn dà, nǐ bù zhīdào cóng nǎlǐ kāishǐ.
*Number four: something is huge and you don't know where to start.*

**波哥：**先做一件小事，再做下一件。
Xiān zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì, zài zuò xià yí jiàn.
*Do one small thing first, then the next one.*

**莎莎：**先……再……，小林的星期一早上。第二集。
Xiān…… zài……, Xiǎolín de xīngqīyī zǎoshang. Dì èr jí.
*先……再…… — Xiaolin's Monday morning. Episode two.*

**波哥：**第五个：每天只听十分钟，真的有用吗？
Dì wǔ ge: měitiān zhǐ tīng shí fēnzhōng, zhēnde yǒu yòng ma?
*Number five: only ten minutes of listening a day — does it really work?*

**莎莎：**你听得越多，懂得越多。
Nǐ tīng de yuè duō, dǒng de yuè duō.
*The more you listen, the more you understand.*

**波哥：**第三集。还有上一集的饭桌呢！
Dì sān jí. Háiyǒu shàng yì jí de fànzhuō ne!
*Episode three. And last episode's dinner table!*

**莎莎：**有人一直给你夹菜，说：多吃一点，别客气！
Yǒu rén yìzhí gěi nǐ jiācài, shuō: duō chī yìdiǎn, bié kèqi!
*Someone keeps putting food in your bowl: eat a little more, don't be polite!*

**波哥：**我笑着说：够了够了，我吃饱了！
Wǒ xiào zhe shuō: gòu le gòu le, wǒ chībǎo le!
*And I smile and say: enough, enough — I'm full!*

**莎莎：**哈哈，你现在是一个很好的客人了。
Hāhā, nǐ xiànzài shì yí ge hěn hǎo de kèrén le.
*Haha — you're a very good guest now.*

**波哥：**再来一个：你想请人帮忙，开口第一句？
Zàiláiyí ge: nǐ xiǎng qǐng rén bāngmáng, kāikǒu dìyí jù?
*One more: you want to ask for help. The opening line?*

**莎莎：**麻烦你帮我一下，可以吗？第十五集。
Máfan nǐ bāng wǒ yíxià, kěyǐ ma? Dìshíwǔjí.
*"Mafan ni bang wo yixia, keyi ma?" Episode 15.*

**波哥：**还有，听不懂的时候呢？
Háiyǒu, tīngbùdǒng de shíhou ne?
*And when you don't understand?*

**莎莎：**请再说一次，可以说慢一点吗？第九集。
Qǐng zàishuō yícì, kěyǐ shuōmàn yìdiǎn ma? Dìjiǔjí.
*"Please say it again, a bit slower?" Episode 9.*

**波哥：**你看，数了一下，已经八个了。
Nǐ kàn, shù le yíxià, yǐjīng bāge le.
*Look at that, we've counted eight already.*

**莎莎：**点菜，慢慢说"不"，对自己说"没关系"，先做一件小事。还有请人帮忙，听不懂再问一次，坐上饭桌。
Diǎncài, mànmànshuō"bú", duì zìjǐ shuō"méiguānxì", xiān zuò yíjiàn xiǎoshì. Háiyǒu qǐngrén bāngmáng, tīngbùdǒng zài wèn yícì, zuò shàng fànzhuō.
*Ordering food, a soft "no", telling yourself "it's okay", starting with one small thing. Plus asking for help, asking again when you don't follow, and taking your seat at the table.*

**波哥：**这些都是真的生活，不是书上的练习。
Zhèxiē dōu shì zhēnde shēnghuó, búshì shū shàng de liànxí.
*All of it real life — not textbook exercises.*

**莎莎：**三个月前，这些句子对你来说，可能只是声音。
Sān ge yuè qián, zhèxiē jùzi duì nǐ lái shuō, kěnéng zhǐshì shēngyīn.
*Three months ago, these sentences may have been nothing but sound to you.*

**波哥：**现在，它们是你会用的话。
Xiànzài, tāmen shì nǐ huì yòng de huà.
*Now they're words you can use.*


### 三个月前和现在 · Then and now

**莎莎：**所以，今天最重要的一句话，现在说给你听。
Suǒyǐ, jīntiān zuì zhòngyào de yí jù huà, xiànzài shuō gěi nǐ tīng.
*So — today's most important sentence, said to you right now.*

**波哥：**你说，我们都听着。
Nǐ shuō, wǒmen dōu tīng zhe.
*Say it. We're all listening.*

**莎莎：**三个月前你听不懂，现在你听懂了。
Sān ge yuè qián nǐ tīng bu dǒng, xiànzài nǐ tīngdǒng le.
*Three months ago you couldn't understand — now you do.*

**波哥：**三个月前你听不懂，现在你听懂了。
Sān ge yuè qián nǐ tīng bu dǒng, xiànzài nǐ tīngdǒng le.
*Three months ago you couldn't understand — now you do.*

**莎莎：**这句话，不是"加油"，也不是"你真棒"。
Zhè jù huà, búshì "jiāyóu", yě búshì "nǐ zhēn bàng".
*This sentence isn't "jiāyóu", and it isn't "you're amazing".*

**波哥：**它是一件真的事。
Tā shì yí jiàn zhēnde shì.
*It's simply a true thing.*

**莎莎：**对。你现在就在听懂这一句——这就是最好的答案。
Duì. Nǐ xiànzài jiù zài tīngdǒng zhè yí jù——zhè jiùshì zuìhǎo de dá'àn.
*Right. You're understanding this very sentence right now — that is the best answer.*

**波哥：**不用别人告诉你，你自己的耳朵知道。
Búyòng biéren gàosu nǐ, nǐ zìjǐ de ěrduo zhīdào.
*Nobody has to tell you. Your own ears know.*

**莎莎：**如果你还是不太相信，有一个很好的办法。
Rúguǒ nǐ háishì bú tài xiāngxìn, yǒu yí ge hěn hǎo de bànfǎ.
*And if you still don't quite believe it, there's a very good way to check.*

**波哥：**什么办法？
Shénme bànfǎ?
*What way?*

**莎莎：**今天听完这一集，回去再听一次第一集。
Jīntiān tīng wán zhè yì jí, huíqù zài tīng yí cì dì yī jí.
*After you finish this episode, go back and listen to episode one again.*

**波哥：**为什么是第一集？
Wèishénme shì dì yī jí?
*Why episode one?*

**莎莎：**因为三个月前的你，和现在的你，听的是同一集。
Yīnwèi sān ge yuè qián de nǐ, hé xiànzài de nǐ, tīng de shì tóng yì jí.
*Because the you of three months ago and the you of today listen to the same episode.*

**波哥：**可是听到的，不一样了。
Kěshì tīngdào de, bù yíyàng le.
*But what you hear won't be the same.*

**莎莎：**路走了多远，回头看一眼，最清楚。
Lù zǒu le duō yuǎn, huítóu kàn yìyǎn, zuì qīngchǔ.
*How far you've walked is clearest when you look back.*

**波哥：**这一眼，比谁的话都有用。
Zhè yìyǎn, bǐ shéi dehuà dōu yǒuyòng.
*That one look is worth more than anyone's words.*

**莎莎：**对。那时候很难的句子，现在你会觉得：咦，这么简单？
Duì. Nà shíhou hěn nán de jùzi, xiànzài nǐ huì juéde: Yí, zhème jiǎndān?
*Right. Sentences that felt hard back then will make you think: huh — this easy?*

**波哥：**这个感觉，就是你走过的路。
Zhège gǎnjué, jiùshì nǐ zǒuguò de lù.
*That feeling is the road you've walked.*


### 谢谢你 · Thank you

**莎莎：**说到这里，我们想对你说一句谢谢。
Shuō dào zhèlǐ, wǒmen xiǎng duì nǐ shuō yí jù xièxie.
*And here, we want to say thank you.*

**波哥：**谢谢你，这三个月和我们在一起。
Xièxie nǐ, zhè sān ge yuè hé wǒmen zài yìqǐ.
*Thank you for being with us these three months.*

**莎莎：**一个人学中文，有一点难；一起学，就容易一点。
Yí ge rén xué Zhōngwén, yǒu yìdiǎn nán; yìqǐ xué, jiù róngyì yìdiǎn.
*Learning Chinese alone is a little hard; learning together makes it a little easier.*

**波哥：**评论区里的每一句话，我们都记得。
Pínglùn qūlǐ de měi yíjù huà, wǒmen dōu jìde.
*Every line in the comments, we remember.*

**莎莎：**你们写的句子，比我们教的还多。
Nǐmen xiě de jùzi, bǐ wǒmen jiāo de hái duō.
*You've written more sentences than we've taught.*

**波哥：**如果你想和我们一起走下面的路，就订阅慢慢中文。
Rúguǒ nǐ xiǎng hé wǒmen yìqǐ zǒu xiàmiàn de lù, jiù dìngyuè Mànmàn Zhōngwén.
*If you'd like to walk the next stretch of road with us, subscribe to Manman Zhongwen.*

**莎莎：**我们休息几天，然后带着新的一季回来。一季，就是一组新的节目。
Wǒmen xiūxi jǐ tiān, ránhòu dài zhe xīn de yí jì huílái. Yí jì, jiùshì yì zǔ xīn de jiémù.
*We'll rest a few days, then come back with a new season. A season is one new set of episodes.*

**波哥：**新的故事，新的对话。还是慢慢说，你慢慢听。
Xīn de gùshi, xīn de duìhuà. Háishì mànmàn shuō, nǐ mànmàn tīng.
*New stories, new conversations. Still speaking slowly, while you listen slowly.*


### 季版快问快答 · The season speed round

Answer out loud **before** Bo does.

**莎莎：**好，最后一次，快问快答！这次是一季的快问快答。
Hǎo, zuìhòu yí cì, kuài wèn kuài dá! Zhè cì shì yí jì de kuài wèn kuài dá.
*Okay — one last time: speed round! This time it's the whole season's speed round.*

**波哥：**三十集的快问快答！准备好了！
Sānshí jí de kuài wèn kuài dá! Zhǔnbèi hǎo le!
*The thirty-episode speed round! Ready!*

**莎莎：**词为什么会忘？
Cí wèishénme huì wàng?
*Why do words get forgotten?*

**波哥：**因为见得太少。词像朋友，常常见，就记住了。
Yīnwèi jiàn de tài shǎo. Cí xiàng péngyou, chángcháng jiàn, jiù jìzhù le.
*Because you met them too few times. Words are like friends — meet often, and you remember.*

**莎莎：**一件事很大，不知道怎么开始？
Yí jiàn shì hěn dà, bù zhīdào zěnme kāishǐ?
*Something is huge and you don't know how to start?*

**波哥：**先做一件小事，再做下一件。
Xiān zuò yí jiàn xiǎo shì, zài zuò xià yí jiàn.
*Do one small thing first, then the next one.*

**莎莎：**每天听一点，有用吗？
Měitiān tīng yìdiǎn, yǒu yòng ma?
*A little listening every day — does it work?*

**波哥：**你听得越多，懂得越多。
Nǐ tīng de yuè duō, dǒng de yuè duō.
*The more you listen, the more you understand.*

**莎莎：**在饭馆，听不懂菜名？
Zài fànguǎn, tīng bu dǒng càimíng?
*At a restaurant, can't understand the dish names?*

**波哥：**手指是最好的翻译！
Shǒuzhǐ shì zuìhǎo de fānyì!
*The finger is the best translator!*

**莎莎：**想让别人说慢一点？
Xiǎng ràng biéren shuōmàn yìdiǎn?
*Want someone to slow down?*

**波哥：**请说慢一点，谢谢！
Qǐng shuō màn yìdiǎn, xièxie!
*"Please speak slower, thanks!"*

**莎莎：**说错了，对自己说什么？
Shuōcuò le, duì zìjǐ shuō shénme?
*Said it wrong — what do you tell yourself?*

**波哥：**说错了没关系，再说一次就好。
Shuōcuò le méi guānxi, zài shuō yí cì jiù hǎo.
*Getting it wrong is okay — just say it again.*

**莎莎：**不想马上说"好"？
Bù xiǎng mǎshàng shuō "hǎo"?
*Don't want to say "yes" right away?*

**波哥：**我再想一想，好吗？
Wǒ zài xiǎng yi xiǎng, hǎo ma?
*Let me think about it, okay?*

**莎莎：**请人帮忙，第一句？
Qǐngrén bāngmáng, dìyí jù?
*Asking for help, the first line?*

**波哥：**麻烦你帮我一下，可以吗？
Máfan nǐ bāng wǒ yíxià, kěyǐ ma?
*"Mafan ni bang wo yixia, keyi ma?"*

**莎莎：**吃不下了？
Chī bu xià le?
*Can't eat any more?*

**波哥：**够了够了，我吃饱了！
Gòu le gòu le, wǒ chībǎo le!
*Enough, enough — I'm full!*

**莎莎：**最后一个。三个月前你听不懂……
Zuìhòu yí ge. Sān ge yuè qián nǐ tīng bu dǒng……
*Last one. Three months ago you couldn't understand…*

**波哥：**现在你听懂了！
Xiànzài nǐ tīngdǒng le!
*Now you do!*

**莎莎：**全对！三十集，你都在。这就是每天听一点的力量。
Quán duì! Sānshí jí, nǐ dōu zài. Zhè jiùshì měitiān tīng yìdiǎn de lìliang.
*All correct! Thirty episodes, and you were here. That's the power of a little every day.*


### 结尾 · Ending

**波哥：**好，今天我们没有学新的东西。
Hǎo, jīntiān wǒmen méiyǒu xué xīn de dōngxi.
*So — today we didn't learn anything new.*

**莎莎：**我们只是数了数：你已经会了这么多。
Wǒmen zhǐshì shǔ le shǔ: nǐ yǐjīng huì le zhème duō.
*We just counted: you already know this much.*

**波哥：**还有那句真的话：三个月前你听不懂，现在你听懂了。
Háiyǒu nà jù zhēnde huà: sān ge yuè qián nǐ tīng bu dǒng, xiànzài nǐ tīngdǒng le.
*And that true sentence: three months ago you couldn't understand — now you do.*

**莎莎：**别忘了你的小事：用"现在我会……了"写一句话，放在评论区。
Bié wàng le nǐ de xiǎo shì: yòng "xiànzài wǒ huì…… le" xiě yí jù huà, fàng zài pínglùnqū.
*Don't forget your small task: one "现在我会……了" sentence in the comments.*

**波哥：**今天没有下集预告，因为下一季的节目单，还没有写完。
Jīntiān méiyǒu xià jí yùgào, yīnwèi xià yí jì de jiémùdān, hái méiyǒu xiě wán.
*No preview today, because next season's programme isn't written yet.*

**莎莎：**我们想请你一起写。
Wǒmen xiǎng qǐng nǐ yìqǐ xiě.
*We'd like you to help write it.*

**波哥：**你想让我们聊什么话题？写在评论区。
Nǐ xiǎng ràng wǒmen liáo shénme huàtí? Xiě zài pínglùnqū.
*What topics would you like us to talk about? Write them in the comments.*

**莎莎：**我们会先看：话题适不适合频道，对学习有没有帮助，安不安全。
Wǒmen huì xiān kàn: huàtí shì bu shìhé píndào, duì xuéxí yǒu méiyǒu bāngzhù, ān bu ānquán.
*We'll look first: does the topic fit the channel, does it help learning, is it safe.*

**波哥：**合适的，我们放进下一季的节目单。
Héshì de, wǒmen fàng jìn xià yí jì de jiémùdān.
*The suitable ones go onto next season's list.*

**莎莎：**不能保证每个都做，写一次就够了。
Bù néng bǎozhèng měi ge dōu zuò, xiě yí cì jiù gòu le.
*We can't promise to make every one — and writing it once is enough.*

**波哥：**你的一句话，可能就是下一季的一集。
Nǐ de yí jù huà, kěnéng jiùshì xià yí jì de yì jí.
*Your one sentence might become an episode next season.*

**莎莎：**最后，再说一次谢谢。谢谢你的每一个十分钟。
Zuìhòu, zài shuō yí cì xièxie. Xièxie nǐ de měi yí ge shí fēnzhōng.
*Finally, thank you once more. Thank you for every single ten minutes.*

**波哥：**也别忘了，回去听听第一集。
Yě bié wàng le, huíqù tīngting dì yī jí.
*And don't forget — go listen to episode one again.*

**莎莎：**你会在里面，听到你自己走过的路。
Nǐ huì zài lǐmiàn, tīngdào nǐ zìjǐ zǒuguò de lù.
*Inside it, you'll hear the road you yourself have walked.*

**波哥：**那是三十集以前的你。
Nà shì sānshí jí yǐqián de nǐ.
*That was you thirty episodes ago.*

**莎莎：**现在的你，已经在路上了。
Xiànzài de nǐ, yǐjīng zài lùshàng le.
*The you of today is already on the road.*

**波哥：**三个月前你听不懂，现在你听懂了。下一季，我们接着走。
Sān ge yuè qián nǐ tīng bu dǒng, xiànzài nǐ tīngdǒng le. Xià yí jì, wǒmen jiēzhe zǒu.
*Three months ago you couldn't understand — now you do. Next season, we keep walking.*

**莎莎：**慢慢走，就走得远。
Mànmàn zǒu, jiù zǒu de yuǎn.
*Walk slowly, and you walk far.*

**波哥：**我是波哥。
Wǒ shì Bōgē.
*I'm Bo.*

**莎莎：**我是莎莎。我们下一季再见。
Wǒ shì Shāsha. Wǒmen xià yí jì zàijiàn.
*I'm Sasha. See you next season.*

**波哥：**再见。慢慢来。
Zàijiàn. Mànmàn lái.
*Goodbye. Take it slow.*
