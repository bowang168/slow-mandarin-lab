**Slow Mandarin Lab** is a slow-Chinese listening podcast for adult learners:
two native hosts, natural conversations, and whole-sentence captions in
**Hanzi + pinyin + English** — so you can follow complete Mandarin thoughts, not isolated words.

▶ Watch on YouTube: [@SlowMandarinLab](https://www.youtube.com/@SlowMandarinLab)

Every episode gets a free study page here: the full transcript (Hanzi · pinyin · English),
key vocabulary, the pattern of the day, and a speaking task.
